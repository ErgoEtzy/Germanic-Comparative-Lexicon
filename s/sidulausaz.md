# *sidulausaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sidulausaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sidulausaz

### Etymology

From *siduz (“custom, manner”) + *-lausaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *lewh₁-, *sh₂ey-, *swedʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsi.ðu.ˌlɑu̯.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ ill-mannered, without manners
