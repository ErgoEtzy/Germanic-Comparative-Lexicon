## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *samafadrijaz

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *somHo-ph₂tr-(i)yos; equivalent to *samaz + *fadēr + *-jaz. Formally identical to Ancient Greek ὁμοπάτριος (homopátrios, “of the same father”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-yós, *peh₂-, *ph₂tḗr, *somHós
