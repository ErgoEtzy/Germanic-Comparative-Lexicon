# *snigilaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/snigilaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *snigilaz

### Etymology

From *sneganą (“to crawl, creeping”) + *-ilaz (agent suffix). Parallel formation with *snagilaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)neg-, *-dʰlom, *-e-ló-s, *-lós, *-tlom, *-trom

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsni.ɣi.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, masculine, reconstruction)_ snail (alternative form of *snagilaz)
