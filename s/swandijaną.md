## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swandijaną

### Etymology

From *swindaną (“to disappear”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)wendʰ-, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *dʰgʷʰey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɑn.di.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to make disappear
2. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to squander, to waste
