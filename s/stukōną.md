# *stukōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stuk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stukōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)tewg- (“to beat, push”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)tewg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstu.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to push, to thrust
