## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *strawjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *strow-éye-ti, a causative from the root *strew- (“to spread, strew”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *strew-, *strow-éye-ti, *strowéyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstrɑu̯.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to strew
