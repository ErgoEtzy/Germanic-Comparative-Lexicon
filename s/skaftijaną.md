[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skaftijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skaftijaną

### Etymology

Factitive derived from unattested *skaftaz ("ready" < "shaped, created"), original past participle to *skapjaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑɸ.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make ready; to prepare
