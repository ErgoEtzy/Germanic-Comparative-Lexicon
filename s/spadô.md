## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spadô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sph₂-dʰ-, from *(s)peh₂-. Cognate with Ancient Greek σπάθη (spáthē, “blade”), Hittite [script needed] (išpatar, “spear”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)peh₂-, *sph₂-dʰ-
