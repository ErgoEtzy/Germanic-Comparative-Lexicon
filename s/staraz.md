# *staraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/staraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *staraz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *storh₁-os, from Proto-Indo-European *sterh₁- (“to be stiff”); compare Tocharian A ṣtare (“effort”), as well as Ancient Greek στερεός (stereós, “firm, solid”). Along with Proto-Germanic *sturrāną, points to an unattested strong verb *steraną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ster-, *ster-os, *sterh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑ.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ stiff
