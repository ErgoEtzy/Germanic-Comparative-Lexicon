## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skuldijōną

### Etymology

From *skuldijaz (“obligated, guilty”) + *-ōną. Compare also West Germanic *skuldijan.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskul.di.jɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to owe
2. _(reconstruction)_ to commit a fault
