# *skrappōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skrapp%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skrappōną

### Etymology

An iterative formed from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker-b- (“to scratch, to engrave”) and influenced by *skrepaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ker-, *(s)ker-b-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskrɑp.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to scrape, scratch
