[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/seht%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sehtô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *swéḱstos, from *swéḱs. An ordinal to *sehs (“six”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swéḱstos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsex.tɔːː/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ sixth
