[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sn%C4%ABwan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *snīwaną

### Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Indo-European *snéygʷʰeti (“to snow”), from *sneygʷʰ-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *snéygʷʰeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsniː.wɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to snow
