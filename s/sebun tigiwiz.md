## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sebun tigiwiz

### Etymology

Either formed analogously from the lower decads, compare *þrīz tigiwiz (“thirty”), from *sebun (“seven”) + *tigiwiz (“decads, groups of ten”), displacing Proto-Germanic *sebuntēhundą (“seventy”); or inherited alongside it, from earlier *seftuntegun, from Proto-Indo-European *septm̥-deḱḿ̥(t) (“seventy”), by-form of *septḿ̥-dḱomt-h₂ (“seventy”).
