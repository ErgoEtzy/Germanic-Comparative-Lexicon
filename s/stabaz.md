# *stabaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stabaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stabaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *stóbʰ-o-s (“staff”), from *stebʰ- (“to stand still; to harden”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *stebʰ-, *stóbʰ-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑ.βɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ staff, stick
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a letter of the (runic) alphabet
