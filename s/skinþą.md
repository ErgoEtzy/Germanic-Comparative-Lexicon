# *skinþą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skin%C3%BE%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skinþą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)kenH- (“skin, rind; to peel”). Cognate with Lithuanian ski̇̀nti (“to pluck, pick”), Latvian šķĩt (“to pluck leaves”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kenH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskin.θɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ animal hide
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ human skin
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ fruit peel
