## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *staþô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *steh₂- (“to stand (up)”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *steh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑ.θɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ support, stand
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ stack
