## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sunuz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *suHnús (“son”), with a long vowel contraction in a pre-stressed position, per Dybo's law.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *suHnús

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsu.nuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ son
