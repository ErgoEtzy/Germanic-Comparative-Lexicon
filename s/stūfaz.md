## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stūfaz

### Etymology

Related to Sanskrit स्तूप (stūpa, “shock of hair”) and Ancient Greek στύπος (stúpos, “stem, stump, block”). Potentially related to *stūkōn.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

### Noun
