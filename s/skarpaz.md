# *skarpaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skarpaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skarpaz

### Etymology

An o-grade adjective derived from *skerpaną, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kerb-, from *(s)ker- (“to cut”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kerb-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑr.pɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ sharp
2. _(reconstruction)_ pointed
