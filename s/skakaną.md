## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skakaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)kek- (“to shake, stir”), with unexpected *-k- instead of *-h-, either through contamination or back-formation from the iterative *skakkōną (“to shake”). See *skehaną (“to jump, emerge”) for more cognates.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kek-, *skek-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑ.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to swing
2. _(reconstruction)_ to shake
3. _(reconstruction)_ to escape
