## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stīganą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *stéygʰeti, from *steygʰ- (“to walk, climb”). Cognate with Ancient Greek στείχω (steíkhō, “I go”) and Old Church Slavonic стигнѫ (stignǫ, “I come”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *stéygʰeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstiː.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to go up; climb, ascend
