# *staluz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/staluz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *staluz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *stel- (“to put, place; be stiff; post, trunk”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *stel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑ.luz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ post, stake, stud
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ staff, rod
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ trunk, stalk
4. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ stump, tail
