## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slidô

### Etymology

Zero-grade form of the root of *slīdaną + *-ô, literally “slider”.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sleydʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsli.ðɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ sled, sleigh
