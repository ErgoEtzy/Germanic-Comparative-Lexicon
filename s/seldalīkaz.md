# *seldalīkaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/seldal%C4%ABkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *seldalīkaz

### Etymology

From *seldaz (“rare”) + *-līkaz (“-like”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *sel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsel.dɑ.liː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ unusual, uncommon; strange
2. _(reconstruction)_ extraordinary; wonderful; miraculous
