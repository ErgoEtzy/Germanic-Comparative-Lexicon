# *slimpaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/slimpan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slimpaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)lembʰ- (“to hang limply”). It likely has the same origins as *limpaną with an s-mobile attached to the root.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)lembʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈslim.pɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to slip, to slide
2. _(reconstruction)_ to let go of, to release
3. _(reconstruction)_ to avoid, to escape
