# *spangijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/spangijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spangijaną

### Etymology

From *spangǭ (“spangle, clasp, fastening”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspɑŋ.ɡi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to spangle, to clasp together
2. _(reconstruction)_ to limit, to constrain
