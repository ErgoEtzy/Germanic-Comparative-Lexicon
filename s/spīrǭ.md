# *spīrǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sp%C4%ABr%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spīrǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *spey- (“peak; summit; sharp point; stick”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *spey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspiː.rɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ peak; summit; top
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ pole; rod; reed; stalk
