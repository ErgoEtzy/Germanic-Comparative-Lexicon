[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/swegl%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sweglōną

### Etymology

From *sweglō (“flute”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *-is, *-yes-, *-yōs, *sweh₂gʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsweɣ.lɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to play the flute
