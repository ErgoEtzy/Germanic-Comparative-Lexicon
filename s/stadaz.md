# *stadaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stadaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stadaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sth₂tós, verbal adjective formation from *steh₂- (“to stand”). Cognate with Ancient Greek στατός (statós, “placed, standing”), Sanskrit स्थित (sthita, “standing”), Latin status (“fixed, set”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *steh₂-, *sth₂tós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑ.ðɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ standing, standing still, resting
