# *swībaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sw%C4%ABban%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swībaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kswéybʰ-e-ti, thematic root present of *ksweybʰ- (“to move swiftly, swing, whip”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kswéybʰ-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswiː.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to revolve; move around; sway; stray
2. _(reconstruction)_ to sweep
3. _(reconstruction)_ to finish
