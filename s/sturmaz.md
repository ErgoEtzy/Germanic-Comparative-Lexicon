[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sturmaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sturmaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *stur-mo-s, a mo-stem nominal formed from *staurijaną (“to destroy, disturb”), for which see *staurijan.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstur.mɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ storm
