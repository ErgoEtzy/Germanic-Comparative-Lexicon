# *strīkaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/str%C4%ABkan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *strīkaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *streyg- (“to stroke, rub, press”). Compare also *streukaną (“to stroke”), which appears to be unrelated, despite strikingly similar phonetics and semantics.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *streyg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstriː.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stroke, to rub
