## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skūmaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)kewH- (“to cover, mask, hide, conceal”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kewH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskuː.mɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ film; grime; scum
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ foam; froth
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ yeast
