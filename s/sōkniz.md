# *sōkniz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/s%C5%8Dkniz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sōkniz

### Etymology

From *sōkijaną (“to seek, look for; to dispute”) + *-niz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-nis, *séh₂gyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɔːk.niz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ investigation; probe; examination; inquiry
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ dispute; quarrel; contest; struggle; fight
