# *swaitaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/swaitan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swaitaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *swoyd-e-ti, thematic o-grade present of *sweyd- (“to sweat”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swoyd-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɑi̯.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sweat
