**Reconstruction:** *swalīkaz

### Etymology

From *swa + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *swé

- IPA: `/ˈswa.liː.kɑz/`

### Determiner

1. _(reconstruction)_ such, that kind of, that sort of
