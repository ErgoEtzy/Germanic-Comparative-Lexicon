# *skallijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skallijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skallijaną

### Etymology

Causative of *skellaną, from Proto-Indo-European *skel- (“to sound, resound”), perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *kelh₁- (“to call, shout, scream, make a sound”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kelh₁-, *skel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑl.li.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make a sound; cause (one) to sound; ring
