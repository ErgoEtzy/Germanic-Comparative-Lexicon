## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *snaidō

### Etymology

Nominal derivative from *snīþaną (“to cut”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsnɑi̯.ðɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a cut, cutting, something cut off
