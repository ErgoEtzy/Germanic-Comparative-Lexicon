[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/st%C5%8Dd%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stōdą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *stéh₂dʰh₁-om (“herd”) from Proto-Indo-European *steh₂- (“stand, set”). Cognate with Proto-Slavic *stado (“herd, flock”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *steh₂-, *stéh₂dʰh₁-om

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɔː.ðɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ a herd of horses
