## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *snak-

### Etymology

Unknown. Possibly onomatopoeic for sniffling; compare *snūtaz (“snout”) and *snuttaz (“snot”). See also *snēkô (“snake”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to blow, waft
2. _(reconstruction)_ to breathe; sigh
3. _(reconstruction)_ to catch wind of; sniff
