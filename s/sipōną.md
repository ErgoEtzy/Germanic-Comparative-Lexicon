[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sip%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sipōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *seyb- (“to pour, leak, trickle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *seyb-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsi.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to drop, fall, drip, trickle
