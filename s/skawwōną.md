**Reconstruction:** *skawwōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)kewh₁- (“to heed, look, feel, take note of”). Cognate with Latin cavēre (“to beware, heed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kewh₁-
