# *sturknaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sturknan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sturknaną

### Etymology

A fientive verb derived from a strong verb *sterkaną (“to stiffen”) (attested only in its past participle).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsturk.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(intransitive, reconstruction)_ to dry up
2. _(intransitive, reconstruction)_ to thicken, congeal
