# *staupaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/staupaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *staupaz

### Etymology

An adjectival formation from *stūpaną (“to stoop”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)tewp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑu̯.pɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ high
2. _(reconstruction)_ steep
