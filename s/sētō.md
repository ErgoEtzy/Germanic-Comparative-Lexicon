# *sētō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/s%C4%93t%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sētō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sed- (“to sit”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sed-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɛː.tɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ place, position
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ lying in wait, ambush
