# *spilþijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/spil%C3%BEijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spilþijaną

### Etymology

From *spelþą (“spoil, ruin”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)pelH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspil.θi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to spoil, ruin
2. _(reconstruction)_ to kill, murder
