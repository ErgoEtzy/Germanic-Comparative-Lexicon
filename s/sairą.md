## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sairą

### Etymology

Nominalized formation from *sairaz (“sore, painful”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ wound
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ pain, soreness
