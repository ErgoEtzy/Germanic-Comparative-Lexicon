## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skaggiją

### Etymology

From *skag- (“to emerge, stick out”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kek-, *(s)keg- (“to jump, skip, move, hurry”) + *-gją, *-gjô, *-gô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kek-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ that which protrudes or sticks out
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ stem (of a ship)
3. _(Germanic, North, neuter, reconstruction)_ beard
