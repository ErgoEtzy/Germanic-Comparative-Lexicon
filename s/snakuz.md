## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *snakuz

### Etymology

Derived from *snak- (“snout”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No PIE form is listed or machine-resolvable in this snapshot.

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ having a snout, snouted
2. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ having a projection used for probing or prodding; pronged
