# *stanikōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stanik%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stanikōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)ten- (“to thunder, rush, roar, groan”) + *-ikōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ten-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to roar repetitively, rumble
2. _(reconstruction)_ to groan repetitively, mutter, mumble
3. _(reconstruction)_ to pant
