## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swīmaną

### Etymology

Possibly related to *swaipaną (“to sweep, sway”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswiː.mɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sway, swoon
