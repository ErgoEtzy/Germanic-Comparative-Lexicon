# *sahtiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sahtiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sahtiz

### Etymology

From *sakaną (“to charge, dispute”) + *-þiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *seh₂g-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑx.tiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ reproach
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ agreement, reconciliation
