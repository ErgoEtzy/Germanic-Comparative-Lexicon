# *sōmiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/s%C5%8Dmiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sōmiz

### Etymology

Unknown; possible vṛddhi gerundive of otherwise unattested strong verb *samaną, from Proto-Indo-European *sem- (“together, one”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɔː.miz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ seemly, suitable, compatible
