## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sandō

### Etymology

From *sandijaną (“to send”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sent-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑn.dɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a sending, mission
