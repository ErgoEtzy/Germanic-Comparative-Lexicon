# *skauniz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skauniz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skauniz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *skowh₁nis, a verbal adjective originally meaning "watchable", derived from a lost strong verb *skawaną, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kewh₁- (“to perceive, pay attention”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kewh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑu̯.niz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ beautiful, shining
