# *smēhōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sm%C4%93h%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *smēhōną

### Etymology

From *smēhaz (“small, little”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)meyg-, *(s)mēyg-, *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsmɛː.xɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to humiliate
2. _(reconstruction)_ to despise
