## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *snutraz

### Etymology

Unclear. Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *snew- (“to be fleet; flow; run; trickle”); or from Proto-Indo-European *snu- (“mind; understanding”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *snew-, *snu-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsnut.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ wise; clever
