# *staupijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/staupijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *staupijaną

### Etymology

From *staupaz (“pitcher, jar, bucket"; originally "steep, high”) + *-janą.

From *stūpaną (“to stoop, fall down”) + *-janą (causative).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑu̯.pi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to pour
2. _(reconstruction)_ to soak, to make wet
3. _(reconstruction)_ to cast metal
