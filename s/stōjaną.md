## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stōjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *stoh₂éyeti (“to cause to stand”) or from *stōō (“a place, location, stowage”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *stoh₂éyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɔː.wi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stow
2. _(reconstruction)_ to dam
