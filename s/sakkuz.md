[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sakkuz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sakkuz

### Etymology

Borrowed from Latin saccus (“sack”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑk.kuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ sack, bag, purse
