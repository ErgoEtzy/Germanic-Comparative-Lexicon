## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swellaną

### Etymology

Unknown. Perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *swel- (“to swell”) or *swelH- (Melchert, 2005).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswel.lɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to swell
