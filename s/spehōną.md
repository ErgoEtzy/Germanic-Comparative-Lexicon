# *spehōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/speh%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spehōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *speḱ- (“to look”). Cognate with Latin speciō (“look, stare, behold”), Ancient Greek σκοπέω (skopéō, “I view, examine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *speḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspe.xɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to peer, look, spy
