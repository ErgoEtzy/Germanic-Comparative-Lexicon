## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skinō

### Etymology

Possibly an n-stem derivative from either Proto-Indo-European *skey- (“to cut, split”) or *(s)ḱeh₃- (“shadow”), *(s)ḱeh₁y- (“to shine”); the former hypothesis appears more semantically convincing.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *skey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈski.nɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ plate; rim
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ piece
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ shinbone
