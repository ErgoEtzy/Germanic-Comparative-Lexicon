# *satīniz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sat%C4%ABniz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *satīniz

### Etymology

From *satjaną (“to set, cause to sit”) + *-īniz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sed-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑ.tiː.niz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a setting, placing, position
