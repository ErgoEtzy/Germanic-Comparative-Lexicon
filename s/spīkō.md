[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sp%C4%ABk%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spīkō

### Etymology

The word seems to derive from Proto-Indo-European *speyg-, a *g-extension of *spey- (“long, sharp”); Orel notes similarities to Latin spīcus (“pin”);.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *speyg-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a large nail, spike
