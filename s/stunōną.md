# *stunōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stun%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stunōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)ten- (“to thunder, rustle, rumble, drone, groan”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ten-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstu.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to make a loud sound; crash
2. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to thunder, rumble
3. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to groan, moan
