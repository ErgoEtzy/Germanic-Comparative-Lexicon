# *selhaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/selhaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *selhaz

### Etymology

Unknown. Likely either from pre-Proto-Finnic *šülkeš (later Proto-Finnic *hülgeh; compare Finnish hylje, Estonian hüljes) or from a related substrate source. Derivation from Proto-Indo-European *selk- (“to pull”), while possible, is ad hoc.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *selk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsel.xɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ seal (animal)
