## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skīdą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *skey-tó-m, from *skey- (“to split”). Compare, in particular, Proto-Celtic *skeitos (“shield”), Proto-Slavic *ščítъ (“idem”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *skey-, *skey-tó-m

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ piece of wood, billet, board
