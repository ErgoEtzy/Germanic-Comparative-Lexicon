# *sweraną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sweran%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sweraną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *swer- (“to ache, fester”). Possibly related to Avestan 𐬓𐬀𐬭𐬀 (xᵛara, “wound, hurt”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswe.rɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to hurt
2. _(reconstruction)_ to fester; ulcerate
