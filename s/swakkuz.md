## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swakkuz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *swegn-, *swekn- (“odour, smell”), from Proto-Indo-European *sweg-, *swek- (“to smell”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sweg-, *swegn-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɑk.kuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ smell; odour; scent
