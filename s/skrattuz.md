# *skrattuz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skrattuz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skrattuz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (“to shrink, wither, dry out, crinkle, wrimple”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ker-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskrɑt.tuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ forest monster; goblin; troll
