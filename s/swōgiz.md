## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swōgiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sweh₂gʰ- (“to sound”, compare *swōganą) + *-iz. Cognate with Ancient Greek ἠχή (ēkhḗ, “sound”), ἠχώ (ēkhṓ, “echo”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-is, *sweh₂gʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɔː.ɣiz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ noise, sound
