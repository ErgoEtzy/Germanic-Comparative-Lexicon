# *sunnǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sunn%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sunnǭ

### Etymology

Possibly feminized from *sunnô (“sun”) in an opposing gender pair with masculine *mēnô (“moon”), akin to Latin cognate sōl m (“sun”) and lūna f (“moon”). Note the possibility of influence on or from the gender of their personifications in Germanic mythology, which are accordingly female and male.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsun.nɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ the sun
