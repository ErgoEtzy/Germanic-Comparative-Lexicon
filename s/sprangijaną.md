# *sprangijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sprangijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sprangijaną

### Etymology

From *springaną (“to jump”) + *-janą (“causative suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *sperǵʰ-, *sprenǵʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsprɑŋ.ɡi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to spring
2. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to burst, to blow up
