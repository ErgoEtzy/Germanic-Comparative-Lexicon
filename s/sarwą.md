[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sarw%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sarwą

### Etymology

Likely from Pre-Germanic *sor-wo-, derived from Proto-Indo-European *ser- (“to tie together; to watch over, protect, guard”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ser-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑr.wɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ equipment, armor
