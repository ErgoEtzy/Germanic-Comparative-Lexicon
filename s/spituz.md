## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spituz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *spey- (“sharp, sharp stick”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *spey-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ rod (for roasting meat)
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ stick; spit
