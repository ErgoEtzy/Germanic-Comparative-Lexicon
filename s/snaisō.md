# *snaisō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/snais%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *snaisō

### Etymology

Likely a nominal derivative of *snīþaną (“to cut”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsnɑi̯.sɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a cut-off twig
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ spit, skewer
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ notch, score
