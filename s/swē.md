## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swē

### Etymology

Often derived from Proto-Indo-European *swē. In any case related to *swa.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swé, *swē

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/swɛː/`

### Adverb

1. _(reconstruction)_ like, similar to
2. _(reconstruction)_ just like, in the same manner as
3. _(reconstruction)_ as if
