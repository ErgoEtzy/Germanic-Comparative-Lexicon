# *siuniz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/siuniz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *siuniz

### Etymology

From earlier *segw-niz, the former part from *sehwaną (“to see”).

Identical to the "sight" sense of Etymology 1, used in an adjectival sense.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sekʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsiu̯.niz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ appearance, sight
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ face
