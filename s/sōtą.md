## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sōtą

### Etymology

From a lengthened o-grade of *sitjaną (“to sit”), reflecting the nature of soot as accumulated particles that sit on surfaces.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sed-

### Pronunciation
