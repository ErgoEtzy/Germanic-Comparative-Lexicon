## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sōkijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *séh₂gyeti, from *seh₂g- (“to seek out”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *séh₂gyeti

### Pronunciation

### Verb
