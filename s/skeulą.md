## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skeulą

### Etymology

According to Orel, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kewH- (“to cover”), from which he also derives *skuwwô (“reflection; shadow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kewH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskeu.lɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, neuter, reconstruction)_ shelter, hiding place
