# *sēaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/s%C4%93an%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sēaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *seh₁- (“to sow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *seh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɛː.ɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sow, to disperse (seeds)
