[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/saliz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *saliz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sol-, *sel- (“human settlement, village, dwelling”). Cognate with Old Church Slavonic село (selo, “courtyard, village”), Lithuanian sala (“village”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sel-, *sol-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑ.liz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ house; dwelling
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ hall; room
