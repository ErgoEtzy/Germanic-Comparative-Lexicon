## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stukkōną

### Etymology

An apparent iterative to *stikaną (“to stick”), with analogical *-u- from the zero-grade in other words. The semantic shift from "stick" to "stop" may be via "to stick" > "to stick many things into one place and get said place stuck" > "to be stuck, stopped" > "to stop". Piecewise doublet of *stikkōną.

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to stick
2. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to stop, halt
