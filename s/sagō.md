## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sagō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sok-éh₂, from *sek- (“to cut”) + *-éh₂.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-éh₂, *sek-, *sok-éh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑ.ɣɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ saw (tool)
