# *slaiwōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/slaiw%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slaiwōną

### Etymology

From *slaiwaz + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)leyH-, *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *sliH-u-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈslɑi̯.wɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to become blunt
2. _(reconstruction)_ to become dim-witted, slow
