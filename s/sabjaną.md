# *sabjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sabjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sabjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sep- (“to taste, try out”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sep-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑβ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to know
2. _(reconstruction)_ to taste; sense; perceive
3. _(reconstruction)_ to notice
