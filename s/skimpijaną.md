# *skimpijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skimpijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skimpijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)ḱh₂emb- (“to arch, bend, curve”), which appears to be an s-mobile b-extension variant of *kh₂em- (“to bend, curve”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ḱh₂emb-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to skip, hop
2. _(reconstruction)_ to dance
3. _(reconstruction)_ to gesture, act out
4. _(reconstruction)_ to mock
