# *saipǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/saip%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *saipǭ

### Etymology

Of unclear origin, with the original sense possibly denoting "amber, resin". Orel prefers to consider the word a secondary nominal formation from *sīpaną (“to drip, flow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *seyb-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑi̯.põː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, feminine, reconstruction)_ soap
