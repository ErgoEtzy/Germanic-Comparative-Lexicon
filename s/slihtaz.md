# *slihtaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/slihtaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slihtaz

### Etymology

From *slīkaną (“to slide, slip”) + *-þaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tos, *sleyg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈslix.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ slippery
2. _(reconstruction)_ smooth; flat; level
