## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spurą

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *spr̥Hom, derived from or related to *spurnaną (“to kick”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *sperH- (“to trample”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sperH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspu.rɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ track
