# *sebundô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sebund%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sebundô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *septm̥mós, from *septḿ̥. An ordinal to *sebun (“seven”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *septm̥mós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈse.βun.ðɔːː/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ seventh
