## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skrīaną

### Etymology

No etymology is supplied.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)krey-, *sker-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskriːɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to caw, screech
2. _(reconstruction)_ to shout, scream, cry out
