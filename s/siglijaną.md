# *siglijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/siglijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *siglijaną

### Etymology

From *seglą (“sail”) + *-janą (“verbal suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *segʰ-, *sek(h₁)-ló-m, *sigʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsiɣ.li.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(ambitransitive, reconstruction)_ to sail
