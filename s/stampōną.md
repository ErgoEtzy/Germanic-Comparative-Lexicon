# *stampōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stamp%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stampōną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *stompnéh₂-, an o-grade iterative possibly related to *stapjaną, *stappōną (“to step”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑm.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to compress, to squeeze
2. _(reconstruction)_ to stamp
