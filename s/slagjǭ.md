## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slagjǭ

### Etymology

From the root of *slahaną + *-jǭ, perhaps onomatopoeic late Proto-Indo-European *slak- (“to hit, strike”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *slak-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈslɑɣ.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ sledge; mallet; hammer
