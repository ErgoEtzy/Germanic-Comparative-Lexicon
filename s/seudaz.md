**Reconstruction:** *seudaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *syewH-tós, from *syewH- (“to sew”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *syewH-tós

- IPA: `/ˈseu̯.ðɑz/`

### Noun
