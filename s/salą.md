[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sal%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *salą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sel- (“human settlement, village, dwelling”). Cognate with Old Church Slavonic село (selo, “courtyard, village”), Lithuanian sala (“village”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑ.lɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ house; dwelling
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ hall; (main) room
