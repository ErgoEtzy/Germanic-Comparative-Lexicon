# *sūraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/s%C5%ABraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sūraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *súHros (“sour, salty, bitter”). Cognate with Proto-Balto-Slavic *sū́ˀras (“salty, bitter”), Old Prussian suris (“soured milk cheese”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *súHros

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsuː.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ sour, acidic
