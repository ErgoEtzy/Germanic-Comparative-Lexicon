# *sunjōniz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sunj%C5%8Dniz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sunjōniz

### Etymology

Derived from *sunjōną (“to put right, deny a charge”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsun.jɔː.niz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a defense, answer, setting oneself right
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ denial, refusal
