# *sinnaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sinnaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sinnaz

### Etymology

Reflecting pre-Germanic *sentnós, from the root of *sinnaną, from Proto-Indo-European *sent- (“to perceive, think, feel”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sent-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsin.nɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ sense, perception
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ sense, meaning
