[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sumaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sumaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sm̥-h₁-o-, from *sem- (“one, whole”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sem-, *sm̥-h₁-o-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsu.mɑz/`

### Determiner

1. _(reconstruction, singular)_ some (one), a certain (one)
2. _(in-plural, reconstruction)_ some, certain
