# *sīmô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/s%C4%ABm%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sīmô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sh₂imṓ, from *sh₂ey- (“to bind, fetter”). Cognate with Ancient Greek ἱμάς (himás, “leather strap, well-rope”), and possibly Sanskrit सीमन् (sīmán, “border, frontier, margin”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sh₂ey-, *sh₂imṓ

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsiː.mɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ rope, cord, string
