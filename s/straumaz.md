# *straumaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/straumaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *straumaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *srow-mo-s, which is derived from Proto-Indo-European *srew- (“to flow”). Cognate with Ancient Greek ῥεῦμα (rheûma, “stream, flow”), Lithuanian srovė (“current, stream, flow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *srew-, *srow-mo-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstrɑu̯.mɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ stream, current, river
