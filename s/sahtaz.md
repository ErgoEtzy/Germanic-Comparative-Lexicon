## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sahtaz

### Etymology

From *sakaną (“to charge, dispute”) + *-þaz. Parralel construction with Old Irish sachtae, -sacht.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

### Pronunciation
