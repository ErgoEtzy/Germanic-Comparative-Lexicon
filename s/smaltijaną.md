# *smaltijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/smaltijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *smaltijaną

### Etymology

From *smeltaną (“to melt”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)meld-, *(s)méldeti, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsmɑl.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to melt, to cause to melt
