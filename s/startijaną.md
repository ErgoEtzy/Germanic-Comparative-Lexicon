[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/startijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *startijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)terd-, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ter- (“to be stiff”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ter-, *(s)terd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑr.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stand out stiffly
