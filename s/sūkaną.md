## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sūkaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sug-, *suk- (“to suck”), from Proto-Indo-European *su-, *sew- (“juice, sap”). Cognate with Latin sūgō (“suck”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *su-, *sug-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsuː.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to suck
2. _(reconstruction)_ to suckle
