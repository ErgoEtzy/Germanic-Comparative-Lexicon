## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Sigiwarduz

### Etymology

From *sigiz (“victory”) + *warduz (“guard”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *wer-, *wor-tús

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsi.ɣi.ˌwɑr.ðuz/`

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
