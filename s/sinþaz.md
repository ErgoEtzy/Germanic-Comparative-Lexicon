# *sinþaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sin%C3%BEaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sinþaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *séntos, from Proto-Indo-European *sent- (“to head for, go”). By synchronic analysis, a nominal formation from *sinnaną (“to feel, consider”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sent-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsin.θɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ journey, way, path
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ time (as in first time, many times), instance
