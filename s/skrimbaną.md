## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skrimbaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *skrembʰ-, related to Lithuanian skrèbti and skrembù (“to shrink”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kerbʰ- (“to turn around, curve”), an extension of *(s)ker- (“to turn”), related to Latin corbis (“basket”), Old Norse skreppa (“scrip”), German Scherbe (“fragment”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kerbʰ-

### Pronunciation

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to contract, to shrink
