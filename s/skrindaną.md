# *skrindaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skrindan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skrindaną

### Etymology

Likely from an extended form of Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (“to cut”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ker-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskrin.dɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to break open, to burst
2. _(reconstruction)_ to split
