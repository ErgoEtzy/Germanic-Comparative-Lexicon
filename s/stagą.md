# *stagą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stag%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stagą

### Etymology

From *stag- (“to be stiff, prop”), from Proto-Indo-European *stak-, *stek- (“to stand, stell, hold”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *stak-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑ.ɣɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ a stay (rope)
