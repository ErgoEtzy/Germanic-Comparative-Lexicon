# *swinkaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/swinkan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swinkaną

### Etymology

Of uncertain origin; possibly related to Old Irish seng (“slender, slim”), though the semantic connection is weak. Compare *swangaz (“thin, slender”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswiŋ.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to toil, labor
