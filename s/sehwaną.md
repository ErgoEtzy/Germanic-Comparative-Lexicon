## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sehwaną

### Etymology

From *sekʷ-e-ti, thematic root present of Proto-Indo-European *sekʷ- (“to see, notice”), possibly related to *sagjaz (“companion, friend”, literally “follower”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sekʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈse.xʷɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to see
