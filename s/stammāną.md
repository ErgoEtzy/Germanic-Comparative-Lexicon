## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stammāną

### Etymology

From *stammaz (“stammering”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti, *stemH-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stammer
