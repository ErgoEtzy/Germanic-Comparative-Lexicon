# *switjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/switjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *switjaną

### Etymology

Zero-grade ye-present, pre-Germanic *swidye/o-, derived from the root of *swait- (“sweat”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sweyd-, *swidyéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswit.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sweat
