## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swarmaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *swer- (“to buzz, hum”). Cognate with Latin susurrus (“whispering, humming, murmur”), Lithuanian surma (“a pipe”), Russian свире́ль (svirélʹ, “a pipe, reed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swer-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ swarm
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ dizziness
