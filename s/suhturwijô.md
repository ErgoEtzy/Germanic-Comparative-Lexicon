# *suhturwijô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/suhturwij%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *suhturwijô

### Etymology

Derived from Proto-Indo-European *suḱ-ter-, from *sweḱ-. Compare Armenian ուստր (ustr, “son”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *suḱ-ter-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsux.tur.wi.jɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ brother's son, nephew
