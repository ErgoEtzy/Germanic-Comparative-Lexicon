## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skirmiz

### Etymology

A nominal derivative of Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (“to cut, divide”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ker-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskir.miz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ fur, hide, pelt
2. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ covering, shelter, screen
