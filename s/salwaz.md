# *salwaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/salwaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *salwaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *solwós (“grey, sallow”). Cognate with Proto-Slavic *solvъ (“yellowish-gray”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *solwós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑl.wɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ dusky, grey
2. _(reconstruction)_ smutty, dirty, muddy
3. _(reconstruction)_ sallow
