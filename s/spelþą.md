## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spelþą

### Etymology

Traditionally derived from a Proto-Indo-European *(s)pelH- (“to split off, separate”), and compared with Sanskrit स्फट् (sphaṭ, “to burst, split”), फलति (phalati, “to burst (in half)”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)pelH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspel.θɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ destruction, ruin
