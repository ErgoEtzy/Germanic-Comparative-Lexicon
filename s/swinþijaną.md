[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/swin%C3%BEijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swinþijaną

### Etymology

From *swinþaz (“strong”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tos, *-wénts, *seǵʰ-, *séǵʰ-wr̥, *séǵʰwr̥, *sǵʰ-wént-s ~ *sǵʰ-unt-és

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswin.θi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to strengthen
