# *skipōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skip%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skipōną

### Etymology

Equivalent to *skipą (“ship”) + *-ōną (“verbal suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *skey-, *skēi-b-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈski.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to arrange, set up
2. _(reconstruction)_ to ship, embark
