## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stammijaną

### Etymology

The causative to an unattested strong verb *stimmaną, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *stemH- (“to stutter, push”), whence also Latvian stumt (“to pull, push”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *stemH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑm.mi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to slow, stop
