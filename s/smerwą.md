## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *smerwą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *smérus (“grease, fat”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *smérus

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsmer.wɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ grease, butter, fat
