# *skaþjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ska%C3%BEjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skaþjaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *skh₁tH-ye-ti, perhaps from a Proto-Indo-European *(s)keh₁t- (“damage, harm”). See *skaþô (“damage”) for more cognates.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)keh₁t-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑθ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to mar; damage
2. _(reconstruction)_ to injure; hurt
