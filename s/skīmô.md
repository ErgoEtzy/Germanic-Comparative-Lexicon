## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skīmô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)ḱeyh₁- (“to shimmer; shadow”) with a suffix *-mô, from Proto-Indo-European *-mō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ḱeyh₁-, *-mō

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskiː.mɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ shine; luminescence
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ light; lamp
