# *swōnō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sw%C5%8Dn%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swōnō

### Etymology

Uncertain; one theory derives the term from Proto-Indo-European *swā-n- (“healthy; whole; active; vigorous”), and compares it with Latin sānus (“healthy”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swā-n-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɔːnɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ reconciliation; atonement
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ agreement
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ appeasement
4. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ offering; sacrifice
