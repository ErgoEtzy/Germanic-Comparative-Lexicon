# *sahsą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sahs%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sahsą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sek- (“to cut”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sek-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑx.sɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ a stone chip used for cutting
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ dagger, knife
