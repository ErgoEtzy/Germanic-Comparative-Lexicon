## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sturiz

### Etymology

Uncertain. Perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *(s)tur-, *(s)twer- (“to turn around, confuse”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)tur-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstur.iz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ turmoil; noise; confusion
