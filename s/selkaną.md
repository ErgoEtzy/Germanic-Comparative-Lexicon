# *selkaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/selkan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *selkaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *selǵ- (“to let go, send”), Cognate with Sanskrit सृजति (sṛjáti, “to send off, discharge”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *selǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsel.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to trickle, drip
2. _(reconstruction)_ to be slow, to languish
