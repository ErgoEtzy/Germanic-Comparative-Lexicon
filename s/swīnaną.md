## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swīnaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰgʷʰey- (“to decline, perish”).
According to Kroonen the development of the initial consonant cluster was unstable, leading to the doublets *dwīnaną and *þwīnaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰgʷʰey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswiː.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to dwindle, to disappear
