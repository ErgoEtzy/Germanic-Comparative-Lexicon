# *saljaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/saljan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *saljaną

### Etymology

A causative formation from Proto-Indo-European *selh₁- (“to take”), either inherited from Proto-Indo-European *solh₁-éye-ti (“to let take”, causative) or derived within Proto-Germanic from an unattested *selaną (“to take”) + *-janą (causative suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *selh₁-, *solh₁-éye-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑl.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to offer, give
2. _(reconstruction)_ to sell
