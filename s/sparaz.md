# *sparaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sparaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sparaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sph₁rós, from *speh₁- (“to succeed, prosper”). Particularly close cognate with Sanskrit स्फिर (sphirá, “fat”), Latin prosperus (“favorable, lucky”). Alternatively, from a Proto-Indo-European *sper- (“to rescue, deliver”), and compared with Sanskrit स्पृ (spṛ, “to release, save”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sper-, *sph₁rós

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ sparing, frugal
