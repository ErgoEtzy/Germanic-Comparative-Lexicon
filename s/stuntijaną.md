## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stuntijaną

### Etymology

From *stuntaz (“short, stunted”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shorten
2. _(reconstruction)_ to make dull
