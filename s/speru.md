## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *speru

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *sperHu-, from Proto-Indo-European *sperH- (“spear; a kind of tree”). Cognate with Latin sparus (“hunting spear”), Albanian shpardh (“Italian oak”).
