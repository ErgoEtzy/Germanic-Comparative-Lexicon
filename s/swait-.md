# *swait-

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/swait-)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swait-

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *swoyd-o-, from Proto-Indo-European *sweyd- (“to sweat”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sweyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɑi̯.tɑz/`
- IPA: `/ˈswɑi̯.tɑ̃/`
- IPA: `/ˈswɑi̯.tɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, neuter, reconstruction)_ sweat; blood
