# *sakaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sakan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sakaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *sh₂g-, from Proto-Indo-European *seh₂g- (“to seek out”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *seh₂g-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑ.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to charge, dispute
