## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sibjaz

### Etymology

Derived from or related to *sibjō (“kinship, friendship”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsiβ.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ related
2. _(reconstruction)_ friendly
