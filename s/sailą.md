# *sailą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sail%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sailą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sh₂éy-ōl ~ *sh₂i-l-és, from *sh₂ey- (“to bind, fetter”). Cognate with Hittite [script needed] (iš-hi-ia-al, “band”), and possibly Proto-Balto-Slavic *séiˀlāˀ (“soul”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sh₂éy-ōl ~ *sh₂i-l-és

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑi̯.lɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ rope; cord
