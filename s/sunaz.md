## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sunaz

### Etymology

From earlier *swunaz, from Proto-Indo-European *sewH- (“to give birth, bear”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sewH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsu.nɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ boar
2. _(in-plural, neuter, reconstruction)_ herd
