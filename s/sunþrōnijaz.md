## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sunþrōnijaz

### Etymology

From *sunþraz + *-ōnijaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-teros, *sh₂ún-teros, *sóh₂wl̥

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsun.θrɔː.ni.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ southern
