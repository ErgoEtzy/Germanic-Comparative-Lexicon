## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *samnōną

### Etymology

From *samanai (“together”) + *-ōną, from Proto-Indo-European *sem- (“one”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑm.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to bring together; gather; collect; assemble
