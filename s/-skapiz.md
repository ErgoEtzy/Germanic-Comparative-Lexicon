## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -skapiz

### Etymology

Suffixal use of a zero-grade i-stem noun derived from *skapjaną.

### Pronunciation

### Suffix

1. _(masculine, morpheme, reconstruction)_ Forms nouns denoting state, originally meaning "shaping, condition".
