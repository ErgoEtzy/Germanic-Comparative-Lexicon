[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sw%C4%ABn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swīną

### Etymology

From an adjectival neuter form *swiHnom of Proto-Indo-European *suH- (“pig”) (compare Latin suīnus, Proto-Slavic *svinьja, Latvian sivēns). Synchronically analyzable as a derivative of *sūz (“sow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *suH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswiː.nɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ pig
