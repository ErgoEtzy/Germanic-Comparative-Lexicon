## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slaihǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)leh₃y- (“bluish”). Compare Proto-Slavic *sliva (“plum”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)leh₃y-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ sloe
