# *straumijaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/straumijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *straumijaz

### Etymology

Derived from either *straumijaną (“to stream, flow”) or *straumaz (“stream”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstrɑu̯.mi.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ flowing, running, having a current
