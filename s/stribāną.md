# *stribāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/strib%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stribāną

### Etymology

Apparently from a *bʰ-extension of Proto-Indo-European *(s)ter(h₁)- (“to be stiff; be rigid; exert”), for which see Proto-Germanic *staraz (“stiff”) for more.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ter(h₁)-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to strive; exert oneself
