## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skupą

### Etymology

Nominal formation related to *skuppōną (“to mock, scoff”), of uncertain further origin; see there for more.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ mockery, joking
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ cheating, deception, trickery
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ poetry
