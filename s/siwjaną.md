# *siwjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/siwjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *siwjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *syewH- (“to sew”). Cognate with Proto-Slavic *šiti (“to sew”) (whence Russian шить (šitʹ)) and with Lithuanian siū́ti (“to sew”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *syewH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsiw.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sew
