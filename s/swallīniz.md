## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swallīniz

### Etymology

From *swallijaną (“to cause to swell”) + *-īniz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *-éyti, *swel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɑl.liː.niz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a causing to swell, a swelling
