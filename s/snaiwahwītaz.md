# *snaiwahwītaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/snaiwahw%C4%ABtaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *snaiwahwītaz

### Etymology

From *snaiwaz + *hwītaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *snóygʷʰos, *ḱweyt-, *ḱweytōr

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsnɑi̯.wɑ.ˌxʷiː.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ snow-white
