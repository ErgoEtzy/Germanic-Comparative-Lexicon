## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skaiþiz

### Etymology

From *skaiþaną (“to separate”) + *-þiz (“nominal suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tis, *skóyt-e-ti

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ sheath, covering
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ something carved out to be concave or hollow
