## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slīmą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sley- (“smooth; slick; sticky; slimy”). Related to Sanskrit आलय (ālaya), whence the second part of Himalaya.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sley-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsliː.mɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ slime
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ mucus
