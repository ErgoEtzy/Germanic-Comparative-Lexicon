## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sunjaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁s-n̥t-yós, from *h₁sónts, participial of *h₁es- (“to be”). Dentals between *-n- and *-y- regularly lost. Cognate with Sanskrit सत्य (satyá).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁s-n̥t-yós

### Adjective
