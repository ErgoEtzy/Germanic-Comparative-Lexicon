# *sunjō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sunj%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sunjō

### Etymology

Derived from or related to *sunjaz (“true, real”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsun.jɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ truth; an actual situation, reality
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ denial, protest (of a charge)
