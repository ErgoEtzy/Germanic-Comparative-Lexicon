## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sūz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *suH- (“pig, hog, swine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *suH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/suːz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ sow (female pig)
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ pig, swine
