# *stikkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stikk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stikkōną

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *stignéh₂-, a zero-grade iterative to *stikaną (“to stick”). Piecewise doublet of *stukkōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *stignéh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstik.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stick, fix
