## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *smeltaz

### Etymology

From *smeltaną (“to melt”), possibly due to the fish's soft flesh.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ smelt (fish)
