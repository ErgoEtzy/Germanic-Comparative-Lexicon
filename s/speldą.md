## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *speldą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)pel- (“to split”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)pel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspel.dɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ a split or broken piece of wood; splinter; board
