## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sīþuz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *séh₁-i-tu-s or *sh₁-éi-tu-s, from Proto-Indo-European *seh₁- (“long, lasting”). Related to Latin sērus (“late, slow”), Old Irish sír (“long, lasting”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *seh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsiː.θuz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ late
