# *swēsaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sw%C4%93saz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swēsaz

### Etymology

Likely from Proto-Indo-European *swēdʰh₁-s-os or *swēdʰh₁-to-s. Related to Ancient Greek ἦθος (êthos, “custom”), Latin suēscō (“to become accustomed to”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swēdʰh₁-s-os

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɛː.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ one's own, familiar, trusted
