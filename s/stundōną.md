## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stundōną

### Etymology

From *stundō (“point in time”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstun.dɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to start, initiate
2. _(reconstruction)_ to strive
3. _(reconstruction)_ to pause, tarry
