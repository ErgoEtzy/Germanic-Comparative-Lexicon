## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sufjōną

### Etymology

From *sufjaz (“sleepy”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

### Verb

1. _(impersonal, reconstruction, with-accusative)_ to become sleepy, to doze off
