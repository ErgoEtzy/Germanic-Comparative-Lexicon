# *sīþaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/s%C4%AB%C3%BEan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sīþaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *séyt-e-ti, thematic root present to Proto-Indo-European *seyt-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *seyt-, *séyt-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsiː.θɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to perform magic, work charms
