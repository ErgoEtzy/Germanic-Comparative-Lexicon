# *swōgijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sw%C5%8Dgijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swōgijaną

### Etymology

From *swōgiz (“noise, sound”) + *-janą, or *swōganą (“to sound, sigh”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-is, *-ye-ti, *-yes-, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-yōs, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *sweh₂gʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɔː.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sound
2. _(reconstruction)_ to sigh; to breathe heavily and noisily
