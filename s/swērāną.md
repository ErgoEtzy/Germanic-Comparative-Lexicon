# *swērāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sw%C4%93r%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swērāną

### Etymology

From both *swēraz (“heavy”) + *-āną (stative-fientive suffix) and *swēraz (“heavy”) + *-āną (factitive suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti, *swer-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be or become heavy
2. _(reconstruction)_ to honour
