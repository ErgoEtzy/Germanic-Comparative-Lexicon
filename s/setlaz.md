# *setlaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/setlaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *setlaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sedlo-, from *sed- (“to sit”). Synchronically analyzed as an l-suffixed nominal formation from *sitjaną (“to sit”). Particularly close cognate with Old Irish séol (“seat”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sed-, *sedlo-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈset.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ seat; chair; settle
