# *seuhslō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/seuhsl%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *seuhslō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sewg- (“grievous; sorrowful; ill”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sewg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈseu̯x.slɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ pain; torment; affliction; suffering; agony
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ addiction; obsession
