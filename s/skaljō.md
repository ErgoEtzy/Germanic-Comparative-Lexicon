## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skaljō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *skolH-yeh₂, from *(s)kelH- (“to split, part, divide”). See also the related *skalō (“shell, husk”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *skolH-yeh₂

### Pronunciation

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ shell; husk; rind; peel
