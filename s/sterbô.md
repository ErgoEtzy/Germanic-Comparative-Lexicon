## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sterbô

### Etymology

From *sterbaną + *-ô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)terp-, *-ō, *sterbʰ-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ that which kills; death, plague
