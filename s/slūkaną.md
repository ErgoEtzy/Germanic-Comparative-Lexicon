## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slūkaną

### Etymology

Cognate with Old Irish sluccim (“to swallow”) and Ancient Greek λύζω (lúzō, “to hiccup”). Pokorny derives these all from Proto-Indo-European *slewk-, *slewg- (“to swallow”), an onomatopoeic root.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry
