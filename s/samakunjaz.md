# *samakunjaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/samakunjaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *samakunjaz

### Etymology

From *sama- + *kunją. Parallel formation with Ancient Greek ὁμόγνιος (homógnios, “of the same origin”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sem-, *ǵenh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑ.mɑ.ˌkun.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ of the same kin, kind; kindred
