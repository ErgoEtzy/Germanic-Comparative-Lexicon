# *satjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/satjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *satjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sodéyeti. Equivalent to *sitjaną (“to sit”) + *-janą (“causative suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sed-, *sodéyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑt.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to set, to place down
