# *skrumpōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skrump%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skrumpōną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *skrm̥bʰ-néh₂-, an iterative verb related to *skrimbaną and *skrimpaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskrum.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to contract, to shrink
