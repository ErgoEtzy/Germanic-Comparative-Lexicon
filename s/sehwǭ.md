# *sehwǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sehw%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sehwǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sekʷ- (“to see, notice”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sekʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈse.xʷɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ seeing, sight
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ eyeball, pupil
