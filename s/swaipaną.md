# *swaipaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/swaipan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swaipaną

### Etymology

The o-grade causative to *swippōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɑi̯.pɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sweep
2. _(reconstruction)_ to sway, swing
3. _(reconstruction)_ to cast, fling
4. _(reconstruction)_ to wrap
