# *swaimōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/swaim%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swaimōną

### Etymology

Likely related to *swīmaną (“to sway, swoon”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɑi̯.mɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to swing, sway
2. _(reconstruction)_ to float, hover
