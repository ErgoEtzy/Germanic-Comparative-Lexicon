[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/spit%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spitō

### Etymology

Orel leaves the origin of this family of words open, though the words seem to derive from Proto-Indo-European *spey-d, a *d-extension of *spey- (“sharp; pointed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *spey-d

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspi.tɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ rod; spike; skewer
