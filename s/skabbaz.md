# *skabbaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skabbaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skabbaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *skabʰ- (“to cut, split, carve, shape”). Cognate with Latin scabiēs (“mange”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *skabʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑb.bɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ scab
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ scabies, mange
