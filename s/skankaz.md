# *skankaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skankaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skankaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)kong-os, from *(s)keng- (“to hobble, tilt”), and cognate with Ancient Greek σκάζω (skázō, “to limp”), Sanskrit खञ्जति (kháñjati, “to limp”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)keng-, *(s)kong-os

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑŋ.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ askew, tilted
