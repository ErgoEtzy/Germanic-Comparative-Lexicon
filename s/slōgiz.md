# *slōgiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sl%C5%8Dgiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slōgiz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *slahaną (“to hit, strike”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈslɔː.ɣiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ able to strike
2. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ sly, cunning, clever
