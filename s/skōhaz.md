# *skōhaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sk%C5%8Dhaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skōhaz

### Etymology

Uncertain. Perhaps derived from *skehaną (“to move quickly”), from Proto-Indo-European *skek- (“to move quickly, jump”). A derivation from an alleged Proto-Indo-European verb *skewk-, supposedly from *(s)kewH- (“to cover”) (compare Proto-Germanic *skiwô (“cloud cover; cloud; sky”)), proposed in older literature, is dubious and not mentioned in Kroonen.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *skek-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɔː.xɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ shoe
