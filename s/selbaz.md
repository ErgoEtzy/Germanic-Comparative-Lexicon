## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *selbaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *selbʰ- (“one's own”), probably from the reflexive pronoun *swé (“self”). Compared with Venetic [script needed] (sselb-).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *selbʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsel.βɑz/`

### Pronoun

1. _(reconstruction)_ self
