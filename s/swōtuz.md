## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swōtuz

### Etymology

From earlier *swātuz, from Proto-Indo-European *swéh₂dus (“sweet”). Compare Latin suāvis, Ancient Greek ἡδύς (hēdús), Sanskrit स्वादु (svādu).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sweh₂d-, *swéh₂dus

### Pronunciation
