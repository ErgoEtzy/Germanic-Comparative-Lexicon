## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swefnaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *swépnos (“sleep”). Related to *swefaną (“to sleep”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swep-, *swépnos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsweɸ.nɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ sleep
