# *smukkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/smukk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *smukkōną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *smuknéh₂-, iterative formation to *smeuganą (“to creep, slip into”). Cognate with Lithuanian smùkti (“to slide, slip”), Czech smeknouti (“to strip”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsmuk.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to slide into, slip through
2. _(reconstruction)_ to creep, slink
