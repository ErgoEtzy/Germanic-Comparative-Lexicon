## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sandakurną

### Etymology

From *sandaz (“sand”) + *kurną (“grain”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *ǵr̥h₂nóm

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑn.dɑ.ˌkur.nɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ grain of sand
