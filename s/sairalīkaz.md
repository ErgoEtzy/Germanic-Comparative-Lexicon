# *sairalīkaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sairal%C4%ABkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sairalīkaz

### Etymology

From *sairaz (“sore, painful”) + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *seh₂i-ro-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑi̯.rɑ.liː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ sore, painful
2. _(reconstruction)_ sad, mournful, lamentable
