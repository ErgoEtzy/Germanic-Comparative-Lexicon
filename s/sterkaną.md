## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sterkaną

### Etymology

A lost strong verb, attested only in its past participle, related to *starkuz (“stiff, strong”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈster.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to become stiff, stiffen
