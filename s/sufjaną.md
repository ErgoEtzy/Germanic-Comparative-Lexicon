## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sufjaną

### Etymology

A zero-grade formation to *swefaną, or from *sufjaz (“sleepy”) + *-janą

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swep-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsuɸ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(impersonal, reconstruction, with-accusative)_ to become sleepy, to doze off
