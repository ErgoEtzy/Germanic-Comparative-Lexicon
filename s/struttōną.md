# *struttōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/strutt%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *struttōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sret- (“to swirl, to flow”).

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ter- (“strong, firm, stiff, rigid”)

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ter-, *sret-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstrut.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to well, to well up
