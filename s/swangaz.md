# *swangaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/swangaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swangaz

### Etymology

Perhaps an adjectival formation related to *swinganą (“to swing”), referring to the "curved or swung-in shape" and "easily swingable" nature of slender beings.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɑŋ.ɡɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ thin, slender, slim
2. _(reconstruction)_ wavering
