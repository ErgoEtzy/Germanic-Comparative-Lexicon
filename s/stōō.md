## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stōō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *stoh₂w-éh₂, from *steh₂- (“to stand, place, put”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *stoh₂w-éh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɔː.ɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a place, location, site
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ stowage
