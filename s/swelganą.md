# *swelganą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/swelgan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swelganą

### Etymology

Of unknown origin. Traditionally derived from a Proto-Indo-European *swelk- (“to gulp”), a k-extension of a Proto-Indo-European *swel- (“to drink, swallow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swel-, *swelk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswel.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to gulp, gobble, swallow, devour
2. _(reconstruction)_ to revel
