## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skuldōną

### Etymology

From *skuldiz (“obligation, debt”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *skel-, *skéltis

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskul.dɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to owe
