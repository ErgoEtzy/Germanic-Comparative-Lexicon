## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *snaisijaną

### Etymology

From *snaisō (“skewer, spit”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsnɑi̯.si.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to skewer, to spit, to impale
2. _(reconstruction)_ to line up
