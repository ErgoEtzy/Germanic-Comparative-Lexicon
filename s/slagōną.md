# *slagōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/slag%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slagōną

### Etymology

From *slagą, *slagiz (“blow, strike”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsla.ɣɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to hit, to knock
