[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/swanaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swanaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *swónh₂-os, from *swenh₂- (“to sound, resound”), that is, a "sounding (singing) bird". See also *swin-.

From Proto-Indo-European *sewH- (“to bear, give birth”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sewH-, *swenh₂-, *swónh₂-os

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɑ.nɑz/`
