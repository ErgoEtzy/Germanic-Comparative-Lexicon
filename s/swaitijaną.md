# *swaitijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/swaitijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swaitijaną

### Etymology

By surface analysis, *swait- (“sweat”) + *-janą. There is a morphologically parallel Sanskrit causative स्वेदयति (svedáyati), which could hint at an inheritance from Proto-Indo-European *swoyd-éye-ti, from the root *sweyd-. though Kroonen rejects this proposal on account of the differing semantics between the Germanic and Sanskrit forms.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sweyd-, *swoyd-éye-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɑi.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sweat
