# *skarō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skar%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skarō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)koréh₂, from the root *(s)ker- (“to part; split; cut off; sever; divide”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ker-, *(s)koréh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑ.rɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a portion; share
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a division; detachment; troop; band
