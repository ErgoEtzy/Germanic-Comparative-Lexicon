## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swarjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *swer- (“to resound loudly, swear”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɑr.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to swear (an oath)
