## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sīdiþō

### Etymology

From *sīdaz + *-iþō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-teh₂, *seh₁-, *sh₁i-tó-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsiː.ði.θɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ droopiness, ampleness, length
