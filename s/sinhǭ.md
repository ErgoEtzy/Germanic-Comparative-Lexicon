# *sinhǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sinh%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sinhǭ

### Etymology

Derived from a lost strong verb *singaną, from Proto-Indo-European *senk- (“to burn”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *senk-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ sinter, spark
