# *sagjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sagjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sagjaną

### Etymology

Either from Proto-Indo-European eye-iterative or eye-intensive *sokʷ-éye-ti, or innovated stative *sokʷ-h₁-yé-ti, both from *sekʷ- (“to say”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sekʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑɣ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to say
