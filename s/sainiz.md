## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sainiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *seh₁- (“long, lasting”). Cognate with Lithuanian atsainus (“careless”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *seh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑi̯.niz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ slow, sluggish
2. _(reconstruction)_ late
