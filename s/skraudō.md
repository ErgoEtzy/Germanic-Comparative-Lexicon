# *skraudō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skraud%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skraudō

### Etymology

From *skraudaną (“to shred”) + *-ō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(o)-éh₂, *-eh₂, *-oh₁, *-éh₂, *skrówdʰ-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskrɑu̯.ðɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ cut, crack
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ piece, shred
