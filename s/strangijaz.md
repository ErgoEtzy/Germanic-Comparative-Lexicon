## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *strangijaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sterh₃- (“to spread, stretch”); equivalent to *strangaz + *-jaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sterh₃-

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ strong, mighty, firm
2. _(reconstruction)_ pulled tightly, taut
3. _(reconstruction)_ stiff, rigid
4. _(reconstruction)_ strict, stern
