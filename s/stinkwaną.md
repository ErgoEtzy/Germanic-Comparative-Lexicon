# *stinkwaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stinkwan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stinkwaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *stengʷ- (“to push, thrust”), *stegʷ- (“to thrust, strike”). Related to Latin stinguō (“to extinguish”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *stengʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstiŋ.kʷɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to hit; thrust; clash
2. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to emit a smell; stink
