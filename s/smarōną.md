# *smarōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/smar%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *smarōną

### Etymology

Uncertain. Perhaps related to *smīlijaną (“to smile”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)mey- (“to laugh”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)mey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsmɑ.rɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to mock; scoff at; deride
