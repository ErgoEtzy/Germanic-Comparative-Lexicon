## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sprintaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)prend-. Cognate with Proto-Slavic *prę̀dati (“to jump, leap”).

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to jump
2. _(reconstruction)_ to emerge, to start
