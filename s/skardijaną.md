## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skardijaną

### Etymology

From *skardaz (“cut up, damaged”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cut, chop
2. _(reconstruction)_ to diminish, impair
