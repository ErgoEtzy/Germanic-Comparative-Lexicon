# *strittōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stritt%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *strittōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *stridʰ-néh₂-ti, an iterative verb from Proto-Indo-European *streydʰ- (“to resist”), the same root gave rise to the strong verb *strīdaną; see there for cognates.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *streydʰ-, *stridʰ-néh₂-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstrit.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to stand upright
2. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to resist
