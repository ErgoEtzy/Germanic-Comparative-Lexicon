## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swinganą

### Etymology

Unknown. If inherited, this could continue a Proto-Indo-European root *swengʷʰ- (*-gʷʰ- is suggested by Gothic 𐌰𐍆𐍃𐍅𐌰𐌲𐌲𐍅𐌾𐌰𐌽 (afswaggwjan)), and may be related to Sanskrit स्वञ्ज् (svañj, “to embrace, clasp, twist, wind around”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswiŋ.ɡɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to swing
