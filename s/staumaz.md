# *staumaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/staumaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *staumaz

### Etymology

From earlier *staubmaz (with loss of the *b triggered by preceding an *m in a heavy syllable), from *staubaz (“dust”), related to *steubaną (“to fly about, fume”). The quasi-Proto-Indo-European reconstruction is *stowbʰ-mo-s.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑu̯.mɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ steam, vapour
