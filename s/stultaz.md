[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stultaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stultaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *stl̥-to-s, from *stel- (“to place, stand”). Equivalent of Latin stultus (“stupid”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *stl̥-to-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstul.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ proud
2. _(reconstruction)_ stately
3. _(reconstruction)_ stiff
