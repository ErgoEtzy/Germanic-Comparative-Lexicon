# *staigraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/staigraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *staigraz

### Etymology

From *staigijaną (“to raise up, elevate”) + *-raz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-rós, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *stéygʰeti

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ elevating
2. _(reconstruction)_ steep
