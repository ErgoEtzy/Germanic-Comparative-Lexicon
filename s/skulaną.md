# *skulaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skulan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skulaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *skel- (“to be obligated, owe, be guilty”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *skel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsku.lɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to owe
2. _(auxiliary, reconstruction)_ to be obliged to, have to
3. _(auxiliary, reconstruction)_ shall, will, to be going to, must
