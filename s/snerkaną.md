## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *snerkaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)nerg-, *(s)nerk- (“to turn; wind; lace; shrink; shrivel”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)nerg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsner.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shrink; shrivel; contract
