[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skam%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skamō

### Etymology

Uncertain, but probably from pre-Germanic *skh₃-m-éh₂, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ḱeh₃- (“dark(ness)”). Related to Proto-Germanic *skandō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ḱeh₃-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑ.mɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ shame
