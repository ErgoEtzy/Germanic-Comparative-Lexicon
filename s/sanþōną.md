[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/san%C3%BE%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sanþōną

### Etymology

Derived from *sanþaz (“true”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁es-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑn.θɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to prove true, show to be true
