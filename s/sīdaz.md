# *sīdaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/s%C4%ABdaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sīdaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sh₁i-tó-s, probably from Proto-Indo-European *seh₁- (“long, lasting”). Has also been derived from Proto-Indo-European *seh₁- (“to send, throw, drop, sow, deposit”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *seh₁-, *sh₁i-tó-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsiː.ðɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ drooping, hanging down, pendulous, long, trailing
2. _(reconstruction)_ excessive, extra
3. _(reconstruction)_ ample
