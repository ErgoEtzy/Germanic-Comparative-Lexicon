# *skiftijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skiftijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skiftijaną

### Etymology

From earlier *skiptijaną, from yet earlier *skipatjaną, from a formation related to *skipōną (“to arrange”) + *-atjaną (“verbal suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-dyé-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskiɸ.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to divide, part, share
2. _(reconstruction)_ to put in order, organize, arrange
