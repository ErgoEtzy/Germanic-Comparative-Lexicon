# *slahaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/slahan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slahaną

### Etymology

From a Proto-Indo-European *slak- (“to hit, strike, throw”), apparently of onomatopoeic origin. Cognate with Middle Irish slachta (“hit”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *slak-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈslɑ.xɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to hit, to strike
2. _(reconstruction)_ to kill
