## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sprīþaną

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *spreyt-, of uncertain origin, with no certain cognates outside of Germanic; possibly from Proto-Indo-European *(s)perh₁- (“to kick”) + *-ey- + *-t-. Variant stem *sprīt- influenced by iterative *spritt-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)perh₁-, *-t-, *-éyti, *sperH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspriː.θɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to break up, become scattered
