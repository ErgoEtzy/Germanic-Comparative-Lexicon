## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *snakkōną

### Etymology

From *snak- + *-kōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No PIE form is listed or machine-resolvable in this snapshot.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsnɑk.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to blow continually, be windy
2. _(reconstruction)_ to boast; chatter, jabber
3. _(reconstruction)_ to smack the lips
4. _(reconstruction)_ to snap at
