# *stallaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stallaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stallaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sth₂-dʰló-, from *steh₂- (“to stand (up)”). Alternatively suggested to be from *stel- ~ *tsel- (“to place, erect”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sth₂-dʰló-, *sth₂dʰlóm

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑl.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ standing, position, stall, stable
