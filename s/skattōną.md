# *skattōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skatt%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skattōną

### Etymology

From *skattaz (“treasure, wealth”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *skatn-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑt.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to collect treasure
2. _(reconstruction)_ to estimate, value
3. _(reconstruction)_ to pay tribute
