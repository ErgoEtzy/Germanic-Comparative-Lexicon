# *skuttōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skutt%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skuttōną

### Etymology

From earlier paradigm *skuttōþi ~ *skutunanþi, from pre-Proto-Germanic *skud-nā́-ti ~ *skud-un-ánti, iterative to *skeutaną (“to shoot”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kewd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskut.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shoot, to throw
2. _(reconstruction)_ to shove, push
