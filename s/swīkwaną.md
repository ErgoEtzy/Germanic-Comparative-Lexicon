# *swīkwaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sw%C4%ABkwan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swīkwaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sweyg- (“to turn, move around, wander, swing”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sweyg-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to move out of the way; dodge; swerve; avoid; shun
2. _(reconstruction)_ to stand (someone) up; betray
3. _(reconstruction)_ to defraud; cheat
