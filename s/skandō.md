# *skandō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skand%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skandō

### Etymology

From earlier *skamdō, pre-Germanic *skomtéh₂, from *sko-m- (root of *skamō (“shame”)) + *-téh₂.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ḱeh₃-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑn.dɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ shame, disgrace
