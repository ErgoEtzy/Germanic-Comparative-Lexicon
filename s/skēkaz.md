## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skēkaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *skeg- (“to jump; move”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *skeg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɛː.kɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ robbery
2. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ abduction
