## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spurjaną

### Etymology

From *spurą (“track”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *sperH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspur.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to feel, touch
2. _(reconstruction)_ to study; to search; to examine
3. _(reconstruction)_ to ask
