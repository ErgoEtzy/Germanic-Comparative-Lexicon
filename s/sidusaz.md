[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sidusaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sidusaz

### Etymology

From *siduz (“custom, habit”) + *-saz (“-er”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *sh₂ey-, *swedʰ-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a member of the Sedusii, a Germanic tribe mentioned by Caesar
