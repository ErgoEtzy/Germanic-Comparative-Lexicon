## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *smiþjǭ

### Etymology

From *smiþaz (“smith”) + *-jǭ.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tus, *-tós, *smey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsmiθ.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a smithy, smith's workplace
