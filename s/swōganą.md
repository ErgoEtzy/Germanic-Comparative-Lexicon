# *swōganą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sw%C5%8Dgan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swōganą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sweh₂gʰ-. Cognate with Ancient Greek ἠχή (ēkhḗ).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sweh₂gʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɔː.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make sound by breathing, sigh, blow noisily
2. _(reconstruction)_ to roar
