# *swinōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/swin%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swinōną

### Etymology

A zero-grade formation to the strong verb *swīnaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswi.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to dwindle
2. _(reconstruction)_ to subside
