## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spurōną

### Etymology

From *spurą (“track, trace”) + *-ōną.

From *spurô (“spur”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *sperH-

- IPA: `/ˈspu.rɔː.nɑ̃/`
