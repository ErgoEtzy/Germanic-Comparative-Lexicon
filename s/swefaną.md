[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/swefan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swefaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *swep- (“to sleep”). Originally either a zero-grade present, or possibly an aorist-present.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swep-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswe.ɸɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sleep
