## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *strībaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *streybʰ- (“to be stiff”), from *ster- (“rigid; stiff”), for which see Proto-Germanic *staraz (“stiff”) for more.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *streybʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstriː.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be stiff; stiffen
2. _(reconstruction)_ to exert; make an effort
