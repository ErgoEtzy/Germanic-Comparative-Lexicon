[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/starf%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *starfą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *storbʰ-o-, from *sterbʰ- (“to be stiff, become stiff”). Synchronically analyzed as a nominal formation from *sterbaną (“to be stiff”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *storbʰ-o-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑr.ɸɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, neuter, reconstruction)_ labor
