## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *smilāną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *smey- (“to laugh; be glad”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)mey-, *smey-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to smile
