[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skiljan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skiljaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)kelH- (“to cut, separate”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kelH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskil.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cut
2. _(reconstruction)_ to split, divide
3. _(reconstruction)_ to separate, distinguish
