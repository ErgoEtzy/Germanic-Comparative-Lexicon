# *snōdō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sn%C5%8Dd%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *snōdō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *snoh₁téh₂ (“yarn, thread”), from *sneh₁(i)- (“to twist, wind, weave, plait”). Cognate with Latvian snāte (“linen cover”), Old Irish snáth (“thread”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *snoh₁téh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsnɔː.ðɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ string; rope
