# *sigizōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sigiz%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sigizōną

### Etymology

Verbal formation from *segaz (“victory, triumph”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsi.ɣi.zɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to win; gain; conquer; triumph; overcome
