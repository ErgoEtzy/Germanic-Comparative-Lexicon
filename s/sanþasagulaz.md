## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sanþasagulaz

### Etymology

From *sanþaz + *sagjaną + *-ulaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₁es-, *h₁s-ónt-s, *h₁sónts, *sekʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑn.θɑ.ˌsɑ.ɣu.lɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ tending to speak the truth
