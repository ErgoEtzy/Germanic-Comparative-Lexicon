# *sinīgaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sin%C4%ABgaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sinīgaz

### Etymology

From *sinaz or *siniz (“old”) + *-īgaz (forms adjectives from nouns, originally i-stem), possibly a modification of an older Proto-Indo-European *sen-ek- (“old”) whence also Latin senex, or *seniḱos. See *senaz for more cognates.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sen-ek-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsiniː.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(East, Germanic, reconstruction)_ old
