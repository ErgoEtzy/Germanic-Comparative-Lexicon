[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sinnan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sinnaną

### Etymology

From earlier *sennaną, *senþnaną, from Proto-Indo-European *sent-n-, from *sent- (“to feel”).
Alternatively, from Proto-Indo-European *senh₂- (“to obtain”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *senh₂-, *sent-, *sent-n-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsin.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to feel
2. _(reconstruction)_ to sense direction
3. _(reconstruction)_ to consider, think about; aim, plan, plot
