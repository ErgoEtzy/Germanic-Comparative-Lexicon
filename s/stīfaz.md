## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stīfaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *stéypos, from Proto-Indo-European *steyp- (“to be stiff, erect”). Related to Latin stīpō (“to compress”), Lithuanian sti̇̀pti (“to stiffen”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *steyp, *steyp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstiː.ɸɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ stiff, rigid
