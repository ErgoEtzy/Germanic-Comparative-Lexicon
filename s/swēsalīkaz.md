## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swēsalīkaz

### Etymology

From *swēsaz + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *swēdʰh₁-s-os

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɛː.sɑˌliː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ friendly
