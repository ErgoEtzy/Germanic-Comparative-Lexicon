# *spananą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/spanan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spananą

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *(s)penh₁- (“to spin (thread); to stretch”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)penh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspɑ.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to lure, to entice
