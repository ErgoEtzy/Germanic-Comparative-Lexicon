## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slīdaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sléydʰ-e-ti, from *sleydʰ- (“slippery”). Cognate with Vedic Sanskrit स्रेधति (srédhati, “to err, blunder”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sleydʰ-, *sléydʰ-e-ti

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to slide, to slip
