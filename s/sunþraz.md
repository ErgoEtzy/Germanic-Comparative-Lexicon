[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sun%C3%BEraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sunþraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sh₂ún-teros, from *sóh₂w-l̥ ~ *sh₂w-én-s (“the sun”) + *-teros (directional suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-teros, *sh₂ún-teros, *sóh₂wl̥

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsun.θrɑz/`
