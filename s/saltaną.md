# *saltaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/saltan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *saltaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *sh₂ld-, from Proto-Indo-European *seh₂l- with *-d- extension. Cognate with Latin sallō (“to salt”), Old Irish saillid.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *seh₂l-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑl.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to salt, pickle
