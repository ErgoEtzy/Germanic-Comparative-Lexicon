## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *selbawiljaz

### Etymology

From *selbaz + *wiljaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *welh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsel.βɑ.ˌwil.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ self-willed, voluntary
