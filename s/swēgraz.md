# *swēgraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sw%C4%93graz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swēgraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *swēḱurós (“brother-in-law”), a vṛddhi derivative of *swéḱuros (“father-in-law”), the latter whence *swehuraz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swēḱurós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɛː.ɣrɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male person that came into a family through marriage
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a brother-in-law
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a father-in-law
