## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skeldaną

### Etymology

From a Proto-Indo-European *skeldʰ- (“to announce”), which may be an extension of Proto-Indo-European *kelh₁- (“to call, to shout, to sound”) with an s-mobile, though Kroonen does not mention this and leaves the origin open.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kelh₁-, *skeldʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskel.dɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to scold
