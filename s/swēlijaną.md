# *swēlijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sw%C4%93lijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swēlijaną

### Etymology

From *swelaną (“to burn”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *swelH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswɛː.li.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to burn
2. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to smoke
