## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skrībaną

### Etymology

From Latin scrībō. Doublet of *hrībaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kreybʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskriː.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to write
