## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skrudōną

### Etymology

From earlier *skruþōną, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)krewt- (“to cut”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (“to cut”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ker-, *(s)krewt-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to examine, search
