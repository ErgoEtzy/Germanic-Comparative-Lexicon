# *skrepaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skrepan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skrepaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)krébʰ-e-ti, from *(s)kerbʰ- (“to scratch, to engrave”). The current form with -p- was influenced by the iteratives, the presumed primary form *skrebaną (“to scratch”) is unattested.
Related to *skerpaną and *skerbaną (“to scratch”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kerbʰ-, *(s)krébʰ-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskre.pɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to scratch, scrape
