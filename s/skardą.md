## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skardą

### Etymology

Nominalized form of *skardaz (“diminished”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑr.ðɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ notch; nick
