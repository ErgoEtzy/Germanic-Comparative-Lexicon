[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stre%C3%BEan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *streþaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sret- (“to swirl, to flow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sret-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstre.θɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to seethe, swirl
