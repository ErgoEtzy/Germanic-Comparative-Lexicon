## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *saigijaną

### Etymology

From *sīhwaną (“to filter”) + *-janą and *sīganą (“to sink, drop”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *seykʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑi̯.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to sink
2. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to percolate
3. _(reconstruction)_ to filter
