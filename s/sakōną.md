# *sakōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sak%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sakōną

### Etymology

From *sakō (“case, lawsuit, quarrel”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-(o)-éh₂, *-eh₂, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *-oh₁, *-éh₂, *seh₂g-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈˈsɑ.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to blame
2. _(reconstruction)_ to dispute
