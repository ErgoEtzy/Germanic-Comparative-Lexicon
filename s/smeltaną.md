# *smeltaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/smeltan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *smeltaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)méldeti, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)meld- (“to melt, smelt”). Compare Ancient Greek μέλδομαι (méldomai, “to melt”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)meld-, *(s)méldeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsmel.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(intransitive, reconstruction)_ to melt
