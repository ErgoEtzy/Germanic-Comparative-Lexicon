[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sadul%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sadulōną

### Etymology

From *sadulaz (“saddle”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sed-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑ.ðu.lɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to saddle
