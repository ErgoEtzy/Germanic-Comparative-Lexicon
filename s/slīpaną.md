# *slīpaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sl%C4%ABpan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slīpaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sley- (“smooth; slick; sticky; slimy”). Cognate with Cornish lym, Ancient Greek ὀλιβρός (olibrós, “smooth”), Latin limax.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sley-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to slide
2. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to grind
3. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to make smooth; sharpen
