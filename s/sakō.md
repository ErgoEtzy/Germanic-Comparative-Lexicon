[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sak%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sakō

### Etymology

From *sakaną (“to quarrel, to argue, to debate”) + *-ō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(o)-éh₂, *-eh₂, *-oh₁, *-éh₂, *seh₂g-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑ.kɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ quarrel
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ charge, case, lawsuit
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ thing, matter
