# *sūpaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/s%C5%ABpan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sūpaną

### Etymology

According to Kroonen, a back-formation from the iterative *suppōną (“to soak”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsuː.pɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to drink; sip; slurp; gulp
