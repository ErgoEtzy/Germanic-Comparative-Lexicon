# *slahtuz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/slahtuz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slahtuz

### Etymology

From *slahaną (“to hit, strike”) + *-þuz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *slak-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈslɑx.tuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ striking, hitting, beating
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ killing
