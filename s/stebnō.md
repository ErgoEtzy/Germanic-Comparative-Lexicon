# *stebnō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stebn%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stebnō

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *stemm-n-eh₂, from Proto-Indo-European *stemn- (“mouth, muzzle”). Cognate with Ancient Greek στόμα (stóma, “mouth, muzzle”); see there for more cognates.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *stemn-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsteβ.nɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ voice
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ sound
