## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stukkaz

### Etymology

Derived from *stikaną (“to stick, stab”). An alternative theory derives the word from Proto-Indo-European *(s)tewg- (“to push; hit”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)tewg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstuk.kɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ stick
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ beam; post
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ stump
