# *slīkaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sl%C4%ABkan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slīkaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sleyg-, *sleyǵ- (“to glide, smooth, spread”). Cognate with Old Irish sligim (“to smear”) and Proto-Slavic *slizъkъ (“smooth, slime”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sleyg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsliː.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to slide, to slip
