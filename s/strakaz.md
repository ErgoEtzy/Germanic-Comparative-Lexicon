# *strakaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/strakaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *strakaz

### Etymology

Probably from a Proto-Indo-European *(s)treg- (“rigid, stiff”), and cognate with Proto-Slavic *strȍgъ (“strict”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)treg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstrɑ.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ stretched, straight
