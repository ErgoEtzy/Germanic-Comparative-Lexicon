[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/saiwal%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *saiwalōną

### Etymology

From *saiwalō (“soul”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *sh₂ey-, *sh₂ey-wo-, *swoy-, *swé, *sóh₂i-wl̥ ~ *sh₂i-wéns, *wolh₁-eh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑi̯.wɑ.lɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to die
