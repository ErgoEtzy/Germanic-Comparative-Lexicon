## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *salō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *selh₁- (“to take, grab”). Cognate with Lithuanian siūlyti (“to propose, offer, upsend”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *selh₁-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a handing over, delivery, surrender
