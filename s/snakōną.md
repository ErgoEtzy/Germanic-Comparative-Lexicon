# *snakōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/snak%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *snakōną

### Etymology

From *snak- (“to breathe, blow; sigh”), of unknown origin. Perhaps ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)neh₂- (“to swim, float”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)neh₂-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to blow, waft
2. _(reconstruction)_ to breathe in or out
3. _(reconstruction)_ to take a whiff, sniff, smell
