## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sitjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sédyeti, from the root *sed- (“to sit”). Formal cognate with Ancient Greek ἕζομαι (hézomai).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sed-, *sédyeti
