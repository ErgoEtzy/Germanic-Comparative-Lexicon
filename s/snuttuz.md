# *snuttuz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/snuttuz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *snuttuz

### Etymology

Reflecting pre-Germanic *snutnús, likely back-formed from the accusative plural of an old n-stem *snéuþô (or similar), whence also the form *snuttaz, possibly from Proto-Indo-European *snew- (“to flow, run; moisture”).
Alternatively, onomatopoeic.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *snew-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsnut.tuz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ Nasal mucus; snot
