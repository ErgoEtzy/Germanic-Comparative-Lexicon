## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *staigō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *stóygʰos (“path; climb; ascent”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *stóygʰos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑi̯.ɣɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ way; path; trail
