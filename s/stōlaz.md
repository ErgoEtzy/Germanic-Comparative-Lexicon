# *stōlaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/st%C5%8Dlaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stōlaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *stoh₂los (“frame, rack, stand”), from *steh₂- (“to stand”). Cognate with Lithuanian stalas (“table”), Russian стол (stol, “table”), Ancient Greek στήλη (stḗlē, “sile, pillar”), Lithuanian pastoliai (“scaffolding”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *steh₂-, *stoh₂los

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɔː.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ stool
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ throne
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ chair
