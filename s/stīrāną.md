# *stīrāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/st%C4%ABr%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stīrāną

### Etymology

From *stīraz (“stiff, motionless”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstiː.rɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be stiff
2. _(reconstruction)_ to stare
