# *stadigōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stadig%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stadigōną

### Etymology

From *stadigaz (“firm”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑ.ði.ɣɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stop, make stand still
2. _(reconstruction)_ to make firm
