# *silāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sil%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *silāną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *seyl- (“still, windless, quiet, slow”). Cognate with Latin sileō (“be silent”). Related to Old English sālnes (“silence”), Old Norse sil (“slow flowing water”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *seyl-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be still, be silent
