# *sūganą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/s%C5%ABgan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sūganą

### Etymology

A back-formation from the iterative *sukkōną (“to suck”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsuː.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to suck
2. _(reconstruction)_ to suckle
