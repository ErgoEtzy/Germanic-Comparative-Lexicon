## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skaiþaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *skóyt-e-ti, the o-grade of a t-extension of *skey- (“to split, dissect”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *skóyt-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑi̯.θɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to separate
