## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stummaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *stm̥Hnós, from Proto-Indo-European *stemH- (“to push, inhibit, stutter, stammer”); see *stammaz (“stammering”) for more.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *stemH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstum.mɑz/`
