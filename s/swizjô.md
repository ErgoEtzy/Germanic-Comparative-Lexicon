# *swizjô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/swizj%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swizjô

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *swes-yós, "of a sister", from Proto-Indo-European *swes-, "to be of one's own, familiar".

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swes-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswiz.jɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ sister's son, nephew
