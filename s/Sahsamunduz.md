## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Sahsamunduz

### Etymology

From *sahsą (“seax, shortsword”) + *mundō (“protection”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *mh₂-nt-éh₂, *sek-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑx.sɑˌmun.duz/`

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
