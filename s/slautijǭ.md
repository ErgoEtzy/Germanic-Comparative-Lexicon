**Reconstruction:** *slautijǭ

### Etymology

Unclear; compare Proto-West Germanic *slaut (“puddle”) and Proto-Slavic *sludъ (“rime”). According to Pokorny, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)lew- (“slack; loose; limp”), see also Gothic 𐍃𐌻𐌰𐍅𐌰𐌽 (slawan).

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ hail, rime
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ slush, sleet
