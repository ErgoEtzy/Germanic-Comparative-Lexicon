# *skankijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skankijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skankijaną

### Etymology

From *skankaz (“askew”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)keng-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑŋ.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to skew, tilt, tip
2. _(reconstruction)_ to pour (a drink)
