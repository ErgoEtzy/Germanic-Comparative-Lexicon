[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/swelan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *swelaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *swelH- (“to burn”). Cognate with Lithuanian svi̇̀lti (“to singe”), Latvian svals (“vapor, smoke”), Ancient Greek εἵλη (heílē, “warmth of the sun”), and possibly Old Armenian նքողիմ (nkʻołim, “to be parched, dried, tired, unwell”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swelH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈswe.lɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to burn
