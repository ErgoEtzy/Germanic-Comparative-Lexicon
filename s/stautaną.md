## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stautaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *stówd-e-ti, an o-grade intensive formation from Proto-Indo-European *(s)tewd- (“to push, hit”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)tewd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑu̯.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to push, to bump, to jolt
