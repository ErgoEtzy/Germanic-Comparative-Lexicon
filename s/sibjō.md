# *sibjō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sibj%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sibjō

### Etymology

Likely derived from pre-Germanic *sebʰi/*sebʰyo, dative singular of reflexive pronoun Proto-Indo-European *s(w)e.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *s(w)e, *swé

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsiβ.jɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ relationship, kinship
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ friendship
