# *skundijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skundijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skundijaną

### Etymology

Unknown; The LIV suggests that the term was inherited from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kuh₁-né-t-ti, itself a nasal-infixed form of the root *(s)kweh₁t-, whence also perhaps Latin quatiō (“to shake”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kuh₁-né-t-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskun.di.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to drive; speed up; accelerate
