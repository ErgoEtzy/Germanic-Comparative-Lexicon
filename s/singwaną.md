# *singwaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/singwan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *singwaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *séngʷʰ-e-ti, from *sengʷʰ- (“to recite, sing”). Cognate with Ancient Greek ὀμφή (omphḗ, “divine voice, oracle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *séngʷʰ-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsin.ɡʷɑ.nɑ̃/`
- IPA: `[ˈs̠ɪŋʷ.ɡʷɑ.nɑ̃]`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sing
