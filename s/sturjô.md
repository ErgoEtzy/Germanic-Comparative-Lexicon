# *sturjô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sturj%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sturjô

### Etymology

Possibly from a non-IE substrate doublet *asetr-, *str-. Alternatively from Proto-Indo-European *sr̥Hyón-. Possible cognate with Proto-Balto-Slavic *eśetras (“sturgeon”), Late Latin hapax fariō (= ſariō (“salmon trout”)).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sr̥Hyón-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstur.jɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ sturgeon
