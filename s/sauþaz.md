# *sauþaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sau%C3%BEaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sauþaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂sewt- (“to roil”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂sewt-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsɑu̯.θɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ boiling; seething
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ wellspring; fountain; source
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ well
4. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ pit
