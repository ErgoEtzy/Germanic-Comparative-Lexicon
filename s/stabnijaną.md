## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stabnijaną

### Etymology

From *stabnō (“voice”) + *-janą.

From *stabnaz (“stem of a ship”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑβ.ni.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make oneself be heard
2. _(reconstruction)_ to tune an instrument
3. _(reconstruction)_ to vote
