## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *singrōniz

### Etymology

From *sin- + *grōniz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʰreh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsin.ˌɣrɔː.niz/`

### Adjective
