[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/snau%C3%BEuz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *snauþuz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ksnéw-tu-s, from *ksnew- (“to scrape, sharpen”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ksnéw-tu-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsnɑu̯.θuz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ bald, shaved, naked
2. _(reconstruction)_ poor, weak
