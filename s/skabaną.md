# *skabaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skaban%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skabaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *skábʰ-e-, present tense of Proto-Indo-European *skabʰ- (“to scratch”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *skabʰ-, *skábʰ-e-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑ.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shave, to scrape
