# *streuną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/streun%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *streuną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *strew- (“to spread around, strew”). Cognate with Latin struēs (“heap, pile”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *strew-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ heap; pile
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ hoard; treasure
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ acquisition; booty
