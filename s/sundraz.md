# *sundraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sundraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sundraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sn̥Htros, from *senH- (“apart, without, for oneself”); whether a relation to *sem- (“one”) exists is unclear. Cognate with Sanskrit सनितुर् (sanitúr), Avestan 𐬵𐬀𐬥𐬀𐬭𐬆 (hanar^ə), and possibly Proto-Celtic *santeros (“half”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *senH-, *sn̥Htros

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsun.drɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ separate, isolated, alone
