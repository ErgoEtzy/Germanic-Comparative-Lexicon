# *slipraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/slipraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *slipraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)leyb- (“slimy”), whence also *slīpaną (“to make smooth”). Probably cognate with Old Armenian լիրբ (lirb, “impudent”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)leyb-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈslip.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ smooth
2. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ slippery
