[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stang%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stangō

### Etymology

A nominal derivative from *stinganą (“to stick out, sting”), with quasi-Indo-European form *stongʰ-eh₂.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑŋ.ɡɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ bar; rod; stake
