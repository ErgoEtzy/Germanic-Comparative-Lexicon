# *stalukaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stalukaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stalukaz

### Etymology

From *staluz + *-ukaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *stel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑ.lu.kɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ small rod or staff
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ handle
3. _(masculine, reconstruction, of a plant)_ stalk
