## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stubbô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)tewb- or *(s)tewbʰ- (“to push, knock, butt”), related to the synonymous root *(s)tewp-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)tewb-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstub.bɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ stump; stub
