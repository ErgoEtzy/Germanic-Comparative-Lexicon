[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stunjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stunjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)ten- (“to thunder, roar, groan”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ten-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstun.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sigh, pant
2. _(reconstruction)_ to moan, groan
