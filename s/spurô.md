## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spurô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sperH- (“to twitch, push, fidget, be quick”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sperH-
