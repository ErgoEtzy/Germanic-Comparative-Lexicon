## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skaiþōną

### Etymology

From *skaiþiz (“sheath”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *-tis, *skóyt-e-ti

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sheath
