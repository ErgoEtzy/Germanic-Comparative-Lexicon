## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sparkaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *sp(h)erag-, from *sper- (“to jerk, push, fidget, spring”) or alternatively from Proto-Indo-European *sperg- (“to strew, scatter, sprinkle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sp(h)erag-, *sperg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspɑr.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ energetic, lively
