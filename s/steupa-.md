[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/steupa-)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *steupa-

### Etymology

From *steupaz (“pushed away; cut off; abandoned; deserted; orphaned”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)tew- (“to push”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)tew-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsteu̯.pɑ/`

### Prefix

1. _(morpheme, reconstruction)_ step-
