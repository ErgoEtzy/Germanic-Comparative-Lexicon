## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skipārijaz

### Etymology

From *skipą + *-ārijaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *skey-, *skēi-b-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈski.pɑː.ri.jɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ one who travels by ship; sailor
