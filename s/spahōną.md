# *spahōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/spah%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spahōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *speḱ- (“to see, to look, to observe”). Equivalent to *spahō (“a looking, peering, prophesy”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *speḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspɑ.xɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to observe
