[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/silubr%C4%ABnaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *silubrīnaz

### Etymology

From *silubrą + *-īnaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂ŕ̥ǵn̥tom

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsi.lu.βriː.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ silvern, made of silver
