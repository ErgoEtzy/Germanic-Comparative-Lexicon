## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skamōlausaz

### Etymology

From *skamō (“shame”) + *-lausaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)ḱeh₃-, *lewh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈskɑ.mɑ.lɑu̯.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ shameless
