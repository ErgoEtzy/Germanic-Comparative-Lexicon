## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spuþþōną

### Etymology

From *spuþþą, *spuþþaz (“mockery”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspuθ.θɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to mock, deride, ridicule
