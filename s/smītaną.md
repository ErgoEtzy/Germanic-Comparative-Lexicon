# *smītaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sm%C4%ABtan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *smītaną

### Etymology

Perhaps from a Proto-Indo-European *smeyd- (“to smear, whick, strike, rub”), and cognate with Proto-Slavic *smědъ (“brown, dirty”), Old Armenian միծ (mic, “fetid mud”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *smeyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsmiː.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cast; sling; hurl
2. _(reconstruction)_ to hit; strike
3. _(reconstruction)_ to smear; dirty
