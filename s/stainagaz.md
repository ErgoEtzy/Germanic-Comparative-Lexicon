## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stainagaz

### Etymology

From *stainaz + *-gaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *steyh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑi̯.nɑ.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ stony, rocky
