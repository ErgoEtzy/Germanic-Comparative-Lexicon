# *sprekaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sprekan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sprekaną

### Etymology

Probably from a Proto-Indo-European *spregʰ- (“to crackle”), and cognate with Old Norse spraka (“to crackle, patter”), Lithuanian spragė́ti (“to crackle, patter”). Alternatively, suggested to be from a Proto-Indo-European *spreg- on the basis of Albanian shpreh (“to express, voice”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *spreg-, *spregʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspre.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to speak
