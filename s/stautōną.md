# *stautōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/staut%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stautōną

### Etymology

From *stautiz (“a push”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)tewd-, *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstɑu̯.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to push
