[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/stu%C3%BEs)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *stuþs

### Etymology

Likely from Pre-Germanic *stút-s, root noun from Proto-Indo-European *stut- (“prop, support”), which may be an extension of a root *stew- (“firm”). Compare Latvian stute (“prop, support”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *stut-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈstuθs/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ support, prop, post
