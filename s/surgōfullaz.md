# *surgōfullaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/surg%C5%8Dfullaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *surgōfullaz

### Etymology

From *surgō (“worry, sorrow”) + *-fullaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *swergʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsur.ɣɔːˌɸul.lɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ sorrowful, mournful
2. _(reconstruction)_ worrisome
