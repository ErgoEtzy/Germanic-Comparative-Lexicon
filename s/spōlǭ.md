## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *spōlǭ

### Etymology

Kroonen suggests an inheritance from Proto-Indo-European *speh₂-tleh₂, from *(s)peh₂-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)peh₂-, *speh₂-tleh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspɔː.lɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ spool; reel
