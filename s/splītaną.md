# *splītaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/spl%C4%ABtan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *splītaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)pleyd- (“to split, splice”). Cognate with Middle Irish slis (“shavings, splinters”). The Indo-European root may be an extension of *(s)pley- (“to split, splice”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)pleyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈspliː.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to split
