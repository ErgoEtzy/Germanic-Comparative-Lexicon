# *sinþōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/sin%C3%BE%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *sinþōną

### Etymology

Derived from *sinþaz (“journey”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sent-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈsin.θɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to go on a journey, travel
