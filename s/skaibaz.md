# *skaibaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/skaibaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *skaibaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *skoy-pó-s, which may be a derivative of *skey- (“to split”). Orel compares Lithuanian skybas (“wedge-like piece of land”), Latvian šķìbs (“crooked”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *skoy-pó-s

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ slanted, oblique, inclined, sloping
