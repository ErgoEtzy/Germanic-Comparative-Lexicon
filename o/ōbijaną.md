## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ōbijaną

### Etymology

The causative-iterative formation to a lost strong verb *afaną, from Proto-Indo-European *h₃ep- (“to labor, work”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃ep-

### Pronunciation

### Verb
