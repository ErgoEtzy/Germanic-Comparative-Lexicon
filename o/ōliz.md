# *ōliz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C5%8Dliz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ōliz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *alaną (“to raise, nourish”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂el-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɔː.liz/`

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ raising, nourishing, feeding
2. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ able or fit to be fed, nourished
