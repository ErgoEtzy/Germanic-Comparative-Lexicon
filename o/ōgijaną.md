# *ōgijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C5%8Dgijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ōgijaną

### Etymology

Causative of *aganą (“to fear”). Compare *agjaną as evidenced by Gothic *𐌰𐌲𐌾𐌰𐌽 (*agjan, “to frighten”), which is the inherited causative with similar meaning.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂egʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɔː.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to scare
