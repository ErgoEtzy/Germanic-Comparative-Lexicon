## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Rīnaz

### Etymology

Ultimately from Proto-Celtic *reinos (“river, waterway”). The vowel excludes a direct borrowing from Gaulish *Rēnos.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃reyH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈriː.nɑz/`

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ the river Rhine
