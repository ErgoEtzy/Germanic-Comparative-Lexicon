# *rōō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C5%8D%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rōō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁roh₁-weh₂, from *h₁reh₁- (“quiet, calm”). Cognate with Avestan 𐬭𐬁𐬨𐬀𐬥 (rāman, “peace, tranquility”) and Sanskrit रात्रि (rātri, “night”), whence Hindi रात (rāt, “night”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁reh₁-, *h₁roh₁-weh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɔː.ɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ calm, quiet
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ rest, repose
