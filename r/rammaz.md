## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rammaz

### Etymology

Unknown; perhaps related to Old Church Slavonic рамѣнъ (raměnŭ, “severe”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑm.mɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ strong
2. _(reconstruction)_ overbearing; overwhelming
