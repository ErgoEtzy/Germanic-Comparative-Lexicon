## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raumaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *réwgʰmn̥ (“cream”), from Proto-Indo-European *rew-, *rewe-, *rū- (“to scratch, dig, scrape, take off, skim”). Possible cognate with Proto-Indo-Iranian *ráwgʰnas, whence Proto-Iranian *ráwgnah (“butter, oil”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *rew-, *réwgʰmn̥

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑu̯.mɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ skin; film
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ milkfat, cream
