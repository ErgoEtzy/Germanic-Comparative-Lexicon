# *raubō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/raub%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raubō

### Etymology

From *reufaną (“to tear”), from Proto-Indo-European *Hrewp- (“to peel; tear; strip”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hrewp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑu̯.βoː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ rift, gap
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ plunder, booty
