## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rīks

### Etymology

An early borrowing from Proto-Celtic *rīxs, from Proto-Indo-European *h₃rḗǵs. Doublet of *rekô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃rḗǵs

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/riːks/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ king, ruler
