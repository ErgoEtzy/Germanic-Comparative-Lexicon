## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raidijaz

### Etymology

By surface analysis, *raidaz + *-jaz, but it is not clear which function of the suffix this would be in a synchronic view of the protolanguage. According to Kroonen, possibly thematicized from an older “gerundive” *raidiz, *ga-raidiz, thereafter conflated with *(ga-)raidaz.
