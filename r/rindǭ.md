## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rindǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *rem- (“to rest, prop up”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *rem-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ bark (tree)
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ crust
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ rind
