# *rōtaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C5%8Dtaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rōtaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *HroHdo-, cognate with Proto-Slavic *radъ.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *HroHdo-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɔː.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ glad, cheerful
