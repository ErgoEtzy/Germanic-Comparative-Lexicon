## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rutāną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *Hrewd- (“to tear; rend”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hrew-, *Hrewd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈru.tɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to rot
