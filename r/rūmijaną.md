[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C5%ABmijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rūmijaną

### Etymology

From *rūmaz (“roomy, spacious”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(H)rewH-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make room, to clear
