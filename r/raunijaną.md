## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raunijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(e)rwō- (to trace, find out).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(e)rwō-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrau̯.ni.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ To attempt; to try
