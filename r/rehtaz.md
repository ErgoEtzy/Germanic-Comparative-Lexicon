# *rehtaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/rehtaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rehtaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₃reǵtós (“right, straightened”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃reǵtós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrex.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ right, straight
2. _(reconstruction)_ morally upright, just
