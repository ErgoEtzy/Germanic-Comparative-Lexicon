## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rankijaną

### Etymology

From *rankaz (“upright, erect”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *h₃reǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈraŋ.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to erect, to make stand upright
2. _(reconstruction)_ to make proud
