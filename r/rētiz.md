# *rētiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C4%93tiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rētiz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *Hrēh₃dis, a long-grade verbal adjective derived to a lost strong verb *rōtaną, from Proto-Indo-European *Hreh₃d-. Related to Latin rōdō (“to gnaw”) and Sanskrit रदति (radati, “to dig”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hreh₃d-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɛː.tiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ fierce, violent
