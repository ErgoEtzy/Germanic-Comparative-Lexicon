# *rōdijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C5%8Ddijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rōdijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂roh₁dʰéyeti, from *Hreh₁dʰ- (“to think, arrange”). Equivalent to *rēdaną (“to decide, advise”) + *-janą.
Cognate with Proto-Slavic *raditi.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hreh₁dʰ-, *h₂roh₁dʰéyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɔː.ði.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to speak, talk about
