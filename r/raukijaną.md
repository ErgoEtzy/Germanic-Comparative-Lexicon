# *raukijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/raukijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raukijaną

### Etymology

From *raukiz (“smoke”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑu̯.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to smoke
