# *rīsaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C4%ABsan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rīsaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁réy-(h₁)se-ti, from *h₁rey- (“to rise”) + *-(h₁)seti (“desiderative imperfect verbal suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-(h₁)seti, *h₁rey-, *h₁réy-(h₁)se-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈriː.sɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to scale; move vertically (either up or down)
2. _(reconstruction)_ to rise
