# *Rīkaharduz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/R%C4%ABkaharduz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Rīkaharduz

### Etymology

From *rīks (“king, ruler”) + *harduz (“hard, brave”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃reǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈriː.kɑ.ˌxɑr.ðuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
