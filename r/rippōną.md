# *rippōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ripp%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rippōną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *Hripnéh₂-, a zero-grade iterative to *rīfaną/*rīpaną (“to tear”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁reyp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrip.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to tear, tear off
