# *regną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/regn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *regną

### Etymology

Uncertain, possibly from a Proto-Indo-European *Hréǵʰ-no-s, from a root *Hreǵʰ- (“to flow water”). May be cognate with Latin rigō (“to irrigate, moisten”) and particularly Albanian rrjedh (“to flow”), but this is uncertain.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hréǵʰ-no-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈreɡ.nɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ rain
