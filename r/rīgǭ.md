## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rīgǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *Hréyk-ō ~ *Hrik-nés, from *Hreyk- (“to line up; to arrange”) + *-ō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-ō, *Hreyk-, *Hréyk-ō ~ *Hrik-nés

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ row, line
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ bar, post
