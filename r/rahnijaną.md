## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rahnijaną

### Etymology

From *rahną (“robbery, plunder”) + *-janą.

From an n-suffixed word like *rahną (“decision, reckoning, counsel”) without direct descendants, close in semantics to Proto-Germanic *raginą and Gothic 𐌲𐌰-𐍂𐌴𐌷𐍃𐌽𐍃 f (ga-rēhsns, “*decision; schedule, plan”), ultimately from the same root *Hrek- (“to speak, declare; to organize”). Possible piecewise doublet of etymology 1.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hrek-
