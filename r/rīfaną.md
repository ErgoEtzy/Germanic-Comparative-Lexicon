[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C4%ABfan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rīfaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁reyp- (“to tear”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁reyp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈriː.ɸɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to tear, rend
