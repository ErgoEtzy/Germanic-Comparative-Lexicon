# *rafsijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/rafsijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rafsijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁rep- (“to rip, snatch, tear, pinch”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁rep-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑɸ.si.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to snatch
2. _(reconstruction)_ to punish; flog; chastise; reprove
3. _(reconstruction)_ to blame
