## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ristijaną

### Etymology

Most likely an intensive formation to *rīstaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈris.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to slice, to cut
