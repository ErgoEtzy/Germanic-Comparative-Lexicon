# *raubaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/raubaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raubaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *Hrewp- (“to peel; tear; strip”), synchronically analyzed as a nominal derivative of *reufaną (“to tear”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hrewp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑu̯.βɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ rape, pillage, robbery
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ that which is stolen, spoils, booty
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ garment, robe
