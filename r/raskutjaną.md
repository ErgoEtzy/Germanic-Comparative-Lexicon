## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raskutjaną

### Etymology

From *raskuz + *-utjaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *Hreth₂-, *Hroth₂skus

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to repeat quickly
