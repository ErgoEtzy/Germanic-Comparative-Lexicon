# *ruppōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/rupp%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ruppōną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *Hrupnéh₂-, a zero-grade iterative to *reufaną (“to break off, open”).

An iterative verb likely of onomatopoeic origin.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrup.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to tear off
2. _(reconstruction)_ to rub
