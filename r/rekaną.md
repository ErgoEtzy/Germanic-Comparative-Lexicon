## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rekaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₃réǵeti (“to make straight”), via semantic shift "make straight" > "erect (a pile)" > "rake and pile up".

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃réǵeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈre.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to rake up, to pile up, to heap up
