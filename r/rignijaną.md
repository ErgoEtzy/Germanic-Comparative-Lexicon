[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/rignijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rignijaną

### Etymology

From *regną (“rain”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *Hréǵʰ-no-s

### Verb

1. _(impersonal, reconstruction)_ to rain
