## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rēdalausaz

### Etymology

From *rēdaz (“advice, counsel”) + *-lausaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *Hreh₁dʰ-, *Hréh₁dʰos, *lewh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɛː.ðɑ.lɑu̯.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ ill-advised, unwise
2. _(reconstruction)_ desperate
3. _(reconstruction)_ clueless, at a loss
