# *reufaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/reufan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *reufaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *Hréwpt, from *Hrewp- (“to break”). Cognate with Latin rumpō (“to burst, break open”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hrewp-, *Hréwpt

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈreu̯.ɸɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to tear
