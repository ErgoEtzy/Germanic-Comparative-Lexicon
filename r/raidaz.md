# *raidaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/raidaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raidaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂roy-dʰ-ó-s (“arranged, ready”), from *h₂rey-dʰ- (“to put in order, arrange, make ready”), from the root *h₂rey- (“to count, order”). Cognate with Latvian raids, though this has been suggested as borrowed from Germanic.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂roy-dʰ-ó-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑi̯.ðaz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ ready, arranged, prepared
