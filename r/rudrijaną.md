## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rudrijaną

### Etymology

Derived from unattested *rudraz, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁rudʰrós.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁rewdʰ-, *h₁rudʰrós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈruð.ri.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to redden, become red
2. _(reconstruction)_ to blush
