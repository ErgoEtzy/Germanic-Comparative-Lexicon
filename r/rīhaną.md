## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rīhaną

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *h₁reyḱ- (“to break, scratch”), whence also Ancient Greek ἐρείκω (ereíkō, “to rend, bruise, pound”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁reyḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈriː.xɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to line up, to string together
