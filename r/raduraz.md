## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raduraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *Hreth₂- (“to run”). Cognate with Latin rota (“wheel”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hreth₂-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ wheel; circle
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ sky, heaven
