## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raskuz

### Etymology

From earlier *raþskuz, from Proto-Indo-European *Hroth₂skus (“rapid”), from Proto-Indo-European *Hreth₂- (“to run, roll”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hreth₂-, *Hroth₂skus

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑs.kuz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ rapid, snell
