# *raustijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/raustijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raustijaną

### Etymology

From *raustiz (“voice”) + *-janą, from Proto-Indo-European *Hres- (“to shout, speak”). Compare the noun *razdō (“voice”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hres-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑu̯s.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shout
2. _(reconstruction)_ to sing
3. _(reconstruction)_ to rejoice, exult
