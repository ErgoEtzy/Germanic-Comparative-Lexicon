# *rūnō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C5%ABn%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rūnō

### Etymology

Possibly a borrowing either from Proto-Celtic *rūnā or from the same source as it; see there for more.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈruː.nɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ secret, mystery
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ inscription written with runes (letters of the Germanic runic alphabet)
