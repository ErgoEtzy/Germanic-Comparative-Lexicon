# *raidijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/raidijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raidijaną

### Etymology

From *rīdaną + *-janą.

From *raidaz (“ready”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *Hreydʰ-, *h₂roy-dʰ-ó-s, *h₃réydʰh₁eti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑi̯.ði.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to move, carry (up, forward, etc.), let fall, etc.
2. _(reconstruction)_ to transport on horseback
