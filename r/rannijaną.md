# *rannijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/rannijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rannijaną

### Etymology

From *rinnaną + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *h₃er-, *h₃reyH-, *h₃r̥-néw-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑn.ni.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to run
2. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to flow
