## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raikijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *Hroyǵ-éye-ti, a causative o-grade of *Hreyǵ- (“to stretch out, reach”). Cognate with Lithuanian ráižytis (“to keep stretching”), réižti (“to stretch, tighten”), Old Irish rigid (“to stretch”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hreyǵ-, *Hroyǵ-éye-ti

### Pronunciation

### Verb
