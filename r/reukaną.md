## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *reukaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁rewg- (“to vomit, burp, roar”) (though Kroonen appears implicitly skeptical).
Compare typologically Russian изрыга́ть (izrygátʹ) (из- (iz-) + рыга́ть (rygátʹ)).

1. _(reconstruction)_ to smoke, to give off a vapour
