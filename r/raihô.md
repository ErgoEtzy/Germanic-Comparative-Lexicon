[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/raih%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raihô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *róyko-, from Proto-Indo-European *rey- (“spotted, streaked”). Compare Irish riabh (“stripe; streak”), Latvian ràibs (“spotted”), Russian рябо́й (rjabój, “mottled fur”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *rey-, *róyko-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑi.xɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ deer
