## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raidōwarjaz

### Etymology

From *raidō (“ride, journey”) + *warjaz (“inhabitant”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *Hreydʰ-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ member of a certain Alemannic tribe (mentioned by Ammianus)
