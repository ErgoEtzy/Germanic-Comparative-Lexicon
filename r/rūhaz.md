# *rūhaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C5%ABhaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rūhaz

### Etymology

Probably related to Sanskrit रूक्ष (rūkṣá, “rough”), from Proto-Indo-European *HruHḱsos. Alternatively from Proto-Indo-European *h₃rewk-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *HruHḱsos, *h₃rewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈruː.xɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ rough
