[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C4%93dan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rēdaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *Hréh₁dʰeti, from *Hreh₁dʰ- (“to think, arrange”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hreh₁dʰ-, *Hréh₁dʰeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɛː.ðɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to decide, advise
2. _(reconstruction)_ to interpret
