# *rōkijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C5%8Dkijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rōkijaną

### Etymology

From *rōkaz (“care”) + *-ijaną, the former from a Proto-Indo-European *h₂reh₁ǵ- (“to help”) and compared with Ancient Greek ἀρήγω (arḗgō, “to aid; to ward off”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂reh₁ǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɔː.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to care, care for, take care of
2. _(reconstruction)_ to mind, heed, attend to
