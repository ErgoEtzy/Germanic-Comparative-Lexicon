[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/razd%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *razdō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *Hres- (“to shout, speak”), extended as *Hros-dʰ(h₁)-eh₂-. Compare Sanskrit रसना (rasanā, “tongue”), रसति (rásati, “to call out, scream, roar”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hres-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑz.dɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ voice
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ sound
