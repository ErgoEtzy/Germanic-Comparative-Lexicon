## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rukkijaną

### Etymology

From *rukkiz (“pull, jerk”) + *-janą, ultimately from the same source as *rukkōną (“to rock”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈruk.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to pull, to tug
2. _(reconstruction)_ to rock
