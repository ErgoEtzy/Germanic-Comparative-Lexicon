[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C4%93sw%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rēswō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂reh₁dʰ-tweh₂, from *h₂reh₁dʰ- (“to arrange; to advise”) + *-tweh₂. Equivalent to *rēdaną + *-þwō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-tweh₂, *Hreh₁dʰ-, *h₂reh₁dʰ-tweh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɛːs.wɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ counsel, deliberation
