## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rēdaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *Hréh₁dʰos, from *Hreh₁dʰ- (“to think, arrange”). Synchronically analyzable as a nominal formation from *rēdaną (“to advise”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hreh₁dʰ-, *Hréh₁dʰos
