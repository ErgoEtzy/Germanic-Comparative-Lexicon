# *razną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/razn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *razną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁rh̥₁-s-nó-m, from *h₁reh₁- (“to rest”) + *-s- + *-nóm, related to *rastō (“rest”) (from Proto-Indo-European *h₁rh̥₁-s-tó-s). Alternatively, cognate with Proto-Balto-Slavic *radás (“birth, genus”), from *(H)rodʰ-sno-m, from a *(H)redʰ- (“to appear, come into existence”) + *-snom.
Compare typologically Russian поко́и (pokói), поко́й (pokój).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(H)redʰ-, *-sneh₂, *h₁rh̥₁-s-nó-m, *h₁rh̥₁-s-tó-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑz.nɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ house, dwelling
