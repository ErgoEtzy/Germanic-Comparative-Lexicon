# *raginōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ragin%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raginōną

### Etymology

From *raginą (“advice”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hrek-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑ.ɣi.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to advise, give counsel
2. _(reconstruction)_ to order, rule
