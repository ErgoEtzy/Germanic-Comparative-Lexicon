## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rankaz

### Etymology

Likely derived unattested verb *rinkaną, from nasal-infix present of Proto-Indo-European *h₃reǵ-, possibly related to *rinkiz (literally “upright man”), whence Old Norse rekkr.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃reǵ-

### Pronunciation

### Adjective
