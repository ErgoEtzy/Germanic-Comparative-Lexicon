## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rainō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *rey- (“to cut, scratch, tear”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *rey-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ baulk, ridge
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ boundary, border, edge
