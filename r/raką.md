## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raką

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *reǵ- (“to bind”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *reǵ-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ cord
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ link; (in the plural) chain
