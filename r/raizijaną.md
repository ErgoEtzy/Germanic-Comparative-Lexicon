# *raizijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/raizijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raizijaną

### Etymology

From *rīsaną + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(h₁)seti, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *h₁rey-, *h₁réy-(h₁)se-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑi̯.zi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause (someone, something) to rise; raise
