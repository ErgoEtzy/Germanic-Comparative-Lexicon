## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *regnōną

### Etymology

From *regną (“rain”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *Hréǵʰ-no-s

### Verb

1. _(impersonal, reconstruction)_ to rain
