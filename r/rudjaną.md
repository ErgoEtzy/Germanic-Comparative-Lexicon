## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rudjaną

### Etymology

From *rudą (“a clearing”) + *-janą, possibly from Proto-Indo-European *(H)rewH- (“to dig out”), see also Latin ruo (“I dig out”), Proto-Slavic *rъvati (“to tear”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(H)rewH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈruð.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to clear
2. _(reconstruction)_ to strip
