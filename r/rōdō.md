## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rōdō

### Etymology

Uncertain; possibly from Proto-Indo-European *reh₁t- (“post”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *reh₁t-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɔː.ðɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ rod, pole
