# *rīrāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C4%ABr%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rīrāną

### Etymology

Seemingly from the same reduplicated Proto-Indo-European *Hrey-Hroy-e- as Sanskrit लेलायति (lelāyáti, “to quiver, tremble”), from *Hrey-. Further compare Latvian raize (“worry, concern”), rieva (“wrinkle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hrey-Hroy-e-

### Verb

1. _(East, Germanic, reconstruction)_ to tremble
