## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rudāną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁rudʰéh₁ti, from *h₁rewdʰ- (“red”) (from which also *raudaz).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁rewdʰ-, *h₁rudʰéh₁ti

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to redden (to become red)
