## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rōaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁roh₁-(ye-)-ti, from *h₁reh₁- (“to row”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁roh₁-(ye-)-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɔː.ɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to row
