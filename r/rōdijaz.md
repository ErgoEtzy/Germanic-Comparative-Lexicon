# *rōdijaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C5%8Ddijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rōdijaz

### Etymology

Derived from *rōdijaną (“to speak, talk about”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hreh₁dʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɔː.ði.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ spoken of, talked about
2. _(reconstruction)_ arranged, counted
