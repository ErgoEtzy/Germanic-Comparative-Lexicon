# *raitijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/raitijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *raitijaną

### Etymology

Causative-iterative form of *wrītan (“to scratch, write”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɑi̯.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stir, to irritate
