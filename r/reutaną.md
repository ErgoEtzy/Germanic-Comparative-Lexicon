# *reutaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/reutan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *reutaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *HréwdHti, from the root *HrewdH- (“to cry, weep”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *HrewdH-, *HréwdHti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈreu̯.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cry, to wail
