# *rīkijaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C4%ABkijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rīkijaz

### Etymology

From *rīks (“king”). Compare Latin rēgius.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈriː.ki.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ kingly, royal, noble
2. _(reconstruction)_ mighty, powerful
3. _(reconstruction)_ rich
