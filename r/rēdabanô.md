# *rēdabanô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C4%93daban%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rēdabanô

### Etymology

From *rēdaz (“counsel, advice”) + *banô (“slayer, murderer”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *Hreh₁dʰ-, *Hréh₁dʰos, *gʷʰen-, *gʷʰon-ō

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɛː.ðɑ.ˌbɑ.nɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ accessory to murder; one involved in the planning of a murder
