## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rēhō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *Hrek- (“to speak; to put in order”). Alternatively from Proto-Indo-European *Hreyk- (“to line up; to arrange”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hrek-, *Hreyk-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ row, line
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ pole, bar
