# *rōdīniz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/r%C5%8Dd%C4%ABniz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *rōdīniz

### Etymology

From *rōdijaną (“to speak, talk about”) + *-īniz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hreh₁dʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈrɔː.ðiː.niz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ an act of speaking, talking about
