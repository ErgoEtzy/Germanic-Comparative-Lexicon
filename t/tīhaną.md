# *tīhaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/t%C4%ABhan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tīhaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *déyḱeti, from *deyḱ- (“to show”). Cognate with Latin dīcō (“to speak, say, tell”), Ancient Greek δείκνυμι (deíknumi, “to show, point out, explain, teach”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deyḱ-, *déyḱeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtiː.xɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to show, point out
2. _(reconstruction)_ to blame, accuse
