# *tremaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/treman%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tremaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *drem- (“to run”). A direct cognate with Sanskrit द्रमति (drámati, “to run (about)”).
Possibly related to Old Norse tramr.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *drem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtre.mɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to wander, waver
