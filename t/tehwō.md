## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tehwō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *déḱweh₂, from *deḱ- (“to fit”). Verner's alternate of *tēwō with different ablaut of the root syllable.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *déḱweh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈte.xʷɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ order, array
