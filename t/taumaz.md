# *taumaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/taumaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *taumaz

### Etymology

From earlier *taugmaz, from *teuhaną, from Proto-Indo-European *dewk- (“to draw, pull”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑu̯.mɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a tie; tether; rope; cord; strap; leash; bridle
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ that which is tied or bound together; group; team
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ birth; line; lineage; offspring
