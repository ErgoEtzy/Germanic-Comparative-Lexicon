## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *teuhaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dewk- (“to pull, lead”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈteu̯.xɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to lead
2. _(reconstruction)_ to pull behind oneself, draw, drag
