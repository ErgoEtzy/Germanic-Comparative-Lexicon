## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tainijǭ

### Etymology

From *tainaz (“rod; twig”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑi.ni.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ wickerwork
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ wicker basket
