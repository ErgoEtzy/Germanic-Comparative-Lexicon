[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/terw%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *terwą

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *derwom, derived from Proto-Indo-European *dóru (“tree”). Related to Lithuanian derva (“resin, tar”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dóru

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈter.wɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ tar
