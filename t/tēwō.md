# *tēwō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/t%C4%93w%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tēwō

### Etymology

From earlier *tēgwō, from Proto-Indo-European *dēḱ-wéh₂, from *deḱ- (“to fit”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dēḱ-wéh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɛː.wɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ order, array
