# *tundijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tundijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tundijaną

### Etymology

A zero-grade intensive verb related to *tinnaną (“to burn”) and *tandijaną (“to kindle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtun.di.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to kindle, to light on fire
