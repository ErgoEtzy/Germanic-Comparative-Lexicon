## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *trugaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *drukós (“trough, vessel”), from the same root as Proto-Indo-European *dóru (“tree, wood”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *drukós, *dóru

### Noun
