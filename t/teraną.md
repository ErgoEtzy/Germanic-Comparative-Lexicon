# *teraną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/teran%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *teraną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *der- (“to flay, split, cleave, crack”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *der-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈte.rɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to tear; snatch or pull off (from); tug
