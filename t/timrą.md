[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/timr%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *timrą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dem(H)-ro-s, from *dem- (“to build”). Cognate with Latin domus (“house; home”), Ancient Greek δέμω (démō, “to build”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dem(H)-ro-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtim.rɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ building, structure
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ timber
