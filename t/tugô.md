## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tugô

### Etymology

From the zero-grade of *teuhaną + *-ô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtu.ɣɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ he who draws; leader
