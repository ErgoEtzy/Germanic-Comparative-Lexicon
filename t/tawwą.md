# *tawwą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/taww%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tawwą

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *dewh₂- (“to tie to; secure”). Kroonen (2013) prefers to derive it from *deh₁-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dewh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑw.wɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ tool; apparatus; gadget
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ equipment; rigging
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ rope, line; fiber
