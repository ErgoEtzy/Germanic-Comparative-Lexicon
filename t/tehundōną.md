[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tehund%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tehundōną

### Etymology

From *tehundô (“tenth”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *deḱm̥tós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈte.xun.dɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to pay a tithe, to pay a tenth of something
2. _(reconstruction)_ to receive a tithe, to receive a tenth of something
