## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *talō

### Etymology

As if from Proto-Indo-European *dolH-éh₂, from the root *del(H)- (“to draw, attract”). Compare Hittite talliije/azi (“to pray to, evoke (a deity)”), Proto-Germanic *tuljaną (“to lure”), Ancient Greek δόλος (dólos, “trick(ery), deceit; bait”), and Old Armenian տող (toł, “line, row”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *delH-, *dolH-éh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑ.lɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ narration, speech
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ number, calculation
