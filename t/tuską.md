# *tuską

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tusk%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tuską

### Etymology

Unknown. Kroonen suggests that it could continue earlier *tud-ska-, perhaps with the same suffix seen in *fruskaz (“frog”). Pokorny further derives it from Proto-Indo-European *deh₂w- (“to burn”), but Liberman rejects this as weak conjecture; see further at Old English tadige (“toad”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deh₂w-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ toad
