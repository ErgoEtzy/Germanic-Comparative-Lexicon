# *turzdaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/turzdaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *turzdaz

### Etymology

Seemingly from Proto-Indo-European *dr̥s-dʰh₁-os, from a root *ders- (“to excrete”) + *dʰeh₁- (“to do, put, place”). Compare Latvian dìrst (“to shit”). The root *ders-, apparently only attested in Baltic and Germanic, may be an extension of the root *der- (“to split, tear”); see also *teraną (“to tear, snatch”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ders-, *dr̥s-dʰh₁-os, *dʰeh₁-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ alternative reconstruction of *turdą (“excrement”)
