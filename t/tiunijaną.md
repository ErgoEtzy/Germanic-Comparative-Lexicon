## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tiunijaną

### Etymology

From *teuną (“ruin, lack, damage”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *dewH-, *h₃d-ew-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtiu̯.ni.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to torture, torment, vex
2. _(reconstruction)_ to claim, use, appropriate
