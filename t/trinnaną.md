## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *trinnaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *drénu-e, a secondary formation from a nasal present form of *der- (“to flay, divide, split”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *drénu-e
