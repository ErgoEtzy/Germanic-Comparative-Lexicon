# *tangijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tangijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tangijaną

### Etymology

Perhaps from *tangō (“tongs, pliers”) + *-janą or *tinganą (“to press”) + *-janą; both derivations ultimately from the same root. The term has a parallel in the form of Sanskrit दंशयति (daṃśayati, “to make bite”), and may also have been inherited from Proto-Indo-European *donḱ-éye-ti.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *denḱ-, *donḱ-éye-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑŋ.ɡi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to press together
2. _(reconstruction)_ to fasten
