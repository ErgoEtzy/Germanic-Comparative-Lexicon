## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ternō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *der-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *der-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈter.nɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, feminine, reconstruction)_ a mountain lake without tributaries
2. _(Germanic, North, feminine, reconstruction)_ watering hole
3. _(Germanic, North, feminine, reconstruction)_ small pool
