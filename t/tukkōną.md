# *tukkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tukk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tukkōną

### Etymology

From earlier paradigm *tukkōþi ~ *tugunanþi, from pre-Proto-Germanic *duk-nā́-ti ~ *duk-un-ánti, iterative to *teuhaną (“to draw, pull”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtuk.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to tug, pull
2. _(reconstruction)_ to grab, snatch
3. _(reconstruction)_ to grasp, touch
