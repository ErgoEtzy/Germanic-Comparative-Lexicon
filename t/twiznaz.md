## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *twiznaz

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *dwis-nó-s (“double”), from Proto-Indo-European *dwís (“twice, in two”) + *-nós. Equivalent to *twiz- + *-naz. This would make it a piecewise doublet of *twinaz.
