## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Twiskô

### Etymology

Masculine definite singular of *twiskaz (“twofold, doubled”), possibly named after the being's hermaphroditic nature.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ Tuisco, the progenitor of the gods and of mankind, mentioned by Tacitus in his Germania
