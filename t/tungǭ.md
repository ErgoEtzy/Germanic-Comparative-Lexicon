## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tungǭ

### Etymology

N-stem variant of Proto-Indo-European *dn̥ǵʰwéh₂s (“tongue”). Cognate with Latin lingua (“tongue”) and Sanskrit जिह्वा (jihvā́, “tongue”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dn̥ǵʰwéh₂s

### Pronunciation

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ tongue
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ speech, language
