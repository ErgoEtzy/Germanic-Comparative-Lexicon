[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/taisan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *taisaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *deh₂y- (“to separate, divide”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deh₂y-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑi̯.sɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to tug, to pull
