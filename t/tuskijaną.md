# *tuskijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tuskijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tuskijaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *dh₂uske-, sk-present to Proto-Indo-European *deh₂w-. Compare Ancient Greek δαίω (daíō, “to burn”), Welsh deifio (“to kindle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deh₂w-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtus.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to burn, be burning
