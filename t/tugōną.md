# *tugōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tug%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tugōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dewk- (“to pull, lead”). Either a variant of *tukkōną or from *tugiz (“a drawing, tug, pull”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtu.ɣɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to pull, tow
