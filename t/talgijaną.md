## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *talgijaną

### Etymology

Causative, probably derived from *telguz (“branch, twig”), from Proto-Indo-European *delh₁gʰ-. See Proto-Indo-European *dl̥h₁gʰós (“long”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *delh₁gʰ-, *dl̥h₁gʰós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtal.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to carve, cut, whittle
