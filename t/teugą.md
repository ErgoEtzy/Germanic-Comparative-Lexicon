[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/teug%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *teugą

### Etymology

From *teuhaną (“to pull”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No PIE form is listed or machine-resolvable in this snapshot.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈteu̯.ɣɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ device for pulling; handle; lever; gear
2. _(collective, neuter, reconstruction)_ tools; rigging; equipment; stuff
