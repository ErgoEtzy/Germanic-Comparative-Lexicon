## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tugiz

### Etymology

An action noun derived from *teuhaną (“to pull, tug”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtu.ɣiz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ a drawing, tug, pull
