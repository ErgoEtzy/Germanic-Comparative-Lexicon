# *tanhuz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tanhuz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tanhuz

### Etymology

A u-stem nominal derived from *tinganą (“to press upon”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *denḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑ̃ː.xuz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ holding together; clinging; sticky; tenacious; tough
