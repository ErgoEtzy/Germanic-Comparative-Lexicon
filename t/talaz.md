## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *talaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *del- (“to count, reckon”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *del-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑ.lɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ pliable, bendsome
2. _(reconstruction)_ submissive, hearsome, quick to obey
