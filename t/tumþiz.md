# *tumþiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tum%C3%BEiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tumþiz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *dḿ̥tis, equivalent to *temaną (“to befit”) + *-þiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtum.θiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ seemliness, agreement
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ association, guild, community
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ homestead, estate
