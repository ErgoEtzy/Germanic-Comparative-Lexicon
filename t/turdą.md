## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *turdą

### Etymology

According to Orel, from Proto-Indo-European *dr̥-to-s, a participle from *der- (“to split, tear”); cognate with Latvian dìrst (“to poop, shit”). Note that Kroonen prefers to reconstruct the Germanic as *turzdaz; see there for more.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *der-, *dr̥-to-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtur.dɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ manure
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ mud
