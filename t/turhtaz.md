# *turhtaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/turhtaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *turhtaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *dr̥ḱtos, originally a past participle from Proto-Indo-European *derḱ- (“to see”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *derḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈturx.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ light; bright; clear
