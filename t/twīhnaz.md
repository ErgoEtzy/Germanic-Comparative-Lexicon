## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *twīhnaz

### Etymology

From *twīhaz (“consisting of two”), from Proto-Indo-European *dwóh₁ (“two”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dwóh₁

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtwiːx.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ two each
2. _(reconstruction)_ double
