[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tr%C5%ABw%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *trūwāną

### Etymology

From *trūwaz (“faithful”) + *-āną, with quasi-Indo-European form *druH-eh₁-ye-ti. See *triwwiz (“loyal, trustworthy”) for more.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to trust
