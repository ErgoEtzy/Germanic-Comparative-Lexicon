## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *talōną

### Etymology

Equivalent to *talō (“narration, calculation”) + *-ōną (“denominative verbal suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *delH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑ.lɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to tell, speak, recount
2. _(reconstruction)_ to count, to reckon
