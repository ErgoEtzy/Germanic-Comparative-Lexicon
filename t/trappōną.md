## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *trappōną

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *drob-néh₂-ti, from Proto-Indo-European *dreb⁽ʰ⁾- (“to tread, to step”). Compare Lithuanian drebė́ti (“to tremble”) and Polish drabina (“ladder”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dreb-, *drebʰ-, *dreb⁽ʰ⁾-

### Pronunciation

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to trample
