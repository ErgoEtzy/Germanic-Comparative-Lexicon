## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tulguz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dl̥h₁gʰós (“long”). Cognate with Lithuanian ilga (“long”), Latin indulgeō (“favour, grant, indulge”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *delh₁gʰ-, *dl̥h₁gʰós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtul.ɣuz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ holding long; firm, steadfast
