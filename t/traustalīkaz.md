# *traustalīkaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/traustal%C4%ABkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *traustalīkaz

### Etymology

From *traustaz + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *deru-, *dóru

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtrɑu̯s.tɑˌliː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ reliable
2. _(reconstruction)_ solacing, comforting
