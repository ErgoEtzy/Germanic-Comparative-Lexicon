## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tainaz

### Etymology

Unknown; expected to reflect pre-Germanic *doynos. Kroonen connects *tiną (“tin”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑi̯.nɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ rod
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ twig
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ straw
4. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ lot
