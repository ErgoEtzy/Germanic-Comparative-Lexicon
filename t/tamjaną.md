# *tamjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tamjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tamjaną

### Etymology

Perhaps from *tamaz (“tame”) + *-janą. Kroonen suggests that the term might continue a Proto-Indo-European causative *domh₂-éye-ti, itself from the root *demh₂-. This same term may also be attested in Latin domō and Sanskrit दमयति (damayati).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *demh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑm.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to tame
