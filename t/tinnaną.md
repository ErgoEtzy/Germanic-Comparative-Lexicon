# *tinnaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tinnan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tinnaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *dendʰ-n-e-ti, from a Germanic root with quasi-Indo-European *dendʰ- (“to burn, kindle”), of unclear ultimate origin, as solid cognates outside of Germanic are lacking. Osthoff suggests Proto-Indo-European *deh₂w- (“kindle, burn”) (compare Ancient Greek δαίω (daíō, “to kindle”)), but Pokorny rejects this.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deh₂w-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtin.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to burn
