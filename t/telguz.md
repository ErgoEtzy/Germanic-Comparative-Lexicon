## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *telguz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *delh₁gʰ- (“to split; carve; hew”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *delh₁gʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtel.ɣuz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, masculine, reconstruction)_ branch; twig
