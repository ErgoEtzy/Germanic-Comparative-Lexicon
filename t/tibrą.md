## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tibrą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dipró- (“sacrificial animal”), from Proto-Indo-European *deyp-. Compare Ancient Greek δεῖπνον (deîpnon, “meal, dinner”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deyp-, *dipró-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ sacrifice, offering
