[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/traust%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *traustą

### Etymology

From *traustaz (“firm, strong”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtrɑu̯s.tɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ shelter
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ help; aid
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ trust; confidence; alliance
