## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *treganą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dréǵʰ-e-ti, Proto-Indo-European *dreǵʰ- (“to be unwilling, vexed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dreǵʰ-, *dréǵʰ-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtre.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to suffer, be sick, grieve
