# *terwaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/terwaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *terwaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *derwos, derived from or related to *terwą (“tar”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *deru- (“wood”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deru-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈter.wɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, masculine, reconstruction)_ handle of resinous wood
2. _(Germanic, North, masculine, reconstruction)_ spear
