## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *temaną

### Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Indo-European *démeti, from the root Proto-Indo-European *dem- (“to put together”). Cognate with Ancient Greek δέμω (démō, “to build”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dem-, *démeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈte.mɑ.nɑ̃/`
