## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tregô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dreǵʰ- (“to be unwilling, vexed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dreǵʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtre.ɣɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ grief, pain
