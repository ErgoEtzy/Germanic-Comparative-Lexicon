[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/taikuraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *taikuraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dayh₂ur-o-s, thematicized from *dayh₂wḗr ~ *dayh₂ur-és (“brother-in-law”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dayh₂ur-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑi̯.ku.rɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ brother-in-law
