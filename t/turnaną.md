# *turnaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/turnan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *turnaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dr̥-nh₂-tór, middle voice of neh₂-present Proto-Indo-European *dr̥-néh₂-ti, from *der- (“to tear apart, shatter”), whence also *teraną (“to tear”). Cognate with Sanskrit दृणाति (dṛṇā́ti, “to tear”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *der-, *dr̥-nh₂-tór, *dr̥-néh₂-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtur.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to tear off
2. _(reconstruction)_ to depart
