# *triwīnaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/triw%C4%ABnaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *triwīnaz

### Etymology

From *trewą + *-īnaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-om, *drew-, *dóru

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtri.wiː.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ of or pertaining to a tree
2. _(reconstruction)_ wooden, made of wood
