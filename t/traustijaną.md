## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *traustijaną

### Etymology

From *traustaz (“firm, reliable”) + *-janą or *traustą (“aid, shelter”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *deru-, *dóru

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtrɑu̯s.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make strong, make reliable
2. _(reconstruction)_ to comfort, console
