## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *turdawibilaz

### Etymology

From *turdą (“manure”) + *wibilaz (“beetle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *der-, *webʰ-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ dung-beetle
