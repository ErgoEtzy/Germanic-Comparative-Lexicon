# *talmōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/talm%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *talmōną

### Etymology

Unclear, possibly related to Proto-Slavic *dьliti.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑl.mɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to hinder
2. _(reconstruction)_ to dawdle, to tarry
