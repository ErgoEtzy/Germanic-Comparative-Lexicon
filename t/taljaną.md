# *taljaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/taljan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *taljaną

### Etymology

From *talō (“narration, calculation”) + *-janą. Cognate with Latin dolus (“guile, deception”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *del-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑl.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to enumerate, count
2. _(reconstruction)_ to account, recount, tell
