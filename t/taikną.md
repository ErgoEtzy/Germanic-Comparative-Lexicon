## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *taikną

### Etymology

From the root of *taikijaną, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *deyḱ- (“to show, indicate, point out, declare”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deyḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑi̯k.nɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ indicator, indication (e.g. mark, sign, token, symbol, etc.)
