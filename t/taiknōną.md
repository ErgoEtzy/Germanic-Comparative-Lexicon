## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *taiknōną

### Etymology

From *taikną (“sign, token, symbol”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deyḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑi̯k.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to show, point
2. _(reconstruction)_ to mark
