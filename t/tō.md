## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *doh₁ (“to”), instrumental singular of *de (“to, towards”). Cognate with Russian до (do, “to”), Latvian da (“until, to”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry
