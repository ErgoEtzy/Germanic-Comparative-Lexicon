[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tuss%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tussōną

### Etymology

An iterative verb from Proto-Indo-European *tus-néh₂-ti.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tus-néh₂-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtus.sɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to fray
