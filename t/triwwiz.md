# *triwwiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/triwwiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *triwwiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *drewH-, *deru- (“firm, hard, solid, faithful, true”). Compare Old Prussian druwit (“to believe”), Old Irish derb (“certain”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *drew-, *drewH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtriw.wiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ true, faithful
