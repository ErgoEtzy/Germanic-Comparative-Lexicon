[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/t%C4%ABm%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tīmô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dh₂i-mon-, from *deh₂y- (“to divide”). Exact cognate to Ancient Greek δαίμων (daímōn, “daemon”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dh₂i-mon-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtiː.mɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ time
