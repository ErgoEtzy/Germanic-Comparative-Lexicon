# *tīraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/t%C4%ABraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tīraz

### Etymology

Probably from Proto-Indo-European *deyh₂- (“to shine”). Compare Sanskrit दी (dī, “to shine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deyh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtiː.rɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ glory, honour
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ fame, renown
