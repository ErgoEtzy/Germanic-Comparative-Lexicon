## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tehuntēhundą

### Etymology

From *tehun (“ten”) + *-tēhundą (“-ty”) on the model of *sebuntēhundą, *ahtōutēhundą, *newuntēhundą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *déḱm̥, *déḱm̥t, *pénkʷedḱomt

### Pronunciation

### Numeral

1. _(reconstruction)_ one hundred, teenty
