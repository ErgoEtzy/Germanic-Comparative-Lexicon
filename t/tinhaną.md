# *tinhaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tinhan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tinhaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *denḱ- (“to bite, sting”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *denḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtĩː.xɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to press, throng
