# *tuhtiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tuhtiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tuhtiz

### Etymology

From *teuhaną (“to lead, pull”) + *-þiz (“abstract nominal suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtux.tiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a pulling
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ discipline
