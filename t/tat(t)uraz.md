[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tat%28t%29uraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tat(t)uraz

### Etymology

Uncertain. Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *deh₂- (“to split, cut, divide”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deh₂-

### Noun

1. _(reconstruction)_ tuft, bushy mass
2. _(reconstruction)_ patch, rag
