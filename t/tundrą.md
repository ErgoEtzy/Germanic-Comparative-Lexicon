# *tundrą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tundr%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tundrą

### Etymology

Derived from the same root as *tandijaną (“to kindle”), with quasi-Indo-European form *dndʰ-ro-m.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtun.drɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ tinder (material used to ignite fires)
