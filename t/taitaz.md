## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *taitaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *doydo-, from *dey- (“to gleam, shimmer, shine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *doydo-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑi̯.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ radiant; bright
2. _(reconstruction)_ beaming; cheerful
3. _(reconstruction)_ tender; delicate
