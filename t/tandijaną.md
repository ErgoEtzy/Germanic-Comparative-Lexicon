# *tandijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tandijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tandijaną

### Etymology

Causative of *tinnaną (“to burn”) (with quasi-Indo-European form *dondʰ-éye-ti), derived at a time when it had not yet assimilated the inherited dental to -nn-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɑn.di.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to kindle, to light on fire
