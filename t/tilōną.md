# *tilōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/til%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tilōną

### Etymology

From *tilą (“goal”) + *-ōną. Also compare *tilaz (“fitting”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *deh₂y-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈti.lɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to strive; reach; mint; aim; hurry
