# *twiznāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/twizn%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *twiznāną

### Etymology

From *twiznaz (“twine”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti, *dwis-nó-s, *dwís

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtwiz.nɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to twine, wind threads
