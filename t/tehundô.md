## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tehundô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *deḱm̥tós, from *déḱm̥. An ordinal to *tehun (“ten”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deḱm̥tós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈte.xun.dɔːː/`

### Adjective
