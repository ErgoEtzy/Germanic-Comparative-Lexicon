## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *twai

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dwóh₁ (“two”) with plural suffix *-i. Compare *þai (“they, those”), from demonstrative root *þa-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dwóh₁

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/twɑi̯/`

### Numeral

1. _(reconstruction)_ two
