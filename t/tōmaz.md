# *tōmaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/t%C5%8Dmaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tōmaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *deH-, further origin unknown. Potentially from Proto-Indo-European *deh₂- (“to divide, share”), but semantically unconvincing.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtɔː.mɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ free, clear, empty, unoccupied
