# *traustaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/traustaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *traustaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *deru-, *drew-, *drewH- (“to be firm, hard, solid; tree”). Particularly close cognate with Old Irish druit (“close, firm, trustworthy”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deru-, *dóru

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtrɑu̯s.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ strong
2. _(reconstruction)_ reliable, confident
