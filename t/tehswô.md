[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/tehsw%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tehswô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *deḱs- (“right”). Cognate with Latin dexter (“right”), Lithuanian dešinys (“right”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deḱs-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtexs.wɔːː/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ right (opposite of left)
