# *tībrą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/t%C4%ABbr%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *tībrą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *deHpro- (“sacrificial animal; cattle; livestock”). Cognate with Old Armenian տուար (tuar).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *deHpro-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈtiː.βrɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ offering, sacrifice
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ victim
