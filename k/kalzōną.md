# *kalzōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kalz%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kalzōną

### Etymology

Possibly derived from an unattested noun *kalzaz, cognate with Proto-Balto-Slavic *galsas (“voice”), from a hypothetical Proto-Indo-European *golsos, from a root *gel(H)- (“to vocalize, call, shout”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *golsos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑl.zɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to call, shout
