# *kanjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kanjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kanjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵonh₁éyeti, causative formation to the root *ǵenh₁- (“to produce, to beget, to give birth”). Cognate with Sanskrit जनयति (janayati, “to beget, produce”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵenh₁-, *ǵonh₁éyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑn.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to beget, bring forth
