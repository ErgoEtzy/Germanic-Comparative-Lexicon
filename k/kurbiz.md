## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kurbiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gerbʰ- (“to scratch”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gerbʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkur.βiz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a cut
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a score; notch
