# *knukōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/knuk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *knukōną

### Etymology

From *knukô (“bone”) + *-ōną, perhaps reinforced by sound-symbolism.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *gnew-, *ǵnu-gon-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈknu.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ To pound on; beat on; knock
