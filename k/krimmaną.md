## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *krimmaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gremH-, which may be related to *h₂ger- (“to gather”). Cognate with Lithuanian grùmti (“to push, shove”), Latin gremium (“lap, bosom; armful”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gremH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkrim.mɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to crumble
2. _(reconstruction)_ to push, squeeze
