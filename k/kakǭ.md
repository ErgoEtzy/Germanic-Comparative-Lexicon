[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kak%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kakǭ

### Etymology

Unknown. The relationship with Lithuanian gúogas (“skull”) by supposed Proto-Indo-European *gag-, *gōg- (“something round, something lumpy”) is highly doubtful. Compare *kōkô (“cake”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gag-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑ.kɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ cake
