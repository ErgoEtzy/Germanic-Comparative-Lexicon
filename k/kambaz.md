## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kambaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵómbʰos (“tooth, peg”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵómbʰos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑm.bɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ comb
