[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kuruz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kuruz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʷréh₂us, specifically the hysterokinetic form *gʷr̥h₂ús.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷréh₂us

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈku.ruz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ heavy
