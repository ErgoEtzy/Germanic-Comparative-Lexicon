# *klībaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kl%C4%ABban%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *klībaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gleybʰ- (“to stick”). Cognate with Latvian gliêbtiês (“to cling”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gleybʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkliː.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stick, cling to
