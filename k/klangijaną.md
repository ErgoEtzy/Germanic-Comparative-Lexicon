# *klangijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/klangijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *klangijaną

### Etymology

From *klinganą + *-janą. This verb gave rise to the variant *klankijaną, due to influence of an unattested iterative.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *gal-, *gley-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈklɑŋ.ɡi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make stick, to make cling
