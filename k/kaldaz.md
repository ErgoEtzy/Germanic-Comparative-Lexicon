# *kaldaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kaldaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kaldaz

### Etymology

From the original past participle of *kalaną, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gel-. Diachronically from Proto-Indo-European *goltós.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gel-, *goltós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑl.dɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ cold
