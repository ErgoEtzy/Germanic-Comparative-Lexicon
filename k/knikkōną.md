[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/knikk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *knikkōną

### Etymology

From a morphologically obscure formation related to *knewą (“knee”) + *-ikōną (“frequentative verbal suffix”), perhaps reinforced by sound-symbolism.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈknik.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to snap, fold
