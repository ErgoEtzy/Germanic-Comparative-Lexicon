# *kinnuz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kinnuz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kinnuz

### Etymology

Paradigmatic leveling of *kenuz ~ *kunniz, from pre-Proto-Germanic *génus ~ *gunwés, from Proto-Indo-European *ǵénus (“cheek; jaw; chin”) ~ *ǵn̥wés.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵénus

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkin.nuz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ cheek
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ chin, jaw
