# *kramjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kramjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kramjaną

### Etymology

From *krimmaną + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gremH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkram.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to squeeze
2. _(reconstruction)_ to cram, stuff
