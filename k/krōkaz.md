## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *krōkaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *greh₂ǵos (“a twist, rope, wicker, a tangle”), from *ger- (“to turn, to wind”). Cognate with Sanskrit ग्रन्थि (granthí, “a knot, tie”).
