# *kweþaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kwe%C3%BEan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kweþaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʷet- (“to say, speak”). Cognate with Old Armenian կոչեմ (kočʻem, “to call, invite”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷet-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkʷe.θɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to say
