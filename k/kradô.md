## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kradô

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *grotHón-, from Proto-Indo-European *gretH- (“to tie”). Cognate with Sanskrit ग्रथ्नाति (grathnāti, “to tie, string together”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gretH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkrɑ.ðɔːː/`

### Noun
