# *kutjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kutjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kutjaną

### Etymology

Possibly related to *ketwą (“meat”). Also compare *kwetwą (“flesh”). For possible sense development, compare Latin carō (“meat”), which probably was originally "a piece of meat cut off".

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to cut
