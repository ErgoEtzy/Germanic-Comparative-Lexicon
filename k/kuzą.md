## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kuzą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵews- (“to taste; choose”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵews-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈku.zɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ choice; choosing; vote; decision
