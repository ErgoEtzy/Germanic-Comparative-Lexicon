## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kumþiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʷém-ti-s ~ *gʷm̥-téy-s. Equivalent to *kwemaną (“to come”) + *-þiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷém-ti-s, *gʷémtis

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkum.θiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ coming, arrival
