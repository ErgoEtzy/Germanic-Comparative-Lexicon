# *kwerraną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kwerran%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kwerraną

### Etymology

Kroonen reconstructs Proto-Indo-European thematic present *gʷérh₃-e-ti, from *gʷerh₃- (“to devour”) + *-eti, yet doesn't explain the geminated *-rr-. Possibly instead a secondary strong verb to lost iterative *kurrōną, from earlier paradigm *kurrōþi ~ *kurunanþi, from Proto-Indo-European *gʷr̥h₃-néh₂-ti ~ *gʷr̥h₃-n̥h₂-énti, from *gʷerh₃- (“to devour”) + *-néh₂ti.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-eti, *-néh₂ti, *gʷerh₃-, *gʷr̥h₃-néh₂-ti ~ *gʷr̥h₃-n̥h₂-énti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkʷer.rɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to devour, glut
