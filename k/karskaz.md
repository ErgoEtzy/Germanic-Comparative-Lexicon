## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *karskaz

### Etymology

Uncertain; possibly from Proto-Indo-European *h₁ǵer- (“to wake up”). If so, then cognate with Ancient Greek ἐγείρω (egeírō, “to awaken, rouse, raise”) and Sanskrit जरते (járate, “to wake up”). It has alternatively been derived from Proto-Indo-European *ǵerh₂- (“to grow old, ripen, mature”).
