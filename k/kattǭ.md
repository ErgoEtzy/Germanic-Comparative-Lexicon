## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kattǭ

### Etymology

Feminine form of *kattuz, which see.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ female cat
