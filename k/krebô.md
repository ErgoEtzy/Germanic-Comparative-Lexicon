## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *krebô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *grébʰ-ōn ~ *grbʰ-n-ōs, from *grebʰ-, a variant of *gerbʰ- (“bunch, bundle, tuft, clump”), from *ger- (“to twist, turn”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *grébʰ-ōn

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ basket
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bucket
