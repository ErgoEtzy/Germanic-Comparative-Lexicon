# *kribjǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kribj%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kribjǭ

### Etymology

From *krebô, from Proto-Indo-European *grebʰ-, *gerbʰ- (“bunch, bundle, tuft, clump”), from *ger- (“to turn, twist”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *grebʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkriβ.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a wickerwork; crate; wooden trough; manger
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a stall; crib
