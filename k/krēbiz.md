# *krēbiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kr%C4%93biz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *krēbiz

### Etymology

Uncertain; possible vṛddhi gerundive of otherwise unattested *krebaną, perhaps related to *krafjaną (“to demand”), *kraftuz (“strength, power”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ strong
