# *kernǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kern%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kernǭ

### Etymology

Unknown. Perhaps related to Proto-Germanic *kernô (“seed, germ”), in the sense of "remove or extract the kernel from," which is related to *kurną (“corn, grain”); yet this is disputed.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈker.nɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ butter tub
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ creme
