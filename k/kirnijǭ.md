# *kirnijǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kirnij%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kirnijǭ

### Etymology

Derived from Proto-Germanic *kernǭ (“creme”)? Alternatively, the latter word is a backformation from this one. Possibly, they are related to *kernô (“core”), which is related to *kurną (“corn, grain”); yet this is disputed.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈker.ni.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a churn
