# *kwelaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kwelan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kwelaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʷelH- (“to hurt”). Related to Lithuanian gélti (“to hurt”), Old Irish atbaill (“to die”). Old Armenian կեղ (keł, “ulcer, sore”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷelH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkʷe.lɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to suffer, hurt
