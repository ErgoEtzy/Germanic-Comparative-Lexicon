# *kunnaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kunnan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kunnaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵn̥néh₃ti, from *ǵneh₃- (“to know”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵneh₃-, *ǵn̥néh₃ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkun.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to know, to be familiar with, to recognise
2. _(reconstruction)_ to know how, to be able to
