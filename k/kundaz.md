## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kundaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵn̥h₁tós (“born”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵenh₁-, *ǵn̥h₁tós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkun.dɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ born
2. _(reconstruction)_ of a particular nature or disposition
