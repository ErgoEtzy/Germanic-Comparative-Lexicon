## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *klibrōną

### Etymology

From *klibāną (“to cling”) + *-rōną (frequentative suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gleybʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkli.βrɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to climb, to clamber
2. _(reconstruction)_ to scratch, to claw
