## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kōpijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵab-, *ǵeb- (“to look after”). Cognate with Lithuanian gebėti (“to cultivate, maintain, be able”), Russian забо́та (zabóta, “care, concern, attention”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵab-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɔː.pi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to look after, observe
2. _(reconstruction)_ to watch, keep, care for
