# *klewô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/klew%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *klewô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *glew- (“to conglomerate, amass; clump, ball, bale”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *glew-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkle.wɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ clump, mass, ball, bale
