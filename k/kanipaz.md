# *kanipaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kanipaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kanipaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵénus (“jaw”). Cognate with Latin gena (“cheek”), Welsh gen (“cheek, chin”), Ancient Greek γενειάς (geneiás, “beard, chin”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵénus

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑ.ni.pɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ hair on the chin or cheek, beard, whiskers, moustache
