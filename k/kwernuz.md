# *kwernuz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kwernuz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kwernuz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *gʷérh₂nus, from Proto-Indo-European *gʷréh₂wō (“heavy stone”), from *gʷréh₂us (“heavy”). Cognate with Sanskrit ग्रावन् (grāvan, “pressing stone”), Lithuanian gi̇̀rna (“grindstone, millstone”), Russian жёрнов (žórnov, “grindstone, millstone”), Old Irish brao (“millstone, handmill”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷreh₂-, *gʷréh₂wō

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkʷer.nuz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ millstone
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ handmill; quern
