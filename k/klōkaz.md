## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *klōkaz

### Etymology

Of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Irish glicc (“shrewd, acute”), Scots gleg (“smart, quick”), Ancient Greek καλχαίνω (kalkhaínō, “to ponder”), Middle English begalewen (“to frighten, stupefy”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ strong, quick, smart
