[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kun%C3%BEaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kunþaz

### Etymology

*kunnaną (“to know”) + *-þaz (past participle suffix). See the etymology of the corresponding lemma form.
May also be analyzed as from pre-Germanic *ǵń̥h₃tos, via unexplained accent shift from Proto-Indo-European *ǵn̥h₃tós, or analogical replacement of the suffix.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵneh₃-, *ǵn̥h₃tós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkun.θɑz/`

### Verb

1. _(form-of, participle, past, reconstruction)_ past participle of *kunnaną

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ known
