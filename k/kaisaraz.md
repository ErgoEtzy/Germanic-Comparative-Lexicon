# *kaisaraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kaisaraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kaisaraz

### Etymology

Borrowed at a post-Proto-Germanic stage from Latin Caesar. According to Ringe, the Gothic 𐌺𐌰𐌹𐍃𐌰𐍂 (kaisar) is an independent borrowing.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑi̯.sɑ.rɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ emperor (of Rome)
