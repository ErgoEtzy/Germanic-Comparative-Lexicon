# *klautaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/klautaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *klautaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *glowdos (“clump, clod”), from *gel- (“to ball up; amass”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gel-, *glowdos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈklɑu̯.tɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ ball, lump
2. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ knob, block, cleat
