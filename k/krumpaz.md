# *krumpaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/krumpaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *krumpaz

### Etymology

Uncertain. Kroonen considers the word a secondary adjectival formation from *krimpaną (“to shrink, draw back”), with Pfeifer adducing Proto-Slavic *grǫbъ (“coarse, rude”) as a cognate.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkrum.pɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ bent, crooked, curved
