[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kl%C5%ABtaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *klūtaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *glewdos, from *gel- (“to ball up; amass; clench”). Compare Proto-West Germanic *klott (“clot”), as well as Old Armenian կեղտ (kełt, “dirt”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *glewdos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkluː.tɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ lump, clod
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ boulder, rock
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ hill
