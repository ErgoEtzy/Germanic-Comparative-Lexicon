## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kernô

### Etymology

Related to *kurną (“corn, grain”), specifically as an n-stem singulative.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈker.nɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ kernel, core
