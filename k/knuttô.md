## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *knuttô

### Etymology

Traditionally derived from a Proto-Indo-European *gnod- (“to bind”), and compared with Latin nōdus (“node”), Proto-Germanic *knudaną (“to knead”). However, Kroonen instead derives the term from Proto-Indo-European *ǵnu-ton, a ton-formation from *ǵónu (“knee; node”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gnod-, *ǵnu-ton
