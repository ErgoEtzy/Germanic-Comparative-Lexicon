[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kimban%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kimbaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gem- (“to grab; hold; press; pack; cram”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkim.bɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ To press together; compress
