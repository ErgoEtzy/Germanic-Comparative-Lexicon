## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kūlǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gewlo-, from the root *gew-, *gēw- (“to bend, curve, arch, buckle”) + *-los . Doublet of *keulaz, *keluz, and *kūliz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gewlo-, *gewlos

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ bump
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ hole
