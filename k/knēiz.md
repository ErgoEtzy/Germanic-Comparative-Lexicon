# *knēiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kn%C4%93iz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *knēiz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *knēaną (“to know”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵneh₃-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈknɛː.iz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ knowing, acknowledging
