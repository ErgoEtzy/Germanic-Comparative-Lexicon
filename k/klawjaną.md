## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *klawjaną

### Etymology

Seemingly from a Proto-Indo-European root *glew- (“to claw, scratch”), with no certain cognates outside of Germanic. Some take the root to be from a base *gel- (“to make round, clench”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No PIE form is listed or machine-resolvable in this snapshot.
