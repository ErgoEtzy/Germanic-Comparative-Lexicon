## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *klintaz

### Etymology

Uncertain; hypothetically from Proto-Indo-European *glendos (“shore”), but the only cognate is Proto-Celtic *glendos (“valley”) (along with its descendants). They are alternatively suggested to be of shared substrate origin.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *glendos

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ hill
