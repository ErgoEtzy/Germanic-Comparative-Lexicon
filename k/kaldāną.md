# *kaldāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kald%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kaldāną

### Etymology

From *kaldaz (“cold”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑl.dɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to become cold
