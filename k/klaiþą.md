## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *klaiþą

### Etymology

Traditionally derived from Proto-Indo-European *gleyt-, an extension of the root *gley- (“to cling to, stick to, adhere”); however, the semantics are not particularly strong, as Kroonen notes, who leaves the origin open.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gleyt-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ cloth
