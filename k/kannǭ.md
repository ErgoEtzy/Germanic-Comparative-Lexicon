# *kannǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kann%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kannǭ

### Etymology

Probably from a western Proto-Indo-European *gandʰ-, *gan- (“a vessel, vat, tub”), and related to *kanô (“vessel, boat”), as well as Middle Irish gand (“vessel, can”). An alternative etymology derives *kannǭ as possibly from Latin canna (“a reed, cane”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gandʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑn.nɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a tankard, mug, cup, can
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a jug, pitcher, pot
