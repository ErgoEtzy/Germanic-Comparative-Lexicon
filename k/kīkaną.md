# *kīkaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/k%C4%ABkan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kīkaną

### Etymology

Unknown. Perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *kéh₂ikos, related to Proto-Italic *kaikos, in which case it would be a doublet of *haihaz (“one-eyed”); this is formally problematic, however. Alternatively suggested to be sound-symbolic.
Perhaps related to German gucken (“to watch, look”), but this word is itself of obscure origin.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kéh₂ikos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkiː.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to look, peep
