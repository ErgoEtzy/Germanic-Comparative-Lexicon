# *kōlnaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/k%C5%8Dlnan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kōlnaną

### Etymology

From *kōlaz + *-naną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɔːl.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to become colder, cool, cool down
