# *kinkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kink%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kinkōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gang-, *ganǵ- (“to mock, insult”). Cognate with Sanskrit गञ्ज (gañja, “disrespect”). Compare also Middle High German kīchen (“to breathe heavy, gasp, wheeze”), Middle Low German kîchen (“to wheeze, breathe heave, cough”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gang-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkin.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to laugh loudly, kink
