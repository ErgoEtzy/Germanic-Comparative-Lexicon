# *kumbaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kumbaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kumbaz

### Etymology

An ancient Wanderwort found throughout Eurasia. Likely related to Proto-Celtic *kumbā, Proto-Indo-Iranian *kʰumbʰas, Proto-Albanian *tˢumba, Ancient Greek κύμβη (kúmbē).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkum.bɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bowl, vessel
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ vat, tub
