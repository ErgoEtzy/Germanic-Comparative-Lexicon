## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *knūjaną

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *gnuH-yé-ti, from Proto-Indo-European *gnewH-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gnewH-
