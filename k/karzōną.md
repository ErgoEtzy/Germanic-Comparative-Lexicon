# *karzōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/karz%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *karzōną

### Etymology

An o-grade formation closely related to to the strong verb *kerzaną and iterative verb *kurzōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gers-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkur.zɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to creak (of birds)
2. _(reconstruction)_ to grunt
3. _(reconstruction)_ to murmur
