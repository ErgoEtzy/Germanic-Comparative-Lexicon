## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kulukaz

### Etymology

From a base derived from Proto-Indo-European *gel-, *gʷel- (“to devour; gullet”) + *-ukaz (diminutive suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a hollow, depression, or well
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ trench, gully; pit
