# *kulbaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kulbaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kulbaz

### Etymology

Apparently from a pre-form *gl̥h₁bʰ-o-, of uncertain further origin; often compared to Latin globus (< *gleh₁bʰ-eh₂-) and Lithuanian glė́bti (“to embrace, clasp”) (< *gleh₁bʰ-ye-ti), Latvian glēba, which are possibly from Proto-Indo-European *gel- (“to form into a ball”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gel-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ round object, lump
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ club, staff
