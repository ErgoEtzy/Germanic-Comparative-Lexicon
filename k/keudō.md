## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *keudō

### Etymology

Of unclear origin. Older theories take the word as a derivative of a Proto-Indo-European *gewt- (“pouch, sack”), which would be an extension of *gew- (“to bend, bow, arch, vault, curve”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gewt-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkeu̯.ðɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ bag
