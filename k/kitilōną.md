## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kitilōną

### Etymology

From *kitōną, from Proto-Indo-European *geyd- (“to sting; prick; tickle”) + *-lōną. Cognate with Proto-Slavic *gъdьlъ (“tickle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *geyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈki.ti.lɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb
