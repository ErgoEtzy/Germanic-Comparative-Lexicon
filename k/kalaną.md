# *kalaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kalan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kalaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gól-, the o-grade root present of *gel- (“cold”). Related to Latin gelū (“frost, cold”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gel-, *gól-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑ.lɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be cold, to freeze
