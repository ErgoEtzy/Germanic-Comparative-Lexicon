# *kwildiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kwildiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kwildiz

### Etymology

A participial formation from *kwelaną (“to suffer”); compare in particular Latvian dzelts (“stung”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷelH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkʷil.diz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ dying; death; downfall; end
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ the end or dying of the day; evening
