# *kwēdaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kw%C4%93daz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kwēdaz

### Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Indo-European *gʷweH-tó-s, *gʷuH-tó-s (“defecated”), from *gʷewH- (“to defecate”) + *-tós.

Substantivization of the adjective. If the tribe did use it as a name for themselves, it was likely first applied by their enemies and then used by them to instill fear.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-tós, *gʷewH-, *gʷweH-tó-s, *gʷuH-tó-s

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ filthy, terrible
