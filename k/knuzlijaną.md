# *knuzlijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/knuzlijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *knuzlijaną

### Etymology

From *knus- (“to crush; grind”), from Proto-Indo-European *gen- (“to squeeze; pinch; kink; bale”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gen-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈknuz.li.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to butt; bump; knock; beat
2. _(reconstruction)_ to mash
