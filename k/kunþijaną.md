# *kunþijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kun%C3%BEijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kunþijaną

### Etymology

From *kunþaz (“known”) + *-janą (factitive suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵneh₃-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkun.θi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make known, announce
