[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kwain%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kwainōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʷey- (“to lament; complain”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkʷɑi̯.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to lament
