# *kaliz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kaliz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kaliz

### Etymology

Along with *kalaną (“to freeze”), from Proto-Indo-European *gel- (“cold”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-is, *gel-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ cold, coldness
