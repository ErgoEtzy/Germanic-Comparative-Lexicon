# *karōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kar%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *karōną

### Etymology

From *karō (“complaint, lament, worry”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *ger-, *ǵeh₂r-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑ.rɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to worry about, to be concerned about
