# *krimpaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/krimpan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *krimpaną

### Etymology

Originally *krimbaną, with contamination of -p- from the iterative *krumpōną, from Pre-Germanic *grembʰ-, a nasalized form of Proto-Indo-European *grebʰ- (“to be crooked”). Possibly related to Proto-Slavic *gъrbъ (“hump”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *grebʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkrim.pɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shrink, draw back
