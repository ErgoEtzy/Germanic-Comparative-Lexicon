## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kustiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵéws-ti-s (“taste”), from *ǵews- (“to enjoy, taste”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵews-, *ǵéws-ti-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkus.tiz/`

### Noun
