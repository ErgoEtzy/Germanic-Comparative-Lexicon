# *klimbaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/klimban%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *klimbaną

### Etymology

Possibly a nasalized variant of *klībaną (“to stick, cleave”), itself from Proto-Indo-European *gleybʰ-, an extended form of *gley- (“to stick”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gleybʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈklim.bɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to climb
