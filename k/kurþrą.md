# *kurþrą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kur%C3%BEr%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kurþrą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂gr̥trom, from *h₂ger- (“to gather”) + *-þrą. Compare Ancient Greek ἀγείρω (ageírō, “to collect, gather”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂gr̥trom

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkur.θrɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ crowd, flock, herd
