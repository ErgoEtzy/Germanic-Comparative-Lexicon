# *kerzaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kerzan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kerzaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gers-. Cognate with Latin garrio (“to chatter”) and Lithuanian gar̃sas (“sound”).
The expected form *kersaną is unattested, the current form with -z- arose by influence of the iterative *kurzōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gers-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈker.zɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to creak (of birds)
2. _(reconstruction)_ to cry (of birds)
