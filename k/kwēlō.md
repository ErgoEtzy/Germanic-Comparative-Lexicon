## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kwēlō

### Etymology

A nominal derivative from *kwelaną (“to suffer”). Cognates with particularly close morphology include Lithuanian gėlà (“pain”), Proto-Slavic *žàlь (“pity”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.
