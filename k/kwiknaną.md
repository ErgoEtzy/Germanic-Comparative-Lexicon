# *kwiknaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kwiknan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kwiknaną

### Etymology

From *kwikwaz (“alive”) + *-naną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-néh₂-, *gʷeyh₃-, *gʷih₃wós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkʷik.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to come to life
