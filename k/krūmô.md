**Reconstruction:** *krūmô

### Etymology

Unknown. Perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *grū-mon- (“something scraped together, lumber, junk”), from a root *grew- or *grewH- (“to claw, scratch”), maybe extended from *ger- (“to turn, bend, twist, wind”).
