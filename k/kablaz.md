## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kablaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵebʰ- (“branch”). Cognate with Lithuanian žãbas (“branch, cane”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵebʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑβ.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ round piece of wood
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ stick, rod
