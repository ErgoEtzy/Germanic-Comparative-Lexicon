## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kwēbijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʷeh₂bʰ- (“to dive, dive in”). Cognate with Ancient Greek βάπτω (báptō, “to dip, submerge”), Persian ژرف (žarf), and Sanskrit गभीर (gabhīra), whence Hindi गहरा (gahrā).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷeh₂bʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkweː.βi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to suffocate, to choke
