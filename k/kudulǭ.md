## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kudulǭ

### Etymology

Perhaps from *kuddô (“bag, sack”) + *-ulǭ.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ cuttlefish, squid, cephalopod
