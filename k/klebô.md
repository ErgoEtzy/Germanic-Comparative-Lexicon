# *klebô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kleb%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *klebô

### Etymology

Of uncertain origin. Traditionally considered a borrowing from Latin clībanus (“baking vessel; oven”), with semantic development "oven" > "heatable room", though this is semantically doubtful. Note that a relation to *kleubaną (“to split, cleave”) is semantically appealing, though phonetically difficult.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ an apartment, a separate room or space
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a compartment, closet
