[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/k%C4%ABm%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kīmô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵéyH-mon-, related to *kīnaną (“to split open, germinate, sprout”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵéyH-mon-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkiː.mɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ sprout, germ
