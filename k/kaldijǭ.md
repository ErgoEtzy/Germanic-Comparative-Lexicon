# *kaldijǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kaldij%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kaldijǭ

### Etymology

From *kaldaz (“cold”) + *-jǭ. Related to Proto-Slavic *koldędzь (probably borrowed from Germanic), *koldьcь, *koldenьcь (“well”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gel-, *goltós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑl.di.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ well, spring
