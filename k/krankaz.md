# *krankaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/krankaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *krankaz

### Etymology

An adjective derived from *kringaną (“to turn, fall, yield”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkrɑŋ.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ bent; crooked
2. _(reconstruction)_ weak
