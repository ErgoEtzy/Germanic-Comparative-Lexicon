## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kwīnaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʷeyH- (“to dwindle”) or possibly, Proto-Indo-European *dʰgʷʰey- (“to decline, perish”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰgʷʰey-, *gʷeyH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkwiː.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to wither, to dwindle
