## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kranô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gerh₂- (“to cry hoarsely”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gerh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkrɑ.nɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ crane (bird)
