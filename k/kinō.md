## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kinō

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *ǵi-n-, originally a nasal-present from Proto-Indo-European *ǵey- (“to split; sprout”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈki.nɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ A split; crack
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ A germ; sprout
