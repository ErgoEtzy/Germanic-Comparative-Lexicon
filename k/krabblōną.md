# *krabblōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/krabbl%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *krabblōną

### Etymology

From *krabbô (“crab”) + *-lōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gerbʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkrɑb.blɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(intransitive, reconstruction)_ to crawl, paw
2. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to touch continuously
