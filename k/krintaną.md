# *krintaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/krintan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *krintaną

### Etymology

Unclear, possibly related to *krītaną (“to shout, to proclaim”) with a nasal-infix.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkrin.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to grumble, to murmur
2. _(reconstruction)_ to complain
