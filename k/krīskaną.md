# *krīskaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kr%C4%ABskan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *krīskaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *grey- (“to shout”), which appears to be related to *gerh₂- (“to cry hoarsely”); see also *krēaną (“to crow, shout”), *krītaną (“to call out, scream”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *grey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkriːskɑnɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to scream, shout, screech
