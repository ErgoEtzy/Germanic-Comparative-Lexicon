[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kalwaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kalwaz

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *golH-wo-s (found also in Balto-Slavic), from Proto-Indo-European *gelH- (“bald, bare; head”). Also suggested as borrowed from early Latin calvus.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gelH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑl.wɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ bald
