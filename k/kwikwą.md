# *kwikwą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kwikw%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kwikwą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʷih₃wós (“alive”), from Proto-Indo-European *gʷeyh₃- (“to live”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷeyh₃-, *gʷih₃wós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkwik.wɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ living creature; animal
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ livestock; sheep; cattle
