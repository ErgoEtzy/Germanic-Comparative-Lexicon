# *kauzijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kauzijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kauzijaną

### Etymology

By surface analysis, *keusaną (“to choose”) + *-janą. Perhaps inherited from Proto-Indo-European *ǵows-éye-ti. Compare Sanskrit जो॒षय॑ते (joṣáyate, “to love, cherish”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵows-éye-ti, *ǵowséyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑu̯.zi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sample, taste
