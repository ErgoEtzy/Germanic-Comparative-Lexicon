## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kwikwǭ

### Etymology

From *kwikwaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a type of grass, similar to wheat; couch grass
