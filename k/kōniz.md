# *kōniz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/k%C5%8Dniz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kōniz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *kunnaną (“to know”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɔː.niz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ able, knowledgeable, skillful, clever
