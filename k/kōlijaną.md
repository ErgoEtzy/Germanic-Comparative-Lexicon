# *kōlijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/k%C5%8Dlijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kōlijaną

### Etymology

From *kōlaz (“cool”) + *-janą, or possibly a causative formation equivalent to *kalaną (“to be cold, to freeze”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɔː.li.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make cold, to cool
