# *kreustaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kreustan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kreustaną

### Etymology

Possibly related to Latvian skraustêt (“to crackle”) and Proto-Slavic *xrustěti (“to crackle”) (compare Russian хруст (xrust, “to crackle, crunch”), which is considered to be imitative).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkreu̯.stɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to gnash, to grind
