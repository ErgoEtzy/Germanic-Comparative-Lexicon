## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kōnį̄

### Etymology

From *kōniz + *-į̄.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-i-h₂, *-ih₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɔː.nĩː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ skillfulness, ableness
