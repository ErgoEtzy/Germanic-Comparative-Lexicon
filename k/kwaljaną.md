# *kwaljaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kwaljan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kwaljaną

### Etymology

From *kwelaną (“to suffer”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷelH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkʷɑl.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make suffer, to hurt
