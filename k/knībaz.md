## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *knībaz

### Etymology

A nominal formation, with quasi-Indo-European form *ǵneybʰ-o(n)-, perhaps from an unattested *knībaną, itself related to *knippōną (“to pinch”); see there for more.

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ pincers; shears
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ knife
