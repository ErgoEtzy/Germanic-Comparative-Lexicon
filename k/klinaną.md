# *klinaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/klinan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *klinaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gleh₁y- (“to smear, glue”). Cognate with Proto-Celtic *glināti (“to glue”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gleh₁y-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkli.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stick, glue
2. _(reconstruction)_ to smear, paste
