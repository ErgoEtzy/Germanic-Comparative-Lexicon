# *kunnāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kunn%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kunnāną

### Etymology

From *kunnaną (“to know”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti, *ǵneh₃-, *ǵn̥néh₃ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkun.nɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to get to know, to become familiar with
2. _(reconstruction)_ to test, to try
3. _(reconstruction)_ to experience
