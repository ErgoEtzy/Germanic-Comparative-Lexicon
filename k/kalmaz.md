## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kalmaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gel- (“to be cold, freeze”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkɑl.mɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ coldness, freeze, frost, frozenness
