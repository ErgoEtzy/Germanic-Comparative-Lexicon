# *kwēdiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kw%C4%93diz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kwēdiz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *kweþaną (“to say”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷet-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkʷeː.ðiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ sayable, able to be said
