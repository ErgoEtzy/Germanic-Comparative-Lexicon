# *keusaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/keusan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *keusaną

### Etymology

From *ǵews- (“to taste, try”). According to Kim, this was originally the subjunctive to a root aorist. Cognate with Ancient Greek γεύω (geúō), Sanskrit जु॒षते॑ (juṣáte).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵews-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkeu̯.sɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to test, to try
2. _(reconstruction)_ to choose
