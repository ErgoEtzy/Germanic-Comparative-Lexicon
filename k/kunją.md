# *kunją

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/kunj%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kunją

### Etymology

Nomininalized from Proto-Indo-European *ǵn̥h₁-yó-m (“begotten, birthed”), from *ǵenh₁- (“to beget, give birth”) + *-yóm.

Nomininalized from Proto-Indo-European *ǵn̥h₃-yó-m (“known”), from *ǵneh₃- (“to know”) + *-yóm.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-yós, *ǵenh₁-, *ǵneh₃-, *ǵn̥h₁-yó-m, *ǵn̥h₃-yó-m

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkun.jɑ̃/`
