## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kweraną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʷerH-, cognate with Lithuanian gùrti (“to scream”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷerH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkwe.rɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sigh
