## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *kustōną

### Etymology

From *kustuz (“trial, choice”) + *-ōną. Compare, in particular, Latin gustō (“to taste, sample”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵews-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈkus.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to try
2. _(reconstruction)_ to taste
