# *ketwą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ketw%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ketwą

### Etymology

Unknown; according to Hamp, from a north European substrate language.
A connection with *kwetwą (“meat”) and *kutjaną (“to cut”) has been suggested, but the relationship is unclear, if any.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈket.wɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, neuter, reconstruction)_ meat; flesh
