[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/klib%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *klibāną

### Etymology

A stative derived from the root of *klībaną (“to stick”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gleybʰ-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stick, to adhere
