## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -itjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *-dyé- (verbal suffix). Cognate with Ancient Greek -άζω (-ázō).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-dyé-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/it.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ Creates verbs from nouns and adjectives, similar to -ise.
2. _(reconstruction)_ Creates intensive verbs from other verbs.
