## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -istaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *-istHos, from *-yōs (relative suffix) + *-tHós (adjectival suffix). Cognate with Ancient Greek -ιστος (-istos), Proto-Indo-Iranian *-ištʰas.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-istHos, *-tós, *-yōs

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/is.tɑz/`

### Suffix

1. _(morpheme, reconstruction)_ -est. Forms the superlative of certain adjectives.
