## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Ingwômēraz

### Etymology

From *Ingwô (“the Germanic god of fertility”) + *mēraz (“fame”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-rós, *-ō, *meh₁-, *meh₁-ró-s, *neḱ-, *néḱus, *n̥ḱús, *us

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈiŋ.ɡu.ˌmɛː.rɑz/`

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
