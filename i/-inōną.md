# -inōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/-in%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -inōną

### Etymology

Formed from the singular oblique stem of masculine n-stems: *-in- + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/i.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ Creates denominatives from nouns referring to persons, meaning to be or perform the duties of that person.
