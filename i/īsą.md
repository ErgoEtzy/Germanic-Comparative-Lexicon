[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C4%ABs%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *īsą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁eyH-so-, from *h₁eyH- (“ice”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁eyH-, *h₁eyH-so-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈiː.sɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ ice
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ name of the rune ᛁ (i)
