## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -iwiz

### Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Indo-European *-ewes, from *-us (u-stem suffix) + *-es (nominative plural suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-es, *-ewes, *-us

### Suffix

1. _(morpheme, reconstruction)_ Forms the nominative/vocative plurals of masculine and feminine u-stem nouns.
   - _sunuz (“son”) + *-iwiz → *suniwiz (“sons”)_
