## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -iþō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *-teh₂ (“-th”), probably by way of Late PIE *-éteh₂, also found in Sanskrit नग्नता (nagn-átā, “nudity”) and Ancient Greek ᾰ̓ρετή (ăretḗ).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-teh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/i.θɔː/`

### Suffix

1. _(feminine, morpheme, reconstruction)_ -ness, -th. Forms abstract nouns from verbs, adjectives or other nouns.
