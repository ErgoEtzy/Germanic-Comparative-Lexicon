[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/idiw%C4%ABt%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *idiwītą

### Etymology

From *idi- + *wītą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *weydós, *éti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌidiˈwiː.tɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ shame, disgrace
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ insult, disdain
