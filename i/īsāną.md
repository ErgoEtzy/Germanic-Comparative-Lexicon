# *īsāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C4%ABs%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *īsāną

### Etymology

From *īsą (“ice”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti, *h₁eyH-, *h₁eyH-so-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈī.sɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to become covered with ice, to freeze over
