## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -itją

### Etymology

Deverbal ending from *-itjaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ Suffix denoting the action of verbs ending in *-itjaną, used similarly as Modern English -age, -ation, -isation, -ism.
