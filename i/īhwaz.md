# *īhwaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C4%ABhwaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *īhwaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁eyHw- (“yew”), with probably intrusive *-gw-/-hw- from *-w-. Cognate with Proto-Celtic *iwos, *īwos.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁eyHw-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈiː.xʷɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ yew
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ name of the rune ᛇ (ī)
