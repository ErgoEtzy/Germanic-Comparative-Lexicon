# *irzijaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/irzijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *irzijaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁ers- (“to flow, wander, waver”). Cognate with Latin errō (“to wander, roam, stray, lose one's way”), Lithuanian įširsti (“to become angry, fly into passion”), Lithuanian aršùs (“fierce”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁ers-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈir.zi.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ bewildered, crazy, confused, lost
2. _(reconstruction)_ erring, wrong, erroneous
