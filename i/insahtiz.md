## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *insahtiz

### Etymology

From *in + *sahtiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tis, *h₁én, *seh₂g-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈin.ˌsɑx.tiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ account, narrative
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ discussion, argument
