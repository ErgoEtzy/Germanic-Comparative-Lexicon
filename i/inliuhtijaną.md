## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *inliuhtijaną

### Etymology

From *in + *liuhtijaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌin.ˈliu̯x.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to enlighten, illumine
