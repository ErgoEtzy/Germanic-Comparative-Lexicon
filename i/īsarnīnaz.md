## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *īsarnīnaz

### Etymology

From *īsarną + *-īnaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₁ésh₂r̥

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈiː.sɑr.niː.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ iron, made of iron
