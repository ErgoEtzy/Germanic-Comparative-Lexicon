# *ilhilaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ilhilaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ilhilaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁elḱ-, compare Proto-Indo-European *h₁élḱos (“sore, ulcer”). Possibly cognate with Proto-Celtic *ulkos (“bad, evil”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁elḱ-, *h₁élḱos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈil.xi.lɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ evil, bad
