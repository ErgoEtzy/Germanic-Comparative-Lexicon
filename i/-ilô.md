## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -ilô

### Etymology

Based on the pattern of *-ilǭ.
From *-ilaz (agent and instrumental suffix) + *-ô (agent suffix) and from a version incorporating the PIE diminutive suffix Proto-Indo-European *-lós (see diminutive *-ilaz) in the weak declension.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-lós

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ diminutive suffix used to form personal names or nicknames from common nouns and adjectives
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ diminutive suffix used to form masculine agent nouns, especially of plants and animals
