# -ilǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/-il%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -ilǭ

### Etymology

From *-ilaz (agent and instrumental suffix) + *-ǭ (agent suffix) and from a version incorporating the PIE diminutive suffix Proto-Indo-European *-lós (see diminutive *-ilaz) in the weak declension.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-lós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈi.lɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ Forms feminine agent nouns, especially of plants and animals.
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ Forms feminine diminutive nouns.
