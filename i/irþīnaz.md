## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *irþīnaz

### Etymology

From *erþō + *-īnaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₁er-, *h₁érteh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈir.θiː.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ earthen, made of earth
