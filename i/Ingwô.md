## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Ingwô

### Etymology

From *Inguz + *-ô (agent noun).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ō, *neḱ-, *néḱus, *n̥ḱús, *us

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ Ing, Yngvi, god of fertility
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a member of the Ingaevones
