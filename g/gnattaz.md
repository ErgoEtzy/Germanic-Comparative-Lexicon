# *gnattaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gnattaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gnattaz

### Etymology

Related to *gnittō (“gnit”) and likely to *gnagan (“to gnaw”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣnɑt.tɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a bite or scratch
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a biting insect; mosquito
