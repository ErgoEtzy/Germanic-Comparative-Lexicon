# *gudjô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gudj%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gudjô

### Etymology

From *gudą (“god; deity”) + *-jô. May also be an agent noun to *geutaną (“to pour”), with influence on the consonant from *gudą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʷʰutóm, *gʷʰutós, *ǵʰutóm, *ǵʰutós

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ one who invokes; a (pagan) priest (responsible for sacrifices and temple custodian)
