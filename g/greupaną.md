# *greupaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/greupan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *greupaną

### Etymology

Kroonen assumes a back-formation from the iterative *gruppōną (“to dig, hollow out”) - *grubōną, with the quasi-Proto-Indo-European form of the root as *gʰrewbʰ- (“to grind”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣreu̯.pɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cut up, to grind
