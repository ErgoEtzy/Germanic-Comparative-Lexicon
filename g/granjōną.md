# *granjōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/granj%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *granjōną

### Etymology

Possibly related to *grīnaną and *grainōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɑn.jɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to howl
2. _(reconstruction)_ to show one's teeth (in pain or anger)
