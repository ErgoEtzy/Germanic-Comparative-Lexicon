# *gardaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gardaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gardaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰórdʰos, from *gʰerdʰ- (“to enclose, encircle”). Cognate with Proto-Balto-Slavic *gardas (“enclosure”), Albanian gardh (“hedge, fence”), Sanskrit गृह (gṛhá, “house”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰerdʰ-, *gʰórdʰos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑr.ðɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ enclosure
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ court, yard
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ garden
