# *gliwāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gliw%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gliwāną

### Etymology

From *ǵʰli-u-eh₁- an extended form of *ǵʰley-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰley-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣli.wɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to glisten, to shine
