[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/Gun%C3%BEiharjaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Gunþiharjaz

### Etymology

From *gunþiz (“battle”) + *harjaz (“warrior”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʷʰéntis, *ker-, *kóryos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣun.θi.ˌxɑr.jɑz/`

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
