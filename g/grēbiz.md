# *grēbiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gr%C4%93biz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grēbiz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *grabaną (“to dig”). The e-grade as opposed to o-grade *grōbiz however, hints at a possible lost strong verb *grebaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɛː.βiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ digging, burying; able to be dug or buried
