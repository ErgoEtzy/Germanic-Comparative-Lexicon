# *gaits

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gaits)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gaits

### Etymology

Cognate with Proto-Italic *haidos, but cannot be traced back to Proto-Indo-European. According to Bjørn, from the same source as Proto-Semitic *gady-, Proto-Berber *e-ɣăyd, Proto-Nakh *gaaʒaᶰ.
See also *kittīną (“young animal”) and *kidją (“goatling”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑi̯ts/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ goat
