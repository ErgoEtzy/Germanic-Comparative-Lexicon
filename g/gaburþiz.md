# *gaburþiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gabur%C3%BEiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gaburþiz

### Etymology

Derived from *gaberaną (“to bear (children), give birth to”). Equivalent to *ga- + *burþiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɣɑ.ˈbur.θiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ birth, the act of giving birth
