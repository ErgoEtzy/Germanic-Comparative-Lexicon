## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Gunþilaibaz

### Etymology

From *gunþiz (“battle”) + *laibō (“remnant”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʷʰéntis, *leyp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣun.θi.ˌlɑi̯.βaz/`

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
