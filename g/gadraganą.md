## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gadraganą

### Etymology

From *ga- + *draganą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *dʰrógʰ-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈdrɑ.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to draw together, bring together; to accumulate
2. _(reconstruction)_ to bear, carry, give birth
