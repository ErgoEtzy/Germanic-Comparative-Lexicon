## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grēdagaz

### Etymology

Alteration of earlier *grēdugaz, equivalent to *grēduz (“hunger”) + *-gaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʰer-, *gʰreh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɛː.ðɑ.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ hungry
2. _(reconstruction)_ greedy
