# *glasą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/glas%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glasą

### Etymology

Traditionally derived by Pokorny from an s-extension of Proto-Indo-European *gʰel-, *ǵʰel- (“to shine, shimmer, glow”). Kroonen prefers to derive the term from an s-stem of *glōaną (“to glow”), which is likely ultimately from the same root.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣlɑ.sɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ amber, glass
