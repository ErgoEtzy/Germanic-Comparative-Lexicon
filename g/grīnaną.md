# *grīnaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gr%C4%ABnan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grīnaną

### Etymology

Possibly from a Proto-Indo-European *gʰrey- (“to flash”), with a nasal suffix; potential cognates include Old Irish grían (“sun”). Alternatively, of ideophonic origin.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰrey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣriː.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to snarl, show teeth, grin
2. _(reconstruction)_ to flash, give light
