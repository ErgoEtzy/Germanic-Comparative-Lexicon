# *gramô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gram%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gramô

### Etymology

From *gramaz (“angry”), from Proto-Indo-European *gʰrem- (“to sound, bellow, growl, roar, be angry”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰrem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɑ.mɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ anger, wrath
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ one who is angry, fiend, enemy
