## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gramjaną

### Etymology

From *grimmaną (“to rage, be furious”) + *-janą or *gramaz (“furious”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰrem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɑm.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to be angry, provoke, irritate, anger
