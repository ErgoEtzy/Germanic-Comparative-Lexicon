# *gawissaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gawissaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gawissaz

### Etymology

From *ga- + *wissaz (past participle of *witaną).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *wid-tó-s, *widtós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈwis.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ certain, sure
2. _(reconstruction)_ known
