# *gutaniskaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gutaniskaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gutaniskaz

### Etymology

From *gutô (“a Goth”) + *-iskaz (“-ish”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-iskos, *ǵʰewd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣu.tɑ.nis.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ of or pertaining to the Goths; Gothic
