# *grasōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gras%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grasōną

### Etymology

From *grasą (“grass”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *gʰreh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɑ.sɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to feed on grass; graze
