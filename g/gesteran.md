## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gesteran

### Etymology

From *gesteraz + *-n. The first element is from Proto-Indo-European *dʰǵʰyésteros.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰǵʰyésteros

### Adverb

1. _(reconstruction)_ yesterday
