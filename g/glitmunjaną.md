## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glitmunjaną

### Etymology

From pre-Proto-Germanic *gʰlidmon-ye-ti, from *gʰléyd-mō ~ gʰlid-men-és (whence *glitmô (“shine”)), from *gʰléideti (“to shine”) (whence *glītaną), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰléy-de-ti, from *ǵʰley- (“to shine”) + *-deti.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-deti, *ǵʰley-, *ǵʰléy-de-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣlit.mun.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shine, glitter
