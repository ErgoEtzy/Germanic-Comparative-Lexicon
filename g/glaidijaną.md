## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glaidijaną

### Etymology

From *glīdaną + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʰleydʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣlai̯.ði.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to slip
