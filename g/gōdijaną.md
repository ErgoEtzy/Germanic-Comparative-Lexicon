# *gōdijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/g%C5%8Ddijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gōdijaną

### Etymology

From *gōdaz (“good”) + *-janą. Cognate with Proto-Slavic *godìti.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *gʰedʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɔː.ði.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make good, improve
