## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gelpōną

### Etymology

From *gelpą (“boast, claim”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to claim, boast
