# *glitmô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/glitm%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glitmô

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *gʰlidmon-; thus equivalent to *glītaną (“to shine”) + *-mô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰley-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣlit.mɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ shine, luster
