## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ganōgijaną

### Etymology

From *ganōgaz (“enough, sufficient”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *gʰe, *gʰo-, *h₂eh₂nóḱe, *h₂neḱ-, *ḱóm

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈnɔː.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to satisfy, to suffice, to satiate
2. _(reconstruction)_ to be present in abundance
