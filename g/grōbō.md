## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grōbō

### Etymology

Functional o-grade ō-stem derived from *grabaną (“to dig, scratch”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰrebʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɔː.βɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ hole, ditch, pit
