[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gnitt%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gnittō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰneydʰn-, *gʰneydn- (“to gnaw”), from *gʰen- (“to bite, scratch”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰneydʰn-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ louse
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ gnat
