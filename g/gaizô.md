## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gaizô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰays- (“pointed stick, spear”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰey- (“to drive, move, fling”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰays-, *ǵʰey-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a wedge-shaped piece
