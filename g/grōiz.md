# *grōiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gr%C5%8Diz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grōiz

### Etymology

A verbal adjective derived from *grōaną (“to grow, green”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɔː.iz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ growing, able to grow
