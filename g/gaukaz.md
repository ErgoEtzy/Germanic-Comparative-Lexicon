## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gaukaz

### Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Indo-European *gʰegʰuǵʰos, from *gʰegʰuǵʰ- (“cuckoo”), of the type of *kʷékʷlos. Cognate with Latvian dzeguze (“cuckoo”).Gąsiorowski makes a reduplicated noun based on *gʰewǵʰ- (“to hide”), which would mean that another valid reconstruction would be *gʰouǵʰnos. This form under normal sound laws of Germanic would become *gaukkaz under Kluge's Law and then reduce to the current form *gaukaz due to reduction of geminates to single consonants after long vowels & diphthongs (compare *taikijaną, which underwent the same process).
