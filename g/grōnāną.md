# *grōnāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gr%C5%8Dn%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grōnāną

### Etymology

From *grōniz (“green”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti, *gʰreh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɔː.nɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to become green, flourish
