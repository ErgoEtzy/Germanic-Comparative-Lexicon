# *galdijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/galdijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *galdijaną

### Etymology

From *galdijaz (“barren, fruitless, castrated”) + *-janą (factitive suffix). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gʰel- (“to cut”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑl.ði.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to geld, castrate
