# *gebō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/geb%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gebō

### Etymology

From *gebaną (“to give”) + *-ō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰebʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣe.βɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ gift
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ name of the rune ᚷ (g)
