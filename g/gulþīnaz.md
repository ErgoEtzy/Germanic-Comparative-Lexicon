# *gulþīnaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gul%C3%BE%C4%ABnaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gulþīnaz

### Etymology

From *gulþą (“gold”) + *-īnaz, possibly corresponding to from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰolh₃-t-inos (“golden”), from *ǵʰelh₃- (“yellow”). Compare Proto-Slavic *zoltьnъ.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰelh₃-, *ǵʰolh₃-t-inos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣul.θiː.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ golden, made of gold
