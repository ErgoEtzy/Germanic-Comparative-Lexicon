# *glōaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gl%C5%8Dan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glōaną

### Etymology

From o-grade intensive Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰlóh₁-e-ti or reduplicated stative *ǵʰe-ǵʰlóh₁-e ~ *ǵʰe-ǵʰl̥h₁-ḗr, from *ǵʰleh₁- (“to shine, glow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰleh₁-, *ǵʰlóh₁-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣlɔː.ɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to glow
