# *gaidō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gaid%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gaidō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰey- (“to drive, move, hurl, fling; missile, projectile”). Cognate with Sanskrit हेति (heti, “missile, projectile”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑi̯.ðɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ prickle; point; ord
