# *gaistaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gaistaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gaistaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰéysd-os, from *ǵʰeys- (“to be angry, agitated”). Cognate with Sanskrit हेड (héḍa, “anger”), Lithuanian žei̇̃sti (“to wound”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰéysd-os

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑi̯s.tɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ terror, fear
2. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ spirit, ghost
3. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ mind
