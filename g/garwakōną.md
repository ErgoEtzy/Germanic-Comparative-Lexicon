[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/garwak%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *garwakōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰrebʰ- (“to grab, take”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰrebʰ-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to prepare, supply
