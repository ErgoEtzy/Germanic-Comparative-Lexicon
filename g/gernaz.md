## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gernaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰerH-no-s, from *ǵʰerH- (“to yearn for, rejoice”). Cognate with Ancient Greek χαίρω (khaírō, “to be very glad”), Latin horior (“to encourage”), Sanskrit हर्यति (háryati, “to enjoy, like”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry
