## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ganhuz

### Etymology

From earlier *ganhwuz, possibly derived from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰengʰ- (“to stride, step”), but Kroonen rejects this derivation and treats the root as having no known etymology, also rejecting connections with Lithuanian guvus (“agile, quick”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰengʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑ̃ː.xuz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ fast, quick
2. _(reconstruction)_ sudden
