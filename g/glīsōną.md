# *glīsōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gl%C4%ABs%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glīsōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰleys-, from *ǵʰley- (“to shine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰleys-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shine, glisten, glitter
2. _(reconstruction)_ to look
