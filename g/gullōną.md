## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gullōną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *gʰl̥néh₂-, a zero-grade iterative to *galaną, *gellaną (“to yell”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

### Verb
