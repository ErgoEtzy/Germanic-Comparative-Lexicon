## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gafrēgijaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *preḱ- (“to ask, request”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *preḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑˈɸrɛː.ɣi.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ famous
