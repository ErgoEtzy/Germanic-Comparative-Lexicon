# *gōlijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/g%C5%8Dlijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gōlijaną

### Etymology

From *gōlaz (“pleasing, charming”) + *-janą, which is the vṛddhi gerundive of *galaną (“to shout; to charm”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɔː.li.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to please, soothe, appease
2. _(reconstruction)_ to greet
