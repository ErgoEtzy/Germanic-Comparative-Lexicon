## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gatą

### Etymology

Kroonen derives the word from Pre-Germanic *gʰodom, itself from *getaną (“to find a way; to get”), and rejects connections to Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰed- (“to defecate”) (whence Sanskrit हदति (hádati)).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰed-, *ǵʰed-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑ.tɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ hole, opening, passage
