# *grōaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gr%C5%8Dan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grōaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰreh₁- (“to become green, grow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰreh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɡrɔː.ɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to green
2. _(reconstruction)_ to grow
