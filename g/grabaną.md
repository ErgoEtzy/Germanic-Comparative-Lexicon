# *grabaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/graban%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grabaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰróbʰ-, o-grade form of Proto-Indo-European *gʰrebʰ- (“to dig, scratch, bury”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰrebʰ-, *gʰróbʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɑ.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to dig
2. _(reconstruction)_ to engrave, scratch
