# *gawjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gawjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gawjaną

### Etymology

Unclear. Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰewH- (“to call, invoke”), related to Proto-Slavic *zъvati (“to call”).
Or from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰeh₂w- (“to gape, yawn”), related to Lithuanian gomurỹs (“palate”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰeh₂w-, *ǵʰewH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑu̯.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to bark
2. _(reconstruction)_ to shout, scream, or yell at
