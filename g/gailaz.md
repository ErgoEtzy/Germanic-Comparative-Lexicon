# *gailaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gailaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gailaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰoylos (“frothing, arrogant, tempestuous, violent, wanton”). Cognate with Proto-Balto-Slavic *gailas (“sharp; angry”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰoylos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑi̯.lɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ merry, excited, lush, beautiful, lustful
