# *grīmô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gr%C4%ABm%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grīmô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰrēy- (“to paint, streak, smear”), from *gʰer- (“to rub, stroke”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰrēy-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣriː.mɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ mask
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ visor; helmet
