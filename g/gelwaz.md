## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gelwaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰelh₃wós, from *ǵʰelh₃- (“green, yellow”) + *-wós. Cognate with Latin helvus (“light-yellow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-wós, *ǵʰelh₃-, *ǵʰelh₃wós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣel.wɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ yellow
