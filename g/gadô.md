## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gadô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰedʰ- (“to join, unite”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰedʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑ.ðɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ associate, partner, comrade, companion
