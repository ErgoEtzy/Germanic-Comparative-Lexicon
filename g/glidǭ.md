## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glidǭ

### Etymology

Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰleydʰ- (“to glide”); compare *glīdaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰleydʰ-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ kite (bird of prey)
