# *galīkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gal%C4%ABk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *galīkōną

### Etymology

From *galīkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈliː.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be like, to resemble
2. _(reconstruction)_ to make alike, to make even
