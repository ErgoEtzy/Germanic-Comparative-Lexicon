# *grippōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gripp%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grippōną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *gʰribʰnéh₂-, a zero-grade iterative to the root of *grīpaną (“to grip”) (though Kroonen considers this form the original, and *grīpaną a back-formation).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrip.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to grasp
