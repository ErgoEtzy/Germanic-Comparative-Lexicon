# *galpōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/galp%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *galpōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰolb-, from *gʰel- (“to call, cry out, shout, scream”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰolb-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɡɑl.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to resound; sound out
2. _(reconstruction)_ to boast
