# *glōdiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gl%C5%8Ddiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glōdiz

### Etymology

From *glōaną (“to glow”) + *-þiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰleh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣlɔː.ðiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ glow
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ embers
