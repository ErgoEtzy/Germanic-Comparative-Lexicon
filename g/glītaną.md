# *glītaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gl%C4%ABtan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glītaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *ǵʰleyd-, possibly an extension of *ǵʰley- as seen in *glīwą (“joy”). Kroonen considers it likely the strong verb was back-formed from the iterative *glittōną (“to shine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰley-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣliː.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to shine, sparkle
