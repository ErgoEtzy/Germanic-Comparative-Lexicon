## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *galaubaz

### Etymology

From *ga- + *laubaz, with the latter derived from *leubaz (“dear, beloved”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewbʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑˈlɑu̯.βɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ trustworthy
2. _(reconstruction)_ valuable
