# *gafulkiją

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gafulkij%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gafulkiją

### Etymology

From *ga- + *fulką + *-ją (collective suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂el-, *pl̥h₁-gós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈɸul.ki.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ a collection of people
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ host, troop
