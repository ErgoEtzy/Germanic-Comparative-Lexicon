# *gasitjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gasitjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gasitjaną

### Etymology

From *ga- + *sitjaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sed-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɣɑˈsit.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sit down, take a seat
