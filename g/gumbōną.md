## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gumbōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʷʰemb- (“to hop, jump”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷʰemb-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to hop, skip, or bounce; to jolt, push, shove
