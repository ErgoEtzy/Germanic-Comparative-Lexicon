# *gawitāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gawit%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gawitāną

### Etymology

From *ga- + *witāną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyd-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to become wise
2. _(reconstruction)_ to be able
