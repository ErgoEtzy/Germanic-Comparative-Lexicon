## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gawrekaną

### Etymology

From *ga- + *wrekaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʰe, *gʰo-, *wreg-, *ḱóm

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɣɑˈwre.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to avenge
