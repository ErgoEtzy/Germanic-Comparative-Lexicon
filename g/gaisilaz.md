# *gaisilaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gaisilaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gaisilaz

### Etymology

From a formation related to *gaizaz (“spear, tip”) + *-ilaz (“diminutive suffix”). The sense development in the West Germanic branch parallels English rod (“staff (of office), rod of punishment, a substitute such as a cane or withe”)

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-dʰlom, *-e-ló-s, *-lós, *-tlom, *-trom

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑi̯.si.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ stick, beam
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ ray (of light, etc.)
