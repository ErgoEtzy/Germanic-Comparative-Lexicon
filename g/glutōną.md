# *glutōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/glut%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glutōną

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *gʰln̥d-néh₂-ti and thus related to *glintaną (“to shine”), *glantaz (“bright”), from the root *gʰlend- (“to shine”); the unusual formation may be due to dissimilation *gʰln̥d-néh₂- > *gʰl̥d-néh₂-. Perhaps ultimately from an even earlier root *gʰel- (“to shine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰlend-, *gʰln̥d-néh₂-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣlu.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to peer
2. _(reconstruction)_ to stare; gawk
