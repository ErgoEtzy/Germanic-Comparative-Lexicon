# *gaigijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gaigijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gaigijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰeyǵʰ-, *gʰeygʰ- (“to gape, protrude”), from *ǵʰēy(w)-, *ǵʰyāw- (“to yawn, gape”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰeyǵʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑi̯.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to move back and forth, to totter
2. _(reconstruction)_ to take a wrong direction, to transgress
