## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gumāną

### Etymology

Kroonen assumes this is a stative verb created to an unattested adjective *gaumaz (“attentive”), whence also *gaumaz (“heed, attention”).
The zero-grade of the verb would be a secondary development.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry
