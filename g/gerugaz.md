# *gerugaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gerugaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gerugaz

### Etymology

From *geruz (“desire, greed”) + *-gaz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰer- (“to desire, like”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣe.ru.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ covetous
2. _(reconstruction)_ greedy
