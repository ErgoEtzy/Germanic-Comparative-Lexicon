# *grimmaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/grimman%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grimmaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *gʰrémne- or *gʰrénme-, from Proto-Indo-European *gʰrem- (“to be angry”), likely from Proto-Indo-European *gʰer- (“to make a noise, rattle, growl, murmur”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰer-, *gʰrem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrim.mɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to rage, roar, be angry
