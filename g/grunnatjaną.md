## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grunnatjaną

### Etymology

From *grunnōną + *-atjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *gʰrem- (“to thunder”) or *gʰrun- (“to shout”), both likely imitative of pigs; compare Latin grunniō (“to grunt”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰrem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrun.nɑt.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to grunt
