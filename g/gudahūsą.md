## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gudahūsą

### Etymology

From *gudą (“god”) + *hūsą (“house”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)kewH-, *(s)kuH-s-o-m, *gʷʰutóm, *gʷʰutós, *ǵʰutóm, *ǵʰutós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣu.ðɑ.xuː.sɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ temple, god-house
