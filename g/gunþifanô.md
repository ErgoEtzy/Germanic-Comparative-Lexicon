## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gunþifanô

### Etymology

From *gunþiz (“battle”) + *fanô (“flag”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʷʰéntis, *peh₂n-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣun.θi.ˌɸɑ.nɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ war banner
