# *ganganą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gangan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ganganą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰengʰ- (“to walk, step”). Cognate with Lithuanian žeñgti (“to step”), as well as perhaps Sanskrit जंगहे (jaṃgahe).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰengʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑŋ.ɡɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to go, walk, step
