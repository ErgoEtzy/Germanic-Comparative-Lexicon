# *gēbiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/g%C4%93biz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gēbiz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *gebaną (“to give”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰebʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɛː.βiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ given, having been given
2. _(reconstruction)_ givable
