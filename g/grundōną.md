## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grundōną

### Etymology

From *grunduz (“ground”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *gʰrem-, *gʰrendʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrun.dɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ To establish, to lay the foundations of
2. _(reconstruction)_ To research, to explore
3. _(reconstruction)_ To think about, to ruminate
