# *glanaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/glanaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glanaz

### Etymology

Normalization of pre-Proto-Germanic *gʰlə-nó-s (“shining, glowing”) (quasi-Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰlh₁-nó-s), past participle of *glōaną (“to glow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰleh₁-, *ǵʰlh₁-nó-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣlɑ.nɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ shine, brightness
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ ember
