# *gabbōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gabb%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gabbōną

### Etymology

Kroonen reconstructs an iterative verb *gʰHbʰnéh₂- derived from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰeh₂- (“to gape, be wide open”), but only the variant with a geminate -bb- in the root is actually attested in the descendants.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰeh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑb.bɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to joke, to mock
2. _(reconstruction)_ to laugh
