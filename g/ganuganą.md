# *ganuganą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ganugan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ganuganą

### Etymology

From *ga- + *nuganą, although the latter has no attested descendants. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂eh₂nóḱe, from *h₂neḱ- (“to reach”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂eh₂nóḱe, *h₂neḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈnu.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to suffice, to be enough/sufficient
