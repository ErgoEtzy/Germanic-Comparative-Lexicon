## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gruppōną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *gʰr̥bʰnéh₂-, with remodeling based on its strong verb, an iterative formation to *grabaną (“to dig”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gʰrebʰ- (“to dig, scratch, bury”). Related to Sanskrit गृभ्णाति (gṛbhṇāti, “to seize, lay hold of”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰrebʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrup.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to dig, scrape, groove
