## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gasehwaną

### Etymology

From *ga- + *sehwaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *sekʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɣɑˈse.xʷɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to see completely
