# *gimrį̄

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gimr%C4%AF%CC%84)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gimrį̄

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰyem- (“winter”) (in o-grade *ǵʰyom- with loss of *-y-). Cognate with Latin hiems (“winter”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰyem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣim.rĩː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a yearling lamb
