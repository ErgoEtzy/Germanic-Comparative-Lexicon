## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gailō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰeh₂- (“to yawn, gape”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰeh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑi̯.lɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a gap; crevice; chasm; ravine
