## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gāną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰeh₁- (“to go, reach”). The suppletive past tense is from Proto-Indo-European *h₁ey- (“to go”), although all modern Germanic languages have eliminated this stem by further resuppletion or innovation.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁ey-, *ǵʰeh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to go, walk
