# *getulaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/getulaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *getulaz

### Etymology

From *getaną (“to attain, acquire, get, receive, hold”) + *-ulaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣe.tu.lɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ acquisitive, prone to acquisition
