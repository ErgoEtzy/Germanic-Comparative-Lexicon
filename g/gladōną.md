# *gladōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/glad%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gladōną

### Etymology

From *gladaz (“bright, smooth, cheerful”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣlɑ.ðɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make cheerful
2. _(reconstruction)_ to smoothen
