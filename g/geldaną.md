# *geldaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/geldan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *geldaną

### Etymology

Akin to Proto-Slavic *želsti (attested only in Old Church Slavonic жлѣсти (žlěsti) and Old East Slavic желести (želesti)). If not an early borrowing from Germanic, these may be from common Proto-Indo-European *gʰeldʰ-. Old Irish gell (“pledge”) may also continue the same root. On the other hand, the pre-Germanic root may need to be *gʰelt- (> *gelþ-) in order to explain Old Swedish gjalla (“to repay”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰeldʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣel.dɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to pay
2. _(reconstruction)_ to have/give value
