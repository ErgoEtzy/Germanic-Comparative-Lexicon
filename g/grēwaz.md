# *grēwaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gr%C4%93waz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grēwaz

### Etymology

Probably from a Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰreh₁- (“to glow, be visible”), and cognate with Proto-Slavic *zьrěti (“to observe, look at”), Lithuanian žėrė́ti (“to shine, sparkle”), and Proto-Germanic *greujaną (“to dawn”) (whence Swedish gry (“to dawn”)).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰreh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɛː.wɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ grey
