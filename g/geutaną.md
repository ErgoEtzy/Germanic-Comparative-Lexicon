## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *geutaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰewd- (“to pour”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰewd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣeu̯.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to pour
