## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gasakaną

### Etymology

From *ga- + *sakaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *seh₂g-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈsɑ.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to argue, dispute
2. _(reconstruction)_ to reprove, correct
3. _(reconstruction)_ to scold, chide
