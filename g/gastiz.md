Possibly attested as -gasti- (in Harigasti-, 450–350 BCE, Negau), from earlier *gostīz, from Proto-Indo-European *gʰóstis (“stranger, guest”). Cognate with Proto-Balto-Slavic *gastís, Latin hostis.
