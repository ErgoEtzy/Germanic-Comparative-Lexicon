# *gauraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gauraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gauraz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *gʰowro-s, probably from a suffixed form of Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰewH- (“to call on, invoke”) or from *gʰewro-. Cognate with Old Irish gúre (“pain, pang”), Sanskrit घोर (ghora, “terrible”), Proto-Slavic *žuriti (“to rage”), and Lithuanian žiaurùs.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰewH-

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ sad, sorrowful
