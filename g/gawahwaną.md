# *gawahwaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gawahwan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gawahwaną

### Etymology

From *ga- + *wahwaną, from Proto-Indo-European *wekʷ- (“to speak”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wekʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈwɑ.xʷɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to say; notify; remind
