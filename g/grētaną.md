[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gr%C4%93tan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grētaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰreh₁d- (“to sound”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰreh₁d-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɛː.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to weep, to cry
