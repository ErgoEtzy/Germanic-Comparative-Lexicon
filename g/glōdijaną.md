# *glōdijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gl%C5%8Ddijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glōdijaną

### Etymology

From *glōdiz (“glow”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰleh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣlɔː.ði.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to glow, to sparkle
