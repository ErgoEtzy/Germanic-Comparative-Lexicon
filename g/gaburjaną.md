## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gaburjaną

### Etymology

From *ga- + *burjaną (“to raise, levy”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈbur.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be due; beseem
2. _(reconstruction)_ to rise; emerge
3. _(reconstruction)_ to occur; happen
