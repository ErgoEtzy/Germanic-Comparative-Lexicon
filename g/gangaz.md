# *gangaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gangaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gangaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰóngʰ-os, from the root *ǵʰengʰ- (“to stride, step”). Synchronically analyzable as a nominal formation from *ganganą (“to go, walk”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰóngʰ-os

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑŋ.ɡɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ pace, step, gait
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ walk
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ way, course
