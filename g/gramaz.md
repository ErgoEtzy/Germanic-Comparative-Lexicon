# *gramaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gramaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gramaz

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *gʰróm-o-s, from *gʰrem- (“to roar; to be angry”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰrem-, *gʰróm-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɑ.mɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ furious
2. _(reconstruction)_ hostile
