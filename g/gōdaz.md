# *gōdaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/g%C5%8Ddaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gōdaz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of otherwise unattested strong verb *gadaną, from Proto-Indo-European *gʰedʰ- (“to unite, be associated, suit”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰedʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɔː.ðɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ good
