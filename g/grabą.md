## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grabą

### Etymology

Derived from the verb *grabaną (“to dig”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ that which is dug (ditch, trench, hole, etc.)
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ grave
