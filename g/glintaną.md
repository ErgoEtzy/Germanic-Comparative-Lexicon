## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glintaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰlend- (“to look, shine”), related to Proto-Slavic *ględati (“to look at”), Latvian glen̂st (“to scarcely perceive”), Old Irish asgleinn (“to examine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰlend-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣlin.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shine
2. _(reconstruction)_ to look
