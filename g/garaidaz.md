## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *garaidaz

### Etymology

From *ga- + *raidaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʰe, *gʰo-, *h₂roy-dʰ-ó-s, *ḱóm

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈrɑi̯.ðaz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ ready, arranged, prepared
