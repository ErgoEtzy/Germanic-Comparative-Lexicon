## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gardiz

### Etymology

Feminine derivative of *gardaz, from
From Proto-Indo-European *gʰórdʰos, from *gʰerdʰ- (“to enclose, encircle”). Cognate with Proto-Balto-Slavic *gardas (“enclosure”), Albanian gardh (“hedge, fence”), Sanskrit गृह (gṛhá, “house”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰerdʰ-, *gʰórdʰos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑr.ðiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ enclosure
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ court, yard
