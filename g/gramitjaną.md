# *gramitjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gramitjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gramitjaną

### Etymology

From *gramaz (“angry”) + *-itjaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-dyé-, *gʰrem-, *gʰróm-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɑ.mit.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to grunt
2. _(reconstruction)_ to rage, roar
