## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *garēdaną

### Etymology

From *ga- + *rēdaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to advise
2. _(reconstruction)_ to support
