## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gibidaz

### Etymology

Likely from *gebaną (“to give”) + Proto-Indo-European *-tós, meaning "the gifted ones" or "the generous ones". More at Gepids.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-tós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣi.βi.ðɑz/`
