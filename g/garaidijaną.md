# *garaidijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/garaidijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *garaidijaną

### Etymology

From *garaidaz (“ready, prepared”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *gʰe, *gʰo-, *h₂roy-dʰ-ó-s, *ḱóm

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈrɑi̯.ði.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to arrange, set in order
