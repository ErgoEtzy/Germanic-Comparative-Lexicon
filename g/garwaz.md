# *garwaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/garwaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *garwaz

### Etymology

Probably from *ga- + *arwaz (“ready, fast”). Although a deverbal derivation would be more natural, such as from a lost strong verb *garwaną, where the verb would be from *ga- + older *arwaną, the suffix *-waz is likely adjectival in function, reflecting Proto-Indo-European *-wós.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-wós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑr.wɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ ready, prepared
