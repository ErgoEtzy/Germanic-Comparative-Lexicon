## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gerdaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰerdʰ- (“to enclose”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰerdʰ-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to gird
