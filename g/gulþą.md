## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gulþą

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *ǵʰĺ̥h₃-to-m (“gold”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰelh₃- (“green, yellow”). Compare Proto-Slavic *zolto, Proto-Baltic *želtas, whence Latvian zelts. Disputed relation to *geldaną (“to pay”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰelh₃-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣul.θɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ gold
