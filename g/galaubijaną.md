## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *galaubijaną

### Etymology

From *ga- + *laubijaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewbʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈlɑu̯.βi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to praise; commend
2. _(reconstruction)_ to allow; grant
3. _(reconstruction)_ to believe
