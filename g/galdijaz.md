# *galdijaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/galdijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *galdijaz

### Etymology

Uncertain. Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *gʰel- (“to cut”) or *gʰel- (“to shout, sing, charm”) via a sense of "bewitched/cursed (with infertility)".

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰel-

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ unfruitful, barren, infertile
