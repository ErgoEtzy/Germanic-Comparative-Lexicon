## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ginōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰh₂ey-, itself from *ǵʰeh₂- (“to yawn, gape”) + *-ey-, with the suffix *-néh₂ti. See also *gīnaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-néh₂ti, *-éyti, *gʰh₂ey-, *ǵʰeh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣi.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to gape
2. _(reconstruction)_ to yawn
