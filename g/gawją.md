# *gawją

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gawj%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gawją

### Etymology

From earlier *gaawją (“riverlands, lakeside land”), from *ga- + *awjō (“land by a waterway”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʰe, *gʰo-, *h₂ékʷeh₂, *ḱóm

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑu̯.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ district, shire, region, area
