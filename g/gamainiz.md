## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gamainiz

### Etymology

From *ga- + *mainiz, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱom-moynis. Related to Latin commūnis (from earlier co(m)moinis).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱom-moynis

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈmɑi̯.niz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ shared, communal, public
2. _(reconstruction)_ common, ordinary
