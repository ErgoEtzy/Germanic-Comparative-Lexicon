# *glēmaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gl%C4%93maz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glēmaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰleh₁-mó-s, from *ǵʰleh₁- (“shine, glow”) + *-mós.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-mós, *ǵʰleh₁-, *ǵʰleh₁-mó-s

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ twilight
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ moon
