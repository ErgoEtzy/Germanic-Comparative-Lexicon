# *gangiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gangiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gangiz

### Etymology

A verbal adjective derived from *ganganą (“to go”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰengʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑŋ.ɡiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ passable, going, able to go
