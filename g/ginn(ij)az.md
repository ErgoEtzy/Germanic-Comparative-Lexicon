# *ginn(ij)az

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ginn%28ij%29az)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ginn(ij)az

### Etymology

Uncertain. Possibly related to Proto-Germanic *ginnaną (“to begin, cut open”) or perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *gʰh₂ey-, from *ǵʰeh₂- (“to yawn, gape”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰh₂ey-

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ wide, vast, spacious
