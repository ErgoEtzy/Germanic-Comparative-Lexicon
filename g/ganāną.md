# *ganāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gan%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ganāną

### Etymology

According to Kroonen this stative verb is backformed from an original nasal-present Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰh₂-néh₂-, which is derived from the root *ǵʰeh₂- and suffix *-néh₂-.
Alternatively it could be derived from the noun *ganaz (“yawn”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰh₂-néh₂-, *ǵʰh₂néh₂ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑ.nɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to gape, yawn
2. _(reconstruction)_ to gaze, stare
