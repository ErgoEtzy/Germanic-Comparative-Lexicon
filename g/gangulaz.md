# *gangulaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gangulaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gangulaz

### Etymology

From *ganganą + *-ulaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰengʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑŋ.ɡu.lɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ going, roaming
