[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gr%C4%ABsaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grīsaz

### Etymology

Unknown. Often related to *grēwaz, which would require a pre-form *greh₁-is-, from Proto-Indo-European *gʰreh₁- (“to grow; to become green”). This is highly speculative, however.

Uncertain. Perhaps from Etymology 1 above, ultimately tied to *grīsaną (“to be frightened, shudder, dread”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰreh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣriː.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ grey
2. _(reconstruction)_ terrible; frightening
