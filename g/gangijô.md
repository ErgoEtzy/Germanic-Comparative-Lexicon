## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gangijô

### Etymology

From *ganganą + *-jô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰengʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑŋ.ɡi.jɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ walker, goer
