# *galaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/galan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *galaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰel- (“to shout, cry”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑ.lɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shout, yell
2. _(reconstruction)_ to charm, sing
