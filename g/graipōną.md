# *graipōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/graip%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *graipōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰroybʰ-néh₂-ti, an o-grade iterative verb from *gʰreybʰ- (“to grab, grasp”).
Or possibly from *graipō (“a grasp”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰroybʰ-néh₂-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɑi̯.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to grab
2. _(reconstruction)_ to grasp
