# *galōfô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gal%C5%8Df%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *galōfô

### Etymology

From *ga- (associative prefix) + *lōfô (“palm of the hand”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *leh₂p-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈlɔː.ɸɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ glove
