## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gilją

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰeh₂- (“to yawn, gape”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰeh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣil.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ cleft; gully
