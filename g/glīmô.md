## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glīmô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰléy-mn̥ ~ *ǵʰli-mén-s, from *ǵʰley- (“to shine”) + *-mn̥. Byform with *glaimô (whence Old High German gleimo) and *glemō (whence Old High German gleomu).
