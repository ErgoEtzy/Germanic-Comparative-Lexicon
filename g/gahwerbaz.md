# *gahwerbaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gahwerbaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gahwerbaz

### Etymology

From *ga- + *hwerbaz (“mobile, turning”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(e)-ós, *(ó)-os, *-os, *-ós, *gʰe, *gʰo-, *kʷerp-, *ḱóm

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈxʷer.βɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ able to be converted, persuadable, pliant
