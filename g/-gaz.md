[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/-gaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -gaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *-kos, *-ḱos. Cognates include Latin -cus, Ancient Greek -κός (-kós), Sanskrit -श (-śa), -क (-ka) and Old Church Slavonic -ъкъ (-ŭkŭ).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-kos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ -y; forms adjectives from nouns and verbs with a sense of “being, doing, or having”.
