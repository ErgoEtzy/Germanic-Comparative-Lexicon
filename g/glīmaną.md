## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glīmaną

### Etymology

A strong verb derived from Pre-Germanic *ǵʰleymós (“shine”), an extension of *ǵʰley-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰley-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣliː.mɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shine
