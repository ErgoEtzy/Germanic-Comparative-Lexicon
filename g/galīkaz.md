[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gal%C4%ABkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *galīkaz

### Etymology

From *ga- + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɣɑ.ˈliː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ alike, similar
2. _(reconstruction)_ equal
