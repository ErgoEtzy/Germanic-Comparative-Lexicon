# *gīnaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/g%C4%ABnan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gīnaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʰh₂ey-, itself from *ǵʰeh₂- (“to gape, yawn”). Could be related to *ginnaną (“to make an opening, to cut open; to begin”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰh₂ey-, *ǵʰeh₂-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to gape, yawn
