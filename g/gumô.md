## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gumô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰm̥mṓ (“human”). Cognate with Latin homō (“human being”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰm̥mṓ, *ǵʰmṓ

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣu.mɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ man
