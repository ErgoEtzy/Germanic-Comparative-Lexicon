# *gadilingaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gadilingaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gadilingaz

### Etymology

From *gadō (“joined, united”) + *-ilingaz, from Proto-Indo-European *gʰedʰ- (“to join, unite”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰedʰ-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ relative, kinsman
