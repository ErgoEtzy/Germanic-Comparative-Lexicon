# *griwjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/griwjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *griwjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰreh₁u-, *ǵʰreh₁- (“to dawn, shine”). Cognate with Hittite 𒅗𒊒𒌑 (ka-ru-ú, “early; up to now”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰreh₁u-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣriw.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to dawn, begin to shine
