# *grappōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/grapp%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *grappōną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *gʰrobʰneh₂-, apparently an iterative to *grabaną (“to dig”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣrɑp.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to grapple, grab
