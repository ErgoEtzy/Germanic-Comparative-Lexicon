[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gnagan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gnaganą

### Etymology

Probably onomatopoeic; or from Proto-Indo-European *gʰnēgʰ- (“to gnaw, scratch”), from Proto-Indo-European *gʰen- (“to gnaw, grate, scrate, whit”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰen-, *gʰnēgʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣnɑ.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to gnaw
