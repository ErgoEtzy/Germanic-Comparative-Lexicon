# *gawerpaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gawerpan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gawerpaną

### Etymology

From *ga- + *werpaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʰe, *gʰo-, *wergʷ-, *ḱóm

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɣɑˈwer.pɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to throw
2. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to cast
