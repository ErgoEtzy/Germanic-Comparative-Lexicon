# *gabīgaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gab%C4%ABgaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gabīgaz

### Etymology

From *gebaną (“to give”) + *-īgaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰebʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑ.βiː.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ rich
