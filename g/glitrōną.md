## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *glitrōną

### Etymology

Possibly from either unattested *glitiz + *-rōną, from *ǵʰlid-í-s, or *glitraz + *-ōną, from *ǵʰlid-ró-s, both from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰleyd-, from *ǵʰley- (“to shine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰley-, *ǵʰleyd-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shine, glisten, glitter
