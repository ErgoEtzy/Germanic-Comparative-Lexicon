# *gamanōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/gaman%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *gamanōną

### Etymology

From *gamaną (“fun, amusement, game”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(dʰ)ǵʰmō, *(dʰ)ǵʰmon-, *(h₂)gʷʰemb-, *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *mon-, *ǵʰmṓ

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑ.mɑ.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to play
2. _(reconstruction)_ to have fun
3. _(reconstruction)_ to joke
