# *ganhāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ganh%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ganhāną

### Etymology

Uncertain. The traditional connection to Proto-Indo-European *gʷʰew- (“to revere, look at”) (whence Proto-Slavic *gově̀ti (“to revere, fast”)), implying a reconstructed Germanic form *gawēną, is contradicted by the nasal vowel in the descendant Elfdalian gą̊, and comparisons to Proto-Germanic *gaumaz (“heed, attention”) are similarly phonetically difficult.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷʰew-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɣɑ̃ː.xɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to inspect; to take into account
