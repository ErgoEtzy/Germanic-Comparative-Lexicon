**Reconstruction:** *gadōbiz

### Etymology

From *ga- + *dōbiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʰe, *gʰo-, *ḱóm

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɣɑ.ˈdɔː.βiz/`

### Adjective
