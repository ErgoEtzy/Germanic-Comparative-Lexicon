[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/batiz%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *batizōną

### Etymology

From *batizô (“better”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-yōs

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑ.ti.zɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make better; improve
