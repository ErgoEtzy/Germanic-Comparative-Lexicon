# *bazją

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bazj%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bazją

### Etymology

Unknown. Has been compared to Old Irish basc (“red”) ("berry-colored"), based on a purported connection with Proto-West Germanic *basu (“purple, crimson”), as well as to Latin fascis (“bundle”) and its relatives, though Latin bāca (“berry”) is likely not related. Older theories tentatively connected the word to Proto-Indo-European *bʰeh₂- (“to shine, glisten”) or *bʰes- (“to blow, chew, rub”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/bɑz.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ berry
