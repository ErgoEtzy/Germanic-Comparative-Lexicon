# *blandaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/blandan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blandaną

### Etymology

An o-grade intensive, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰlendʰ- (“to blend”). Related to Old Church Slavonic блѧсти (blęsti, “to chatter, talk nonsense”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰlendʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɑn.dɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to blend, make murky; to mix, mingle
