# *bullô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bull%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bullô

### Etymology

An ablaut variant of *ballô (“ball”), whence also *balluz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbul.lɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ round object; ball; bowl
