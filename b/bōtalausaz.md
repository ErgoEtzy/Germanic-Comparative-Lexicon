# *bōtalausaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/b%C5%8Dtalausaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bōtalausaz

### Etymology

From *bōtō (“recompense, atonement”) + *-lausaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *bʰoHd-eh₂-, *lewh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɔː.tɑ.lɑu̯.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ getting no redress, without reparation
