## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *belganą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰelǵʰ- (“to swell”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰelǵʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbel.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to swell
