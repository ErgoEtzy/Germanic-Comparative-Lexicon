# *babalōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/babal%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *babalōną

### Etymology

From *baba- + *-lōną, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰa-bʰa-, perhaps a reduplication of Proto-Indo-European *bʰā- (“to say”), or a variant of onomatopoeic *baba- (“to talk vaguely, mumble”), or a merger of the two.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰa-bʰa-, *bʰeh₂-, *bʰā-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑ.βɑ.lɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to chatter, babble
