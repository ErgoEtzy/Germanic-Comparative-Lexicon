# *budô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bud%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *budô

### Etymology

Derived from the zero-grade of *beudaną (“to command; to offer”) + *-ô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰewdʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbu.ðɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ messenger
