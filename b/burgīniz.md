## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *burgīniz

### Etymology

From *burgijaną (“to keep safe”) + *-īniz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰergʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbur.ɣiː.niz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a keeping safe, preservation
