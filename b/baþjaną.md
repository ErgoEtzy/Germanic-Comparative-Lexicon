# *baþjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ba%C3%BEjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *baþjaną

### Etymology

From *baþą (“bath”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑθ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to moisturize, to make wet (with warm water)
2. _(reconstruction)_ to boil
