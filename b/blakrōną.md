## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blakrōną

### Etymology

Possibly a direct cognate with Latin flagrō (“I burn”). Equivalent to *blakōną (“to burn, to blacken”) + *-rōną (frequentative suffix).

2. _(reconstruction)_ to burn, to scorch
