# *bukkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bukk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bukkōną

### Etymology

From earlier paradigm *bukkōþi ~ *bugunanþi, from pre-Proto-Germanic *bʰugʰ-nā́-ti ~ *bʰugʰ-un-ánti, iterative to *beuganą (“to bend, bow”). Equivalent to *bugô + *-(u)kōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰewgʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbuk.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to bend
