## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brehaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰreḱ-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰreḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbre.xɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to flicker, glitter
