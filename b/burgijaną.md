# *burgijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/burgijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *burgijaną

### Etymology

Zero-grade ye-present, pre-Germanic *bʰr̥gʰye-, from the root of *berganą (“to preserve”).
Compare typologically Russian храни́ть (xranítʹ) (akin to хорони́ть (xoronítʹ)).

From *burgaz (“promise, debt”) + *-janą, or *burgijô (“surety”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰergʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbur.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to keep safe, hide, bury
