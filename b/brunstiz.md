## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brunstiz

### Etymology

From *brinnaną + *-þiz, with intrusive *-s- after nasals.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tis, *bʰrenw-, *bʰrewh₁-, *bʰrénuh₁e-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrun.stiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ burning, heat
