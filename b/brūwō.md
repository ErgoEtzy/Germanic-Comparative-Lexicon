[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/br%C5%ABw%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brūwō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₃bʰrúHs (“eyebrow, rim, edge”). Cognate with Sanskrit भ्रू (bhrū́, “eyebrow”), Lithuanian brùvē (“brow”), Old Irish brū (“edge, shore”), Russian бровь (brovʹ, “eyebrow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃bʰrúHs

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbruː.wɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ eyebrow
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ bridge
