# *bruhtiwarjaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bruhtiwarjaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bruhtiwarjaz

### Etymology

From *bruhtiz (“bay”) + *warjaz (“dweller”). The first element is likely from *brūkiz + *-þiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tis

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a bay-dweller; a member of the Bructeri
