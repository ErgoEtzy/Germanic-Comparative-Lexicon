## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blaikijaną

### Etymology

From *blīkaną (“to shine”) + *-janą or *blaikaz (“pale”) + *-janą, both from the same root.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *bʰleyǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɑi̯.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to bleach, to make pale, to whiten
