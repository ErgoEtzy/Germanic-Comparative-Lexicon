## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bīdaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰéydʰeti, from *bʰeydʰ- (“to trust”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeydʰ-, *bʰéydʰ-, *bʰéydʰeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbiː.ðɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to wait
2. _(reconstruction, with-genitive)_ to wait for, to await
