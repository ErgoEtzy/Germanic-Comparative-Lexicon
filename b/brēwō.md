## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brēwō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₃bʰrḗwH-o- (“eyebrow”), a lengthened grade formation from *h₃bʰrúHs (“idem”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃bʰrḗwH-o-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrɛː.wɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ eyebrow
