## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brūnaz

### Etymology

Perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *bʰerH- (“grey, brown”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰerH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbruː.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ brown
