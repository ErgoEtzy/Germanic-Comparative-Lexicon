# *balþaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bal%C3%BEaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *balþaz

### Etymology

Appears to be from pre-Germanic *bʰóltos (“inflated, strong”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰel- (in either the “blow, swell, inflate” sense or the “shiny, white” sense, with the former being semantically more attractive) + *-tos, the suffix equivalent to Proto-Germanic *-þaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-tós, *bʰel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑl.θɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ strong, bold
