## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bikjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰeg- (“to smash, break”). Orel's alternate derivation from Proto-Indo-European *bʰegʷ- (“to flee, run”) is less semantically convincing.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeg-, *bʰegʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbik.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to attack, stab, hack, thrust
