# *budilaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/budilaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *budilaz

### Etymology

From *budą + *-ilaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *bʰewdʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbu.di.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ one who carries out an order; beadle; bailiff
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ messenger; herald
