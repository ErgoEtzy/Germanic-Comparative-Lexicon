# *beuganą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/beugan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *beuganą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰewgʰ- (“to bend”). Cognate with Sanskrit भुजति (bhujáti, “to bend, curve”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰewgʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbeu̯.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to bend, bow
