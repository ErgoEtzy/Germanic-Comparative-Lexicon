## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *baitilaz

### Etymology

From *bait- (“to make bite”) + *-ilaz (suffix deriving names of tools). The o-grade might indicate a lost strong verb *baitaną; compare *baitraz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry
