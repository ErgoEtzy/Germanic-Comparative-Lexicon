# *būsniz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/b%C5%ABsniz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *būsniz

### Etymology

From earlier *budsniz, from zero-grade of *beudaną (“to offer, bid”) + *-(s)niz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰewdʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbuːs.niz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ something offered
