## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *būlǭ

### Etymology

Seemingly from a Proto-Indo-European *bʰew- (“to blow; swell”); see *būkaz (“belly”) for more.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰew-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbuː.lɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ swelling; bump; boil
