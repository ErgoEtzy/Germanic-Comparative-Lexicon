## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brutjōną

### Etymology

Likely from *brutjô (“distributor, donor, giver”) + *-ōną. Closely related to the strong verb *breutaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to divide, distribute
