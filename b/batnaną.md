[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/batnan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *batnaną

### Etymology

From *bataz (“good”) + *-naną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-néh₂-, *bʰed-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑt.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to get better
