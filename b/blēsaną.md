# *blēsaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bl%C4%93san%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blēsaną

### Etymology

According to Kroonen, from Pre-Germanic *bʰleh₁s-, sigmatic aorist to *bʰleh₁- (“to blow”). Kölligan, however, alternatively suggests a lexicalized sigmatic desiderative of the shape *bʰleh₁(s)-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰleh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɛː.sɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to blow
