# *blindijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/blindijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blindijaną

### Etymology

From *blindaz (“blind”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(e)-ós, *(ó)-os, *-os, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *-ós, *bʰlendʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblin.di.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to blind, to make blind
