## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brantaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰren- (“to protrude”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰren-

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ tall, high, towering
2. _(reconstruction)_ steep
