# *bōkstabaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/b%C5%8Dkstabaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bōkstabaz

### Etymology

From *bōks (“book”) + *stabaz (“staff, stick”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *stebʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɔːkˌstɑ.βɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a staff made of beechwood
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ letter of the alphabet
