[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/biwindan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *biwindaną

### Etymology

From *bi- + *windaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₁epi, *h₁pi, *wendʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/biˈwin.dɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to wind around or about, enwrap
