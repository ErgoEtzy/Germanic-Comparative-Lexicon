[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/blandijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blandijaną

### Etymology

From *blandaną + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰlendʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɑn.dijɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to blind
