## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bundą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰendʰ- (“to tie; bind”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰendʰ-

### Noun
