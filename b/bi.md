Proto-Germanic *bi
From Pre-Germanic *h₁pi, zero-grade of Proto-Indo-European *h₁epi (“on, at”), with voicing due to Verner's law. Cognate with Ancient Greek ἐπί (epí, “on, at”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁epi, *h₁pi

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/bi/`

### Preposition

1. _(reconstruction)_ by, at
2. _(reconstruction)_ near, around
