# *bakiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bakiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bakiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰog-i-s, from *bʰeg- (“flowing water, brook, stream”). Cognate with Proto-Slavic *bagnò (“swamp, bog, marsh”), as well as perhaps Old Irish búal (“water; bathing, healing, cure”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeg-, *bʰog-i-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑ.kiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ brook, stream
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ beach, strand
