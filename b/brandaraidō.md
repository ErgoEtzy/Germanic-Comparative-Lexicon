[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/brandaraid%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brandaraidō

### Etymology

From *brandaz (“fire; flash; flame”) + *raidō (“ride”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(e)-ós, *(ó)-os, *-os, *-ós, *Hreydʰ-, *bʰrenw-, *bʰrewh₁-, *bʰrénuh₁e-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrɑn.dɑˌrɑi̯.ðɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ grate, brandreth
