# *balwaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/balwaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *balwaz

### Etymology

Uncertain. Possibly related to Old Church Slavonic болѣти (bolěti, “to be sick, be in pain”), Proto-Slavic *bȍlь (“pain”), Cornish bal (“disease”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑl.wɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ sick
2. _(reconstruction)_ bad, evil, wicked
3. _(reconstruction)_ torturous, painful, agonising
