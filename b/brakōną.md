# *brakōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/brak%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brakōną

### Etymology

Either from a denominative formation equivalent to *braką (“burst, crack”) + *-ōną, or from an o-grade iterative formation.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰreg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrɑ.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to burst, to crash
2. _(reconstruction)_ to creak
