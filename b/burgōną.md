# *burgōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/burg%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *burgōną

### Etymology

From *burgaz (“promise, debt”) + *-ōną. *burgaz is from Proto-Indo-European *bʰergʰ- (“to protect”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰergʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbur.ɣɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to borrow
2. _(reconstruction)_ to take care (of), vouch for
