# *blīkaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bl%C4%ABkan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blīkaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰleyǵ- (“to shine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰleyǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbliː.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to gleam; shine
