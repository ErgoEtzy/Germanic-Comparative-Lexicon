# *brutą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/brut%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brutą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰrewd- (“to cut, break”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰrewd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbru.tɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ that which is broken off (e.g. piece, fragment etc.)
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ that which breaks forth (e.g. sprout, bud, etc.)
