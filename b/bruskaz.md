# *bruskaz

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bruskaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰrews- (“to roar, roil, rustle, sprout”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰrews-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrus.kɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ scrub, undergrowth, underbrush
2. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ thicket, copse
3. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ sprout, shoot; twig
