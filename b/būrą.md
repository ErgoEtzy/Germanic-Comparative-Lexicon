## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *būrą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰuH-ro-, a nominal formation from Proto-Indo-European *bʰuH- (“to grow, be”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰuH-, *bʰuH-ro-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ room, chamber
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ dwelling, residence
