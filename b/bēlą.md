# *bēlą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/b%C4%93l%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bēlą

### Etymology

Probably from a Proto-Indo-European *bʰḗlH-o-m, from *bʰel- (“shiny; bright; gleaming; white”). Cognate with Proto-Slavic *bělъ (“white”), Lithuanian bãlas, báltas (“white”), Latvian balts (“white”), Ancient Greek φαλός (phalós, “white”), Sanskrit भाल (bhāla, “splendour”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰḗlH-o-m

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɛː.lɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ fire
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ pyre
