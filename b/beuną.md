[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/beun%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *beuną

### Etymology

The present tense is from Proto-Indo-European *bʰuH-yé-ti, zero-grade yé-present of *bʰuH- (“to grow, become, come into being, appear”), although this is uncertain.
For the past tense see *wesaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰuH-, *bʰuH-yé-ti, *bʰuHyéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbeu̯.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be
2. _(reconstruction)_ to become
