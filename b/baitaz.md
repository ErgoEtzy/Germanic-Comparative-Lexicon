# *baitaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/baitaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *baitaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰóyd-o-s, from *bʰeyd- (“to break, split”) + *-os. The word originally referred to a small nautical vessel made of a tree, hollowed (i.e. split) out like a trough.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-os, *bʰeyd-, *bʰóyd-o-s, *bʰóydos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑi̯.tɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ boat, ship
