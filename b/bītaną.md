[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/b%C4%ABtan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bītaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰéydt, from *bʰeyd- (“to split”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeyd-, *bʰéydt

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbiː.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to bite
