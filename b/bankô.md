# *bankô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bank%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bankô

### Etymology

Related to *bankiz (“slope, hill, shelf”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑŋ.kɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bank, embankment
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ hill
