## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brestuz

### Etymology

A nominal formation from *brestaną (“to burst, break”).

### Pronunciation

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ break; fracture
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ impairment; handicap
