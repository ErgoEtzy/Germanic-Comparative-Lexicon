# *balwōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/balw%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *balwōną

### Etymology

Either derived from *balwą (“evil, misfortune”) + *-ōną or *balwaz (“evil, bad”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑl.wɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make worse, spoil, damage
2. _(reconstruction)_ to torture, torment
3. _(reconstruction)_ to speak evil, swear, curse
