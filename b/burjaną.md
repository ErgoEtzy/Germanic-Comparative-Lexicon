# *burjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/burjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *burjaną

### Etymology

Zero-grade ye-present, pre-Germanic *bʰr̥ye/o-, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰer- (“to bear, carry”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbur.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to lift up; levy
2. _(reconstruction)_ to be seemly; be fitting
