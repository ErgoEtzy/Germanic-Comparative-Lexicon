# *bēaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/b%C4%93an%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bēaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰeh₁- (“to heat up; bathe”). The LIV suggests that Old High German bāen may derive from Proto-Indo-European *bʰéh₁-ye-ti, though it also considers the existence of the underlying root to be uncertain.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeh₁-, *bʰéh₁-ye-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɛː.ɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to warm
2. _(reconstruction)_ to foment
3. _(reconstruction)_ to bathe
