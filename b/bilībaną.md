# *bilībaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bil%C4%ABban%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bilībaną

### Etymology

From *bi- + *lībaną, the latter from Proto-Indo-European *leyp-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leyp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌbi.ˈliː.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stay, to remain in place
