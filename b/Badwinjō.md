[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/Badwinj%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Badwinjō

### Etymology

From *badwō (“battle”) + *-injō (“feminine agent suffix”). The second element is a variant of *-inī.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Proper noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ the goddess Baduhenna
