# *baugijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/baugijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *baugijaną

### Etymology

From *beuganą (“to bow, bend”) + *-janą (“causative suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰewgʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑu̯.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to bend, bow
