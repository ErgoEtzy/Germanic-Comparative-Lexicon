## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *braką

### Etymology

From *brekaną (“to break”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrɑ.kɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ a creaking noise
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ a breaking, fracture
