[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/braud%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *braudą

### Etymology

Disputed. Probably a conflation of Proto-Indo-European *bʰerw-, *bʰrewh₁- (“to boil, seethe”) (compare brew, broth) and Proto-Indo-European *bʰerH- (“to pierce; to strike”). According to Kroonen, however, derived from *brewwaną (“to brew”) directly, with a pre-form *bʰrowh₁-tó-s.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰerH-, *bʰerw-, *bʰrewh₁-, *bʰrewh₁-

### Pronunciation
