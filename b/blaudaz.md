## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blaudaz

### Etymology

Unknown, but probably related to *blautaz (“wet, weak”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɑu̯.ðɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(derogatory, offensive, reconstruction)_ meek, soft, cowardly
