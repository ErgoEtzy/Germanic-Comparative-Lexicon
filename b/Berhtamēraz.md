## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Berhtamēraz

### Etymology

From *berhtaz (“bright, shining”) + *mēraz (“great, famous”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃reǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈberx.tɑ.ˌmɛː.rɑz/`

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
