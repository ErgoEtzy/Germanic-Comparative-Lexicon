# *blinkaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/blinkan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blinkaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *bʰli-n-ǵ-, a nasal-infix present formed from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleyǵ- (“to shine”), whence also Proto-Germanic *blīkaną (“to gleam, shine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰleyǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbliŋ.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shine, glitter, twinkle
2. _(reconstruction)_ to blink, wink
