[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bl%C4%93dr%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blēdrǭ

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *bʰleh₁tréh₂(n) or *bʰleh₁dʰreh₂(n), instrumental formation to Proto-Indo-European *bʰleh₁- (“to blow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰleh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɛːð.rɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ bubble, blister, something inflated
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ bladder (organ)
