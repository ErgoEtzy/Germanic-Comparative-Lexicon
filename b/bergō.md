# *bergō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/berg%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bergō

### Etymology

From *berganą (“to preserve, to keep”) + *-ō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(o)-éh₂, *-eh₂, *-oh₁, *-éh₂, *bʰergʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈber.ɣɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ help
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ protection, shelter, defense
