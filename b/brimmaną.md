# *brimmaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/brimman%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brimmaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰrem- (“to make noise”). This verb is closely related to *bremaną, the geminate 'm' in the root seems to have arisen due to the influence of the iterative verb *brummōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰrem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrim.mɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to drone, to hum
2. _(reconstruction)_ to roar
