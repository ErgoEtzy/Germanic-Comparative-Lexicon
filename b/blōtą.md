[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bl%C5%8Dt%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blōtą

### Etymology

Traditionally from Proto-Indo-European *bʰlā-d- (“to offer, sacrifice”), comparing Latin flāmen (“priest”), but this root is largely abandoned by modern scholars. Possibly from the same root as *blōþą (“blood”); compare the development of Proto-West Germanic *blōdisōn (“to mark with blood > to bless”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰlā-d-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɔː.tɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ offering, sacrifice
