# *bōsmaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/b%C5%8Dsmaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bōsmaz

### Etymology

Exact origin unclear. Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *bʰōw- (“to inflate, swell”); or from earlier *bōhsmaz ("the space between the arms"; compare *faþmaz), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰāǵʰus (“arm”), whence *bōguz (“upper arm, shoulder”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰāǵʰus, *bʰōw-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɔːs.mɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bosom
