## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bulstraz

### Etymology

From earlier *bulhstraz, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰelǵʰ- (“bag, pillow, paunch”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰel- (“to swell, blow, inflate, burst”). See also *balgiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰel-, *bʰelǵʰ-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bolster, pillow, cushion
