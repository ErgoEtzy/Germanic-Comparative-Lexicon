# *bitōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bit%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bitōną

### Etymology

From *bitô (“piece, bit, chunk”) + *-ōną, or of iterative origin (see *bītaną).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbi.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cut into bits
2. _(reconstruction)_ to crunch, gnash
