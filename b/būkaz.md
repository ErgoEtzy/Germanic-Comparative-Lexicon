# *būkaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/b%C5%ABkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *būkaz

### Etymology

Traditionally derived from Proto-Indo-European *bʰōw-go-s, from a root *bʰew- (“to swell, inflate”); however, the existence of this root is in doubt, and it is likely that most, if not all, of its supposed descendants are independent onomatopoeic and sound-symbolic formations, which Orel appears to implicitly espouse.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰōw-go-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbuː.kɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ belly, abdomen
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ body
