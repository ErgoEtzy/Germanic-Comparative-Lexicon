[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bak%C4%81rijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bakārijaz

### Etymology

From *baką (“baking”) + *-ārijaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *bʰeg-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ one who creates baked goods; baker
