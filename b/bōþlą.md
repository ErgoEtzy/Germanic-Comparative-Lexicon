# *bōþlą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/b%C5%8D%C3%BEl%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bōþlą

### Etymology

Possibly from *bōwwjaną (“to settle, inhabit”) + *-þlą (instrumental suffix), or perhaps from *bōþō (“building, dwelling”) + *-(i)lą, or from Proto-Indo-European *bʰoh₂u-tlo-m (compare Lithuanian būklà (“presence (of mind), dwelling”)), all possibly from *bʰweh₂- (“to grow, arise, become”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰoh₂u-tlo-m

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɔːθ.lɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ house, dwelling
