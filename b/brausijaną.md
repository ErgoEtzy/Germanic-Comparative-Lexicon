## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brausijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰrews- (“to break”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰrews-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrɑu̯.si.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to break; crush
