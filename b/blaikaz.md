## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blaikaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *bʰloyǵ-os, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleyǵ- (“to shine”). By synchronic analysis, an o-grade adjective formed from *blīkaną (“to gleam, shine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰleyǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɑi̯.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ pale, white
