# *brūkaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/br%C5%ABkan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brūkaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰrewHg- (“to make use of”). Cognate with Latin fruor (“enjoy”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰrewHg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbruː.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make use of, employ, use, consume
