[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bal%C3%BEijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *balþijaną

### Etymology

From *balþaz (“bold”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑl.θi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make bold; animate; encourage
