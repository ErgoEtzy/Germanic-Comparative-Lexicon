[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bl%C5%8Dan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blōaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰleh₃- (“to bloom, to flower”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰleh₃-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɔː.ɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to bloom, to flower
