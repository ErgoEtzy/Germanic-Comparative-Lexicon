**Reconstruction:** *birkīnaz

### Etymology

From *berkō + *-īnaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *bʰerHǵós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbir.kiː.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ birchen, made of birch
