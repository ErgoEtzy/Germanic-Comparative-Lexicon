## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bitraz

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *bʰidrós (“biting, splitting”), equivalent to *bītaną (“to bite”) + *-raz (“adjectival suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeyd-

### Pronunciation
