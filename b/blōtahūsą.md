[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bl%C5%8Dtah%C5%ABs%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blōtahūsą

### Etymology

From *blōtą (“act of worship, sacrifice”) + *hūsą (“house”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)kewH-, *(s)kuH-s-o-m, *bʰlā-d-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbloː.tɑ.xuː.sɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ temple, house of worship
