# *budōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bud%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *budōną

### Etymology

From *budą (“message”) + *-ōną or *budô (“messenger”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰewdʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbu.ðɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to proclaim, announce, lere, instruct
2. _(reconstruction)_ to summon, call
