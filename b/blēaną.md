# *blēaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bl%C4%93an%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blēaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰleh₁- (“to blow”). Cognate with Latin flō (“to breathe, blow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰleh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɛː.ɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to blow
