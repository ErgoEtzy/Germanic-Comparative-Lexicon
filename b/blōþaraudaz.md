## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blōþaraudaz

### Etymology

From *blōþą + *raudaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *bʰleh₃-, *bʰleh₃-tó-m, *h₁rowdʰós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɔː.θɑ.ˌrɑu̯.ðɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ blood-red
