# *bidjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bidjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bidjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *gʷʰédʰyeti, from *gʷʰedʰ- (“to ask for; beseech”), or alternatively from *bʰedʰ- (“to bend, bow”) (compare the root of *bindaną). Not, as sometimes suggested, related to *bīdaną (“to wait”). More at *bedō (“a request, prayer”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰedʰ-, *gʷʰedʰ-, *gʷʰédʰyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbið.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make a request (of/for);
2. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to ask (of person)
3. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to request, ask (for something)
