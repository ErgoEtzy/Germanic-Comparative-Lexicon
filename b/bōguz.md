# *bōguz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/b%C5%8Dguz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bōguz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰeh₂ǵʰús (“arm”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeh₂ǵʰús

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɔː.ɣuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ upper arm
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ shoulder
