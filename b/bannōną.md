# *bannōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bann%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bannōną

### Etymology

From *banną (“order, ban”) + *-ōną, closely related to the strong verb *bannaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈˈbɑn.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to command, to order
2. _(reconstruction)_ to summon
3. _(reconstruction)_ to forbid
