# *bisitjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bisitjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bisitjaną

### Etymology

From *bi- + *sitjaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₁epi, *h₁pi, *sed-, *sédyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/bi.ˈsit.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sit among, sit near
