# *braidaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/braidaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *braidaz

### Etymology

Of uncertain origin. Perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *bʰr-oy-tos, a participle from an i-present of *bʰer- (“to bear, carry”); compare Lithuanian ber̃ti (“to strew, scatter”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰer-, *bʰr-oy-tos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrɑi̯.ðɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ broad, wide
