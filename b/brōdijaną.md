## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brōdijaną

### Etymology

From *brōduz (“warmth, breed”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ To keep warm; brood
2. _(reconstruction)_ To breed
