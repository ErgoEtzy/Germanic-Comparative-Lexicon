## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blauþį̄

### Etymology

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-i-h₂, *-ih₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɑu̯.θĩː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ cowardice
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ apathy
