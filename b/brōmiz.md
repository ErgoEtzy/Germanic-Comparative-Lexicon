# *brōmiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/br%C5%8Dmiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brōmiz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of otherwise unattested *bramaną, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰrem- (“to make noise”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰrem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrɔː.miz/`

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ famous
