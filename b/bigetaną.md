## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bigetaną

### Etymology

From *bi- + *getaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰed-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌbi.ˈɣe.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to get anew, to beget
2. _(reconstruction)_ to reach, to find, to discover
