# *bibāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bib%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bibāną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰe-bʰóyh₂-e ~ *bʰe-bʰih₂-ḗr, reduplicated stative of *bʰeyh₂- (“to fear, to be afraid”). Cognate with Proto-Balto-Slavic *bajā́ˀtei (“to fear, be afraid”), Sanskrit बिभाय (bibhā́ya, “to fear”), Avestan 𐬠𐬌𐬡𐬌𐬎𐬎𐬁𐬵 (biβiuuāh, “to be afraid”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰe-bʰóyh₂-e ~ *bʰe-bʰih₂-ḗr

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbi.βɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to tremble, quake, shiver
