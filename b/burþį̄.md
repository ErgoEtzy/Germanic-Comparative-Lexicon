## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *burþį̄

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰer- (“to carry, bear”). Direct cognate with Sanskrit भृति (bhṛti, “bearing; support”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbur.θĩː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ burden
