## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blōþarinnandz

### Etymology

From *blōþą (“blood”) + *rinnaną + *-andz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *bʰleh₃-, *bʰleh₃-tó-m, *h₃er-, *h₃reyH-, *h₃r̥-néw-ti

### Pronunciation

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ bleeding
2. _(reconstruction)_ having an issue of blood, hemorrhaging
