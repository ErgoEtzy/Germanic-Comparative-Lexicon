[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bukkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bukkaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰuǵ-ko-s, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰuǵ- (“goat, buck, ram”). Cognate with Proto-Celtic *bukkos (“goat”), Old Armenian բուծ (buc, “lamb”), Persian بز (boz, “goat”), Avestan 𐬠𐬏𐬰𐬀 (būza, “ram”), Sanskrit बुख (bukha, “male goat”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰuǵ-, *bʰuǵ-ko-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbuk.kɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ buck
