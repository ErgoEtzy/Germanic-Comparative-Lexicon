## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bruþą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰru(h₁)-to-m, from *bʰrewh₁- (“to seethe; roil; brew”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰrewh₁-, *bʰru(h₁)-to-m

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbru.θɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ broth
