## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blōþagaz

### Etymology

From *blōþą (“blood”) + *-gaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-kos, *bʰleh₃-, *bʰleh₃-tó-m

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɔː.θɑ.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ bloody
