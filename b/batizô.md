## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *batizô

### Etymology

*batiz + *-izô

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-yōs

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑ.ti.zɔːː/`

### Adjective

1. _(comparative, form-of, reconstruction)_ comparative degree of *gōdaz
2. _(comparative, form-of, reconstruction)_ comparative degree of *bataz
