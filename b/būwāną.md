# *būwāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/b%C5%ABw%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *būwāną

### Etymology

Related to *būaną (“to dwell”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰuH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbuː.wɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to settle, to inhabit
2. _(reconstruction)_ to build
