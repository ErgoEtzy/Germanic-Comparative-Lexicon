## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bēganą

### Etymology

Uncertain; perhaps, related to Old Irish bág (“war”) and Old Irish bágaid (“to battle”), which has been proposed to stem from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeh₁ǵʰ- (“to dispute, quarrel”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to fight, argue, quarrel
