# *brigdilaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/brigdilaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brigdilaz

### Etymology

From *bregdaną (“to tug, move, swing”) + *-ilaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *bʰrēḱ-, *mrekʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbriɣ.ði.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ strap, rein
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bridle
