# *bistalkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bistalk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bistalkōną

### Etymology

From *bi- + *stalukōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/bi.ˈstal.kɔ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stalk at (someone)
2. _(reconstruction)_ to move stealthily
