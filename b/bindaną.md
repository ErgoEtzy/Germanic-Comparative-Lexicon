## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bindaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰéndʰ-e-ti, from *bʰendʰ- (“to tie, bind”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰendʰ-, *bʰéndʰ-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbin.dɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to bind, to tie
