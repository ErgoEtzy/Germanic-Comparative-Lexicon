## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blankaz

### Etymology

A late formation from *blinkaną (“to shine, blink”), with the *-i- interpreted as an e-grade vowel. Thus, its formation must have followed the raising of *e to *i before a nasal + consonant, which made this analysis possible. Doublet of *blaikaz.
Alternatively, from a Pre-Germanic *bʰlongós, a nasal-infixed from of *blakaz (see there for more).
