## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bandiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰendʰ- (“to tie; bond, band”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰendʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑn.diz/`

### Noun
