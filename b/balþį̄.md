## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *balþį̄

### Etymology

From *balþaz (“bold”) + *-į̄.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑl.θĩː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ boldness, strength, courage
