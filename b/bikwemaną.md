## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bikwemaną

### Etymology

From *bi- + *kwemaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌbi.ˈkʷe.mɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to come near, come around, come by, arrive
2. _(reconstruction)_ to come about, happen, befall
3. _(reconstruction)_ to come across, come by, chance upon, find, obtain
