# *bansaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bansaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bansaz

### Etymology

From earlier *bandstaz, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰendʰ- (“to tie; bind”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰendʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑn.sɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ crib; cradle
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ stall (for animals)
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ barn
