# *bannaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bannan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bannaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *bʰh₂-nw-, a zero grade nu-present form of Proto-Indo-European *bʰeh₂- (“to speak”), similar in form to Vedic Sanskrit भनति (bhánati, “idem”). The cluster -nw- seems to have caused gemination; see *kinnuz, *þunnuz for the same development.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑn.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to proclaim, to order
2. _(reconstruction)_ to summon
3. _(reconstruction)_ to ban
