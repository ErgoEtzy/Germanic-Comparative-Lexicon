## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *barwijǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰer- (“to carry”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰer-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ bier; stretcher
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ barrow
