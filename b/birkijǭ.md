**Reconstruction:** *birkijǭ

### Etymology

From *berkō + *-jǭ.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *bʰerHǵós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbir.ki.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ birch
