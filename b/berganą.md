# *berganą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bergan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *berganą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰergʰ- (“to take care of, protect, preserve”). Cognate with Proto-Balto-Slavic *bergtei (“to take care of”), Old Irish commairge (“protection, security”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰergʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈber.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to take care of
2. _(reconstruction)_ to preserve, to keep
3. _(reconstruction)_ to protect, to save
