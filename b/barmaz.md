# *barmaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/barmaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *barmaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰór-mo-s, from *bʰer- (“to bear”) + *-mos. Cognate with Ancient Greek φορμός (phormós, “basket for carrying”).

Origin uncertain. Perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *bʰern- (“to project”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-mos, *bʰer-, *bʰern-, *bʰór-mo-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑr.mɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ lap
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bosom
