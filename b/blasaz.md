# *blasaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/blasaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blasaz

### Etymology

From some derivative of Proto-Indo-European *bʰel- (“shiny, white”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɑ.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ white, pale (of animals)
