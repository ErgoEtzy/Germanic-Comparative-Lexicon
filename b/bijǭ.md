# *bijǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bij%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bijǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰey- (“bee”). Cognate with Latin fūcus (“drone”), Old Irish bech (“bee”), Lithuanian bitė (“bee”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbi.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ bee
