# *brēdaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/br%C4%93dan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brēdaną

### Etymology

Related to *brējaną (“to roast, smell”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrɛː.ðɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to warm, heat, broil
2. _(reconstruction)_ to roast (meat)
