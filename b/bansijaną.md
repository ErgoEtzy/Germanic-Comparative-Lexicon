# *bansijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bansijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bansijaną

### Etymology

From *bansaz (“stall”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰendʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑn.si.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to put (an animal) in a stall
