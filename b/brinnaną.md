## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brinnaną

### Etymology

Kroonen reconstructs Pre-Germanic *bʰrénuh₁e-, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰrewh₁- (“to boil, brew”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰrenw-, *bʰrewh₁-, *bʰrénuh₁e-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrin.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to burn, to be on fire
