## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brewwaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰrewh₁-, related to Ancient Greek ἀπέφρυσεν (apéphrusen, “brewed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰrewh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrew.wɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to brew
