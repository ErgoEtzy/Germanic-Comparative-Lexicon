# *brunnô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/brunn%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brunnô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰréh₁wr̥ (“a bubbling forth; a fountain, wellspring, source”), from the root *bʰrew- (“to well up, bubble, seethe”), perhaps from *bʰer- (“to well up”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰréh₁wr̥

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrun.nɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ source; wellspring; fountain
