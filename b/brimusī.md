# *brimusī

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/brimus%C4%AB)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brimusī

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰerem- (“to buzz”), from *bʰer- (“to hum, buzz”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰerem-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ gadfly, horsefly
