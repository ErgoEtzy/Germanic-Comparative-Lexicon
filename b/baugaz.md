# *baugaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/baugaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *baugaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰowgʰ-o-s, from *bʰewgʰ- (“to bend”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰowgʰ-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑu̯.ɣɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a bend; bow
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ something bent (round); circle
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a ring; bracelet
4. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a wreath; crown
5. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ collar
