## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bōtō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰoHd-eh₂-, from *bʰed-, *bʰeHd- (“to improve”). Synchronically analyzed as an ō-stem nominal formation from the root *bat underlying *batiz (“better”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰoHd-eh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɔː.tɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ improvement, betterment
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ recompense, atonement
