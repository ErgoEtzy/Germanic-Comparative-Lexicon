## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *burþiz

### Etymology

From the oblique stem of Proto-Indo-European *bʰértis, from *bʰer- (“to bear”). Equivalent to *beraną + *-þiz. Compare Sanskrit भृति (bhṛti, “bearing”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰer-, *bʰértis

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbur.θiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ bearing, carrying
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ pregnancy
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ birth
