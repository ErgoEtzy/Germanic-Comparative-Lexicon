## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *baþą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰh₁-tó-s, a zero-grade nominal formation from *bʰeh₁- (“to foment, warm”). Though Kroonen considers the word to have no cognates outside Germanic, Acharian has deduced Old Armenian բով (bov, “furnace”) as a cognate.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeh₁-, *bʰh₁-tó-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑ.θɑ̃/`
