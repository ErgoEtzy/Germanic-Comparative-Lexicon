## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bōniz

### Etymology

Either from *bōjaną + *-niz, or inherited from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeh₂-ni-s, of the same root, from *bʰeh₂- (“to say, speak”) + *-nis. Cognate with Old Armenian բան (ban, “word, speech”), Ancient Greek φωνή (phōnḗ, “sound, tone”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-nis, *bʰeh₂-, *bʰeh₂-ni-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɔː.niz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ supplication, prayer
