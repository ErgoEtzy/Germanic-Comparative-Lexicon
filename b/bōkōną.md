# *bōkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/b%C5%8Dk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bōkōną

### Etymology

From *bōks (“letter, written message”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *bʰeh₂g-, *bʰeh₂ǵos

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to book (i.e. make a book, put in a book, write a book, etc.)
