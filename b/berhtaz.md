## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *berhtaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰer(H)ǵ-tó-s, from *bʰerHǵ- (“to shine”) + *-tós. Cognate with Proto-Celtic *berxtos, Proto-Albanian *bardza (“white”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-tós, *bʰer(H)ǵ-tó-s, *bʰerHǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈberx.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ bright, shining
