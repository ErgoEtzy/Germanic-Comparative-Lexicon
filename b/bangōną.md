## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bangōną

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *bʰen- (“to hit, strike, injure”) or *bʰe(n)g- (“to smash, break”); ultimately onomatopoeic.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeg-, *bʰen-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to bang; pound; beat
