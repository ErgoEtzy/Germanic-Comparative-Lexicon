## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bernuz

### Etymology

Reanalyzed as a u-stem from *bernunz, accusative plural of *berô (“bear”). Also note *barō (“man”), *barn (“child”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈber.nuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bear
