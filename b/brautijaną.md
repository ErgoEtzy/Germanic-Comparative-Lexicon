# *brautijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/brautijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brautijaną

### Etymology

From *breutaną (“to destroy, crush, break”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *bʰrewd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrɑu̯.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to break
2. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to make changes, alter
