## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *baką

### Etymology

Of uncertain origin. Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeg- (“to vault; arch”), via an extension *bʰóg-o-m (see *-os).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑ.kɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ back, rear
