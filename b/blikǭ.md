## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blikǭ

### Etymology

A nominal formation related to *bliką (“shine, look, metal”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰleyǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbli.kɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ shine
