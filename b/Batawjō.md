## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Batawjō

### Etymology

From *bataz (“good”) + *awjō (“island”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *bʰed-, *h₂ékʷeh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑt.ˌɑu̯.jɔː/`

### Proper noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ Batavia, land of the Batavians
