# *būaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/b%C5%ABan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *būaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰúHt, from *bʰuH- (“to be, grow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰuH-, *bʰúHt

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbu.wɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to dwell, reside
