# *buhtiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/buhtiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *buhtiz

### Etymology

From *beuganą (“to bend, bow”) + *-þiz (“nominal suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰewgʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbux.tiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ bend, curve, arch, angle; corner
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a bend in a body of water, bay
