[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/Brunj%C5%8Dhildiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Brunjōhildiz

### Etymology

From *brunjǭ (“breastplate”) + *hildiz (“battle, fight”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *kelh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrun.jɔː.ˌxil.diz/`

### Proper noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a female given name
