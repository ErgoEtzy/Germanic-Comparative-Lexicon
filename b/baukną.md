[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/baukn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *baukną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰeh₂- (“to glow, light, shine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑu̯k.nɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ sign, symbol
