# *bilaibijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bilaibijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bilaibijaną

### Etymology

From *bilībaną + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leyp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌbi.ˈlɑi̯.βi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to stay, to leave behind
