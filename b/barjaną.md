# *barjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/barjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *barjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰerH- (“to pierce, strike”). Cognate with Latin ferīre (“to beat, strike”), Proto-Slavic *borniti (“to defend”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰerH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑr.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to beat, strike
