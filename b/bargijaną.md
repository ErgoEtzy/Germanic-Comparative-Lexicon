# *bargijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bargijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bargijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰergʷ-, *bʰergʷʰ- (“to feed”). Cognate with Ancient Greek φέρβω (phérbō, “to feed, nourish, graze”), Sanskrit भर्वति (bhárvati, “he chews, eats, devours”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰergʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑr.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to taste
