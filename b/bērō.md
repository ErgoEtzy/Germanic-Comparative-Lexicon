## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bērō

### Etymology

*bēriz + *-ō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɛː.rɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ that which bears or carries; stretcher, bier
