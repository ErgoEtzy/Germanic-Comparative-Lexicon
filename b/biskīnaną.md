## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *biskīnaną

### Etymology

From *bi- + *skīnaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)ḱeh₁y-, *h₁epi, *h₁pi

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/bi.ˈskiː.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shine upon or about
