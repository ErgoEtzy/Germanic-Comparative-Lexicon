# *buljaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/buljan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *buljaną

### Etymology

A zero-grade ye-present from the root of *bellaną (“to roar, ring”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbul.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to roar, bark
