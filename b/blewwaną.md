[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/blewwan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blewwaną

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *mléw-e-, from Proto-Indo-European *mlew-. Related to Ancient Greek ἀμβλύς (amblús, “blunt; dim, faint”), Avestan 𐬨𐬭𐬎𐬙𐬀 (mruta, “crushed (?), weak”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *mlew-, *mléw-e-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblew.wɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to beat
