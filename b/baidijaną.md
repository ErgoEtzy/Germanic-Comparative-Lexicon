# *baidijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/baidijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *baidijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰoydʰéyeti (“to make trust”). By surface analysis, *bīdaną (“to wait”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeydʰ-, *bʰoydʰéyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑi̯.ði.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to force, compel, demand
2. _(reconstruction)_ to constrain, cause to stay
