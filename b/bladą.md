# *bladą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/blad%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bladą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰl̥h₃-tó-m, from *bʰleh₃- (“bloom, flower”); compare Tocharian A pält (“leaf, blade”). Synchronically analyzed as a nominal derivative of *blōaną (“to bloom”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰleh₃-, *bʰl̥h₃-tó-m

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɑ.ðɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ leaf (of a plant)
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ sheet
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ blade (flat surface of a tool or weapon)
