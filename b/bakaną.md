## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bakaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *bʰh₃g-, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeh₃g- (“to roast, bake”). Related to Ancient Greek φώγω (phṓgō, “to roast”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeh₃g-
