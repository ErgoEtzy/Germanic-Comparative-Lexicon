[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bur%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *burô

### Etymology

Derived from the zero-grade of *beraną + *-ô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbu.rɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bearer; one who carries or brings
