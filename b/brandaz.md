# *brandaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/brandaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brandaz

### Etymology

From *brinnaną (“to burn”) + *-az (“-er”, agent noun suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(e)-ós, *(ó)-os, *-os, *-ós, *bʰrenw-, *bʰrewh₁-, *bʰrénuh₁e-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbrɑn.dɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ fire; flame
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ flash
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ burning, flaming
4. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ firebrand, torch
5. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ sword
