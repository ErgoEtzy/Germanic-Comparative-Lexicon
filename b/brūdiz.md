[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/br%C5%ABdiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *brūdiz

### Etymology

Unknown, apparently from a pre-Germanic *bʰruH- with no known cognates outside Germanic. Perhaps a borrowing from a substrate word *brūd(i). Kroonen notes the similar suffix in *magaþs/*magaþiz (“girl”), another word of unknown, probable substrate origin.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbruː.ðiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ bride
