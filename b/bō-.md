## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *bō-

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bʰā-, *bʰāt- (“father, (elder) brother”), which appears to be a papa-type nursery word. Cognate with Lithuanian batis (“father, dad, friend”), Proto-Slavic *batę (“older male relative or brother, dad”) (whence Russian ба́тя (bátja, “dad”)).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry
