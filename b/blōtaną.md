# *blōtaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/bl%C5%8Dtan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *blōtaną

### Etymology

Derived from *blōt- (“to sacrifice”) + *-aną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-onom, *bʰlā-d-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈblɔː.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to worship
2. _(reconstruction, with-instrumental)_ to sacrifice
