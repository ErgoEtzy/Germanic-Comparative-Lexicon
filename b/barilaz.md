# *barilaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/barilaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *barilaz

### Etymology

From *beraną (“to carry, transport”) + *-ilaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈbɑ.ri.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ vessel for transport, container, jug; basket
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ carrying equipment; stretcher
