# *nēhwaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/n%C4%93hwaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nēhwaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *h₂nḗḱwos, a lengthened-grade adjective derived from Proto-Indo-European *h₂neḱ- (“to reach”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂neḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɛː.xʷɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ nigh, near, close
