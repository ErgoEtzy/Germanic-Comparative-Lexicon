## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nakwô

### Etymology

Perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *néh₂us (“boat”); a possible explanation of the kʷ is Cowgill's Law.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *néh₂us

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɑ.kʷɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ boat, ship
