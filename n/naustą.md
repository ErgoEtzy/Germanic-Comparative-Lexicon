# *naustą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/naust%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *naustą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *neh₂u- (“sea-boat, ship”) + *sth₂om, from *steh₂- (“to stand”). More at *nōaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *neh₂u-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɑu̯s.tɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, neuter, reconstruction)_ a ship-shed, boathouse
