# *nazjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/nazjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nazjaną

### Etymology

From *nesaną (“to survive, recover, heal”) + *-janą (“causative suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *nes-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɑz.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to save; rescue; heal
