# *ninþaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/nin%C3%BEan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ninþaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *nent-, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *nant- (“to fight, dare”). Related to Old Irish néit (“battle, combat”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *nant-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnin.θɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to dare, venture
