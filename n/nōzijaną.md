# *nōzijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/n%C5%8Dzijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nōzijaną

### Etymology

Secondary causative of a lost strong verb *nasaną, from the same root as *nesaną and *nazjaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *nes-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɔː.zi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to refresh, relieve
2. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to light or feed (a fire)
3. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to keep or breed (animals)
