## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nazīniz

### Etymology

From *nazjaną (“to save, heal”) + *-īniz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *nes-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɑ.ziː.niz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a healing, salvation, rescue
