## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *newun tigiwiz

### Etymology

Either formed analogously from the lower decads, compare *þrīz tigiwiz (“thirty”), from *newun (“nine”) + *tigiwiz (“decads, groups of ten”), displacing Proto-Germanic *newuntēhundą (“ninety”); or inherited alongside it, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁newn̥-deḱḿ̥ (“ninety”), from *h₁néwn̥ (“nine”) + *déḱm̥ (“ten”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry
