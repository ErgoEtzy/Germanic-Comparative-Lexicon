# *nakwōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/nakw%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nakwōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *negʷ- (“naked”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *negʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɑ.kʷɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make naked, bare, expose (oneself)
