[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/naz%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nazō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *nes- (“to return; recover; make whole”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *nes-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɑ.zɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ deliverance; rescue
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ assistance; aid; support
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ food; sustenance
