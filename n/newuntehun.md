## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *newuntehun

### Etymology

Compound of *newun (“nine”) + *tehun (“ten”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈne.wun.te.xun/`

### Numeral

1. _(reconstruction)_ nineteen
