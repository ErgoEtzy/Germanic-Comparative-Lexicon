# *niftiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/niftiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *niftiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *néptih₂ (“grandchild; sister's son”). Cognate with Latin neptis (“granddaughter, niece”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *néptih₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈniɸ.tiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a female descendant or relative
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ granddaughter
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ niece
