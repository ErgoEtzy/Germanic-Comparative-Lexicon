# *nēhwagabūrô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/n%C4%93hwagab%C5%ABr%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nēhwagabūrô

### Etymology

From *nēhwaz (“nigh, close by”) + *gabūrô (“dweller”) (see *būraz).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰuH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɛː.xʷɑ.ɣɑ.ˌbuː.rɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ neighbour
