# *nēdiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/n%C4%93diz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nēdiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)neh₁- (“to spin; sew”). Cognate with Ancient Greek νέσις (nésis, “spinning”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)neh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɛː.ðiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ that which is sewn; seam; stitch
