## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *niwjaz

### Etymology

From earlier *newjaz, from Proto-Indo-European *néwyos (“new”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *néwos, *néwyos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈniu̯.jɑz/`

### Adjective
