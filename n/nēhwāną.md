# *nēhwāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/n%C4%93hw%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nēhwāną

### Etymology

From *nēhwaz (“near, close”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti, *h₂neḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɛː.xʷɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to come closer, to approach
