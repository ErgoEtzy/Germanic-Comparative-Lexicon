## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nu

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *nu (“now”). Cognate with Lithuanian nù (“now, well now”), Lithuanian nūnái (“now, today, nowadays”), Latin num (“now”), Latin nunc (“now, soon, today, nowadays”), Sanskrit नु (nu), नू (nū, “now”), Old Irish no-, Russian ны́не (nýne).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *nu

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/nu/`

### Adverb

1. _(reconstruction)_ now
