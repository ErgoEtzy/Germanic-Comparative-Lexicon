## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nōtō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *nōd- (“knot, net”). Cognate with Latin nōdus (“node, knot, tie, band”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *nōd-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ net, seine
