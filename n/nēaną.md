[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/n%C4%93an%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nēaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)neh₁- (“to spin thread, sew”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)neh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɛː.ɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sew
