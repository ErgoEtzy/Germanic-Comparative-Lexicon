# *nidwō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/nidw%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nidwō

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *nitwéh₂, derived from Proto-Indo-European *ní (“down”) + *-þwō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ní

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnið.wɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a sinking, going down, downfall
