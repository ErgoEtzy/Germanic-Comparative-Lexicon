## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nainaz

### Etymology

Etymology tree
Proto-Indo-European *né
Proto-Germanic *ne
Proto-Indo-European *ís?

From *ne + *ainaz. Compare Old Latin noenum, Old Prussian niains.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁óynos, *né, *ís?

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɑi̯.nɑz/`

### Numeral

1. _(reconstruction, singular)_ zero, nought
2. _(reconstruction, singular)_ none, no one, nothing
3. _(in-plural, reconstruction)_ not any, no, none
