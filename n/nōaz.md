## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nōaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *néh₂us (“sea-boat, ship”). Cognate with Latin nāvis (“ship”), Old Irish nau (“ship”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *néh₂us

### Noun
