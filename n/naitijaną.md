## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *naitijaną

### Etymology

From earlier *hnaitijaną, from Proto-Indo-European *h₃neyd- (“to revile, insult”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃neyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/nɑi̯.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to abuse; mistreat
2. _(reconstruction)_ to revile; blaspheme; calumniate
