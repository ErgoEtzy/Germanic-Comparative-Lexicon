# *newundô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/newund%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *newundô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁newn̥nós, from *h₁néwn̥. An ordinal to *newun (“nine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁newn̥nós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈne.wun.dɔːː/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ ninth
