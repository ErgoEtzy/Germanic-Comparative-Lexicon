# *nēþlō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/n%C4%93%C3%BEl%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nēþlō

### Etymology

From *nēþlą + *-ō (feminine suffix), the former from Proto-Indo-European *(s)néh₁-tlo-m, from *(s)neh₁- (“to sew”) + *-tlom (instrumental suffix). Equivalent to *nēaną (“to sew”) + *-þlą + *-ō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)neh₁-, *(s)néh₁-tlo-m, *-tlom

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɛːθ.lɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ needle
