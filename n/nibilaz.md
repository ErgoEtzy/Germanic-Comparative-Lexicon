[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/nibilaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nibilaz

### Etymology

Derived from or related to *nebulaz (“fog, darkness”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *nebʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈni.βi.lɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ dark, misty
