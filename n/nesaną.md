# *nesaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/nesan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nesaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *nésetor, from *nes- (“to return home”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *nes-, *nésetor

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈne.sɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to survive, recover, heal
