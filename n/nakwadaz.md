## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nakwadaz

### Etymology

Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *negʷ- (“naked, bare”); see there for the cognates.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *negʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɑ.kʷɑ.ðɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ naked
