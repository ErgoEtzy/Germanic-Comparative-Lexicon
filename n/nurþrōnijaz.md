## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nurþrōnijaz

### Etymology

From *nurþraz + *-ōnijaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *nér

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnur.θrɔː.ni.jɑz/`

### Adjective
