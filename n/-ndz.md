## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -ndz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *-onts.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-onts

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈndz/`

### Suffix

1. _(masculine, morpheme, reconstruction)_ -ing one, -er. Forms agent nouns from verbs. Identical in form and meaning to the present participle.
