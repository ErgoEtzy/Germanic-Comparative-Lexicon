[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/nastijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *nastijaną

### Etymology

Probably an infixed formation from *natją (“net”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɑs.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to bind, tie, sew
