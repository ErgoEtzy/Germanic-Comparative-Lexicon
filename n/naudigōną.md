# *naudigōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/naudig%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *naudigōną

### Etymology

From *naudigaz (“wanted, needed”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnɑu̯.ði.ɣɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to compel, to force
2. _(reconstruction)_ to invite, to compel to come
