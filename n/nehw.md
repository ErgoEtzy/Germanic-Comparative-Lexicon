### Etymology

Proto-Germanic *nehw
From Proto-Indo-European *nékʷe. Equivalent to *ne + *-hw. Cognate with Latin neque (“nor”), Proto-Celtic *nekʷe.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *nékʷe

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈnexʷ/`

### Conjunction

1. _(reconstruction)_ nor, and not
