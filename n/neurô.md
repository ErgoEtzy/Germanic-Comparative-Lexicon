# *neurô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/neur%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *neurô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *négʷʰrō. Cognate with Latin nefrōnēs pl (“testicles”) (Praeneste), nebrundinēs pl (“testicles”) (Lanuvium), Ancient Greek νεφρός (nephrós, “kidney”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *negʷʰ-, *negʷʰrós, *négʷʰrō

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈneu̯.rɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, neuter, reconstruction)_ kidney
2. _(masculine, neuter, reconstruction)_ testicle
