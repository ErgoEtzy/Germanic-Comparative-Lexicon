## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lōgą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *legʰ- (“to be situated, lie”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *legʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɔː.ɣɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ a place, position, camp
