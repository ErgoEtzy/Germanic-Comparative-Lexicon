# *listiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/listiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *listiz

### Etymology

Probably formed from the root of *lizaną (“to know, understand”), from Proto-Indo-European *leys- (“track, furrow, trail, trace”), equivalent to *lizaną + *-þiz. Compare also Proto-West Germanic *liʀnōn (“to learn”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leys-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlis.tiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ art, craft
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ cunning, guile
