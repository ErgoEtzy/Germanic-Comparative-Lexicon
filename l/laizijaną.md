# *laizijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/laizijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *laizijaną

### Etymology

From *lizaną + *-janą, stative from the root *leys- (“track, furrow, trace, trail”)

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(le-)lóys-e, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *leys-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑi̯.zi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to teach
