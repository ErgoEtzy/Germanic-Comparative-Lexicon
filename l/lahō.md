# *lahō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lah%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lahō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lókus (“lake; pond”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lókus

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑ.xɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ lake
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ pond; pool; puddle
