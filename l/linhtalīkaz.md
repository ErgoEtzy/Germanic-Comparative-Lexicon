# *linhtalīkaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/linhtal%C4%ABkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *linhtalīkaz

### Etymology

From *linhtaz (“light, not heavy”) + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₁lengʷʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlĩːx.tɑ.liː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ light
2. _(reconstruction)_ insignificant
3. _(reconstruction)_ effortless
