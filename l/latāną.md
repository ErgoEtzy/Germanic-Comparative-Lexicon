# *latāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lat%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *latāną

### Etymology

From *lataz (“late”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti, *leh₁d-, *lh₁d-o-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑ.tɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be slow, to tarry
2. _(reconstruction)_ to become slow
