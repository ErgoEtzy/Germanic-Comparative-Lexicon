# *lamjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lamjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lamjaną

### Etymology

From *lamaz (“lame”) + *-janą. Cognate with Old Church Slavonic ломити (lomiti) and Old Prussian limtwei.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lemH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑm.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to bash, hit
2. _(reconstruction)_ to lame, cripple
