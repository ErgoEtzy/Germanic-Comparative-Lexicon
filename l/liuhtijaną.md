## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *liuhtijaną

### Etymology

From *leuhtą (“light”) + *-janą (denominative suffix) or *leuhtaz (“light, bright”) + *-janą (factitive suffix), both ultimately from the same Proto-Indo-European root.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈliu̯x.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to give light, shine
