# *linþaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lin%C3%BEaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *linþaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lént-o-s. Cognate with Latin lentus (“flexible, sticky”), Lithuanian lentà (“board”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lent-, *lént-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlin.θɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ bendsome; flexible
2. _(reconstruction)_ pliable; supple
3. _(reconstruction)_ weak; soft; mild
