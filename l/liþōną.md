# *liþōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/li%C3%BE%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *liþōną

### Etymology

Likely from *liþuz (“joint, limb”) + *-ōną.

Closely related to the strong verb *līþaną (“to go, pass”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *h₂el-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈli.θɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cut, divide, dissect
2. _(reconstruction)_ to bend
3. _(reconstruction)_ to organize, to put in order
