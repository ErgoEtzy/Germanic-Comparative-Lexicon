# *lizaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lizan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lizaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(le-)lóys-e, stative from the root *leys- (“track, furrow, trace, trail”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(le-)lóys-e, *leys-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈli.zɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(East, Germanic, reconstruction)_ to know, to be familiar with, to understand
