## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *linnaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *leyh₂- (“to avoid, elude, decline, shrink away from, recede”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leyh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlin.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to turn, move aside, swerve from, dodge
2. _(reconstruction)_ to cease, quit
