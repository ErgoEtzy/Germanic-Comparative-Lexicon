# *lutōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lut%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lutōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lewd- (“to duck; feign; be low; be small”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlu.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to conceal
