## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lustōną

### Etymology

From *lustuz (“lust”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *-tus, *leh₂s-, *lewH-, *lewHs-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlus.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to lust, desire
