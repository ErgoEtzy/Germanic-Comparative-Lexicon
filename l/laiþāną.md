[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lai%C3%BE%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *laiþāną

### Etymology

From *laiþaz (“loathsome”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti, *h₂leyt-, *h₂leytos

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to loathe, to detest, to revile
