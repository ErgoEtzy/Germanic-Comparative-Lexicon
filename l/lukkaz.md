# *lukkaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lukkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lukkaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lugnós, from *lewg- (“to bend”) + *-nós. Synchronically analyzed as an a-stem nominal formation from *lūkaną (“to close, shut”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-nós, *lewg-, *lugnós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈluk.kɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ lock (of hair)
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ curl
