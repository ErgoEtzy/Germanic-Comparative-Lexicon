# *laikaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/laikaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *laikaz

### Etymology

A nominal derivative of *laikaną (“to jump, play”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑi̯.kɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ dance
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ game, sport
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ hymn
