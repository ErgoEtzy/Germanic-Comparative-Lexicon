# *libjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/libjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *libjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *leyp- (“leave, cling, linger”), with semantic development "to leave, cling" > "to remain" > "to live".

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leyp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈliβ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to live, to be alive
