# *leugō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/leug%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *leugō

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *(h₂)lewgʰeh₂, from either Proto-Indo-European *lewgʰ- or *h₂lewgʰ- ("to bind by oath"). Related to Proto-Celtic *lugiyom, Old Irish luige (“oath”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewgʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈleu̯.ɣɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ marriage, arrangement; a binding by oath
