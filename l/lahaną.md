# *lahaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lahan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lahaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lok- (“to blame”). Compare Old Irish locht (“fault, vice”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lok-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑ.xɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to blame, fault, reproach
