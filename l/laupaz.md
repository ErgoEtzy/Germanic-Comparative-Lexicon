# *laupaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/laupaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *laupaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lewb- (“to shed, peel, break off”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewb-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑu̯.pɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ vessel; container; basket
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a measure; portion; allotment
