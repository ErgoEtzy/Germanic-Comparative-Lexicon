# *līsaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/l%C4%ABsaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *līsaz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *lizaną (“to know, to be familiar with”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leys-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈliː.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(East, Germanic, reconstruction)_ knowledgeable, knowing, familiar with
