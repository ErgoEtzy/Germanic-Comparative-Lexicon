## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lagōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *legʰ- (“to lie”). Possibly derived from *lagą (“law, situation, shape”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *legʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑ.ɣɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to establish, fasten
2. _(reconstruction)_ to fix, mend
