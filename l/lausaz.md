# *lausaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lausaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lausaz

### Etymology

An o-grade adjectival formation from *leusaną (“to lose”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑu̯.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ loose, free
2. _(reconstruction)_ empty, devoid
3. _(reconstruction)_ false, untrue
4. _(in-compounds, reconstruction)_ -less, lacking, devoid of
