# *linhtaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/linhtaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *linhtaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *h₁lengʷʰ-tos, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁lengʷʰ- (“lightweight; swift”). By synchronic analysis, a to-stem adjectival derivative of *lingwaną (“to make progress, succeed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁lengʷʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlĩːx.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ light (in weight)
