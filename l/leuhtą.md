## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *leuhtą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lewktom, ultimately from the root *lewk- (“to shine”) and the suffix *-tós.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewk-, *lewktom
