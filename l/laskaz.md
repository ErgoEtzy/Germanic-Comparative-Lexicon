## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *laskaz

### Etymology

Compare also Latin laxus (“loose, slack, yielding”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ false
2. _(reconstruction)_ weak, feeble, lax
3. _(reconstruction)_ mellow, soft
