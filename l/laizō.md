## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *laizō

### Etymology

From *laizijaną (“to teach”), from *lizaną + *-janą, stative from the root *leys- (“track, furrow, trace, trail”). Piecewise doublet of *laisō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leys-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑi̯.zɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ teaching; lore; doctrine
