## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lōfô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *leh₂p- (“to be flat; palm”). Cognate with Lithuanian lópa (“paw, claw”), Proto-Slavic *lapa (“paw”), as well as perhaps dialectal Armenian լափ (lapʻ), լուփ (lupʻ, “palm, flat of the hand”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ the palm or hollow of the hand
