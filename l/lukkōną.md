# *lukkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lukk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lukkōną

### Etymology

Unclear, possibly related to *leuganą (“to lie”), from Proto-Indo-European *lewgʰ-.
Or possibly a zero-grade iterative related to Lithuanian lùnginti (“to allure”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewgʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈluk.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to caress
2. _(reconstruction)_ to lure, entice
