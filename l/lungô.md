# *lungô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lung%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lungô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁ln̥gʷʰ-on-, a neuter n-stem nominal formation from *h₁lengʷʰ- (“light in weight; nimble”). Compare Old Armenian լանջք (lanǰkʻ, “breast”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁ln̥gʷʰ-on-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈluŋ.ɡɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ lung
