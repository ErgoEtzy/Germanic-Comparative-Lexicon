[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/-l%C4%ABkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -līkaz

### Etymology

See *līką (“body, corpse”) for more; the sense of "similar to" appears to be the original sense.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌliː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ -like, -ly. Forms adjectives from nouns and adjectives with the sense of 'similar to, characteristic of'.
