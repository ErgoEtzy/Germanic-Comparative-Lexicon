# *lītilaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/l%C4%ABtilaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lītilaz

### Etymology

*luttiz (“little, few”) + *-ilaz (“adjectival suffix”), the former likely a derivative of *lūtaną (“to bow down”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-dʰlom, *-e-ló-s, *-lós, *-tlom, *-trom

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈliː.ti.lɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ small, little (alternative form of *lūtilaz)
2. _(reconstruction)_ few, little (small amount of)
