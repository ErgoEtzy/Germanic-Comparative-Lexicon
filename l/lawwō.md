## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lawwō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lowH-éh₂, from *lewH- (“to cut”) + *-éh₂. Direct cognate with Proto-Balto-Slavic *lā́ˀwāˀ (“bench, bed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-ós, *lewH-, *lowH-éh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑw.wɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ groove
