## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *leuhmô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *léwk-mn̥ ~ *luk-mén-s, from *lewk- (“bright”) + *-mn̥.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-mn̥, *lewk-, *léwk-mn̥ ~ *luk-mén-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈleu̯x.mɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ light; lustre
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ brightness, radiance
