**Reconstruction:** *laihną

### Etymology

From earlier *laihwnam, from Proto-Indo-European *leykʷ- (“to leave, leave over”). Cognate with Avestan 𐬭𐬀𐬉𐬑𐬆𐬥𐬀𐬵 (raēxənah), Sanskrit रेक्णस् (rékṇas, “property, inheritance”).

- IPA: `/ˈlɑi̯x.nɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ that which is lent, loan
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ fief
