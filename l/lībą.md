[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/l%C4%ABb%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lībą

### Etymology

Likely derived from *lībaną (“to remain”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈliː.βɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ body
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ life
