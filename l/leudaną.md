# *leudaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/leudan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *leudaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁léwdʰ-e-ti, from *h₁lewdʰ- (“to grow; people”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁lewdʰ-, *h₁léwdʰ-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈleu̯.ðɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ To sprout; spring up; grow
