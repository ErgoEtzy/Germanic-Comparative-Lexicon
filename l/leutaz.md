# *leutaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/leutaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *leutaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lewd- (“to duck, crouch, feign, cringe, become small”). Cognate with Lithuanian liū̃dnas (“sad”), Proto-Slavic *lȗdъ (“crazy, foolish”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈleu̯.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ deceitful, deceptive
