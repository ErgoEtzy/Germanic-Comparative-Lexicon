# *liudiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/liudiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *liudiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁léwdʰis (“people”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₁lewdʰ- (“to grow (up)”). Doublet of *lūdiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁lewdʰ-, *h₁léwdʰis

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈliu̯.ðiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, masculine, reconstruction, singular)_ man, person
2. _(feminine, in-plural, masculine, reconstruction)_ men, people
