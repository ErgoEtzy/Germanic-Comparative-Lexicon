[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/l%C4%93taz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lētaz

### Etymology

From *lētaną (“to let; free; release”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɛː.tɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ freeman
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bondsman; serf
