## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *laguz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lókus (“lake, pond”). Cognate with Latin lacus (“hollow, pond”), Old Irish loch (“lake, pond”), Ancient Greek λάκκος (lákkos, “waterhole, pond, pit”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑ.ɣuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ water, liquid
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ sea, ocean
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ wetness
4. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ name of the rune ᛚ (l)
