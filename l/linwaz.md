## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *linwaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *leyh₂- (“to decrease, dwindle; lean, slim”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leyh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlin.wɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ soft; mild; gentle
