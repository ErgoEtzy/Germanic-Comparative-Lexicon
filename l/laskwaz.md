# *laskwaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/laskwaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *laskwaz

### Etymology

Uncertain. Possibly from earlier *latskwaz, from *lataz (“slow, late”), from Proto-Indo-European *leh₁d- (“to let, leave”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leh₁d-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑsk.wɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ sluggish, dull, lazy, idle
2. _(reconstruction)_ limp, lax, feeble
