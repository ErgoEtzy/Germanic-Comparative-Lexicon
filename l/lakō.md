# *lakō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lak%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lakō

### Etymology

From *lekaną (“to leak, drain”), from Proto-Indo-European *leg- (“to leak”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑ.kɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a gully or stream
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ an area where water has run off and collected; drainage ditch
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a puddle, pool
4. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a pond or small lake
