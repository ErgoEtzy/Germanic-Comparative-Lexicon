# *likkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/likk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *likkōną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *liǵʰnéh₂-, from Proto-Indo-European *leyǵʰ- (“to lick”). Cognate with Latin lingō (“lick”, verb).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leyǵʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlik.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to lick
