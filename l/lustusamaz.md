## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lustusamaz

### Etymology

From *lustuz + *-samaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tus, *leh₂s-, *lewH-, *lewHs-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlus.tu.ˌsɑ.mɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ pleasing; pleasant
