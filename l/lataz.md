# *lataz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lataz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lataz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lh₁d-o-, verbal adjective from the root *leh₁d- (“to be tired”). Cognate with Latin lassus (“weary, faint”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leh₁d-, *lh₁d-o-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑ.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ slow, sluggish
2. _(reconstruction)_ lazy
3. _(reconstruction)_ late
