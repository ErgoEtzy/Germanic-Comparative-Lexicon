## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *leuhtaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lewk-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈleu̯x.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ light, bright
