# *limpaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/limpan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *limpaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)lembʰ- (“to hang limply”), which Pfeifer appears to consider a nasalized extension of *leb- (“to hang loosely; lip”). Compare Tocharian A läm- (“to sit, remain”), Sanskrit लम्ब् (lamb), रम्ब् (ramb, “to hang down”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)lembʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlim.pɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to glide
2. _(reconstruction)_ to go
3. _(reconstruction)_ to suit, fit
4. _(reconstruction)_ to happen
