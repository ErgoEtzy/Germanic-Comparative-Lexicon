## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lagą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *legʰ- (“to lie”). Synchronically analyzable as a nominal formation from *ligjaną (“to lie, rest on”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *legʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑ.ɣɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ situation
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ law
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ a settled amount (cost, price, payment, etc.)
