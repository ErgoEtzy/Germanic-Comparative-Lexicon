## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *linhtijaną

### Etymology

From *linhtaz (“light, having little weight”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make light
2. _(reconstruction)_ to make easy
