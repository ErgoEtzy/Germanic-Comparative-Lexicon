**Reconstruction:** *lausungō

### Etymology

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ releasing, liberation; removal
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ deficiency, want, lack
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ lack of truth, deceit, fraud
