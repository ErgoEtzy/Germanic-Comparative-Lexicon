# *liþulausaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/li%C3%BEulausaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *liþulausaz

### Etymology

From *liþuz (“limb”) + *-lausaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂el-, *lewh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈli.θu.lɑu̯.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ limbless, without limbs
2. _(reconstruction)_ loose-jointed, loose-limbed, limp
