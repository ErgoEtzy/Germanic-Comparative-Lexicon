## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *landą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lendʰ- (“land, heath”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-om, *lendʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑn.dɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ land, area of ground
