# *lasiwaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lasiwaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lasiwaz

### Etymology

Kroonen suggests a possible derivation from Proto-Indo-European *losi-u-. Adams suggests a late Proto-Indo-European pre-form *lósiwo-. Probably related to Proto-Slavic *lošь (“bad”), Tocharian B leswi (“attacks of weakness”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *losi-u-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑ.si.wɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ strengthless, feeble, weak
