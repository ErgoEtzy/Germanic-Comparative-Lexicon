# *lubagernaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lubagernaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lubagernaz

### Etymology

From *lubą + *gernaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʰer-, *lewbʰ-, *ǵʰerH-no-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlu.βɑ.ˌɣer.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ desirous of praise, honour
