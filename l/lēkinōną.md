# *lēkinōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/l%C4%93kin%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lēkinōną

### Etymology

From *lēkijaz (“doctor”) + *-inōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂leg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɛː.ki.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to heal, to cure (by medicine)
