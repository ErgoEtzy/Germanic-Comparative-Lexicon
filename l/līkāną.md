## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *līkāną

### Etymology

From *-līkaz (“like”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leyg-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to conform, to be of an agreeable kind
2. _(reconstruction)_ to please (+ dative object), to like (impersonal) (+ dative subject)
