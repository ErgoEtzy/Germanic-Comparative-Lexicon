## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *laþō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂lot-eh₂, an isogloss that only appears to be found in Germanic and Hittite. Anatolian cognates include Luwian [script needed] (ḫalta, “to call”), Hittite [script needed] (halzai, “to invoke, recite, call out”), both from Proto-Anatolian *halt-. Not related to *hlōaną (“to shout”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂lot-eh₂
