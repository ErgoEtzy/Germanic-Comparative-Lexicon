# *līhwaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/l%C4%ABhwan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *līhwaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *léykʷt, from *leykʷ- (“to leave behind”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leykʷ-, *léykʷt

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈliː.xʷɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to lend
