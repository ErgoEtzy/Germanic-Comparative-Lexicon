# *leuhsaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/leuhsaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *leuhsaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lewk-s-ós, a thematic derivative of *léwkos (“light”), from *lewk- (“to shine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewk-, *lewk-s-ós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈleu̯x.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ light, bright
