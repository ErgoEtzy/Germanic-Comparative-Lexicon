# *lapilaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lapilaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lapilaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *leb-, *leh₂b- (“to slurp; lick; smack”), equivalent to *lapaną (“to drink; slurp; lap”) + *-ilaz (agent suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leb-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑ.pi.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ spoon
