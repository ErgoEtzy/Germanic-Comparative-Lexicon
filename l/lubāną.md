# *lubāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lub%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lubāną

### Etymology

From *lubō (“love”) + *-āną. Related to Latin libet (“it is pleasing”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewbʰ-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be loving, desiring, hoping
