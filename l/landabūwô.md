## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *landabūwô

### Etymology

From *landą (“land”) + *būwô (“dweller”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-om, *lendʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑn.dɑ.ˌbu.wɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ inhabitant, countryman
