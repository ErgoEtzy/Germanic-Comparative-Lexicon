[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/leuhs%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *leuhsą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lewk-so-m, ultimately from the root *lewk- (“to shine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewk-, *lewk-so-m

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈleu̯x.sɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, neuter, reconstruction)_ light
