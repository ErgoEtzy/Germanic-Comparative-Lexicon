# *leuþą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/leu%C3%BE%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *leuþą

### Etymology

Unclear. Traditionally derived from Proto-Indo-European *lew-t-, an extension of a tentative root *lew- (“to sing, praise”), whence also Latin laus (“praise”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lew-t-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈleu̯.θɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ song, lay, song of praise
