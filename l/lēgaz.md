# *lēgaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/l%C4%93gaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lēgaz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *ligjaną via earlier *legjaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *legʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɛː.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ low
2. _(reconstruction)_ flat, level with the ground
