## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lambaz

### Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Indo-European *h₁l̥h₁-on-bʰ-os- (related to Scottish Gaelic lon (“elk”), Ancient Greek ἔλᾰφος (élăphos, “red deer”)), enlargement of *h₁elh₁én, from *h₁el- (“deer, elk”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁l̥h₁-on-bʰ-os-
