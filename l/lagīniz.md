**Reconstruction:** *lagīniz

### Etymology

From *lagjaną (“to lay”) + *-īniz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *legʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑ.ɣiː.niz/`

### Noun
