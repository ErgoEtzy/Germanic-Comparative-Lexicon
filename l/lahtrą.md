# *lahtrą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lahtr%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lahtrą

### Etymology

Possibly an o-grade variant of *lehtrą (see Proto-West Germanic *lehtr), but more likely, together with the formation *lōgą, it implies an unattested *laganą + *-þrą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *legʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑx.trɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, neuter, reconstruction)_ lair
