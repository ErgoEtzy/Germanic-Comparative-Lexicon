## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *landasiduz

### Etymology

From *landą (“land”) + *siduz (“culture”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-om, *lendʰ-, *sh₂ey-, *swedʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑn.dɑ.ˌsi.ðuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ custom of the land
