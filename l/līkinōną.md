# *līkinōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/l%C4%ABkin%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *līkinōną

### Etymology

Unclear, possibly from *līką (“body, shape”) + *-inōną or *līkinō (“mercy, recovery”) + *-ōną, or a merger of both. West Germanic only has the meaning "to compare", whereas Nordic also has the meanings "to show mercy" and "to care for".

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *leyg-, *leyǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈliː.ki.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to compare
2. _(reconstruction)_ to show mercy
3. _(reconstruction)_ to care for
