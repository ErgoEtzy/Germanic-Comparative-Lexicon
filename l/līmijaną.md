# *līmijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/l%C4%ABmijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *līmijaną

### Etymology

From *līmaz (“glue”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)ley-, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *h₂leyH-, *h₂leyH-mo-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈliː.mi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to adhere
2. _(reconstruction)_ to glue together
