## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *landijaną

### Etymology

From *landą (“land”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lendʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑn.di.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to land
