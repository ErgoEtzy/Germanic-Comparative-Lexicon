## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lēziz

### Etymology

Origin uncertain. Perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *les- (“to gather, lease”) (whence *lesaną (“to gather, collect”)), with original sense "harvestable (of land)" > "released (of land for harvesting)" > "cleared (of land)" > "empty".

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *les-
