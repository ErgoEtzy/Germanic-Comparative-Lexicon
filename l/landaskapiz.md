## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *landaskapiz

### Etymology

From *landą (“land”) + *-skapiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-om, *lendʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑn.dɑ.ˌskɑ.piz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ landscape
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ region, area
