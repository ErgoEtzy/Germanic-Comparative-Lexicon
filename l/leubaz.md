# *leubaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/leubaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *leubaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lewbʰós (“beloved”), from *lewbʰ- (“to desire, love”). Given the existence of a cognate in Proto-Slavic *ľubъ, this adjective is one of the oldest of the e-grade *-az adjectives.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewbʰós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈleu̯.βɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ dear, beloved
