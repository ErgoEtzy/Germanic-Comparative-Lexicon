# *luką

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/luk%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *luką

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lewg- (“to bend; turn”). Synchronically analyzable as a nominal formation related to *lūkaną (“to close, lock”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlu.kɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ lock
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ shutter
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ opening, aperture, hole
