## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lausijaną

### Etymology

From *lausaz (“loose, free”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑu̯.si.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make loose
2. _(reconstruction)_ to release, to set free
