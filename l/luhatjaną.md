# *luhatjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/luhatjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *luhatjaną

### Etymology

From the root of *leuhtaz + *-atjaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlu.xɑt.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sparkle, to flash brightly
