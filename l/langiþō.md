## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *langiþō

### Etymology

From *langaz + *-iþō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *dlongʰos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑŋ.ɡi.θɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ longness, length
