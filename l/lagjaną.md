# *lagjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lagjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lagjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *logʰéyeti. Equivalent to *ligjaną (“to lie”) + *-janą (“causative suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *logʰéyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑɣ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to lay, to place in a resting position
