## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lungraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁ln̥gʷʰrós (“light”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁ln̥gʷʰrós

- IPA: `/ˈluŋ.ɡrɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ swift
