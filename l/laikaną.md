# *laikaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/laikan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *laikaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *leyg- (“to jump around, bounce, shake, tremble”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leyg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑi̯.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to jump, play, move, swing about
