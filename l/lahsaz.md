## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lahsaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *laḱsos, from Proto-Indo-European *laḱs- (“salmon, trout”). Cognate with Proto-Slavic *lososь, Ossetian лӕсӕг (læsæg, “salmon”), Tocharian B laks (“fish”). See lax and Latvian lasis for further non-Germanic cognates.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *laḱs-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑx.sɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ salmon
