# *lugô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lug%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lugô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂lewgʰ- (“to lie, tell a lie”).

From Proto-Indo-European *lewk- (“bright, to shine, to see”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂lewgʰ-, *lewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlu.ɣɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ liar
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ traitor
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ one who breaks (an agreement, a covenant, an engagement, etc.)
