## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lubō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lewbʰ- (“to love, care for, desire”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewbʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlu.βɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ love, affection
