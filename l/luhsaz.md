## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *luhsaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lewk- (“to be bright, be light, shine”), because of the cat's seemingly glowing eyes and ability to see in the dark.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry
