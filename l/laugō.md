# *laugō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/laug%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *laugō

### Etymology

Derived from Proto-Indo-European *lewh₃- (“to wash”), perhaps via pre-Germanic *lowh₃-k-éh₂.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewh₃-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑu̯.ɣɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, feminine, reconstruction)_ bath, wash
2. _(Germanic, West, feminine, reconstruction)_ soap, lye
