## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lēgiþō

### Etymology

From *lēgaz + *-iþō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *legʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɛː.ɣi.θɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ lowness, depression (an area where the ground is lower)
