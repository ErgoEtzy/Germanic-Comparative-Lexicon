# *lauhaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lauhaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lauhaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lówkos (“clearing”), which is derived from Proto-Indo-European *lewk- (“bright”). Cognate with Latin lūcus (“sacred grove”), Sanskrit लोक (loká, “open space; world”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewk-, *lówkos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑu̯.xɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ clearing
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ meadow
