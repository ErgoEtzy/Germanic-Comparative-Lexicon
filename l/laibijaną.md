# *laibijaną

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *laibijaną

### Etymology

By surface analysis, *lībaną + *-janą. Kroonen and the LIV alternatively suggest an inheritance from Proto-Indo-European *loyp-éye-ti, a causative formed from the root *leyp-. This same causative may be the source of Sanskrit lepáyati and Proto-Slavic *lěpiti. However, the linguist Axel Palmér suggests that the Germanic form may be a later innovation due to the differing semantics between the Germanic term and the proposed cognates.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leyp-, *loyp-éye-ti

### Pronunciation

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to leave behind, to leave remaining
