## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lēgiz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *ligjaną (“to lie, rest”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *legʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɛː.ɣiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ lying, able to lie down
