# *līþu

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/l%C4%AB%C3%BEu)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *līþu

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *léyH-tu ~ *liH-téw-s, from *leyH- (“to pour”) + *-tu.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-tus, *leyH-, *léyH-tu ~ *liH-téw-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈliː.θu/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ cider
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ strong drink; liquor
