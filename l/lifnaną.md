# *lifnaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lifnan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lifnaną

### Etymology

From *lībaną (“to remain”) + *-naną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-néh₂-, *leykʷ-, *leyp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈliɸ.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to remain, to be left
