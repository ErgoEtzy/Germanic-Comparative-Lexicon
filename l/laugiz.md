# *laugiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/laugiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *laugiz

### Etymology

Probably from pre-Germanic *lowkís, from Proto-Indo-European *lewk- (“to shine”). Compare Old Norse logi, from the zero-grade masculine n-stem noun *lugô. Cognate with Proto-Slavic *lučь.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑu̯.ɣiz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ flame, blaze
