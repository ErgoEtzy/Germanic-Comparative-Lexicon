# *lappaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/lappaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lappaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *lopnó-, from Proto-Indo-European *leb- (“to hang loosely, flop”). Compare Ancient Greek λοβός (lobós, “lobe”), Albanian labë (“bark”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *leb-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑp.pɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ flap, lobe
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ strip
