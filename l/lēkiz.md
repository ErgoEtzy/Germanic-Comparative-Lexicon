# *lēkiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/l%C4%93kiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *lēkiz

### Etymology

Possible vṛddhi gerundive of otherwise unattested *lekaną (“to heal”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂lég-e-ti, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂leg- (“to care for”). Cognate with Ancient Greek ἀλέγω (alégō, “to care for”), Proto-Italic *legō (“to care”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂leg-, *h₂lég-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɛː.kiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ healing
