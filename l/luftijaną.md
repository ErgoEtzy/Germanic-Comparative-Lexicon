# *luftijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/luftijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *luftijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lewp- (“to flake off, peel, damage”). Equivalent to *luftuz (“air”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈluɸ.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to air; put or raise into the air; raise aloft; elevate; lift
