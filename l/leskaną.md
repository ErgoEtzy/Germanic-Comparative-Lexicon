# *leskaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/leskan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *leskaną

### Etymology

Thought to be from Proto-Indo-European *legʰ-sḱé-ti, sḱe-present to *legʰ- (“to lie down”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *legʰ-, *legʰ-sḱé-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈles.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cease, to stop
2. _(reconstruction)_ to quench
