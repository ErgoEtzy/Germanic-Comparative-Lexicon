## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *laubō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *lewbʰ- (“to desire, covet; praise, admire; love”). Related to *lubą (“praise”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *lewbʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈlɑu̯.βoː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, feminine, reconstruction)_ permission
