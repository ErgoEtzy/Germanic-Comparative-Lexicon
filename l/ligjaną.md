# *ligjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ligjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ligjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *légʰyeti, from *legʰ- (“to lie”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *légʰyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈliɣ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to lie, to rest (on a surface)
2. _(reconstruction)_ to lead (e.g. of a road)
