# *līkijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/l%C4%ABkijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *līkijaną

### Etymology

From *līkiją (“shape, appearance”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈliː.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make similar to, to make alike, to even
2. _(reconstruction)_ to compare
