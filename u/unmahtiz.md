# *unmahtiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/unmahtiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *unmahtiz

### Etymology

From *un- + *mahtiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *megʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈunˌmɑx.tiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ inability
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ weakness, strengthlessness
