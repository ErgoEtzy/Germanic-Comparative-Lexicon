## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uzwurkijaną

### Etymology

From *uz- + *wurkijaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂ew, *h₂u-s-, *ud-s-, *werǵ-, *wr̥ǵyéti, *úd

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/uz.ˈwur.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to do, effect or achieve by labor
