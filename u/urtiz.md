## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *urtiz

### Etymology

A dissimilated form of *wurtiz, *wurt-, itself derived from *wrōts.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈur.tiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ plant
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ field; garden
