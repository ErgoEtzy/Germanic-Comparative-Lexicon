## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uftō

### Etymology

Uncertain; according to Kroonen, an adverbial formation derived from *ub (“under”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈuɸ.tɔː/`

### Adverb

1. _(reconstruction)_ oft; often
