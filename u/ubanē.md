## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ubanē

### Etymology

From *ub + *-anē.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *upó

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈu.βɑ.neː/`

### Adverb
