[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/uzlag%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uzlagą

### Etymology

From *uz- (“out”) + *lagą (“situation, law”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *legʰ-, *úd-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈuz.lɑ.ɡɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ fate, destiny
