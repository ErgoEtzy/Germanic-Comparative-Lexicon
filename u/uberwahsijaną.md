## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uberwahsijaną

### Etymology

From *uber- + *wahsijaną.

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *h₂uks-é-ti, *h₂weks-, *weg-, *woḱso

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌu.βerˈwɑx.si.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to overgrow, grow excessively
