## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ūrigaz

### Etymology

From *ūrą (“water, urine, rain”) + *-gaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-kos, *uh₁r-

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ watery, wet
