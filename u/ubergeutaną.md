# *ubergeutaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ubergeutan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ubergeutaną

### Etymology

From *uber- + *geutaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *ǵʰewd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌu.βerˈɣeu̯.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to pour over
2. _(reconstruction)_ to pour to overflowing, to overpour
