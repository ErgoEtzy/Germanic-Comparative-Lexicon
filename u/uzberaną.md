# *uzberaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/uzberan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uzberaną

### Etymology

From *uz- + *beraną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌuz.ˈbe.rɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to bear off, away
2. _(reconstruction)_ to bring forth, produce
