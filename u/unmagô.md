# *unmagô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/unmag%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *unmagô

### Etymology

From *un- (“un-”) + a derivative of *maganą (“be able, may”), from Proto-Indo-European *megʰ-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *megʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈun.mɑ.ɣɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, masculine, reconstruction)_ dependent / dependant (person who depends on another for support, i.e. cannot live independently)
