[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/uzgeban%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uzgebaną

### Etymology

From *uz- + *gebaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰebʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌuz.ˈɣe.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to give up, give back, repay
