# *ufumô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ufum%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ufumô

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *h₁úpm̥Ho-, equivalent to *ub + *-umô. Cognate with Sanskrit उपम (upamá, “uppermost”), Latin summus (“highest”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *upó

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈu.ɸu.mɔːː/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ uppermost, highest
