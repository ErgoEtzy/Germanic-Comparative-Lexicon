## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ūtlandijaz

### Etymology

From *ūt + *landijaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *úd

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈuːt.ˌlɑn.di.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ foreign, outlandish
