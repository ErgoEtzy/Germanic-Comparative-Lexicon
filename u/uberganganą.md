# *uberganganą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ubergangan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uberganganą

### Etymology

From *uber + *ganganą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰengʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌu.βer.ˈɣɑŋ.ɡɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to go over, go beyond; to cross
2. _(reconstruction)_ to transgress
