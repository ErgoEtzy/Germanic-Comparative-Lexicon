[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C5%ABr%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ūrą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *uh₁r-, zero grade form of *weh₁r- (“water”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *uh₁r-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ liquid, especially water, urine, rain
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ wetness
