# *unberandz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/unberandz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *unberandz

### Etymology

From *un- (“not”) + *berandz, present participle of *beraną (“to bear, carry”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈun.ˌbe.rɑndz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ not bearing, barren, unfertile
