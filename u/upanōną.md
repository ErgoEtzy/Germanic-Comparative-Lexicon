## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *upanōną

### Etymology

From *upanaz (“open”) + *-ōną. Compare *uppijaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈu.pɑ.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to open
2. _(reconstruction)_ to lift
