## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uberstīganą

### Etymology

From *uber- + *stīganą.

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *steygʰ-

### Pronunciation

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to ascend or climb over
2. _(reconstruction)_ to surpass, transcend, exceed
