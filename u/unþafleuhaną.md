## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *unþafleuhaną

### Etymology

From *unþa- (“away”) + *fleuhaną (“to flee”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂u-nt-, *plewk-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to flee away, escape
