# *unhtwǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/unhtw%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *unhtwǭ

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *n̥kʷ-tw-, derived from Proto-Indo-European *nókʷts (“night”). Compare Sanskrit अक्तु (aktú, “dark, night”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *nekʷ-, *nókʷts

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈũːx.twɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ last part of the night
