[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/unwissaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *unwissaz

### Etymology

From *un- + *wissaz (past participle of *witaną).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈun.ˌwis.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ uncertain, unsure
