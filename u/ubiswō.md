## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ubiswō

### Etymology

A close relative of *upp.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈu.βi.swɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ eaves
