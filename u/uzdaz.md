# *uzdaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/uzdaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uzdaz

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *úd-s (“up, off”) (> *uz (“out of”)) + *dʰeh₁- (“to set, put, place”). Alternatively connected to the root *wes- (“to prick”) (connected to Albanian usht (“thistle”)).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *úd, *úd-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈuz.dɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ sharp point
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ place
