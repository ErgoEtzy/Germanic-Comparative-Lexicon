## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Uhtaharjaz

### Etymology

From *uhtô (“fear”) + *harjaz (“army; warrior”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *ker-, *kóryos

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
