## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *umbigangaz

### Etymology

Derived from *umbiganganą; equivalent to *umbi + *gangaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰengʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈum.bi.ˌɣɑŋ.ɡɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a going around, going about, circuit
