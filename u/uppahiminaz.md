# *uppahiminaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/uppahiminaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uppahiminaz

### Etymology

*uppa- (“up-, above”) + *himinaz (“heaven, sky”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂éḱmō, *kem-, *ḱey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌupː.ˈxi.mi.nɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, poetic, reconstruction)_ literally 'up-heaven', often translated as 'high heaven'
