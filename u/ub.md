## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ub

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *upó (“up; from below”). Cognate with Latin sub, Ancient Greek ῠ̔πό (hŭpó), Sanskrit उप॑ (úpa).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *upó

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/uβ/`

### Preposition

1. _(reconstruction, with-accusative)_ under (going beneath)
2. _(reconstruction, with-dative)_ under (located beneath)

### Adverb

1. _(reconstruction)_ under
