# *unseraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/unseraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *unseraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *n̥-s-ero- (“our”). Genitive form of *wīz (“we”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *n̥-s-ero-, *wéy

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈun.se.rɑz/`

### Determiner

1. _(reconstruction)_ our, ours; 1st person plural possessive
