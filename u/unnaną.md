# *unnaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/unnan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *unnaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₃n̥-né-h₂-ti, from the root *h₃neh₂- (“to enjoy”). Cognate with Ancient Greek ὀνίνημι (onínēmi, “to be of use, help”), ἀπόνητο (apónēto, “to have joy of something”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃neh₂-, *h₃n̥-né-h₂-ti, *h₃n̥néh₂ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈun.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to grant, bestow
