## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *unlustuz

### Etymology

From *un- + *lustuz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tus, *leh₂s-, *lewH-, *lewHs-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈun.ˌlus.tuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ apathy, listlessness, lack of desire
