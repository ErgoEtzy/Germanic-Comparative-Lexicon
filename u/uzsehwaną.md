# *uzsehwaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/uzsehwan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uzsehwaną

### Etymology

From *uz- + *sehwaną (verb).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂ew, *h₂u-s-, *sekʷ-, *ud-s-, *úd

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌuz.ˈsex.wɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to look out; look up
2. _(reconstruction)_ to view; look at
3. _(reconstruction)_ to see; notice
