# *uzhaitaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/uzhaitan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uzhaitaną

### Etymology

From *uz- + *haitaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂ew, *h₂u-s-, *key-d-, *ud-s-, *úd, *ḱeyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌuz.ˈxɑi̯.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to call out, call forth
