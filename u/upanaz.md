# *upanaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/upanaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *upanaz

### Etymology

Seemingly the past participle of a verb *eupaną, *ūpaną, (though no other evidence of such a verb exists), with said verb being derived from *upp (“up”) and thus originally meaning "lying on top" > "clear, open".

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈu.pɑ.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ open
