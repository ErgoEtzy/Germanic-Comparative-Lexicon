## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uppijaną

### Etymology

From *upp (“up, upwards”) + *-janą. Compare *upanōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈup.pi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to lift, to raise, to hold up
2. _(reconstruction)_ to reveal, to disclose
