## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -umô

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *-m̥Hon-, from Proto-Indo-European *-mHo-. Cognate with Latin -imus, Sanskrit -अम (-ama).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-mHo-, *-tm̥mos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/u.mɔːː/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ Forms comparative or superlative adjectives.
