## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uwwulǭ

### Etymology

From *uwwô (“eagle-owl”) + *-ulǭ (plant and animal suffix). Probably ultimately of imitative origin, like Proto-West Germanic *hūō (“owl”).
