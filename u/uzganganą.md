[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/uzgangan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uzganganą

### Etymology

From *uz- + *ganganą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰengʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌuzˈɣɑŋ.ɡɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to go out
