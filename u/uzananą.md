## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uzananą

### Etymology

From *uz- + *ananą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂enh₁-, *h₂ew, *h₂u-s-, *ud-s-, *úd

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/uzˈɑ.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to breathe out, expire
