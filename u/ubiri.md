## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ubiri

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *upéri, from *upér (ultimately from *upo + *-i).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *upér, *upéri

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈu.βi.ri/`

### Preposition

1. _(reconstruction, with-accusative)_ over (moving across)
2. _(reconstruction, with-dative)_ over, above (positioned above)

### Adverb

1. _(reconstruction)_ over, above
