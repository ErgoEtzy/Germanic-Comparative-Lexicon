[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/understandan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *understandaną

### Etymology

From *under- + *standaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stand beneath
2. _(reconstruction)_ to understand
