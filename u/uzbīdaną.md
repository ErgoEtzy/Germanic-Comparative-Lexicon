# *uzbīdaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/uzb%C4%ABdan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *uzbīdaną

### Etymology

From *uz- + *bīdaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰeydʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌuz.ˈbiː.ðɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to wait out, be patient
2. _(reconstruction)_ to await, expect
