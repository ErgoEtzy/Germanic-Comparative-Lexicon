## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wunskaz

### Etymology

A nominal formation from Proto-Indo-European *wnh₁-ske- (“to desire”), an sk-present from *wenh₁- (“to wish, love”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wenh₁-, *wnh₁-ske-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwun.skɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ wish
