# *walkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/walk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *walkōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wolg- (“to twist; turn; move”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wolg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑl.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ To roll about; wallow
2. _(reconstruction)_ To full
