# *waibijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/waibijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *waibijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *woypéyeti, causative of *weyp- (“to shake, swing”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyp-, *woypéyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑi.bi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to wind (around), wrap
2. _(reconstruction)_ to move around, to turn
3. _(reconstruction)_ to waver, to go back and forth
