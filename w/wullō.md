[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wull%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wullō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂wl̥h₁néh₂, a suffix-accented form of *h₂wĺ̥h₁neh₂ (“wool”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂welh₁-, *h₂wl̥h₁néh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwul.lɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ wool
