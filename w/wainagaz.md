# *wainagaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wainagaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wainagaz

### Etymology

From *wainōną (“to whine”) + *-gaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *wáy

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑi̯.nɑ.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ woeful, sad
2. _(reconstruction)_ pathetic, pitiable
