[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C4%93taz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wētaz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi derivative from Proto-Indo-European *wed- (“water, wet”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wed-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɛː.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ wet
