## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wrōgijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wrōk- (“speech, complaint”), from Proto-Indo-European *werh₁- (“to speak, say, tell”). Cognate with Russian врать (vratʹ, “to lie, fib”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *werh₁-, *wrōk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwrɔː.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to tell, speak, proclaim, shout
2. _(reconstruction)_ to accuse
