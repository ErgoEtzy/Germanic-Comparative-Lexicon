## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *walhiskaz

### Etymology

From *walhaz + *-iskaz. Compare Proto-Slavic *volšьskъ (“Roman”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *wĺ̥kʷos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑl.xis.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ foreign, non-Germanic (Celtic, later also Roman)
