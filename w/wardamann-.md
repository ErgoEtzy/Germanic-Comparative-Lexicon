## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wardamann-

### Etymology

From *wardaz (“guard”) + *mann- (“man”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(dʰ)ǵʰmō, *(dʰ)ǵʰmon-, *mon-, *wer-, *wert-, *wor-tós, *ǵʰmṓ

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ watchman, warder
