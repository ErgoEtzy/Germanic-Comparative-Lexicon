## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wulþuz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *wl̥tus, from Proto-Indo-European *wel- (“to see”) + *-tus. Cognate with Latin vultus, voltus.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wel-
