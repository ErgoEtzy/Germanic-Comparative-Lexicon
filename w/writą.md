# *writą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/writ%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *writą

### Etymology

Nominal formation from *wrītaną (“to write”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wreyd-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ fissure; crevice; groove
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ engraving; writing; inscription
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ written work; writ
