## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *weraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wiHrós (“man, hero”), with pretonic shortening before a resonant. Compare Latin vir, Sanskrit वीर (vīrá), Irish fear, Welsh gŵr and Lithuanian vyras.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wiHrós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwe.rɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ man
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ husband
