## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wakwō

### Etymology

From the same root as *wakwaz, namely Proto-Indo-European *wegʷ-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wegʷ-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a hole in the ice, a polynya
