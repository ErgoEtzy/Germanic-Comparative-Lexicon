# *wiljakwemô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wiljakwem%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wiljakwemô

### Etymology

From *wiljaną (“to want”) + *kwemaną (“to come”). Literally "desired comer" (or "desired come!").

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʷémt, *welh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwil.jɑ.ˌkʷe.mɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(reconstruction)_ A wished-for guest or arrival.
