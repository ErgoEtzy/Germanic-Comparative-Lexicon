# *wandōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wand%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wandōną

### Etymology

Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wendʰ- (“to turn, wind, braid”), analyzable as *wandaz (“fluctuating, difficult”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wendʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑn.dɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to turn aside; veer; change
