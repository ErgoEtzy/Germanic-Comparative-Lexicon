## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wiþrą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wíterom, adverbial accusative of *wíteros, from *wí (“apart”) + *-teros (comparative). Particularly close cognate with Sanskrit वि॒त॒रम् (vitarám, “further”). For the suffix development, compare *aftrą, but contrast the adjective *anþeraz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-teros, *wí, *wíterom
