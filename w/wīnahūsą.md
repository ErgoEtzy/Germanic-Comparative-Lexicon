[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C4%ABnah%C5%ABs%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wīnahūsą

### Etymology

From *wīną (“wine”) + *hūsą (“house”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)kewH-, *(s)kuH-s-o-m

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwiː.nɑ.ˌxuː.sɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ a place where wine is stored, wine house.
