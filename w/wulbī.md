## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wulbī

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wl̥p-íh₂, an alteration of *wl̥kʷ-íh₂, ultimately derived from *wĺ̥kʷos. Compare Proto-Germanic *wulgī.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry
