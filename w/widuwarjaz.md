## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *widuwarjaz

### Etymology

From *widuz (“forest”) + *warjaz (“dweller”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(h₁)wéydʰ(h₁)us, *-us, *h₁weydʰh₁-, *h₁widʰ-u-s

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ forest-dweller; a member of the Viduarii
