# *writiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/writiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *writiz

### Etymology

An i-stem nominal formation from *wrītaną (“to write”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wreyd-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ fissure; crevice; groove
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ engraving; writing; inscription
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ stroke; character; letter
