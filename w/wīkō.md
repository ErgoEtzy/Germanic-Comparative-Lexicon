# *wīkō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C4%ABk%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wīkō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *weyg-, *weyk- (“to bend, curve”), perhaps from earlier *wīks.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyg-, *weyk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwiː.kɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ bay, inlet, fjord
