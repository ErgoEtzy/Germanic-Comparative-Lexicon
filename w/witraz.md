## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *witraz

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *widrós, equivalent to *witaną + *-raz, a ro-stem adjective derive from Proto-Indo-European *weyd- (“to know, see”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwit.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ knowing, wise
