# *wurmiją

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wurmij%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wurmiją

### Etymology

From *wurmiz (“worm”) + *-ją (collective suffix). Kroonen points out that an identical neuter collective formation also appears in Proto-Slavic *vьrmьje.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *wr̥mis

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ vermin
