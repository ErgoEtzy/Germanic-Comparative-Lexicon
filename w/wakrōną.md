# *wakrōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wakr%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wakrōną

### Etymology

From *wakraz (“awake”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *weǵ-, *wóǵ-ros

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑk.rɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to wake up, to rouse
