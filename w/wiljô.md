## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wiljô

### Etymology

Derived from *wiljaną (“to want”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *welh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwil.jɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ will, wish, desire
