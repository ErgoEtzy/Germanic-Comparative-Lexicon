## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *witōþą

### Etymology

Probably derived from *witōdaz, past participle of *witōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwi.tɔː.θɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ law; (literally) what is known or observed
