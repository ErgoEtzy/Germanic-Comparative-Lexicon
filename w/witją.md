[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/witj%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *witją

### Etymology

Nominal formation from *witaną (“to know”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwit.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ knowledge
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ reason; sense; understanding
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ wit
