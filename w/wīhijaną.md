# *wīhijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C4%ABhijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wīhijaną

### Etymology

From *wīhaz (“sacred, holy”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-os, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *weyk-, *wéyk-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwiː.xi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to hallow, to consecrate
