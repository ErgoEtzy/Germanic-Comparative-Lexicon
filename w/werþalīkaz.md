## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *werþalīkaz

### Etymology

From *werþą + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwer.ðɑ.ˌliː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ Of great worth; valuable; important
