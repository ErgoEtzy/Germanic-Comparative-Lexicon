# *wlispaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wlispaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wlispaz

### Etymology

Of onomatopoeic origin. Other theories derive the word from a Proto-Indo-European *wlis-, *wleys- (“rod”), from *wel- (“to roll”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wlis-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwlis.pɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ stammering; lisping
