# *walgōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/walg%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *walgōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wolk- (“to roll”), from *wel- (“to turn; wind; roll”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wolk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑl.ɣɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to roll
