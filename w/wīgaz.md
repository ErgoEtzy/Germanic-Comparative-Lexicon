## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wīgaz

### Etymology

From earlier *wīhaz, vṛddhi gerundive of *wiganą (“to fight, battle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No PIE form is listed or machine-resolvable in this snapshot.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwiː.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ fighting, warlike; able to fight
2. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ able to be defeated
