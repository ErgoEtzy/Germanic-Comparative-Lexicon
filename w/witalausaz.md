# *witalausaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/witalausaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *witalausaz

### Etymology

From *witą (“knowledge, sense, wit”) + *-lausaz (“-less”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwi.tɑˌlɑu̯.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ witless
