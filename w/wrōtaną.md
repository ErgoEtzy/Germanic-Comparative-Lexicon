# *wrōtaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wr%C5%8Dtan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wrōtaną

### Etymology

Of uncertain origin. Perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *Hreh₃d- (“to scrape, gnaw”), or alternatively a verbal formation related to *wrōts (“root”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hreh₃d-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwrɔː.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to root, to dig with the nose
