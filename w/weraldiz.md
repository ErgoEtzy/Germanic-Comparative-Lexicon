# *weraldiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/weraldiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *weraldiz

### Etymology

From *weraz (“man”) + *aldiz (“age, lifetime”), literally “era of man”.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂el-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwe.rɑl.diz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ lifetime, worldly existence
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ mankind
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ world
