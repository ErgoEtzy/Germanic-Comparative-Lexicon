## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wirsiz

### Etymology

Perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *wers- (“to confuse, mix up; to beat, thresh, grind”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wers-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwir.siz/`

### Adverb
