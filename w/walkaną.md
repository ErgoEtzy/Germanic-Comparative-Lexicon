# *walkaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/walkan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *walkaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *welk-, *welg-, extended form of *welH- (“to turn, to wind, roll”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *welk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑl.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to turn, wind, roll, toss
2. _(reconstruction)_ to wend, walk, wander
3. _(reconstruction)_ to trample, full (cloth)
