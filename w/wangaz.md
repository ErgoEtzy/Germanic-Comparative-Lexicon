[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wangaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wangaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wengʰ-, *wenk- (“to be bent or bowed”), the same source as *wangô (“cheek”). Compare Old Prussian wangus (“oak forest”), Albanian vang (“fallow”), Sanskrit वञ्च् (vañc, “to move crookedly”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wengʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑŋ.ɡɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ field, meadow, pasture, plain
