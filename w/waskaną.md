# *waskaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/waskan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *waskaną

### Etymology

From earlier *watskaną, from Proto-Indo-European *wod-sḱé-ti, an o-grade sḱe-present of *wed- (“wet, water”). Doublet of *watōr (“water”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wod-sḱé-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑskɑnɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to wash
