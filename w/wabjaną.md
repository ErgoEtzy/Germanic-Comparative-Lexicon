## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wabjaną

### Etymology

From *webaną (“to weave”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *webʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑβ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to weave
2. _(reconstruction)_ to wrap, to entangle
