## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wīhiþō

### Etymology

From *wīhaz (“sacred, holy”) + *-iþō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-os, *-teh₂, *weyk-, *wéyk-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwiː.xi.θɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ holiness, sacredness
