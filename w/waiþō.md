## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *waiþō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wóyh₁-teh₂, from *weyh₁- (“to chase, pursue, suppress”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wóyh₁-teh₂

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a hunt, pursuit, chase
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a pasture, grassland, food
