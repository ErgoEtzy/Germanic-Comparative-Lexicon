## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wasô

### Etymology

Presumably related to German Wrasen (“water vapor (in kitchen, etc.)”), itself of unclear origin, though possibly related to *waisǭ (“mire, muck”). The semantic shift to "meadow" is perhaps via sense development "vapor" > "wettened land" > "meadow, field".
