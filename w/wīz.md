## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wīz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wéy, plural of *éǵh₂.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wéy

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/wiːz/`
