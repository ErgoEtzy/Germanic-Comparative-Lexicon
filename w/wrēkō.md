## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wrēkō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wreg- (“to drive”). Kroonen suggests that the term is a "lengthened-grade formation" from *wrekaną. Perhaps cognate with Latin urgeō (“urge”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wreg-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ persecution
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ revenge; vengeance
