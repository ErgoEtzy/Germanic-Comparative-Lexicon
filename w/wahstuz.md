# *wahstuz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wahstuz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wahstuz

### Etymology

From *wahsijaną + *-þuz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂weg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑxs.tuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ size, growth
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ stature, build
