## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *warnōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wer- (“to become aware; take heed”). Equivalent to *warnō (“warning”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑr.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be careful
2. _(reconstruction)_ to warn
