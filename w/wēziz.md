## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wēziz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *wesaną (“to be”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂wes-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɛː.ziz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ being, existing, remaining
2. _(reconstruction)_ able to be or stay (in a place)
