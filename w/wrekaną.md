## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wrekaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wreg- (“track, hunt, follow”). Cognate with Hittite ūrki- (“track”), Tocharian B werke (“to chase, hunt”), Sanskrit व्रजति (vrájati, “to go, walk, move”), and perhaps Latin urgēre (“to be urgent”), Proto-Slavic *vèrťi (“to throw, cast”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wreg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwre.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to pursue, to drive out
