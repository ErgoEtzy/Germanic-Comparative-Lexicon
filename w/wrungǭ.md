# *wrungǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wrung%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wrungǭ

### Etymology

A zero-grade derivation from *wringaną (“to wring”) + *-ǭ. Compare the masculine *wrunga-, which gives Dutch wrong (“wreath”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-éh₂, *-ō, *-ō(n, *wrengʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwrun.ɡɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ snare
