## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *werduz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *(H)wer-tú-s, which could be related to *waraz (“watchful, aware”), in which case the original sense was "attender"; the related Old Norse verðr (“meal”) would then have had an earlier meaning of "attendance." Or, possibly related to *werþaną (“to come about, happen”), with sense development "turn" > " participation".
