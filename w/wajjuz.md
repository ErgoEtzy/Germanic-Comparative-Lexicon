## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wajjuz

### Etymology

From pre-Proto-Germanic *wəyús (quasi-Proto-Indo-European *wh̥₁y-ú-s), from Proto-Indo-European *wéh₁y-u-s ~ *uh₁y-éw-s, from *weh₁y- (“to twist”) + *-us.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-us, *weh₁y-, *wh̥₁y-ú-s, *wéh₁y-u-s ~ *uh₁y-éw-s

### Pronunciation
