## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wīną

### Etymology

Borrowed, as one of the earliest loanwords in Germanic, from Latin vīnum.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwiː.nɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ wine
