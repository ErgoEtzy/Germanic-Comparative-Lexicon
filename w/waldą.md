## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *waldą

### Etymology

From *waldaną (“to rule”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂welh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑl.dɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ might, power, authority, force
