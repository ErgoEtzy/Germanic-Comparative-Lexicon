## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wōgiz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *weganą (“to move”), or a related, unattested *waganą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weǵʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɔː.ɣiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ moving, moving a lot
