## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *waiþijaną

### Etymology

From *waiþiz, *waiþō, from Proto-Indo-European *weyh₁- (“to chase, pursue”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑi̯.θi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to hunt, pursue
2. _(reconstruction)_ to graze, feed
