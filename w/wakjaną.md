# *wakjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wakjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wakjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *woǵ-éye-. By surface analysis, *wakaną (“to wake”) + *-janą (“causative suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weǵ-, *woǵ-éye-, *woǵéyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑk.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to wake up, to awaken
