## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wraiþį̄

### Etymology

From *wraiþaz (“wrathful, hostile”) + *-į̄.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wreyt-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ anger, fury, wrath
