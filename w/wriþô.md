[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wri%C3%BE%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wriþô

### Etymology

From *wrīþaną (“to writhe”) + *-ô (agentive suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ō, *wreyt-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ A coil or twist; something bent or coiled.
