[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C4%93pnalausaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wēpnalausaz

### Etymology

From *wēpną (“weapon”) + *-lausaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *lewh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɛːp.nɑ.lɑu̯.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ weaponless, unarmed
