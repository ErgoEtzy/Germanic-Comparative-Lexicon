## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wakraz

### Etymology

From *wakaną + *-raz. Possibly corresponds to Proto-Indo-European *wóǵ-ros, from *weǵ- (“be lively, awake, strong”). Compare Proto-Indo-Iranian *wáȷ́ras.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wóǵ-ros

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑk.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ awake
2. _(reconstruction)_ watchful, alert
