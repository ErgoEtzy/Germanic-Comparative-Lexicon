## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *waltaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *welH- (“to turn, to wind”), most likely an o-grade intensive formation to the verb *weltaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *welH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑl.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to roll, toss
2. _(reconstruction)_ to turn
