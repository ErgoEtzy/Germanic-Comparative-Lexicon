# *wraiþaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wrai%C3%BEaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wraiþaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *wróytos, from Proto-Indo-European *wreyt- (“to turn, twist”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wreyt-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwrɑi̯.θɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ twisted, curled, bent, wry, uneven
2. _(reconstruction)_ angry, furious, wroth
3. _(reconstruction)_ hostile, violent
