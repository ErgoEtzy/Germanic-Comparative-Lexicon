## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *waiwô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wai.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wai

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ woe, grief, misery
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ evil, wickedness, malice
