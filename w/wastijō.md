# *wastijō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wastij%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wastijō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wes- (“to dress, put on clothes”). The form *wastiz comes directly from Proto-Indo-European *wéstis (“clothing”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wes-, *wéstis

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ dress, garment
