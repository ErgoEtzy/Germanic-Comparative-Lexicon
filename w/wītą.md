## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wītą

### Etymology

Nominalization of Proto-Indo-European *weydós (“seen”). See also *wītiją (“punishment”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weydós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwiː.tɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ punishment
