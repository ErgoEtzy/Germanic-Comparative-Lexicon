## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wetmô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁wéd-mn̥ ~ *h₁ud-mén-s (“dowry”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁wéd-mn̥ ~ *h₁ud-mén-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwet.mɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ dowry, bride price
