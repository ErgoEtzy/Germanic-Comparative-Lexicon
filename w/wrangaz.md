# *wrangaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wrangaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wrangaz

### Etymology

An adjectival formation from *wringaną (“to wring, twist”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwrɑŋ.ɡɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ twisted, warped, bent, crooked, wry
2. _(reconstruction)_ wretched, wicked, unjust
