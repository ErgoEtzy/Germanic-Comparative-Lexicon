## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wōsą

### Etymology

Derived from Proto-Indo-European *wes- (“wet”). Cognate with Sanskrit वसा (vásā).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wes-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɔː.sɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ juice, moisture
