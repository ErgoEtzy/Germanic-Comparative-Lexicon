# *wibilaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wibilaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wibilaz

### Etymology

Possibly from *webaną (“to weave”) + *-ilaz. Suggested to be related to Lithuanian vãbalas (“beetle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *webʰ-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ beetle, weevil
