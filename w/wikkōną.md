## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wikkōną

### Etymology

From earlier paradigm *wikkōþi ~ *wigunanþi, from pre-Proto-Germanic *wik-nā́-ti ~ *wik-un-ánti, from Proto-Indo-European *weyk- (“to separate (out as holy)”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyk-

### Pronunciation
