# *wēgijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C4%93gijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wēgijaną

### Etymology

From *weganą (“to move”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *wéǵʰeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɛː.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to move
2. _(reconstruction)_ to give way, to yield
