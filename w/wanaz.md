# *wanaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wanaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wanaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁weh₂- (“to leave, abandon, give out”). Cognate with Latin vānus, Sanskrit ऊन (ūná).

From *wonh₁-os, from Proto-Indo-European *wenh₁- (“to wish, desire, love”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁weh₂-, *wenh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑ.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ lacking, deficient, absent
