# *wadą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wad%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wadą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wadʰom, *wh₂dʰóm, from *weh₂dʰ-. Cognate with Latin vadum (“ford”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wadʰom, *weh₂dʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑ.ðɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ shallow water, ford
