# *wela

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wela)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wela

### Etymology

Probably from earlier *welat, neuter accusative to an unattested adjective *welaz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *welh₁- (“to want”). Cognate with Sanskrit वर (vára, “better, best”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *welh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwe.lɑ/`

### Adverb

1. _(reconstruction)_ well (in a good manner)
