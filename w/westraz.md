## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *westraz

### Etymology

PIE word
 *wek⁽ʷ⁾speros
From Proto-Indo-European *wek⁽ʷ⁾speros (“evening, dusk”), with the -p- passing to -t- under the influence of the dentals in *austraz, *nurþraz, *sunþraz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wek⁽ʷ⁾speros

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwes.trɑz/`
