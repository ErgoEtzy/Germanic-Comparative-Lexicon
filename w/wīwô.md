[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C4%ABw%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wīwô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wéyh₁-ow-, from *weyh₁- (“to chase, pursue”). Possibly cognate with Ancient Greek ἱέρᾱξ (hiérāx, “hawk, eagle”) and αἰετός (aietós, “eagle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wéyh₁-ow-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwiː.wɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bird of prey (heron, kite, falcon)
