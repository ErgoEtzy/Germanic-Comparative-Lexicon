## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wissaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wid-tó-s.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wid-tó-s, *widtós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwis.sɑz/`

### Verb

1. _(form-of, participle, past, reconstruction)_ past participle of *witaną

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ certain, sure
2. _(reconstruction)_ known
