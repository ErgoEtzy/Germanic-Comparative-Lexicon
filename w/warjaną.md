# *warjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/warjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *warjaną

### Etymology

From an o-grade ablaut of Proto-Indo-European *wer- (“to heed”) + *-yeti. Cognate with Sanskrit वृणोति (vṛṇóti, “to cover, stop”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-yeti, *wer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑr.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to ward off, to defend against
2. _(reconstruction)_ to thwart, to stop
