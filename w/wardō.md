# *wardō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ward%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wardō

### Etymology

Nominal formation related to *wardaz (“guard”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑr.ðɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ protection, care
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ attention, heed
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ watch post, lookout
