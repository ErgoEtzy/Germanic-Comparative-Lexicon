# *wēdijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C4%93dijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wēdijaną

### Etymology

From *wēdiz (“cloth, garment”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *wedʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɛː.ði.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to clothe
2. _(reconstruction)_ to wear
