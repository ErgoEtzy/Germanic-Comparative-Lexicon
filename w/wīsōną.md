## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wīsōną

### Etymology

From *wīsaz (“wise”) + *-ōną (factitive verb–forming suffix); literally “make wise”.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwiː.sɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to visit
2. _(reconstruction)_ to show, guide, direct
