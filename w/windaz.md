[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/windaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *windaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂wéh₁n̥ts (“blowing; that which blows, i.e. air, wind”) (genitive *h₂uh₁n̥tés), present participle of *h₂weh₁- (“to blow”). Cognate with Latin ventus (“wind”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂wéh₁n̥ts

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwin.dɑz/`

### Noun
