# *wrīhaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wr%C4%ABhan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wrīhaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wreyḱ- (“to twist, weave, bind”). Cognate with Latin rīca (“veil, toile”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wreyḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwriː.xɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to wind, turn
2. _(reconstruction)_ to wrap, cover, shroud, envelop
