## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wiskaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wisgéh₂ (“flexible rod”) (cf. the feminine ō-stem variant *wiskō); cognate with Latin virga (“twig, shoot, rod”). The root may further derive from Proto-Indo-European *weys- (“to produce, procreate”), though this is uncertain.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weys-, *wisgéh₂
