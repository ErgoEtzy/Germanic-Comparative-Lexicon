## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *waldiz

### Etymology

Verbal adjective derived from *waldaną (“to rule”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂welh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑl.diz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ managing, manageable
2. _(reconstruction)_ having power, powerful
