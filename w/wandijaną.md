# *wandijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wandijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wandijaną

### Etymology

From *windaną (“to wind, wrap”) + *-janą (“causative suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wendʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑn.di.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to turn
