## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Wanhijô

### Etymology

From *wangaz (“field, meadow”) + *-ô (“-er”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ō, *wengʰ-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a member of the Vangiones, a Germanic tribe that fought alongside Ariovistus against Caesar
