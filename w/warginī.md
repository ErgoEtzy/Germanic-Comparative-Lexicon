**Reconstruction:** *warginī

### Etymology

From *wargaz + *-inī.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *werǵʰ-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ female criminal
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ female wolf
