## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wiganą

### Etymology

Originally a zero-grade present, from Proto-Indo-European *weyk- (“to fight”). Related to Latin vincō (“to conquer”), Old Irish fichid (“to fight”). Likely related to English weaken (“make yield”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwi.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb
