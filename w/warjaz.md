# *warjaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/warjaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *warjaz

### Etymology

Derived from *warjaną (“to defend against”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑr.jɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ defender
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ inhabitant; citizen
