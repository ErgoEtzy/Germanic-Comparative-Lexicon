# *wallijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wallijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wallijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *welH- (“to turn; wind; roll”). A causative or intensive formation from either *wellaną (“to well up”) + *-janą or *wallaną + *-janą.

From *wellaną (“roll”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *welH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑl.li.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to (cause to) well up, well over, boil
