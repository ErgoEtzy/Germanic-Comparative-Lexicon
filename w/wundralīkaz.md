[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wundral%C4%ABkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wundralīkaz

### Etymology

From *wundrą + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *wn̥h₁-tro-m

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwun.drɑ.ˌliː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ wondrous, marvellous
