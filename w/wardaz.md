[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wardaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wardaz

### Etymology

From earlier *warþaz, *werþaz, from Proto-Indo-European *wert- (“to turn”). Compare Latin versus.

From Proto-Indo-European *wor-tós, *wor-tús, from the root *wer- (“to watch, heed, guard”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wer-, *wert-, *wor-tós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑr.ðɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ turned
2. _(reconstruction)_ turned toward; facing
