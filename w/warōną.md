# *warōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/war%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *warōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wer- (“to become aware of, heed”). Equivalent to *waraz (“cautious”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑ.rɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to watch, to protect
