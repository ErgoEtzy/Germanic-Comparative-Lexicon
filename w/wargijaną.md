# *wargijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wargijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wargijaną

### Etymology

From *wargaz (“criminal”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *werǵʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑr.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to fordeem; condemn
2. _(reconstruction)_ to curse
