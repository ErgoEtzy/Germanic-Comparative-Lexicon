# *wītaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C4%ABtan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wītaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *weyd- (“to see; look; look for; find”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwiː.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to see; know
2. _(reconstruction)_ to go; go forth; part
3. _(reconstruction)_ to punish; torment
