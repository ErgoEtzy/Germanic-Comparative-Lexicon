[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wahsijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wahsijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂uks-é-ti, from *h₂weks- (“to grow”).

From *wahsą (“wax”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂uks-é-ti, *h₂weks-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑx.si.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to grow
