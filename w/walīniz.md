## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *walīniz

### Etymology

From *waljaną (“to choose”) + *-īniz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *welh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑ.liː.niz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a choosing, choice
