# *wallōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wall%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wallōną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *wolH-néh₂- from *welH- (“to turn, coil”). An iterative verb related to strong verb *wellaną (“to roll, toss”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *welH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑl.lɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to roll, turn
2. _(reconstruction)_ to wander, stroll
