# *wurhtaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wurhtaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wurhtaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wr̥ǵtós. Equivalent to *wurkijaną (“to work, to do work”) + *-þaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *werǵ-, *wr̥ǵtós

### Verb

1. _(form-of, participle, past, reconstruction)_ past participle of *wurkijaną

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ done; made; having happened
