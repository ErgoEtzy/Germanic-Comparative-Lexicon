# *wrībaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wr%C4%ABban%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wrībaną

### Etymology

Unknown; possibly from Proto-Indo-European *wreyp- / *wrīp-, from a labial extension of Proto-Indo-European *wer- (“to turn”), whence Ancient Greek ῥίπτω (rhíptō, “to throw, cast”), though this is disputed.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wer-, *wreyp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwriː.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to rub
2. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to wipe
