## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wadaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *weh₂dʰ-. Cognate with Proto-Italic *wāðō, Old Armenian գամ (gam).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weh₂dʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑ.ðɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to go, walk
2. _(reconstruction)_ to pass through, wade
