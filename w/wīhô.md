[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C4%ABh%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wīhô

### Etymology

From *wīhaz (“holy, sacred”) + *-ô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-os, *-ō, *weyk-, *wéyk-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwiː.xɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ one that is or makes holy, a priest
