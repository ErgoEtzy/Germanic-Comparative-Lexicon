# *wunāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wun%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wunāną

### Etymology

A stative verb derived from the adjective *wuna(n)- (“accustomed”), a formation related to *wanaz (“accustomed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ To be satisfied, be pleased with, enjoy
2. _(reconstruction)_ To be accustomed (to)
3. _(reconstruction)_ To remain, dwell, live, reside
