## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wīhaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wéyk-o-s, from *weyk- (“to choose, separate out, set aside as holy, consecrate, sacrifice”) + *-os. Cognate with Latin victima (“offering, sacrifice”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-os, *weyk-, *wéyk-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwiː.xɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ sacred, holy
