## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *waigō

### Etymology

Derived from Proto-Indo-European *weyk- (“to fight”). Cognate with Lithuanian viẽkas and Russian век (vek).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑi̯.ɣɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ strength
