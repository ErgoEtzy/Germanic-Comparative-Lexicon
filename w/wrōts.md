## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wrōts

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wréh₂ds (“root”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/wrɔːts/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ root
