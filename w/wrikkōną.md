[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wrikk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wrikkōną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *wriḱnéh₂-, a zero-grade iterative from Proto-Indo-European *wreyḱ- (“to twist, turn”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wreyḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwrik.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to twist, turn
