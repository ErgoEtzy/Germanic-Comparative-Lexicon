## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wīnabasją

### Etymology

From *wīną (“wine”) + *bazją (“berry”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *bʰeh₂-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ grape
