## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Wē₂landuz

### Etymology

Unknown; first element suggested to be related to Old Norse vél, from Proto-Germanic *wīlą (“craft; deceit”), however this is disputed. Second element may be from agent noun suffix Proto-Germanic *-andz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ Wayland, a great smith in Germanic legend, brother of Egil
