## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wadilą

### Etymology

From *wadą, from Proto-Indo-European *wadʰom. Cognate with Latin vadum (“ford”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wadʰom, *weh₂dʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑ.ði.lɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ ford
