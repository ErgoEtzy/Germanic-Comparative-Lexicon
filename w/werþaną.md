# *werþaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wer%C3%BEan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *werþaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wért-e-ti (“to turn”), with semantic shift "to turn" > "to become (turn into)". Typologically compare descendants of Latin tornō and *virō (see Italian viràre).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wert-, *wért-e-ti, *wértetor

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwer.θɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to become
2. _(intransitive, reconstruction)_ to come to be, to come into existence
3. _(intransitive, reconstruction)_ to happen, to come about
4. _(auxiliary, reconstruction)_ used to form the passive voice, together with a past participle
