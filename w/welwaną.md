[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/welwan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *welwaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *welh₃- (“to hit, strike”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *welh₃-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwel.wɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ To rob, plunder
