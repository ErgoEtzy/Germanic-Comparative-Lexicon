# *weltaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/weltan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *weltaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *welH- (“to turn, to wind”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *welH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwel.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to roll, toss
2. _(reconstruction)_ to turn
