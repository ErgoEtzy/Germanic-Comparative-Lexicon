# *wadaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wadaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wadaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *awAdʰ-, *audʰ- (“band, weave, plait”). Cognate with Latvian vads (“dragnet”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *awAdʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑ.ðɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ string; rope; chord
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ dragnet; trawl
