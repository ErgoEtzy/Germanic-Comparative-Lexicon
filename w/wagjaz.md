## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wagjaz

### Etymology

Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *weǵʰ- (“to move”). Cognate with Lithuanian vãgis (“peg, hook”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weǵʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑɣ.jɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ wedge
