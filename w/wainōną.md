## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wainōną

### Etymology

From *wai (“woe! (expression of anguish)”) + *-inōną (“verbal suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *wáy

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑi̯.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cry, to lament, to grieve
