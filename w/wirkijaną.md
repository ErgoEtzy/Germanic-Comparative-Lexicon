# *wirkijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wirkijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wirkijaną

### Etymology

From *werką (“work”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *werǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwir.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make, produce
2. _(reconstruction)_ to effect, make happen
