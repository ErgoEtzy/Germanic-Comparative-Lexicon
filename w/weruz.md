## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *weruz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wers- (“high, raised area, tuber, lump, blister”). Compare Latin varus (“pimple”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wers-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwe.ruz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ lip
