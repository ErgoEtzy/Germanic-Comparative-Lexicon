## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wallaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *wolH-n- (“to undulate”), an o-grade intensive formation. Both *wallaną and e-grade *wellaną (“to well up”) were probably created from the archaic zero-grade present *wulaną (“idem”).
