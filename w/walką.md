[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/walk%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *walką

### Etymology

From or related to *walkaną (“to roll”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *welH-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ tossing, turning
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ curl of hair
