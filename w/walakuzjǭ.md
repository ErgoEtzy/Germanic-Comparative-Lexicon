# *walakuzjǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/walakuzj%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *walakuzjǭ

### Etymology

From *walaz (“corpse; battlefield”) + *keusaną (“choose, decide”) + *-jǭ (“-er”, feminine agent noun suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *welh₃-, *ǵews-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑ.lɑ.ˌkuz.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ valkyrie, literally "chooser of the slain"
