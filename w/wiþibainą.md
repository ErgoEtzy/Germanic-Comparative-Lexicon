[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wi%C3%BEibain%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wiþibainą

### Etymology

From *wiþiz (“rope, thong”) + *bainą (“bone”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *wéh₁itis

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwi.θi.ˌbɑi̯.nɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ collar-bone
