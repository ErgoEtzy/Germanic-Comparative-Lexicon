## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *widuwǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁widʰéwh₂ (“widow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁widʰéwh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwi.ðu.wɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ widow
