# *wardijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wardijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wardijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wortéye- (“to turn”). Originally the causative of *werþaną (“to become”), although it was probably no longer recognised as such by Germanic times.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wert-, *wortéye-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑr.ði.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to destroy, ruin
