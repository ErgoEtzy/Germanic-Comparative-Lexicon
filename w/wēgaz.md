## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wēgaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *weǵhe- (“to drag, carry”), from *weǵʰ- (“to transport, bring”). See also *weganą (“to move”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weǵhe-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ wave
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ sea
