# *wlaitōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wlait%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wlaitōną

### Etymology

An o-grade iterative to *wlītaną (“to see”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwlɑi̯.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to look around, look for
