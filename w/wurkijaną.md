# *wurkijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wurkijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wurkijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wr̥ǵyéti, from *werǵ-, whence also *werką and *wirkijaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *werǵ-, *wr̥ǵyéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwur.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to work, to do work
2. _(reconstruction)_ to make
