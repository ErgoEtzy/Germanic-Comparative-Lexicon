# *walô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wal%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *walô

### Etymology

From *wala, *wela (“as wished, as desired, well”, adverb).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑ.lɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ wellness; weal
