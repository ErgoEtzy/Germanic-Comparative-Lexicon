# *wēraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C4%93raz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wēraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *weh₁ros (“true”), from *weh₁-. Cognate with Latin vērus (“true”), Irish fíor (“true”), Proto-Slavic *vě̀ra (“faith/belief”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weh₁ros

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɛː.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ true; reliable
2. _(reconstruction)_ friendly; kind
