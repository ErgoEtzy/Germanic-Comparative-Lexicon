[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C4%93ragangij%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wēragangijô

### Etymology

From *wēraz (“true”) + *gangijô (“goer”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵʰengʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌwɛːrɑˈɣɑŋ.ɡi.jɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ foreigner (wanting protection)
