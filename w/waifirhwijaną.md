# *waifirhwijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/waifirhwijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *waifirhwijaną

### Etymology

Likely from *wai (“expression of grief”) + *firhwijaną, the latter verb is not attested in any of the descendants.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑi̯.fir.xʷi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to howl, to wail
2. _(reconstruction)_ to lament, to mourn
