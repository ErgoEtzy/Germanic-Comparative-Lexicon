[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C4%93giz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wēgiz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *weganą (“to move, carry, weigh”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weǵʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɛː.ɣiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ weighing, balancing
2. _(reconstruction)_ able to be weighed
