## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wōhmaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wekʷ- (“to speak, sound”) + Proto-Indo-European *-mos

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-mos, *wekʷ-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ noise, sound, shout, voice
