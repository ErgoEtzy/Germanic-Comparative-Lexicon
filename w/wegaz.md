## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wegaz

### Etymology

According to Kroonen, a nominal derivative from *weganą (“to move, carry”).
Villanueva Svensson, while maintaining the root etymology, derives this noun more directly from an e-grade *-az adjective meaning "ridden (on)".

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weǵʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwe.ɣɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ way
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ path
