## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *writtōną

### Etymology

An iterative verb to the strong verb *wrītaną (“to write”), perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *wrid-néh₂-ti.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wrid-néh₂-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwrit.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to carve
2. _(reconstruction)_ to write
