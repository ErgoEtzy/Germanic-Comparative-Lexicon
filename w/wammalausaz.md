# *wammalausaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wammalausaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wammalausaz

### Etymology

From *wammaz (“spot, blemish”) + *-lausaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *lewh₁-, *wemh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑm.mɑ.lɑu̯.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ spotless, stainless
2. _(reconstruction)_ blameless
