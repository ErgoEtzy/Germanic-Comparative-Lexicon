## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wangiją

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *weng- (“to bow, be bent”). Related to *wangô (“cheek”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weng-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑŋ.ɡi.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ side of the face; cheek
2. _(neuter, reconstruction, for the head or cheek)_ pillow, cushion
