## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wulfaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *wúlpos, from Proto-Indo-European *wĺ̥kʷos (“wolf”). In detail, the development can be reconstructed as *wúlkʷos > *wúlpos > *wulfaz (which is comparable to *pénkʷe > *pémpe > *fimf). If *wúlkʷos hadn't changed to *wúlpos, it would look like **wulhwaz in Proto-Germanic. The relevant sound change, a kind of assimilation at a distance, is known in German as artikulatorischer Sprung.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wĺ̥kʷos
