# *wōdaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C5%8Ddaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wōdaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *woh₂t-u-s, from *weh₂t- (“to be excited”). Related to Latin vātēs.

From the adjective *wōdaz (“excited, frenzied, furious”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weh₂t-, *woh₂t-u-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɔː.ðɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ excited, energised, spirited
2. _(reconstruction)_ frenzied, obsessed
3. _(reconstruction)_ furious, angry
