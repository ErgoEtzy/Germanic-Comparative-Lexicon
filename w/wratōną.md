# *wratōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wrat%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wratōną

### Etymology

Obvious cognates are only found in Baltic. See Lithuanian rodyti (“to display, show, present”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwrɑ.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to wander, roam
2. _(reconstruction)_ to travel
