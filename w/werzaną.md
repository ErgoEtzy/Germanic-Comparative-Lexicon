# *werzaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/werzan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *werzaną

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *wers- (“to haul, drag, grind, hone, whet, polish”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wers-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwer.zɑ.nã/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause confusion, disarray
