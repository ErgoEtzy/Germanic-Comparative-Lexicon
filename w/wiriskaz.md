## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wiriskaz

### Etymology

From *weraz (“man”) + *-iskaz (“-ish”).

Substantivization of the adjective.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-iskos, *wiHrós

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ manly
