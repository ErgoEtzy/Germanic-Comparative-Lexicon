# *wōdijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C5%8Ddijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wōdijaną

### Etymology

From *wōdaz (“rage”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weh₂t-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɔː.ði.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to rage, to be furious
