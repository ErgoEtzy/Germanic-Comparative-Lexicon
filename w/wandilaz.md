## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wandilaz

### Etymology

From the root of *wandōną (“to wander”) + *-ilaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-dʰlom, *-e-ló-s, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *-lós, *-tlom, *-trom, *wendʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑn.di.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ wanderer
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ A Vandal
