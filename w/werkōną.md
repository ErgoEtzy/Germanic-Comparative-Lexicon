## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *werkōną

### Etymology

From *werką (“work”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɛr.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to work
