## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wakāną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *weǵ- (“to wake, be strong”). Equivalent to *wakaną (“to wake up”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

### Verb

1. _(intransitive, reconstruction)_ to wake, to be awake
