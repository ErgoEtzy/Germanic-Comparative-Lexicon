# *wēdiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C4%93diz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wēdiz

### Etymology

Presumedly the vṛddhi gerundive of *wedaną (“to join, bind, tie”), via an unattested adjective *wēdiz (“bound, joined, tied, sewn together”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wedʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɛː.ðiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ piece of cloth, garment
