## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *witą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *weyd- (“to know”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwi.tɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ knowledge
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ reason; sense; understanding
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ wit
