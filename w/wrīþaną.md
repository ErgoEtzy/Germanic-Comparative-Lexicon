[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wr%C4%AB%C3%BEan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wrīþaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wreyt- (“to twist, writhe”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wreyt-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwriː.θɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to weave, to twist
