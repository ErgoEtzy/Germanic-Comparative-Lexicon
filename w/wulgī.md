## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wulgī

### Etymology

From earlier *wulgwī, from Proto-Indo-European *wl̥kʷíh₂s (“she-wolf”), though with the suffix replaced with the ablauting *-ih₂. The delabialization is probably triggered by following *j, though the expected interference of Siever's Law may complicate this explanation. Compare Proto-West Germanic *wulbi.
