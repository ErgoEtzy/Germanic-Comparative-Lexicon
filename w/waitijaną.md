# *waitijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/waitijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *waitijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *woydéyeti, causative of *weyd- (“to see”). Equivalent to *wītaną (“to know”) + *-janą. Cognate with Sanskrit वेदयति (vedayati, “announce”) and Old Irish foídid (“send”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *woydéyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwai̯.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to know, to show
