# *wadją

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wadj%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wadją

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wodʰ-yo-m, from *wedʰ- (“to bind”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wedʰ-, *wodʰ-yo-m

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑð.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ wager, stake, pledge
