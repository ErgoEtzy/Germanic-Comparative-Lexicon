## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *winnilaz

### Etymology

From *winnaną (“to struggle, win”) + *-ilaz (“-er”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-dʰlom, *-e-ló-s, *-lós, *-tlom, *-trom, *wen-, *wenh₁-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ an older tribal name of the Lombards
