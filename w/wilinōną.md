# *wilinōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wilin%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wilinōną

### Etymology

From *wiljô + *-inōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *welh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwi.li.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to want, desire
2. _(reconstruction)_ to do a favour for someone, please
3. _(reconstruction)_ to expect
