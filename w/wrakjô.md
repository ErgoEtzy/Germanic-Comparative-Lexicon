## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wrakjô

### Etymology

From *wrekaną (“to pursue”) + *-jô.

- IPA: `/ˈwrɑk.jɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ an exile, one who is persecuted or displaced, fugitive
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a warrior
