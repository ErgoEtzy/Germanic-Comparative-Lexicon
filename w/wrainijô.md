## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wrainijô

### Etymology

From *wrainiz + *-jô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *wer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwrɑi̯.ni.jɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ that which is in heat or rutting, especially a stallion or stud
