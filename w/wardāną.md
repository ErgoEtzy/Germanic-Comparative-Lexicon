## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wardāną

### Etymology

From *warduz (“a guard”) + *-āną or *wardō (“protection”) + *-āną, both ultimately from the same root.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti, *wer-, *wor-tús

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to guard, protect, watch over
