## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wēnijaną

### Etymology

From *wēniz (“hope, expectation”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *wenh₁-, *wénh₁-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɛː.ni.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to hope, to expect
2. _(reconstruction)_ to believe, to imagine
3. _(reconstruction)_ to think (be of a certain opinion)
4. _(impersonal, reconstruction, with-dative)_ to think (be of a certain opinion)
