[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wahtw%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wahtwō

### Etymology

From *wakaną (“to wake, be awake”) + *-þwō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑxt.wɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ watch, vigil
