## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *weralīkaz

### Etymology

From *weraz + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *wiHrós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwe.rɑ.ˌliː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ manly, masculine, male
