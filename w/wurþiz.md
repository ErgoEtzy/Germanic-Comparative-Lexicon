## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wurþiz

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *h₂wŕ̥tis, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂wer- (“to raise”). Cognate with Ancient Greek ἄρσις (ársis, “raising”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂wer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwur.θiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ raising, elevation
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ mound
