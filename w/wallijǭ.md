## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wallijǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *welH- (“to turn; wind; roll”), synchronically analyzed as a nominal formation from *wallaną (“to well up”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *welH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑl.li.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ well
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ swirl
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ wave
