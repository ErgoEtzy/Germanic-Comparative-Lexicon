## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *waiþiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *weyh₁- (“to desire; move toward; pursue”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyh₁-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ catch
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ hunt
