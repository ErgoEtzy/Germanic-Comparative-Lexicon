# *wiljaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wiljan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wiljaną

### Etymology

From the optative of *wḗlh₁-ti ~ *wélh₁-n̥ti, Narten present from *welh₁- (“to choose, to want”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *welh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwil.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to want
