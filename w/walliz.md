[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/walliz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *walliz

### Etymology

A verbal adjective derived from *wallaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑl.liz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ welling, flowing, bubbling forth
