## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *weganą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wéǵʰeti, from *weǵʰ- (“to convey, transport”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wéǵʰeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwe.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to move, to carry
2. _(reconstruction)_ to weigh
