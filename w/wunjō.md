# *wunjō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wunj%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wunjō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wénh₁-ih₂ ~ wn̥h₁-yéh₂-s, from *wenh₁- (“to love”) + *-ih₂. Cognate with Latin venia (“indulgence, kindness”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-ih₂, *wenh₁-, *wénh₁-ih₂ ~ wn̥h₁-yéh₂-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwun.jɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ joy, delight, pleasure, lust
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ name of the rune ᚹ (w)
