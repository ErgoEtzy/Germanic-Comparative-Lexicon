## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wiþiz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *witís, generalised from the oblique stem of Proto-Indo-European *wéh₁itis.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wéh₁itis

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwi.θiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ cord, rope, thong
