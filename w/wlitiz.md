# *wlitiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wlitiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wlitiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wél-ti-s ~ *wl̥-téy-s, from *wel- (“to see”) + *-tis. Cognate with Latin vultus (“feature, appearance, visage, aspect, look”), Old Armenian գեղ (geł). Compare *wulþuz (“glory”). By synchronic analysis, a nominal formation from *wlītaną (“to see, look”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-tis, *wel-, *wél-ti-s ~ *wl̥-téy-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwli.tiz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ sight, appearance
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ countenance, face
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ look, aspect, blee
