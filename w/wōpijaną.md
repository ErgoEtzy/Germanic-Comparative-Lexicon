[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/w%C5%8Dpijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wōpijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *weh₂b- (“to call, shout, complain”). Cognate with Proto-Slavic *vabiti (“to lure”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weh₂b-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɔː.pi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cry out, to lament
