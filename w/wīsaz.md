## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wīsaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *weyd-s-ós (“knowledgeable”), from the s-stem noun *wéydos (“knowledge”), ultimately from the root *weyd- (“to know”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *weyd-s-ós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwiː.sɑz/`
