## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *watōr

### Etymology

From pre-Proto-Germanic *wódōr ~ *wodén(o)s, from Proto-Indo-European *wédōr, the collective of *wódr̥ ~ *udéns (“water”); e-grade *wed- was replaced with o-grade *wod- by leveling.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wed-, *wédōr, *wódr̥
