# *wanhaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/wanhaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *wanhaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *wónkos (“crooked, bent”), from *wenk- (“to bend, slant, incline, twist, turn”). Cognate with Sanskrit वङ्क (vaṅka, “curve, bend”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wónkos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈwɑ̃ː.xɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ bent, crooked, twisted
