# *jōsijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/j%C5%8Dsijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *jōsijaną

### Etymology

From otherwise unattested *jōsiz (“tumultuous”) + *-janą, which is the vṛddhi gerundive of *jasaną (“to be loud; to struggle”), from Proto-Indo-European *yes- (“to well, seethe, foam, ferment”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *yes-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈjɔː.si.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to stir up, excite
