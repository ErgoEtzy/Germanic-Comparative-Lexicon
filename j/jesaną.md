## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *jesaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *yes- (“to well, seethe, foam, ferment”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *yes-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈje.sɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to ferment
