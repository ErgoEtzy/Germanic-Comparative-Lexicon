# *jeukāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/jeuk%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *jeukāną

### Etymology

Likely from *yewǵ- (“to excite”). Possibly related to Proto-West Germanic *jukkjan. Middle High German jouchen (“to chase”) is likely related but seems to show a different ablaut grade.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *yewǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈjeu̯.kɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to fight, conquer, overcome
