## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *jut

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *yú, dual of *túh₂. Cognate with Lithuanian jùdu (“the two of you”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *yú

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/jut/`

### Pronoun

1. _(reconstruction)_ you two
