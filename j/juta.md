## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *juta

### Etymology

Compound of *ju (“already”, adverb), perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *h₂yew-, oblique of *h₂óyu (“long time”) (see the main entry for further discussion) and the Proto-Germanic *ta (“to, towards”), from Proto-Indo-European *do.
