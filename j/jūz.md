## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *jūz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *yúHs, plural of *túh₂.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *yúHs

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/juːz/`

### Pronoun

1. _(reconstruction)_ you (plural)
