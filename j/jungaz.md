# *jungaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/jungaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *jungaz

### Etymology

From earlier *yunhós (from which /h/ > /g/ due to Verner's law), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂yuHn̥ḱós (“young”), from *h₂yuh₁en- (“idem”). The shortening of the vowel from earlier *-uHun- is due to Osthoff's law.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂ey-, *h₂yuHn̥ḱós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈjuŋ.ɡɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ young
