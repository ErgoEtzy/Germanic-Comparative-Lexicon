## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *jazjaną

### Etymology

From *jesaną + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *yes-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈjɑz.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to ferment
