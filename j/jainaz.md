## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *jainaz

### Etymology

From *ja, of uncertain origin. Some have connected this to Proto-Indo-European *ís, *éy, and *Hyós. This would make it related to Old Church Slavonic онъ (onŭ, “he”), Latin idem (“the same”), Persian اون (“the, that, this, yon, he, she, it”) and Sanskrit एन (ena, “that; he”). This term could also be the nasal-extended a-stem prononimal form of *jai.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ís
