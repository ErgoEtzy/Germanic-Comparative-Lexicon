# *jērą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/j%C4%93r%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *jērą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *yóh₁r̥ (“year; harvest season”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *yóh₁r̥

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈjɛː.rɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ year
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ harvest
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ name of the rune ᛃ (j)
