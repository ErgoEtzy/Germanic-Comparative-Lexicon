## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *jehaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *yék-e-ti, from *yek- (“to speak”). Cognate with Proto-Italic *jokos (“utterance”), as well as perhaps Sanskrit याचति (yā́cati, “to ask, entreat”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *yek-, *yék-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈje.xɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to speak, say, express
