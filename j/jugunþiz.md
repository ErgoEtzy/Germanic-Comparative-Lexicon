# *jugunþiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/jugun%C3%BEiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *jugunþiz

### Etymology

From earlier *juwunþiz (possibly influenced in form by *dugunþō), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂yu-Hn̥-tí-s, from *h₂yéwHō (“young”). Compare Sanskrit युवति (yuvatí).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂ey-, *h₂yu-Hn̥-tí-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈju.ɣun.θiz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, feminine, reconstruction)_ the state of being young; youth
