# *jakkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/jakk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *jakkōną

### Etymology

From earlier paradigm *jakkōþi ~ *jagunanþi, from pre-Proto-Germanic nā-iterative (*-ōną) *yogʰ-nā́-ti ~ *yogʰ-un-ánti, likely rebuilt from eh₂ye-iterative Proto-Indo-European *h₁yoǵʰ-eh₂yé-ti, from metathesized *h₁eyǵʰ- (“to strive, chase”) + *-eh₂yéti.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-eh₂yéti, *h₁eyǵʰ-, *h₁yoǵʰ-eh₂yé-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈjɑk.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to hunt
