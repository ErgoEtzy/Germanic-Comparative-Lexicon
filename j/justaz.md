## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *justaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *yéwHs (“sap, juice, broth”), from *yewH- (“to blend, mix (food), knead”). Cognate with Latin iūs (“juice, broth”), Welsh uwd (“porridge”), and dialectal Lithuanian jū́šė (“fish soup”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *yéwHs

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈjus.tɑz/`

### Noun
