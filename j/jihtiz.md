# *jihtiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/jihtiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *jihtiz

### Etymology

From *jehaną (“to speak”) + *-þiz (“abstract noun suffix”). Particularly close cognate with Proto-Celtic *yextis (“language”).
The "pain" and "paralysis" senses have been considered linked to the "statement" sense, via an earlier meaning of "curse", but this is speculative, and the senses may well be of separate and potentially unknown origin.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *yek-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ statement, confession
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ pain, cramp
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ paralysis
