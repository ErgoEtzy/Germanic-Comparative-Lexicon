# *furhtijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/furhtijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *furhtijaną

### Etymology

A union of two related formations, a factitive from *furhtaz (“fearful, afraid”) + *-janą and denominative *furhtō (“fright, fear”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(o)-éh₂, *-eh₂, *-oh₁, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éh₂, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *pr̥k-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸurx.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to frighten, make afraid
2. _(reconstruction)_ to fear, be afraid
