[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fifald%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fifaldǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *pa(l)-pal- (“fluttering; butterfly”), a reduplication of Proto-Indo-European *pal- (“to touch, tap, pat”), possibly of onomatopoeic origin. Cognate with Latin pāpiliō (“butterfly”), Lithuanian pi̇́epala (“quail”), Proto-Slavic *perpelъ (“quail”), Lithuanian papelučkà (“moth, owlling”), Old Mazanderani پاپلی (pāp(e)lē, “butterfly”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pa(l)-pal-, *pal-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸi.ɸɑl.dɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ butterfly
