## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *flakurōną

### Etymology

From *flakuraz + *-ōną. Perhaps related to the origin of *flōkaną (“to strike, beat”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to beat repeatedly
2. _(reconstruction)_ to flutter
