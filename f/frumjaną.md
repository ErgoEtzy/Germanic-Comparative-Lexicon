## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frumjaną

### Etymology

From *fram (“from”), from Proto-Indo-European *pr-, *pro-, *perəm-, *prom- (“forth, forward”), from *por- (“forward, through”), *per-, with suffix *-janą. More at English furnish, from.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *per-, *pr-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to perform, promote
2. _(reconstruction)_ to accomplish
