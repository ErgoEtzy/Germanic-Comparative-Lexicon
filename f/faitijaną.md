# *faitijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/faitijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *faitijaną

### Etymology

From *faitaz (“fat”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peyH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑi̯.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to fatten, to make fat, plump up
