# *fērijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/f%C4%93rijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fērijaną

### Etymology

From *fērō (“danger, harm”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *per-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸeː.ri.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to terrify, to frighten
2. _(reconstruction)_ to slight
