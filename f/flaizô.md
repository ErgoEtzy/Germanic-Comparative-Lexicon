[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/flaiz%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *flaizô

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *plóh₁-ison-, comparative of *feluz, *felu (“much, a lot”). Related to Ancient Greek πλείων (pleíōn, “more”), Latin plūs (“more”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸlɑi̯.zɔːː/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ more
