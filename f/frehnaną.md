## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frehnaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *preḱ- (“to ask, woo”). Cognate with Lithuanian prašýti (“to ask, request”), Latin precor (“ask, beseech”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *preḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrex.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to ask, inquire
