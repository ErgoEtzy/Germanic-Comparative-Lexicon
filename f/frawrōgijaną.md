# *frawrōgijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/frawr%C5%8Dgijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frawrōgijaną

### Etymology

From *fra- + *wrōgijaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *pro-, *werh₁-, *wrōk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɸrɑˈwrɔː.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to accuse
2. _(reconstruction)_ to blame
