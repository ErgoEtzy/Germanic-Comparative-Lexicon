# *fastāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fast%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fastāną

### Etymology

From *fastuz (“firm”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti, *pastV-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to observe, hold firm to
2. _(reconstruction)_ to fast, to abstain from food and/or drink
