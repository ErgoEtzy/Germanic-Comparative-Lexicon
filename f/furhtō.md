# *furhtō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/furht%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *furhtō

### Etymology

From *furhtaz (“fearful”) + *-ō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(o)-éh₂, *-eh₂, *-oh₁, *-éh₂, *pr̥k-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸurx.tɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ fright, fear
