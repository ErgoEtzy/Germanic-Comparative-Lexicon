# *faimnijǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/faimnij%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *faimnijǭ

### Etymology

From *faimô ~ *faim(i)niz (“girl, young woman”) + *-jǭ (femininizing suffix), variant of *faimijō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peyH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑi̯m.ni.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ young woman, maid, maiden
