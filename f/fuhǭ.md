## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fuhǭ

### Etymology

Related to *fuhsaz. The variation *h ~ *gg is due to the original n-stem inflection pattern before the application of Kluge's law.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸu.xɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ vixen, female fox
