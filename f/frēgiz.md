# *frēgiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fr%C4%93giz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frēgiz

### Etymology

A vrddhi-gerundive from *frehnaną (“to announce, rumor”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrɛː.ɣiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ renowned, famous
