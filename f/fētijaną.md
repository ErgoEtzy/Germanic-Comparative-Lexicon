## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fētijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ped- (“to walk, stumble, fall”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ped-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɛː.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to adorn, to equip
2. _(reconstruction)_ to manage
