# *fraisaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fraisan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fraisaną

### Etymology

This old strong verb was replaced in the Northwest Germanic dialects.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrɑi̯.sɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(East, Germanic, reconstruction)_ to attempt, to dare (something dangerous)
2. _(East, Germanic, reconstruction)_ to tempt, to test
