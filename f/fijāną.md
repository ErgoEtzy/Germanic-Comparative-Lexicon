## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fijāną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *ph₁ih₁yé-, from an original stative *ph₁yéh₁-, to a ye-present formation from Proto-Indo-European *peh₁- (“to hate”). Cognate with Sanskrit पीयति (pī́yati, “to blame”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸi.jɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to hate
2. _(reconstruction)_ to dislike
