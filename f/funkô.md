[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/funk%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *funkô

### Etymology

Two possibilities have been proposed:
* From Proto-Indo-European *ph₂un-, oblique stem of *péh₂wr̥ (“fire”).
* From Proto-Indo-European *(s)peng- (“to shine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)peng-, *ph₂un-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸuŋ.kɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ spark
