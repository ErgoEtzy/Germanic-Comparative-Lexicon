# *falþaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fal%C3%BEan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *falþaną

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *polt-, with suffixal *-t-, from Proto-Indo-European *pel- (“to fold”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑl.θɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to fold
