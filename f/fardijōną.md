## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fardijōną

### Etymology

From *fardiz (“journey”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to journey, travel
2. _(reconstruction)_ to confront, go to battle
