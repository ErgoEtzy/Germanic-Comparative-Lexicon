# *fawaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fawaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fawaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ph₂w-os, from *peh₂w- (“few, little”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peh₂w-, *ph₂w-os

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑ.wɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ few
2. _(reconstruction)_ little (small amount of)
