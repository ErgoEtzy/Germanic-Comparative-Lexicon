## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fēgōną

### Etymology

Uncertain, either from:
* Proto-Indo-European *peh₂ḱ- (“secure, attach, strengthen”), for a similar semantic development see Lithuanian puõsti (“to decorate, make pretty”).
* Or from Proto-Indo-European *peḱ- (“joyful, pretty”), closely related to the strong verb *fehaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peh₂ḱ-, *peḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈfɛː.ɣɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to polish
2. _(reconstruction)_ to clean
3. _(reconstruction)_ to sweep
