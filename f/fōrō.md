## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fōrō

### Etymology

Functional o-grade ō-stem derived from *faraną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *per-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɔː.rɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, feminine, reconstruction)_ a going, traveling, journey
