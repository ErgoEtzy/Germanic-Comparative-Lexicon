# *faigijaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/faigijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *faigijaz

### Etymology

Probably related to one of the forms of *faihaz (“hostile”) or ("colored"), with the latter potentially indicating a meaning of "marked for death".

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑi̯.ɣi.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ fey, doomed to die
