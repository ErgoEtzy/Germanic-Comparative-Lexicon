## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *farjaną

### Etymology

Probably the causative-iterative to the lost strong verb *feraną; compare *faraną (“to travel, to go”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *per-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑr.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to take somewhere, to carry, to ferry
