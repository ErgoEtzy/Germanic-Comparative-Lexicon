# *feukaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/feukan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *feukaną

### Etymology

Probably backformed to the iterative *fukkōną (“to blow, gust”). From Proto-Indo-European *pewg- (“billow, bulge, drift”). Cognate with Latvian pūgà (“gust, blast, storm, blizzard”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pewg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸeu̯.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to blow
2. _(reconstruction)_ to be blown away
