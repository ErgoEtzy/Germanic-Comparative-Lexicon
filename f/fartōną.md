[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fart%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fartōną

### Etymology

An o-grade iterative verb to the strong verb *fertaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑr.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to fart
