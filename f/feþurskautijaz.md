# *feþurskautijaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fe%C3%BEurskautijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *feþurskautijaz

### Etymology

From *feþur- (“four”) + *skautijaz (“cornered”); an adjective derived from *skautaz (“corner, wedge”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *kʷetwóres

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɸe.θur.ˈskɑu̯.ti.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ square, rectangular
