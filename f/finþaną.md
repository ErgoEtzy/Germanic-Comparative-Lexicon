## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *finþaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *pent- (“to go, pass; path, bridge”). Akin to Proto-Slavic *pętiti, whence Slovene and Kajkavian Serbo-Croatian pripetiti se (“befall, happen”) .
For the meaning development compare Proto-Slavic *najьti > Russian найти́ (najtí), akin to Proto-Slavic *jьti > идти́ (idtí); Russian находи́ть (naxodítʹ), нахо́дка (naxódka), akin to ход (xod), ходи́ть (xodítʹ).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry
