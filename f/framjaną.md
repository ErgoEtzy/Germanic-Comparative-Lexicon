# *framjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/framjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *framjaną

### Etymology

From *framaz + *-janą, from Proto-Indo-European *pró-mo-s, from *pró (“toward, leading to”). Compare Latin framea (“spear”) probably from *framjō (literally “forward going one”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *per-, *pró-mo-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrɑm.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to promote, to further
