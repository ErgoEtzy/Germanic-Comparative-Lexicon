**Reconstruction:** *feþlą

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *péth₂lom, from Proto-Indo-European *peth₂-. Cognate with Ancient Greek πέταλον (pétalon).

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peth₂-
