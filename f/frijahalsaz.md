# *frijahalsaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/frijahalsaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frijahalsaz

### Etymology

From *frijaz (“free”) + *halsaz (“neck”), thus "having a free (unhampered by a yoke, chain, etc.) neck" > "being free, freedom".

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *preyH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸri.jɑ.ˌxɑl.sɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ freedom, liberty, the state of not being constrained or imprisoned
