## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fersnō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *tpḗrsneh₂ (“heel”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tpḗrsneh₂

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ heel
