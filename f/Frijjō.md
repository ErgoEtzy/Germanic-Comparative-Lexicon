[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/Frijj%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Frijjō

### Etymology

Possibly related to *frijōną, *frijaz + *-jō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrij.jɔː/`

### Proper noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ Frigg or Frigga, the Germanic goddess of love and home, wife of Odin. Identified in later times with the Roman goddess Venus.
