# *fēriz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/f%C4%93riz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fēriz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of otherwise unattested *feraną, from Proto-Indo-European *per- (“to go forth”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *per-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɛː.riz/`

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ crossing, able to cross
2. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction, of persons)_ able, fit for action or travel
3. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction, of ships)_ seaworthy
