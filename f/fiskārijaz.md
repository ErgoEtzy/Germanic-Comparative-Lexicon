## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fiskārijaz

### Etymology

From *fiskaz (“fish”) + *-ārijaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *pisk-ó-s, *péysks

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸis.kɑː.ri.jɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ fisher, fisherman
