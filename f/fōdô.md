## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fōdô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *peh₂- (“protect, guard, graze, feed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɔːðɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ food; nourishment
