# *flatją

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/flatj%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *flatją

### Etymology

From *flataz (“flat”) + *-ją (“neuter collective suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *pleth₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸlɑt.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ a level surface; level ground
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ the floor of a house
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ house; dwelling
