# *failijaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/failijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *failijaz

### Etymology

Uncertain; has been linked with Proto-Indo-European *peh₂- (“to protect”), but the -l- is unusual.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑi̯.li.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ true, friendly, familiar, good
