## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fuldō

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *pl̥th₂éh₂, feminine form of the adjective "flat", from Proto-Indo-European *pleth₂- (“flat”). In view of the Indo-Iranian cognates (Sanskrit पृथिवी (pṛthivī), Avestan 𐬞𐬆𐬭𐬆𐬚𐬡𐬍 (pər^əθβī)), Kroonen suggests it was used with the word for "land" in a sacral context.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pleth₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸul.dɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ earth, ground
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ field
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ the world
