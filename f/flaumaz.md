# *flaumaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/flaumaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *flaumaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *plowmos, from Proto-Indo-European *plew- (“to sail”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *plew-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸlɑu̯.mɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ stream; current; flow
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ flood
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ eddy
4. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ din, noise; commotion, tumult
5. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ joy, glee, mirth
