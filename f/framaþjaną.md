[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/frama%C3%BEjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *framaþjaną

### Etymology

From *framaþiz (“foreign, alien, strange”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *per-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrɑ.mɑθ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to alienate, to estrange
