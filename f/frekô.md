## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frekô

### Etymology

Derived from *frekaz (“active, bold, desirous, greedy”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ an active or eager man
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a bold warrior
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ wolf
