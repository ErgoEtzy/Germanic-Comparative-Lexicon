# *fōrijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/f%C5%8Drijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fōrijaną

### Etymology

From *faraną (“to go, travel”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *per-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɔː.ri.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause (one) to go; lead (someone; take (someone's) lead
2. _(reconstruction)_ to move, carry, bring
