# *fawjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fawjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fawjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *puH- (“rotten, foul”).

From Proto-Indo-European *pewH- (“to cleanse, purify”). Cognate Latin purus.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pewH-, *puH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑu̯.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to let it rot
