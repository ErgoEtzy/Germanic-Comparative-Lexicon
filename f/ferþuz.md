## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ferþuz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *pértus, equivalent to *feraną (“to cross”) + *-þuz. The oblique stem *pr̥téw- gave rise to *furduz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *per-, *pértus

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸer.θuz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, masculine, reconstruction)_ inlet, fjord
