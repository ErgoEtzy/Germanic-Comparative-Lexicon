# *furistaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/furistaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *furistaz

### Etymology

Superlative of *furai, *furi (“before”), from Proto-Indo-European *preh₂- (“idem”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *preh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸu.ri.stɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ first, foremost
