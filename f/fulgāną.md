# *fulgāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fulg%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fulgāną

### Etymology

Uncertain.
May be derived from a Proto-Indo-European *pelḱ- (“to turn, twist, wind”), which itself might have merged with another verb to form Proto-Germanic *felhaną (“to conceal”, but also “to go, proceed”). Both meanings of the latter may be explained by an original sense “to encircle” (> “to wrap around”; > “to roll along, trot”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pelḱ-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to follow
