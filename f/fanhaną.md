# *fanhaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fanhan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fanhaną

### Etymology

Different origins have been proposed:
* From Proto-Indo-European *ph₂-né-ḱ- ~ *ph₂-n̥-ḱ-, nasal-infix present of the root *peh₂ḱ- (“secure, attach, strengthen”). Related to *fōgijaną.
* From Pre-Germanic *ponḱ-, apparently a univerbation of Proto-Indo-European *h₂pó + *h₂n(e)ḱ- (“to reach, attain”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peh₂ḱ-, *ph₂-né-ḱ- ~ *ph₂-n̥-ḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑ̃ː.xɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to take, to seize
2. _(reconstruction)_ to capture, to catch
