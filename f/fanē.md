## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fanē

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂pó (“off, away”), originally an allative form of a root noun *h₂ép-s ~ *h₂p-ós + *-né-h₁ (“ablative suffix”).
Alternatively, this is the locus of the reinterpretation of the emphatic suffix *-nē as an ablative suffix, as alluded to by Dunkel.
See also German von for another slightly different theory.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂pó
