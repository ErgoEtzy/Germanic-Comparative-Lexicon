[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fad%C4%93r)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fadēr

### Etymology

From earlier *faþḗr, from Proto-Indo-European *ph₂tḗr (“father”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peh₂-, *ph₂tḗr

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑ.ðɛːr/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ father
