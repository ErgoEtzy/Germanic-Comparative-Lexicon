# *fulgijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fulgijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fulgijaną

### Etymology

A zero-grade present ye-stem, presumably pre-Germanic *pl̥kyéti, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)pelk-; see *fulgāną (“to follow”) for more.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)pelk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸul.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to follow
