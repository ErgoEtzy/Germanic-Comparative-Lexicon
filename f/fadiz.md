## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fadiz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *potís, via unexplained accent shift from Proto-Indo-European *pótis.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pótis

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑ.ðiz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ lord, master, commander, ruler
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ husband
