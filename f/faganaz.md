[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/faganaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *faganaz

### Etymology

The past participle of *fehaną (“to rejoice”), lexicalized as a distinct adjective.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑ.ɣɑ.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ glad
