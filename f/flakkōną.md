# *flakkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/flakk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *flakkōną

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *plh₂knéh₂-, an iterative formation to *flōkaną (“to slap, mourn”).

Considered by Kroonen to be a secondary o-grade iterative formation to zero-grade *flukkōną (“to fly”), from *fleuganą (“to fly”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pleh₂k-, *plewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸlɑk.kɔː.nɑ̃/`
