# *felgō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/felg%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *felgō

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *pelǵʰ- (“wheel rim”), cognate with Russian по́лоз (póloz, “skid, runner”), Slovene plâz (“plow sole, strip”). Alternatively, it could be connected with Proto-Germanic *felhaną (“to go, proceed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pelǵʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸel.ɣɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ felly, outer rim of a wheel
