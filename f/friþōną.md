[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fri%C3%BE%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *friþōną

### Etymology

Derived from *friþuz (“peace”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *preyH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸri.θɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make peace, pacify, keep the peace
