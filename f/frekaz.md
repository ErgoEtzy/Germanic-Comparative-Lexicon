## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frekaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *preg- (“to yearn, covet”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *preg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸre.kɑz/`

### Adjective
