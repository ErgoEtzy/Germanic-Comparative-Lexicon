## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fargijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂poh₂orké-ye-ti, itself from *h₂epo (“off, away”) + *h₂erk- (“to hold”); particularly close in formation to Latin porceō (“to prevent”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂epo, *h₂erk-, *h₂poh₂orké-ye-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑr.ɣi.ja.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to push (down)
