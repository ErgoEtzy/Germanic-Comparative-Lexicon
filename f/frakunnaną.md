## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frakunnaną

### Etymology

From *fra- + *kunnaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵneh₃-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɸrɑ.ˈkun.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to despise, scorn, mistrust
