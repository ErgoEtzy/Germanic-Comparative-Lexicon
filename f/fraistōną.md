# *fraistōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fraist%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fraistōną

### Etymology

A North Germanic variant of *fraisōną. Both forms have replaced the strong verb *fraisaną still extant in Gothic.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrɑi̯s.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to attempt, to try
