## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *furtuz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *pr̥d-u-. Zero grade of *perd- (“to fart loudly, pass gas, break wind”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *perd-, *pr̥d-u-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸur.tuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ fart
