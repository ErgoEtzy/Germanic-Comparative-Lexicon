## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ferrai

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *per-nóy (locative), from *per- (“to cross, pass”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *per-, *per-nóy

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɸer.rɑi̯/`

### Adverb

1. _(reconstruction)_ far
