## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fōgijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *poh₂ḱ-éye-, causative of the root *peh₂ḱ- (“to join, attach”), which implies an unattested strong *fahaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peh₂ḱ-, *poh₂ḱ-éye-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɔː.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to fit or join together
