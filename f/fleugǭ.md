# *fleugǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fleug%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fleugǭ

### Etymology

Nominal formation from *fleuganą (“to fly”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸleu̯.ɣɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ flying insect, fly
