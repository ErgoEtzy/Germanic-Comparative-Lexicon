# *frijōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/frij%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frijōną

### Etymology

Derived from Proto-Indo-European *priHós (“pleased, loved”). By surface analysis, *frijaz (“free”) + *-ōną. Cognate with Proto-Slavic *prijati (“to please, favor”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *priHós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸri.jɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to love
2. _(reconstruction)_ to free
3. _(reconstruction)_ to like
