# *finkaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/finkan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *finkaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *penk-. The current form with -k- was influenced by the iteratives, the presumed primary form *finhaną is unattested.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *penk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸiŋ.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sparkle, to burn
