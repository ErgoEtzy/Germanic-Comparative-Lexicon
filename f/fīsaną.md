## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fīsaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)peys-. Cognate with Latin spīrō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)peys-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to blow
2. _(reconstruction)_ to fart
