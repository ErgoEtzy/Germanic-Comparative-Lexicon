## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fanô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *peh₂n- (“something woven; weave; tissue; fabric; cloth”). Cognate with Latin pannus (“cloth, rag”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peh₂n-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑ.nɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ cloth; dook/duck (fabric)
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ banner; flag
