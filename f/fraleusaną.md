[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fraleusan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fraleusaną

### Etymology

From *fra- + *leusaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *lewHs-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɸrɑ.ˈleu̯.sɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to lose, to cease to have
