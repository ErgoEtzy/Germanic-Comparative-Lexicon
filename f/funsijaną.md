# *funsijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/funsijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *funsijaną

### Etymology

From *funsaz (“ready, willing”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *pn̥tstós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸun.si.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to predispose; make favourable; make ready
