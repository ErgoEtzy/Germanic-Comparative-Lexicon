# *fraetaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fraetan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fraetaną

### Etymology

From *fra- + *etaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁ed-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɸrɑ.ˈe.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to devour, eat up, consume
