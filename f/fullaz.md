## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fullaz

### Etymology

From earlier *pulnos, from Proto-Indo-European *pl̥h₁nós, the original past participle of *pleh₁- (“to fill”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pleh₁-, *pl̥h₁nós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸul.lɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ full (not empty)
2. _(reconstruction, strong)_ the whole, the entire
