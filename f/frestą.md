## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frestą

### Etymology

From an earlier *fres (“forward”), abstracted from a Proto-Indo-European *pres-sto-, composed of *per- (“forward, forth, over, beyond”) + *steh₂- (“to stand”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *per-, *pres-sto-, *steh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸres.tɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ an appointed time; date
