# *fatōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fat%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fatōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *pod-néh₂-ti, from *pod- (“to fetch”), and cognate with Old Church Slavonic попасти (popasti, “to fetch”). Whether the root is further connected to *ped- (“to walk, stumble, fall”) is unclear and semantically dubious.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ped-, *pod-néh₂-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑ.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to hold; seize
