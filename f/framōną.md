## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *framōną

### Etymology

From *fram (“forth, forward”) + *-ōną or *framaz (“forward, prominent”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *per-, *pro-, *pró, *prómos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸra.mɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to advance
2. _(reconstruction)_ to benefit
3. _(reconstruction)_ to be beneficial, to be of use
