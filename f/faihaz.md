# *faihaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/faihaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *faihaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *peyḱ- (“to be angry, hostile, evil; enemy”).

From Proto-Indo-European *póyḱos, from *peyḱ- (“to mark, color, decorate, embroider”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peyḱ-, *póyḱos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑi̯.xɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ hostile, criminal
