# *flutōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/flut%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *flutōną

### Etymology

The iterative of *fleutaną (“to float, stream”), from Proto-Indo-European *plewd- (“to float, swim, fly”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *plewd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸlu.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to float; drift
