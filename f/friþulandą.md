# *friþulandą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fri%C3%BEuland%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *friþulandą

### Etymology

From *friþuz + *landą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-om, *-tus, *lendʰ-, *preyH-, *priHós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸri.θu.ˌlɑn.dɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ friendly land, country with which one is at peace
