# *fradraganą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fradragan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fradraganą

### Etymology

From *fra- (“for-”) + *draganą (“to draw”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *dʰrógʰ-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɸrɑ.ˈdrɑ.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to draw away; seduce
2. _(reconstruction)_ to pull through; endure
