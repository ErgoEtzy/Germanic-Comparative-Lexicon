# *fōdīniz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/f%C5%8Dd%C4%ABniz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fōdīniz

### Etymology

From *fōdijaną (“to feed, make to eat”) + *-īniz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɔː.ðiː.niz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a feeding, nourishment
