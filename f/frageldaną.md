# *frageldaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/frageldan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frageldaną

### Etymology

From *fra- + *geldaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʰeldʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɸrɑ.ˈɣel.dɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to pay up; remunerate
2. _(reconstruction)_ to pay back; repay; restore
