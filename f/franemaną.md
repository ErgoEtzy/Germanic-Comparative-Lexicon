[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/franeman%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *franemaną

### Etymology

From *fra- + *nemaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *nem-, *németi, *ḱóm

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɸrɑ.ˈne.mɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to take away, plunder
