# *framaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/framaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *framaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *prómos (“leading”), derived from Proto-Indo-European *pró.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pró, *prómos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrɑ.mɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ forward, forthright
