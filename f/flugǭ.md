## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *flugǭ

### Etymology

From *fleuganą, from Proto-Indo-European *plewk- (“to fly”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *plewk-

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, feminine, reconstruction)_ flying insect, fly
