## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *furaikwemaną

### Etymology

From *furai- + *kwemaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʷémt, *per-, *preh₂-, *pr̥h₂-éy

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to come before, to precede
