[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/falhuwarjaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *falhuwarjaz

### Etymology

From *falhuz + *warjaz. The first element means "fallow", the second "dweller".

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a member of the Falchovarii
