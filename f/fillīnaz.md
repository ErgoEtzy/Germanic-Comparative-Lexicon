# *fillīnaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fill%C4%ABnaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fillīnaz

### Etymology

From *fellą (“skin”) + *-īnaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸil.liː.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ made of skin, leathern
