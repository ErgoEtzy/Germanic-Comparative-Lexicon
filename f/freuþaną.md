# *freuþaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/freu%C3%BEan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *freuþaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *prewtH- (“to splash”). Related to Sanskrit प्रोथति (próthati, “to snort”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *prewtH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸreu̯.θɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to froth
