## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fōr

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *péh₂wr̥ ~ *ph₂wéns (“fire”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *péh₂wr̥

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɔːr/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ fire
