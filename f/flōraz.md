# *flōraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fl%C5%8Draz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *flōraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *pleh₂ros (“floor”), from *pleh₂- (“flat”). Cognate with Proto-Celtic *ɸlārom (“floor”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pleh₂ros

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸlɔː.rɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ ground
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ floor
