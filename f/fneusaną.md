# *fneusaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fneusan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fneusaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *pnew-, but presumably interpreted as onomatopoeic judging from the variety in the initial consonants.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pnew-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸneu̯.sɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sneeze
