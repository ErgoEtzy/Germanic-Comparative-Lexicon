[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/frij%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frijō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *priHéh₂, feminine form of *priHós, from the root *preyH- (“to love”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *priHéh₂, *priHós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸri.jɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, feminine, reconstruction)_ loved one, spouse, wife
