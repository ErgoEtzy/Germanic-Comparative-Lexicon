[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/frakun%C3%BEaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frakunþaz

### Etymology

Equivalent to *frakunnaną (“to despise”) + *-þaz, or *fra- + *kunþaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ǵneh₃-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrɑ.ˌkun.θɑz/`

### Verb

1. _(form-of, participle, past, reconstruction)_ past participle of *frakunnaną

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ despised, wicked, infamous
