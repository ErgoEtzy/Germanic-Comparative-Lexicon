## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *funsaz

### Etymology

From earlier *fundsaz, from Proto-Indo-European *pn̥tstós, from *pent- (“to tread, go”) + *-tós, with epenthetic -s- between dentals.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pn̥tstós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸun.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ ready, willing
