## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *finnaz

### Etymology

Disputed; possible nominalization of unattested *finniz, from earlier *fēnþniz, vṛddhi gerundive of *fanþijaną (“to travel by foot, walk”). Alternatively, according to Huld (1994), from earlier *feznaz (“(free) man”), from Proto-Indo-European *pes- (compare Hittite 𒁉𒌍𒈾𒀸 (pé-eš-na-aš /⁠pešnaš⁠/)).
