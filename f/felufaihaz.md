# *felufaihaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/felufaihaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *felufaihaz

### Etymology

Compound of *felu + *faihaz. Compare Ancient Greek πολυποίκιλος (polupoíkilos, “much variegated, manifold”), Sanskrit पुरुपेश (purupeśa, “multiform”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *pelh₁-, *peyḱ-, *pélh₁us, *póyḱos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸe.lu.ˌɸɑi̯.xɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(East, Germanic, reconstruction)_ manifold, multicolored
