# *furdiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/furdiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *furdiz

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *pr̥tís, a ti-stem formation to a lost strong verb *feraną (“to cross, come to a place”), compare *fēriz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *per-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸur.ðiz/`

### Noun

1. _(East, Germanic, feminine, reconstruction)_ a crossing, going across, coming to a place
