# *friskaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/friskaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *friskaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *preyskos (“fresh, unsalted”), and cognate with Proto-Balto-Slavic *prēˀskas (“unleavened, unfermented, unsalted”) (though there are phonetic difficulties).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *preyskos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸris.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ fresh
2. _(reconstruction, of water)_ not salty, unsalted
