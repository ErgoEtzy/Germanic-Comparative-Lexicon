## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fleutaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *plewd- (“to flow, run”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *plewd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸleu̯.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to float, stream
