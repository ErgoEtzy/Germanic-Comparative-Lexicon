# *flōduz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fl%C5%8Dduz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *flōduz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ploh₃tús (“overflow, deluge”), from the root *pleh₃(w)- (“to flow, run”). Synchronically analyzed as a tu-stem nominal formation from *flōaną (“to flow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *plew-, *ploh₃tús

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸlɔː.ðuz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, masculine, reconstruction)_ river
2. _(feminine, masculine, reconstruction)_ flood
