## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fralētaną

### Etymology

From *fra- + *lētaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *leh₁d-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrɑ.ˌlɛː.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to leave, dismiss
