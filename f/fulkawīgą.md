## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fulkawīgą

### Etymology

From *fulką (“folk”) + *wīgą (“battle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂el-, *pl̥h₁-gós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸul.kɑ.ˌwiː.ɣɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ warfare
