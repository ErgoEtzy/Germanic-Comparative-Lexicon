# *furaikweþaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/furaikwe%C3%BEan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *furaikweþaną

### Etymology

From *furai- + *kweþaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *preh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɸu.rɑi̯.ˈkʷe.θɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to foretell, prophesy, predict, say beforehand
