[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/f%C5%8Dstr%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fōstrōną

### Etymology

Derived from *fōstrą (“fostering”) + *-ōną

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *peh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɔːs.trɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to foster, nurture
