## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fullą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *pl̥-nó-, from *pel(w)- (“a kind of vessel”). Cognate with Ancient Greek πέλλα (pélla, “milk pail, drinking bowl, goblet”), πελλίς (pellís), πελίς (pelís, “basin”), Old Church Slavonic поль (polĭ, “scoop”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pl̥-nó-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸul.lɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ cup, beaker
