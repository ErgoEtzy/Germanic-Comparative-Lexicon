## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *flahaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *plok-. Kroonen connects with Ancient Greek πλάξ (pláx, “plane, plain, something flat”), from Proto-Indo-European *pleh₂- (“flat, broad, plain”), sharing several Germanic cognates with Proto-Germanic *flaką (“something flat”); more at English flake.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pleh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸlɑ.xɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to skin, to flay
