# *flutą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/flut%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *flutą

### Etymology

From earlier *pludom, from Proto-Indo-European *plewd- (“to fly; flow; run”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *plewd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸlu.tɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ A body or mass of water.
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ The movement of water; flowing.
