## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frawaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *prow-os, likely from Proto-Indo-European *prew- (“to jump, hop”). Cognate with Sanskrit प्रवते (právate, “to jump, leap”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *prew-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrɑ.wɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ happy
2. _(reconstruction)_ energetic
