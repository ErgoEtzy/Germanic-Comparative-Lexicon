[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fijandz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fijandz

### Etymology

Present participle of *fijāną, from Proto-Indo-European *peh₁- (“to hate”), equivalent to *fijāną + *-ndz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸi.jɑndz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ enemy, fiend
