## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fraiwą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)per-, *(s)prey- (“to strew, sow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)per-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈfrɑi̯.wɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ seed, semen
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ gender
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ offspring
