## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frawardijaną

### Etymology

From *frawerþaną + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wert-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɸrɑ.ˈwɑr.ði.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to destroy
