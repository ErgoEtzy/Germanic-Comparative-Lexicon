## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Frōdô

### Etymology

From the masculine definite of *frōdaz (“wise”), literally "the wise."

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pret-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrɔː.ðɔːː/`

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
