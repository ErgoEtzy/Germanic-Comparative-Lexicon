## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fraberaną

### Etymology

From *fra- (“off, away (from), completely”) + *beraną (“to bear, carry”, verb).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No PIE form is listed or machine-resolvable in this snapshot.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɸrɑ.ˈbe.rɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to hold back or keep away (from); restrain or refrain (oneself) from
2. _(reconstruction)_ to bear throughout; endure
