# *frawurkijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/frawurkijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frawurkijaną

### Etymology

From *fra- + *wurkijaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *werǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɸrɑ.ˈwur.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to work ill, do wrong; to sin
