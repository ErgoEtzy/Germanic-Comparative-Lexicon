## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frawerþaną

### Etymology

From *fra- + *werþaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *wert-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɸrɑˈwer.θɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to perish
