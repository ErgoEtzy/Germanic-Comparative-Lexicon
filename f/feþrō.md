[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fe%C3%BEr%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *feþrō

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *péth₂reh₂, extended from the nominative stem of the Proto-Indo-European heteroclitic noun *péth₂r̥ ~ pth₂én-, from *peth₂- (“to fly”). Compare Latin penna from Proto-Italic *petnā, which is a similar extension but from the oblique stem.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *péth₂r̥

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸeθ.rɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ feather
