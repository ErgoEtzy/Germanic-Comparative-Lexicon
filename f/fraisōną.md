# *fraisōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/frais%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fraisōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *per- (“to go forth, risk, try”). Equivalent to *fraisō (“fear”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *per-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrɑi̯.sɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to attempt; try
