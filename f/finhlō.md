[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/finhl%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *finhlō

### Etymology

From *finhlōną + *-ō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peyḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸĩx.lɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ rasp, file
