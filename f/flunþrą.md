## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *flunþrą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *pl-n̥-h₂-tro-m, from nasal-infixed *pleh₂- (“flat”) + *-trom.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-trom, *pl-n̥-h₂-tro-m, *pleh₂-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ something flat
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ board, plank
