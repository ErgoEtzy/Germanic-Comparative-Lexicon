# *fangōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fang%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fangōną

### Etymology

From *fangą (“catch”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peh₂ḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑŋ.ɡɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to catch, capture
