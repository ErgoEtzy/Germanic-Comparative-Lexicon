# *finhlōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/finhl%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *finhlōną

### Etymology

From unattested strong verb *finhaną + *-lōną, from Proto-Indo-European *pi-né-k-ti ~ *pi-n-k-énti, nasal-infix of *peyḱ- (“to engrave, adorn, shape”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peyḱ-, *pi-né-k-ti ~ *pi-n-k-énti

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to file, polish
