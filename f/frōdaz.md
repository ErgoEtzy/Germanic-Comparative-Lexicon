[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fr%C5%8Ddaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frōdaz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *fraþjaną (“to understand”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pret-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrɔː.ðɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ wise, experienced, knowledgeable
