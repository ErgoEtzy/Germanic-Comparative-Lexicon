[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/feluwurdijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *feluwurdijaną

### Etymology

From *felu + *wurdijaną or *felu + *wurdą + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *pelh₁-, *pélh₁us, *werdʰom, *werh₁-, *wr̥dʰom

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸe.lu.ˌwur.ði.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to use many words, be talkative
