## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fehu

### Etymology

From early *péku, from Proto-Indo-European *péḱu (“livestock”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *péḱu

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸe.xu/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ livestock, cattle
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ property, wealth
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ name of the rune ᚠ (f)
