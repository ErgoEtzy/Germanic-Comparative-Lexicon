## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *framaþiz

### Etymology

From an unattested-in-descendants *framaþaz, related to *framaz (“forward, prominent”), *fram (“forward”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *per-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrɑ.mɑ.θiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ foreign, alien, strange
