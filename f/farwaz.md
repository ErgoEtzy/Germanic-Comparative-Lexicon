# *farwaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/farwaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *farwaz

### Etymology

From earlier *fargwaz, from Pre-Germanic *porḱwós, from Proto-Indo-European *perḱ- (“spotted”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *perḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑr.wɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ colored, colorful
