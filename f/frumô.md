# *frumô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/frum%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frumô

### Etymology

From *fr- (“for, forward”) + *-umô (“superlative”), from Proto-Indo-European *pr- (“before, formerly; through, throughout”) (see *per-) + Pre-Germanic *-m̥Hon-, from Proto-Indo-European *-mHo-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-mHo-, *-tm̥mos, *per-, *pr-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸru.mɔːː/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ first
