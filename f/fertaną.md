# *fertaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fertan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fertaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *perd- (“to fart”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *perd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸer.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to break wind, fart
