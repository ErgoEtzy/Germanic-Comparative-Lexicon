## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fedwōr tigiwiz

### Etymology

From *fedwōr (“four”) + *tigiwiz, plural of *teguz (“group of ten”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸeð.wɔːr ˈti.ɣi.wiz/`

### Numeral

1. _(reconstruction, with-genitive)_ forty
