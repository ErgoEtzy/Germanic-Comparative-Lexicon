## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *friþulausaz

### Etymology

From *friþuz + *-lausaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tus, *lewh₁-, *preyH-, *priHós

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ lacking peace, peaceless
