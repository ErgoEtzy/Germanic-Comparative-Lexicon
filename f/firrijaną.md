## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *firrijaną

### Etymology

From *ferrai (“far”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *per-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸir.ri.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to remove, take away, deprive
2. _(reconstruction)_ to depart from, withdraw
