## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *frijōndlīkaz

### Etymology

From *frijōndz + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-onts, *priHós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸri.jɔːnd.liːkɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ friendlike; friendly
