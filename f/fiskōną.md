# *fiskōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fisk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fiskōną

### Etymology

From *fiskaz (“fish”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peysḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸis.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to fish, to catch fish
