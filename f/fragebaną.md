## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fragebaną

### Etymology

From *fra- + *gebaną. Possibly calqued from Late Latin perdōnō, or vice-versa.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʰe, *gʰebʰ-, *gʰebʰ-e-ti, *gʰo-, *h₁ep-, *ḱóm

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɸrɑ.ˈɣe.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to give, grant
2. _(reconstruction)_ to forgive
