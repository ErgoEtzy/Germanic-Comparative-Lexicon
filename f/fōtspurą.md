# *fōtspurą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/f%C5%8Dtspur%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fōtspurą

### Etymology

From *fōts (“foot”) + *spurą (“track”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ trail of footprints, visible footsteps
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ footprint
