# *fehaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fehan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fehaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *peḱ- (“joyful, pretty”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *peḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸe.xɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to rejoice, be glad
