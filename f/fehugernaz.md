# *fehugernaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fehugernaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fehugernaz

### Etymology

From *fehu + *gernaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʰer-, *péḱu, *ǵʰerH-no-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸe.xu.ˌɣer.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ desirous of money, avaricious, covetous
