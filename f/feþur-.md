## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *feþur-

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *kʷétwr̥-, probably resegmented as *kʷétur-, combining form of Proto-Indo-European *kʷetwóres.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kʷetwóres

### Pronunciation

### Prefix

1. _(morpheme, reconstruction)_ four, pertaining to four
