## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fuþiz

### Etymology

Possibly rebuilt from an-stem *feuþô ~ *futtaz, from pre-Proto-Germanic *péut-ō ~ *put-nós, from Proto-Indo-European *pewt-, cognate with Latin pūtos (“penis”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pewt-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸu.θiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ vagina
