# *fraþjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fra%C3%BEjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fraþjaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *prot-, o-grade of Proto-Indo-European *pret- (“to understand”). Compare, in particular, Lithuanian pràsti (“to become accustomed to”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pret-, *prot-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸrɑθ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(East, Germanic, reconstruction)_ to understand
