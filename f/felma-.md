# *felma-

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/felma-)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *felma-

### Etymology

Probably an original men-stem from Proto-Indo-European *pélmō which was re-formed as if from *pél-mo-s, from the root *pel- (“hide, skin; to cover, wrap”). Cognate with Ancient Greek πέλμα (pélma, “sole of the foot”), which is from *pélmn̥.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pel-, *pélmō

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸel.maz/`
- IPA: `/ˈɸel.mɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ covering, skin, hide, fleece
