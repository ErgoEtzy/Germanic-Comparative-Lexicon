[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/furaisagjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *furaisagjaną

### Etymology

From *furai- + *sagjaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *per-, *preh₂-, *pr̥h₂-éy, *sekʷ-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to say beforehand
2. _(reconstruction)_ to predict
