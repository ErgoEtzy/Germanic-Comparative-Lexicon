## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *flukkōną

### Etymology

From earlier paradigm *flukkōþi ~ *flugunanþi, from pre-Proto-Germanic *pluk-nā́-ti ~ *pluk-un-ánti, iterative to *fleuganą (“to fly”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *plewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸluk.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to fly, flutter
