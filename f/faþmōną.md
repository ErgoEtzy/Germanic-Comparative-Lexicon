## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *faþmōną

### Etymology

From *faþmaz (“fathom, outstretched arms”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *peth₂-, *poth₂-mo-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑθ.mɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to fathom, to measure with outstretched arms
2. _(reconstruction)_ to embrace, to hug
