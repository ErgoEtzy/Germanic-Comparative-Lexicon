## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *furibeudaną

### Etymology

From *furi- + *beudaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bʰewdʰ-, *preh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɸu.ri.ˈbeu̯.ðɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to forbid, prohibit, command against (doing something)
