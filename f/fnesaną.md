**Reconstruction:** *fnesaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *pnew- (“to breathe, pant, gasp; snort, sneeze”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

1. _(reconstruction)_ to breathe heavily, pant, gasp
