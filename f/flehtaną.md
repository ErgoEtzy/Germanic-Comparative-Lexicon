# *flehtaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/flehtan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *flehtaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *pleḱt-, an extension of Proto-Indo-European *pleḱ- (“to twine”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pleḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸlex.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to plait, to weave, to braid
