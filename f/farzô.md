## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *farzô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *perh₃- (“to provide”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *perh₃-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑr.zɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bull; ox
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bullock; steer
