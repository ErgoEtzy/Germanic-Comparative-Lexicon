# *furhtaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/furhtaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *furhtaz

### Etymology

A zero-grade derivation in *-to- from Proto-Indo-European *pr̥k- (“fear”). Compare Tocharian B pärsk-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pr̥k-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸurx.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ fearful, afraid
