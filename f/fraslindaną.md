[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fraslindan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fraslindaną

### Etymology

From *fra- + *slindaną. For the semantics, compare *fraetaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *sel-, *slendʰ-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to gobble up, devour, eat greedily
