# *fandōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/fand%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *fandōną

### Etymology

An o-grade iterative derived from *finþaną (“to find”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *pent-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸɑn.dɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to seek, search for, examine
