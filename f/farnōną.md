# *farnōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/farn%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *farnōną

### Etymology

Unclear, but likely related to the strong verb *faraną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈfɑr.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be lucky, have luck
2. _(reconstruction)_ to succeed
