# *furai

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/furai)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *furai

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *pr̥h₂-éy, an innovative dative formation from *preh₂-. Compare Latin prae, Ancient Greek παραί (paraí) and Sanskrit पुरे (pure). Alternatively from *pr̥h₃-éy or *pr̥h₃-óy, from *perh₃-. In any case, ultimately from the root *per-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *per-, *preh₂-, *pr̥h₂-éy

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɸu.rɑi̯/`

### Preposition

1. _(reconstruction)_ before (spatial and temporal)
2. _(reconstruction)_ for (benefactive)
3. _(reconstruction)_ for (causal): because of

### Adverb

1. _(reconstruction)_ before, in front
