## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Hrōþiwulfaz

### Etymology

From *hrōþiz (“praise, fame, glory, renown”) + *wulfaz (“wolf”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *kreH-, *wĺ̥kʷos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxrɔː.θi.ˌwul.ɸɑz/`

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
