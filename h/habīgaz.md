# *habīgaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hab%C4%ABgaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *habīgaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kap- (“to take, grasp, have, hold”), equivalent to *habjaną + *-īgaz. Compare Latin capāx (“large, wide, roomy, spacious, capacious”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kap-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑ.βiː.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ heavy, weighty
2. _(reconstruction)_ grave, severe
