# *hlainijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hlainijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlainijaną

### Etymology

From *hlināną (“to lean”) + *-janą (causative suffix), the former a stative of an old nasal present from Proto-Indo-European *ḱley- (“to lean”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱley-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxlɑi̯.ni.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to lean, cause to lean
