## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlūtraz

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *ḱluHdrós, equivalent to an unattested *hleutaną (“to clean”) + *-raz, the former being an extension in *-d- from Proto-Indo-European *ḱlewH- (“to clean”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱlewH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxluː.trɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ clean, clear, pure
