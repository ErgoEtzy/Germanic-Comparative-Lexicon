# *hanþaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/han%C3%BEaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hanþaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *ḱóntos, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱent- (“to stab”). Related to *hinþaną (“to reach for, obtain”); see there for more cognates (which Kroonen gives for this entry, but not for *hinþaną).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱent-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑn.θɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ sharp, clever
