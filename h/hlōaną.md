# *hlōaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hl%C5%8Dan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlōaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kloh₁-ye-, frequentative form of *kleh₁- (“to call, shout”), from *kelh₁- (“to shout”). Cognate with Latin clāmō (“cry out, shout, claim”), Ancient Greek καλέω (kaléō, “call, summon, invite”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kelh₁-, *kloh₁-ye-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/xlɔː.ɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shout
2. _(reconstruction)_ to bellow; roar
