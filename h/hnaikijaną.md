# *hnaikijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hnaikijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hnaikijaną

### Etymology

Unclear, possibly related to *hnītaną. Could be a causative verb derived from an unattested *hnīkaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxnɑi̯.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to strike
2. _(reconstruction)_ to kill
