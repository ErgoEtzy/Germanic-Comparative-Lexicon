# *harjanaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/harjanaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *harjanaz

### Etymology

From *harjaz (“army, warband”) + *-anaz. Cognate with Ancient Greek κοίρανος (koíranos, “king, ruler, lord”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(e)-ós, *(ó)-os, *-os, *-ós, *-ō, *ker-, *kóryos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑr.jɑ.nɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ leader of a warband or army
2. _(masculine, reconstruction, as a proper noun)_ a name for *Wōdanaz
