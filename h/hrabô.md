[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hrab%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrabô

### Etymology

Apparently from a quasi-Proto-Indo-European *krobʰ-on-, of likely onomatopoeic origin, similar to that of Proto-Indo-European *ḱer-, the latter whence Latin corvus, Proto-Balto-Slavic *śárˀkāˀ.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *krobʰ-on-, *ḱer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxrɑ.βɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ raven
