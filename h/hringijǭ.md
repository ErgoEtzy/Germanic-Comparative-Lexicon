# *hringijǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hringij%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hringijǭ

### Etymology

From *hringaz + *-ijǭ (diminutive suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)ker-, *(s)kreg-, *(s)krengʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxriŋ.ɡi.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ ringlet, loop
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ brooch, clasp
