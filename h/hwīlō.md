# *hwīlō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hw%C4%ABl%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwīlō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kʷih₁-l-éh₂, from *kʷyeh₁- (“to rest”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kʷih₁-l-éh₂, *kʷyeh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷiː.lɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ time, period of time, while
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ period of rest, break, pause
