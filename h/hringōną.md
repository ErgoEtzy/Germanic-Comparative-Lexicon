# *hringōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hring%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hringōną

### Etymology

From *hringaz (“a ring, a circle”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)ker-, *(s)kreg-, *(s)krengʰ-, *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxriŋ.ɡɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make (into) a ring
2. _(reconstruction)_ to encircle, to surround
3. _(reconstruction)_ to place a ring on
