## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Harjamōdaz

### Etymology

From *harjaz (“army”) + *mōdaz (“mind, courage, anger”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *ker-, *kóryos, *mō-tó-s

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
