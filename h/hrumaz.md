[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hrumaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrumaz

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *krew- (“to fall, collapse”) or ("to hit, break").

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *krew-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxru.mɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ weak, frail
