# *hwataz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hwataz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwataz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kʷh₁dós, synchronically analyzed as an adjectival derivation from Proto-Germanic *hwētaną (“to attack, stab”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kʷh₁dós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷɑ.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ quick, sharp, brave
