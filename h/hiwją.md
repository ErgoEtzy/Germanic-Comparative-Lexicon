# *hiwją

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hiwj%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hiwją

### Etymology

Disputed. Either from Proto-Indo-European *kew-, *ḱew- (“skin, cuticle, hide; beauty, splendour”) or from Proto-Indo-European *ḱyeh₁- (“grey, dark shade”), or a merger of the two. Compare Sanskrit छवि (chavi, “skin, beauty”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kew-, *ḱyeh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxiw.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ appearance, blee
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ form, shape
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ mold, mildew
