[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/habjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *habjaną

### Etymology

From an innovated post-Indo-European stative present (see Proto-Indo-European *-éh₁yeti, Proto-Germanic *-janą), built on Proto-Indo-European *kap- (“to seize”).

From Proto-Indo-European *kapyéti, from the root *kap- (“to seize”), and thus related to the "have" sense of Etymology 1.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-éh₁yeti, *kap-, *kapyéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑβ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`
