## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *haitį̄

### Etymology

From *haitaz + *-į̄.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-i-h₂, *-ih₂, *keHid-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑi̯.tĩː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ heat
