# *halþaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hal%C3%BEaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *halþaz

### Etymology

Past participle of an unattested verb *halaną (“to slope; be inclined”). From Proto-Indo-European *kelH-, *ḱel- (“to tend, lean, incline, bend, tip”). Formation like *balþaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kelH-, *ḱel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑl.θɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ sloping, inclined
