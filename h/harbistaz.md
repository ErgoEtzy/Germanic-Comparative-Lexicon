# *harbistaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/harbistaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *harbistaz

### Etymology

From *harbaz or *harbijaną, from Proto-Indo-European *kerp- (“to pluck”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kerp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑr.βis.tɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ harvest
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ autumn, fall
