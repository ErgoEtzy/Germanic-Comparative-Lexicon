## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlīþrō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱléytreh₂ (“shelter, covering”). Equivalent to *hlīdaną + *-þrō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱley-, *ḱléytreh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxliː.θrɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ shelter, covering, tent
