# *hlūdaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hl%C5%ABdaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlūdaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱlutós, from *ḱlew- (“to hear; famous”). The origin of the long vowel is unclear.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱlutós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxluː.ðɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ loud
2. _(reconstruction)_ famous
