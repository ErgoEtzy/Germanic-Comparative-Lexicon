# *hindiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hindiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hindiz

### Etymology

From earlier *hemdiz, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱem- (“hornless”), as does generally do not have antlers or horns. Cognate with Ancient Greek κεμάς (kemás, “young deer, pricket”), Sanskrit शम (śáma, “hornless”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxin.diz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ doe, hind
