# *harmāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/harm%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *harmāną

### Etymology

From *harmaz (“harm, pain”) + *-āną or *harmaz (“painful, harmful”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti, *pḱór-mo-s

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to harm, injure, pain
2. _(reconstruction)_ to shame
