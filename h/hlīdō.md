## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlīdō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱleytéh₂ (“something leaned, inclined”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱley-, *ḱleytéh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxliː.ðɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ slope, incline
