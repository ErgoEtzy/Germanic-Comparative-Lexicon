# *hwētaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hw%C4%93tan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwētaną

### Etymology

Apparently from a Proto-Indo-European *kʷeh₁d- (“to stab; pointy, sharp”), albeit without any secure cognates outside of Germanic. Earlier identifications of the root with Proto-Indo-European *(s)kewd- (“to propel”) are untenable morphologically.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kewd-, *kʷeh₁d-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷeː.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to attack, assault
2. _(reconstruction)_ to stab, pierce
