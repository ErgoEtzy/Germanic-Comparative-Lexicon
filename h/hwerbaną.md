## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwerbaną

### Etymology

Uncertain. Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *kʷerp-, in which case the original form would have been *hwerfaną. If so, then it might be related to Ancient Greek καρπός (karpós, “wrist”), though deriving the latter from a root with initial labiovelar presents difficulties.

### Pronunciation

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to turn; turn around
2. _(reconstruction)_ to move; move about
3. _(reconstruction)_ to change
