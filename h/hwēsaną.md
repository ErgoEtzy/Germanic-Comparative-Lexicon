# *hwēsaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hw%C4%93san%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwēsaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱwes- (“to pant”). Cognate with Latin queror.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱwes-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷɛː.sɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to wheeze
