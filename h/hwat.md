## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwat

### Etymology

From earlier *hʷód, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷód (“what”), *kʷod, neuter form of *kʷós (“who”), *kʷós, *kʷís.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kʷís, *kʷód

### Pronoun

1. _(accusative, form-of, neuter, nominative, reconstruction, singular)_ nominative/accusative singular neuter of *hwaz
