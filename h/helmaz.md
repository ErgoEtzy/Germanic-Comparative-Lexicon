## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *helmaz

### Etymology

Probably from Proto-Indo-European *ḱelmos (“covering”), from the root *ḱel- (“to cover, hide, protect”).
Doublet of *helaną (“to hide”) and cognate with cēlō (“to hide”) and Latin clam (“secretly”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱelmos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxel.mɑz/`

### Noun
