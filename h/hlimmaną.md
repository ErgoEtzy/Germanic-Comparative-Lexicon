# *hlimmaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hlimman%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlimmaną

### Etymology

Uncertain. May be connected to Proto-Indo-European *kelh₁- (“to shout”); compare Latin clāmō (“to cry, shout”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kelh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxlim.mɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sound
2. _(reconstruction)_ to growl, roar
