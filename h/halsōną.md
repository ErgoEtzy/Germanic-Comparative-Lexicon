# *halsōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hals%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *halsōną

### Etymology

From *halsaz (“neck, throat”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)ker-, *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *kólsos, *wert-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈhɑl.sɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to embrace, hug
2. _(reconstruction)_ to decapitate
