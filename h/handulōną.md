# *handulōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/handul%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *handulōną

### Etymology

From *handuz (“hand”) + *-lōną (frequentive suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *ḱómt

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑnd.lɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to take or hold in the hand, seize, grip
