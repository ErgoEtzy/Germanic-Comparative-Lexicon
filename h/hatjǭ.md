# *hatjǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hatj%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hatjǭ

### Etymology

*hatjaną (“to pursue, attack”) + *-ǭ (“-ess”), thus "pursuer, attacker" > "(well-traveled) warrior" > "hero".

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱeh₂d-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑt.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, feminine, reconstruction)_ hero
