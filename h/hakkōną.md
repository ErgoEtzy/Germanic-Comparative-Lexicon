## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hakkōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *keg-, *keng- (“to be sharp; peg; hook; handle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *keg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑk.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to hack; chop
2. _(reconstruction)_ to hoe
