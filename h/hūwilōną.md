# *hūwilōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/h%C5%ABwil%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hūwilōną

### Etymology

Ultimately of imitative origin. Said by Pokorny to be from Proto-Indo-European *kū-, but others dispute this. Also compare *heufaną (“to lament”), *uwwulǭ (“owl”) and Proto-West Germanic *hūō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kū-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxuːwi.lɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to howl; scream
