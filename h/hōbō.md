# *hōbō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/h%C5%8Db%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hōbō

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *keh₂péh₂, from Proto-Indo-European *keh₂po- (“land, garden”), from *kap- (“to seize”). Related to Ancient Greek κῆπος (kêpos, “garden, orchard”) and possibly Albanian kopsht (“garden”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *keh₂po-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɔː.βɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ piece of land
