[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hlin%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlināną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱley- (“to lean”). Cognate with Latin clīnō (“slope, incline, bend”), Ancient Greek κλίνω (klínō, “bend, slant, lean”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱley-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to lean
