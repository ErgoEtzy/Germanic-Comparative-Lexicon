## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hangijaną

### Etymology

From *hanhaną + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱenk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑŋ.ɡi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to cause to hang
