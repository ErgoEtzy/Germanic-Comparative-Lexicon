**Reconstruction:** *hrespaną

### Etymology

From earlier *hrepspaną, *hrepsaną, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kreb-, *(s)krebʰ- (“to turn; bend; shrink”), from *(s)ker- (“to turn; bend”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to tear; rip
2. _(reconstruction)_ to plunder
