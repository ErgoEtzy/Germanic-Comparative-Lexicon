## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hōlōną

### Etymology

From *hōlą (“vain speech”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *ḱeh₁l-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɔː.lɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to speak vainly (of)
2. _(reconstruction)_ to calumniate, slander
