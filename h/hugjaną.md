[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hugjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hugjaną

### Etymology

Uncertain. Synchronically analyzable as *hugiz (“understanding, mind”) + *-janą (“denominative suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *kʷeḱ-, *ḱewk-, *ḱk-í-s ~ *ḱk-éy-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxuɣ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to think, consider
