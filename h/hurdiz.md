# *hurdiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hurdiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hurdiz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *kr̥h₂tis, from Proto-Indo-European *kreh₂- (“wickerwork”). Cognate with Latin crātis (“wickerwork frame”), Old Prussian corto (“fence”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kreh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxur.ðiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ wickerwork door or frame, hurdle
