# *hnukāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hnuk%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hnukāną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *knewg- (“to press; push; bend”), from *ken- (“to press; pinch”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *knewg-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be bent, flex, curve, draw up, shrink, wrinkle, become pinched
