[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hal%C3%BEijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *halþijaną

### Etymology

From *halþaz (“sloping, inclined”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *kelH-, *ḱel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑl.θi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make incline, tilt, incline
