## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrīmą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *krey- (“to streak, graze, touch”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *krey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxriːmɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ frost, hoarfrost
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ soot
