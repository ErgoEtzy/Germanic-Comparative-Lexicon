## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hauzijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂ḱh₂owsyéti (“to be sharp-eared”), from *h₂eḱ- (“sharp”) + *h₂ows- (“ear”) + *-yéti (denominative suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry
