# *hrainisōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hrainis%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrainisōną

### Etymology

From *hrainiz (“clean, pure”) + *-isōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *krey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxrɑi̯.ni.sɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to clean, cleanse, purify
