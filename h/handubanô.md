# *handubanô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/handuban%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *handubanô

### Etymology

From *handuz + *banô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *gʷʰen-, *gʷʰon-ō, *ḱómt

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑn.du.bɑ.nɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ One who slays or murders another by hand; a murderer
