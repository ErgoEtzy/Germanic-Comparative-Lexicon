## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hawwaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kewh₂- (“to beat, hew, forge”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kewh₂-

### Pronunciation

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to hew, to chop
2. _(reconstruction)_ to forge
