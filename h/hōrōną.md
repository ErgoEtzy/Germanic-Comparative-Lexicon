# *hōrōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/h%C5%8Dr%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hōrōną

### Etymology

From *hōrą (“adultery”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *keh₂-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to commit adultery
