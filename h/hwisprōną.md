# *hwisprōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hwispr%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwisprōną

### Etymology

A formation related to *hwistlōną (“to whistle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱwey-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to whistle, hiss, whisper
