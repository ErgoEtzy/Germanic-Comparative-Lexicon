# *hlutą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hlut%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlutą

### Etymology

Nominal formation derived from the root of *hleutaną (“to cast lots; to undergo, endure, be fated (to)”). Cognate with Latvian kļũtas (“fate”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxlu.tɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ lot, share
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ fate
