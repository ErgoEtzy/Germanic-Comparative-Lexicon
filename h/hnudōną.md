# *hnudōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hnud%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hnudōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *knḍʰ-, from *knu-, *knew- (“to scratch; scrape; rub”), from Proto-Indo-European *ken- (“to scratch; scrape; rub”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ken-, *knḍʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxnu.ðɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ To pound; hammer; rivet
