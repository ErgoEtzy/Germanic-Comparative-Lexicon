# *helpaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/helpan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *helpaną

### Etymology

Appears to derive from a Proto-Indo-European *ḱelb-, *ḱelp- (“to help”), with Lithuanian šel̃pti (“to help, support”) as a cognate; however, there are subtle phono-grammatical issues (for which see Kroonen for more), in addition to the seemingly-related Lithuanian gélbėti (“to help”) being phonetically irreconcilable with regular sound laws.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱelb-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxel.pɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to help
