## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hazwaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kes- (“to scrape, comb”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kes-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑz.wɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ flax
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ linen
