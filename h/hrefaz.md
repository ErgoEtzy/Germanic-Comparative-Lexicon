## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrefaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *krép-os ~ *krép-es-os, from *krep-. Cognate with Proto-Italic *korpos (“body”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *krep-, *krép-os ~ *krép-es-os

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxre.ɸɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ body
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ torso; stomach
