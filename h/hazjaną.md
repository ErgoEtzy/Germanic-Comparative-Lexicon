## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hazjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱh₁s-ié, derived from *ḱeh₁s- (“to call, to command, to instruct”).
Related to Sanskrit शस्ति (śasti, “praise, command”), Avestan 𐬯𐬁𐬯𐬙𐬎 (sāstu, “to teach”) and Old Armenian սաստ (sast, “scolding, rebuke”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱh₁s-ié

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑz.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to call, shout
2. _(reconstruction)_ to praise
