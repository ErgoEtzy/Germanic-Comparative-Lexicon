# *heuhmô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/heuhm%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *heuhmô

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *kéwkmō, *kéwkmon-, from Proto-Indo-European *kewk- (“to elevate; height”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxeu̯x.mɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(East, Germanic, masculine, reconstruction)_ mass, multitude, heap
