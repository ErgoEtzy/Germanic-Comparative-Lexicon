## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hirdijaz

### Etymology

From *herdō (“herd”) + *-jaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)kerdʰ-, *-yós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxir.ði.jɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ herder, herdsman
