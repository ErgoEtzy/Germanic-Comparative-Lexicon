# *harmijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/harmijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *harmijaną

### Etymology

From *harmaz (“to harm, pain”) + *-janą or *harmaz (“to harmful, painful”) + *-janą.

From Proto-Indo-European *kerh₂-, *kerh₃- (“to praise (loudly)”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kerh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxar.mi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to harm, injure, pain
2. _(reconstruction)_ to shame
