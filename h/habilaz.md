# *habilaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/habilaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *habilaz

### Etymology

From *habjaną + *-ilaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *keh₂p-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ That which raises or lifts; lifter.
