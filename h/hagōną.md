# *hagōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hag%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hagōną

### Etymology

From *hagaz (“abled; comfortable”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱek-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make possible
2. _(reconstruction)_ to comfort
