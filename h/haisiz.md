# *haisiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/haisiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *haisiz

### Etymology

From earlier *haissiz, from Proto-Indo-European *kóyd-ti-s (“act of calling”). Equivalent to *haitaną (“to call”) + *-þiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kóyd-ti-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑi̯.siz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ command
