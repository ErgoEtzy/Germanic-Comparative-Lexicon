## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrunkwǭ

### Etymology

From *hrinkwaną (“to bend, curl, recoil”); according to Pokorny, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (“to bend, curve, turn”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ker-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ wrinkle, crease
