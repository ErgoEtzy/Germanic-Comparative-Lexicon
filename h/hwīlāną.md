# *hwīlāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hw%C4%ABl%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwīlāną

### Etymology

From *hwīlō (“while, period, pause”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kʷyeh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷiː.lɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to rest, to pause, to take a break
