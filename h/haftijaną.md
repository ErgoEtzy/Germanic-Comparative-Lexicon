# *haftijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/haftijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *haftijaną

### Etymology

From *haftaz (“captive, bound”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *keh₂p-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑɸ.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make bound; to bind, secure
