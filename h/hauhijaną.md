# *hauhijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hauhijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hauhijaną

### Etymology

From *hauhaz (“high”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kew-, *kewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑu̯.xi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make high; to raise, exalt
