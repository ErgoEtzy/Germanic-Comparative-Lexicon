# *hnawwaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hnawwaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hnawwaz

### Etymology

A u-stem adjectival formation from *hnewwaną (“to hit”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxnɑw.wɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ tight; narrow
2. _(reconstruction)_ sparse; meager; lean; scarce; stingy
