## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hatjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱeh₂d- (“strong emotion”). Equivalent to *hataz (“hate”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱeh₂d-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑt.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to hunt down, pursue
2. _(reconstruction)_ to rush, incite, attack
