# *hulþijaną

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hulþijaną

### Etymology

From *hulþaz (“inclined, favourable”) + *-janą or *hulþô (“friend, trustee”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tós, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *ḱel-, *ḱl̥-tó-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxul.θi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make into a friend, to court one's friendship
2. _(reconstruction)_ to calm, to comfort
3. _(reconstruction)_ to praise
