## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlidą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱlitóm (“that which is covered”), from the adjective *ḱlitós (“covered”), from the root *ḱley- (“to shelter, cover”). Compare, in particular, Sanskrit श्रित (śritá, “lying, situated in”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱley-, *ḱlitóm

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ lid, cover
