## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrappijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)kreb- (“to turn, bend, shrink, rotate, touch, attack”). Cognate with Lithuanian krapštyti (“to pick, peck, scratch, claw”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kreb-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxrɑp.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to rush
2. _(reconstruction)_ to grasp
