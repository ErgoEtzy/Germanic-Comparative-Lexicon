# *hūdiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/h%C5%ABdiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hūdiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kéwHtis (“bedecking, hide, skin”), and cognate with Latin cutis (“skin”). The root is probably further derived from *(s)kewH- (“to cover, hide”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kewH-, *kéwHtis

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxuː.ðiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ skin, hide
