# *hraukaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hraukaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hraukaz

### Etymology

Unknown; but apparently cognate with Lithuanian kruvà, krûvà (“pile, heap, stack”), Old Irish crúach (“stack of corn, rick”). Possibly Proto-Indo-European *krewH- (“to heap up”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *krewH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxrɑu̯.kɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ pile (of stones); heap
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ haystack
