## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrawaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *krewh₂- (“raw meat, fresh blood”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *krewh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxrɑ.wɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ raw, uncooked
