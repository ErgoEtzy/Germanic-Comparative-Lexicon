## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hauzīniz

### Etymology

From *hauzijaną (“to hear”) + *-īniz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-éyti, *h₂ḱh₂owsyéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑu̯.ziː.niz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ hearing
