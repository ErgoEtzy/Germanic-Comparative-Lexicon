## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwōsaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kʷeh₂s- (“to cough”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kʷeh₂s-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷɔː.sɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cough
