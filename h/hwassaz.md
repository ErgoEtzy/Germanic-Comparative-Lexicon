## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwassaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kʷh₁dtós (“sharpened”); by surface analysis, the original participle of *hwētaną (“to attack, stab”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kʷh₁dtós
