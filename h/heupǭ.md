# *heupǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/heup%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *heupǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱewb- (“thorn, briar”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱewb-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ thornbush (e.g. hawthorn, wild rose, etc.)
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ the fruit of a thornbush (e.g. rosehip, brambleberry, etc.)
