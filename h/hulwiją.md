# *hulwiją

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hulwij%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hulwiją

### Etymology

From *hulaz, *hulhwaz (“hollow”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- (“to tend, lean, incline, bend, tip”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxul.wi.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ depression, hollow
