# *hawją

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hawj%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hawją

### Etymology

According to Kroonen, from a ja-collective form of *hawwaną (“to hew; cut down”) (with quasi-Indo-European form *koh₂w-yo-m).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑu̯.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ hay
