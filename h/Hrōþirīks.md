## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Hrōþirīks

### Etymology

From *hrōþiz (“fame”) + *rīks (“king, ruler”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃reǵ-

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
