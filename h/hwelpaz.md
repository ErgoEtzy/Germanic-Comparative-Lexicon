# *hwelpaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hwelpaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwelpaz

### Etymology

Of uncertain origin. Probably a derivative, albeit with irregular phonetic development, of Proto-Indo-European *gʷelbʰ- (“womb”).
Alternatively, perhaps from *hwelpaną (“to yelp; bark”), maybe from Proto-Indo-European *kel- (“to call; shout; cry; make a noise”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʷelbʰ-, *kel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxwel.pɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ puppy
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ used also of other baby animals: cub, pup
