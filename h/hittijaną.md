## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hittijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kh₂id-néh₂-ti, from *kh₂eyd- (“to fall upon; encounter; hit; cut; hew”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kh₂eyd-, *kh₂id-néh₂-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxit.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to encounter; meet with; make contact; hit
