## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hagaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱok-ó-s, from *ḱek- (“to be able, capable”). Compare Sanskrit शक् (śak, “to be able”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱek-, *ḱok-ó-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑ.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ abled, capable
2. _(reconstruction)_ suitable, comfortable
