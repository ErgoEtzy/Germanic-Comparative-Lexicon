## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *habanō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kh₂póneh₂ or *kh₂pnéh₂, from the root *kap- (“to take, seize, grasp”). Close cognate with Old Irish cúan m (“port, harbor”), however this may be due to borrowing at an early stage.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kap-, *kh₂póneh₂

### Pronunciation

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ harbour, haven
