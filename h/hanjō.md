## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hanjō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *keh₂n- (“to sing”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *keh₂n-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑ.njɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ hen
