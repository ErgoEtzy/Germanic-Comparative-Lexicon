# *hufą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/huf%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hufą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kéh₂wp-os, from *keh₂wp- (“to bend, arch, vault”). Related to *haupaz (“heap”); see there for more.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kéh₂wp-os

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxu.ɸɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ hill, elevated place
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ house, hall, estate
