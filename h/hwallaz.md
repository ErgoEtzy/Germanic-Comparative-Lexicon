# *hwallaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hwallaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwallaz

### Etymology

Uncertain. Possibly from Pre-Germanic *kʷolh₁nos, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷelh₁- (“to turn, go round”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kʷelh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷɑl.lɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ forward, bold, persistent
