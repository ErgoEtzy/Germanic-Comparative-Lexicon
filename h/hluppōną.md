## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hluppōną

### Etymology

From earlier paradigm *hluppōþi ~ *hlubunanþi, from pre-Proto-Germanic *klub-nā́-ti ~ *klub-un-ánti, from Proto-Indo-European *klewb- (“to stumble; to hop”), iterative to *hlaupaną (“to leap, jump”). Cognate with Latvian klupinât (“to keep stumbling”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *klewb-

### Pronunciation

### Verb
