## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hauhaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kówk-o-s, from Proto-Indo-European *kewk- (“to elevate”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kewk-, *kówk-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑu̯.xɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ high
