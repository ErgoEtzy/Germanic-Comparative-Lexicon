# *happōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/happ%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *happōną

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *kh₂pnéh₂-, a zero-grade iterative to *habjaną (“to have”).

From pre-Germanic *kobʰnéh₂- an o-grade iterative to Proto-Indo-European *kobʰ- (“to succeed”). Probably related to Old Irish cob (“victory”), Old Church Slavonic кобь (kobĭ, “fate”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kobʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑp.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to grab, snatch
