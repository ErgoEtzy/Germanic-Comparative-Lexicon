# *hwilīkaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hwil%C4%ABkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwilīkaz

### Etymology

From *hwī (“what”) + *-līkaz (“-like”), the former being the stem of *hwaz (“what, who”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷi.liː.kɑz/`

### Determiner

1. _(reconstruction)_ what kind of, what sort of
