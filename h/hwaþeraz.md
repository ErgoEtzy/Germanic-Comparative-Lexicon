# *hwaþeraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hwa%C3%BEeraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwaþeraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kʷóteros (“which of two”), from *kʷos (“which”). Cognate with Sanskrit कतर (katará), Ancient Greek πότερος (póteros), Proto-Slavic *koterъjь / *kotorъjь, Latin uter.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kʷ-, *kʷóteros

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷɑ.θe.rɑz/`

### Determiner

1. _(reconstruction)_ which, what (of two, interrogative)
