# *hiurijaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hiurijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hiurijaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱeywo- (“trusted; familiar”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱey- (“to recline; settle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱey-, *ḱeywo-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxiu̯.ri.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ trusted; familiar
2. _(reconstruction)_ mild; gentle; dear
