# *hafnaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hafnan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hafnaną

### Etymology

From *habjaną (“to lift”) + *-naną. Compare also Proto-West Germanic *habinōn (“to handle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-néh₂-, *-éh₁yeti, *kap-, *kapyéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑɸ.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(intransitive, reconstruction)_ to become exalted
2. _(intransitive, reconstruction)_ (+ dative object) to consider oneself above, to reject, to abandon
