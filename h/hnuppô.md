## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hnuppô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *knew-, *kenw- (“to scratch, scrape, rub”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *knew-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a tuft or flock of wool
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a knot in a cloth or fabric; nap
