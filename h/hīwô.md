[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/h%C4%ABw%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hīwô

### Etymology

A derivative of Proto-Indo-European *ḱey-wo-s, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱey- (“to be lying down”). Cognate with Sanskrit शेव (śéva, “dear, precious, friendly”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱey-, *ḱey-wo-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxiː.wɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ married couple, household
