# *hrīþō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hr%C4%AB%C3%BE%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrīþō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)kreyt- (“to wind; twist; bend”), from *(s)ker- (“to wind; twist; bend”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kreyt-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxriː.θɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ A sudden onset; attack
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ A seizure; fit
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ A storm
