# *huppijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/huppijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *huppijaną

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *kewb- (“to bend; a bend, joint”); see *hupiz (“hip”). Or, derived from the iterative *huppōną ~ *hubbōną, from
a Proto-Indo-European *kup-; see there for more.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kewb-, *kewp-, *kup-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxup.pi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to move up and down; hop
