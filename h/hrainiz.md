## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrainiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *króy-n-is, a possibly-vṛddhied adjectival formation from an unattested *hrinaną, from Proto-Indo-European *krey- (“to divide, sift”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *krey-, *króy-n-is

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxrɑi̯.niz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ clean, not dirty
2. _(reconstruction)_ pure
3. _(reconstruction)_ innocent
