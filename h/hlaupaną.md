# *hlaupaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hlaupan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlaupaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *klewb- (“to spring; to hop”). Compare Lithuanian klùpti, Latvian klupt (“to stumble”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *klewb-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxlɑu̯.pɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to jump forward, to leap
