# *hwarbōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hwarb%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwarbōną

### Etymology

From *hwarbą, *hwarbaz + *-ōną, the former terms from *hwerbaną (“to turn, move”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷɑr.βɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to turn
2. _(reconstruction)_ to move
