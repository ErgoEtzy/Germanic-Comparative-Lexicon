# *hlammōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hlamm%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlammōną

### Etymology

An iterative formation to Proto-Indo-European *kelh₁- (“to sound”), closely related to the strong verb *hlimmaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kelh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxlam.mɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to sound, to rumble, to roar
