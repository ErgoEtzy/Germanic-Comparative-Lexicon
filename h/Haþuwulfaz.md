## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Haþuwulfaz

### Etymology

From *haþuz (“battle, fight”) + *wulfaz (“wolf”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *keh₃-, *kéh₃tus, *wĺ̥kʷos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑ.θu.ˌwul.ɸɑz/`

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
