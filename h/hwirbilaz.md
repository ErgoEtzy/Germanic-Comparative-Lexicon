## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwirbilaz

### Etymology

From *hwerbaną + *-ilaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-dʰlom, *-e-ló-s, *-lós, *-tlom, *-trom, *kʷerp-

- IPA: `/ˈxʷir.βi.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ circle, ring, whirl
