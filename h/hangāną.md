# *hangāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hang%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hangāną

### Etymology

From *hanhaną (“to hang”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱenk-

### Verb

1. _(intransitive, reconstruction)_ to hang, to be suspended
