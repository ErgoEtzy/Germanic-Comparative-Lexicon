# *hirmijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hirmijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hirmijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱermH-ye, the ye-present of *ḱermH-. Cognate with Sanskrit श्राम्यति (śrāmyati, “to weary”).
Related to Proto-Norse *ᚺᛖᚱᛗᚨ (*herma), as found in Proto-Norse ᚺᛖᚱᛡᛗᛡᛚᛡᛋᛡᛦ (herᴀmᴀlᴀsᴀʀ).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱermH-, *ḱermH-ye

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxir.mi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to rest
