# *hanhaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hanhaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hanhaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kenk-, *kemǝk- (“joint, legbone”). Cognate with Lithuanian ki̇̀nka (“haunch”), Sanskrit कङ्काल (kaṅkāla, “skeleton”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kenk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑ̃ː.xɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ heel
