## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hūsōną

### Etymology

From *hūsą (“house”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)kewH-, *(s)kuH-s-o-m, *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxuː.sɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to house
