# *hamalaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hamalaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hamalaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱom-olo-s, from *ḱem- (“hornless”). Cognate with Sanskrit शम (śáma, “hornless”), Proto-Slavic *komolъ (“hornless”) (> Russian комо́лый (komólyj)).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱom-olo-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑ.mɑ.lɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ mutilated, truncated
