## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *haitaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *keHid-o-s, from *keHy- (“to be hot”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *keHid-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑi̯.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ hot
