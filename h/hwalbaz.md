## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwalbaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *kʷolpós, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷelp-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kʷelp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷɑl.βɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ curved, vaulted
