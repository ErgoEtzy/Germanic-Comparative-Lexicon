# *harskaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/harskaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *harskaz

### Etymology

Dissimilated from earlier *hardskaz, from *harduz (“hard”) + *-skaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kret-

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ rough-textured, coarse
2. _(reconstruction)_ harsh-tasting, sour, astringent
