# *haihaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/haihaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *haihaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kéh₂ikos (“one-eyed”). Cognate with Latin caecus (“blind”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kéh₂ikos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑi̯.xɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ one-eyed
