# *hneupaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hneupan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hneupaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *knew-, *kenw- (“to scratch, scrape, rub”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *knew-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxneu̯.pɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to pluck, tear
