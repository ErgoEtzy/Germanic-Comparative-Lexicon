## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlankiz

### Etymology

From *hlankaz (“flexible; slim”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxlɑŋ.kiz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ ring; chainlink
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bond; fetter
