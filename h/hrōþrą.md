## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrōþrą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kreH-tro-, from *kreH- (“to shout”). Cognate with Sanskrit कीर्ति (kīrti, “speech, report; fame”) (from the related root कृ (kṛ, “to commemorate, honor”)), Avestan 𐬗𐬀𐬭𐬆𐬐𐬆𐬭𐬆 (car^əkər^ə, “to praise”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kreH-tro-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxrɔː.θrɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ praise
