# *hēliz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/h%C4%93liz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hēliz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *helaną (“to conceal”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɛː.liz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ concealing, deceitful, treacherous
