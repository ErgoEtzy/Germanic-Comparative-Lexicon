# *husǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hus%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *husǭ

### Etymology

Probably a nominal formation from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kewHs-, an s-extension of *(s)kewH- (“to cover, bewrap”), though Orel is implicitly skeptical and leaves the origin open.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kewH-, *(s)kewHs-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxu.sɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ an outer covering; hull; shell; husk; case
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ covering for the legs; leggings, trousers
