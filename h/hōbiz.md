# *hōbiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/h%C5%8Dbiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hōbiz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *habjaną (“to have; to hold”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *keh₂p-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɔː.βiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ fitting, proper
