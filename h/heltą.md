## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *heltą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kel- (“to hit; beat”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kel-, *kelh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxel.tɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ handle; hilt
