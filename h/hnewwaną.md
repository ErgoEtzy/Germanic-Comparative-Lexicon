# *hnewwaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hnewwan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hnewwaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *knewH-e-ti, from *kneh₂- (“to scrape, scratch, rub”). Cognate with Ancient Greek κνάω (knáō, “to scratch”)/Ancient Greek κνύω (knúō, “to scratch”), Latvian knūt (“to itch”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *knewH-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxnew.wɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to knock, to push, to shove
