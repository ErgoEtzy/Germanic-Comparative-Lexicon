# *halmaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/halmaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *halmaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱolh₂mos, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱelh₂- (“to stick; prick; stab”). Cognate with Latin culmus, Ancient Greek κάλαμος (kálamos). Kroonen, who adds Lithuanian kélmas (“tree trunk”) as a cognate, reconstructs the original athematic paradigm as *ḱélh₂-m̥ ~ *ḱl̥h₂-m-ós (cf. *elm-, *alm-, *ulm- (“elm”)), rejecting Beekes' *ḱólh₂-m̥ ~ *ḱl̥h₂-ém-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱelh₂-, *ḱolh₂mos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑl.mɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ halm; stem; stalk
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ straw; hay
