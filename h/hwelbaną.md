[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hwelban%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwelbaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kʷelp- (“to bend”), with -b- instead of -f- due to influence from either the adjective or causative.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kʷelp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷel.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to curve, vault
