# *hliuþijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hliu%C3%BEijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hliuþijaną

### Etymology

From *hleuþą (“hearing, listening”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱlew-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxliu̯.θi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to listen, obey
