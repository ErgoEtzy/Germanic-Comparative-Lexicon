## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwaþjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kweth₂- (“to bubble, foam”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kweth₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷɑθ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to foam
