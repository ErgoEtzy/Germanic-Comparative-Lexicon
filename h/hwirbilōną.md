## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwirbilōną

### Etymology

From *hwirbilaz (“circle, ring, whirl”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-dʰlom, *-e-ló-s, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *-lós, *-tlom, *-trom, *kʷerp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷir.βi.lɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to whirl
