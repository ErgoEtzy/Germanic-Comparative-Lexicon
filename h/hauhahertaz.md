# *hauhahertaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hauhahertaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hauhahertaz

### Etymology

From *hauhaz + *hertô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kew-, *kewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑu̯.xɑ.ˌxer.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ proud, haughty
