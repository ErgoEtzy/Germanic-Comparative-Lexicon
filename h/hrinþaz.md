# *hrinþaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hrin%C3%BEaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrinþaz

### Etymology

Probably from Proto-Indo-European *ḱrént-os ~ *ḱrn̥t-és-s, from the root *ḱer- (“head; horn”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱerh₂-, *ḱrént-os ~ *ḱrn̥t-és-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxrin.θɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ A horned beast, as in a neat or ox
