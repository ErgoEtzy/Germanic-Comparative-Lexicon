# *hrapōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hrap%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrapōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)kreb- (“to turn, bend, shrink, rotate, touch, attack”), from *(s)ker- (“to turn; bend”). Cognate with Lithuanian krapštyti (“to pick, peck, scratch, claw”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kreb-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxrɑ.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to scratch, scrape
2. _(reconstruction)_ to graze, make contact (with), touch
3. _(reconstruction)_ to snatch
