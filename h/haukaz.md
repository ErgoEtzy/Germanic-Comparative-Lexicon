# *haukaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/haukaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *haukaz

### Etymology

Substantivization of a variant of *hauhaz (“high”), thus meaning "the high ones", likely in reference to them living on artificial mounds.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑu̯.kɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a member of the Chauci
