# *hrindaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hrindan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrindaną

### Etymology

Probably a nasal-infixed formation from Proto-Indo-European *kret- (“to beat, strike, hit, shake”); compare Lithuanian krañtas (“shorebank”) for a potential cognate with similar formation.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kret-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxrin.dɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to push, thrust
