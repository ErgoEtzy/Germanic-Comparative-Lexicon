## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hnuppōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *knebʰ- (“to pick”). Kroonen suggests that the strong verb *hneupaną was derived from this iterative verb.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *knebʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxnup.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ To tear off
