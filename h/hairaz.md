## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hairaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)ḱeh₃-ro-s (“grey, dark”). Cognate with Old Irish cīar (“dark”), as well as probably Proto-Slavic *śěrъ (“gray”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)ḱeh₃-, *(s)ḱeh₃-ro-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑi̯.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ grey
2. _(figuratively, reconstruction)_ aged; old; lofty; distinguished
