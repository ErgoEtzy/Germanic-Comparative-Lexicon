# *handuwurhtaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/handuwurhtaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *handuwurhtaz

### Etymology

From *handuz (“hand”) + *wurhtaz (“worked”, past participle of *wurkijaną (“to work”)).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *werǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑn.du.ˌwurx.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ made by hand, handwrought
