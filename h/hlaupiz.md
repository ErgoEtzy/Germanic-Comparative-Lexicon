[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hlaupiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlaupiz

### Etymology

From *hlaupaną (“to walk”) + *-iz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-yes-, *-yōs, *klewb-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxlɑu̯.piz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ walking
