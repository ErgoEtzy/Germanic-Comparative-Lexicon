## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hnuts

### Etymology

From Pre-Proto-Germanic *knuds, from a root *knu- also seen in Proto-Celtic *knūs (source of Irish cnó) and Latin nux (“nut”). Based on the form of the nouns and the restriction of the root to Germanic, Celtic and Italic, it has been argued to be of non-Indo-European origin.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.
