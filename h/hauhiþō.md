## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hauhiþō

### Etymology

From *hauhaz (“high”) + *-iþō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-teh₂, *kewk-, *kówk-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑu̯.xi.θɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ height
