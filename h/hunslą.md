# *hunslą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hunsl%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hunslą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱwen- (“holy”) + *-slą. Cognate with Lithuanian šventas (“holy”), Avestan 𐬯𐬞𐬆𐬧𐬙𐬀 (spəṇta, “holy”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱwen-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxun.slɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ offering, sacrifice
