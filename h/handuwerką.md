# *handuwerką

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/handuwerk%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *handuwerką

### Etymology

From *handuz + *werką.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *wer(k/ḱ)-nó-, *werǵ-, *werǵʰ-nó-, *wérǵom, *ḱómt

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑn.du.wɛr.kɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ handwork; manual labour
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ handiwork, creation
