# *hleumundaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hleumundaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hleumundaz

### Etymology

Likely originally neuter, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱléwmn̥tom, extension and thematicization of *ḱléwmn̥, from *ḱlew- (“to hear”). Cognate to Sanskrit श्रोमत (śromata, “fame”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱlew-, *ḱléwmn̥tom

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxleu̯.mun.dɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ reputation, fame
