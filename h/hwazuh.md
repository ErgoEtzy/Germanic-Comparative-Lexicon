## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwazuh

### Etymology

From *hwaz + *-hw. Cognate with Latin quisque.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷɑ.zux/`

### Pronoun

1. _(East, Germanic, reconstruction)_ each, everyone, everything
