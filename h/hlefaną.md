**Reconstruction:** *hlefaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *klep- (“to steal”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *klep-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxle.ɸɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to steal
