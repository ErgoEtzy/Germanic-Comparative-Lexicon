## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *haulaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kh₂ewl-o-s, from *kh₂ewl- (“tumor, rupture, hernia”). Cognate with Proto-Balto-Slavic *kū́ˀlāˀ (> Lithuanian kūlas (“umbilical hernia”), Proto-Slavic *kỳla (“outgrowth, hernia”)).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kh₂ewl-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑu̯.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ breach, crack
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ rupture, hernia
