## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hringijaną

### Etymology

From *hringaz (“a ring, sound”) + *-janą.

From *hringaz (“a ring, a circle”) + *-janą. Compare Proto-Slavic *krǫžiti.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)ker-, *(s)kreg-, *(s)krengʰ-, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxriŋ.ɡi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to ring, sound
