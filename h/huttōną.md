## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *huttōną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *kudnéh₂-, a zero-grade iterative to Proto-Indo-European *(s)kewd- (“to advance, push”). Related to Sanskrit चोदति (codati, “to urge, impel, incite”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kewd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxut.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to run, hasten
