# *harbistamēnōþs

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/harbistam%C4%93n%C5%8D%C3%BEs)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *harbistamēnōþs

### Etymology

From *harbistaz (“harvest”) + *mēnōþs (“month”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *kerp-, *mḗh₁n̥s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑr.βis.tɑ.ˌmɛː.nɔːθs/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a month of the Germanic calendar, corresponds roughly to September.
