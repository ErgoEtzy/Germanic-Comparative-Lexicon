## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *haftaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kh₂ptós (“grabbed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *keh₂p-, *kh₂ptós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑɸ.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ captive, bound
