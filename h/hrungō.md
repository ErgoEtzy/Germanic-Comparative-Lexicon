## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrungō

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *(s)krengʰ- (“to twist, bend”), from *(s)ker- (“to twist, bend”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)krengʰ-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ stake, spoke, rod, spike, rung
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ staff, post, pole
