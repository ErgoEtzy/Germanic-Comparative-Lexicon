[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/Harjawaldaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Harjawaldaz

### Etymology

From *harjaz (“army”) + *waldaz (“ruler, authority”). Compare *Waldaharjaz, composed of the same elements but in the reverse order.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂welh₁-, *ker-

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
