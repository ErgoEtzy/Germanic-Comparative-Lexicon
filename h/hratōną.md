## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hratōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)krad-, *(s)kerd- (“to move, swing, spring”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)krad-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxrɑ.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to swing; sway; shake.
2. _(reconstruction)_ to stagger; stumble; tumble.
