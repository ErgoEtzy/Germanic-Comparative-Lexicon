## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hahtuz

### Etymology

Unknown; possibly dissimilated from earlier *hwahtuz, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷóḱ-tu-s, from *kʷeḱ- (“to see, appear”), perhaps cognate with Old Irish cucht (“external appearance; colour”), Tocharian B kektseñe (“body”). Alternatively from Proto-Indo-European *kóḱ-tu-s, from *ḱek- (“to be able, capable”), whence also *hagaz (“skilled, abled”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kóḱ-tu-s, *kʷóḱ-tu-s
