# *haganą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hagan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *haganą

### Etymology

Unknown. Possibly related to *hagōną (“to please, comfort”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑ.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to feed, to nourish
2. _(reconstruction)_ to use, to apply
