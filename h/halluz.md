[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/halluz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *halluz

### Etymology

Uncertain; possibly from Proto-Indo-European:
* *ḱHl-n-u-s, cognate with Sanskrit शिला (śilā́, “stone”), Old Armenian սալ (sal, “slab, flagstone; anvil”);
* or *kolHn-u-s, from earlier n-stem *kólH-ō ~ *kl̥H-n-és (“hill, height”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑl.luz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ A rock; stone; boulder
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ A rockface; cliff
