# *hrībaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hr%C4%ABban%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrībaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)kreybʰ-. Doublet of *skrībaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kreybʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxriː.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to scratch
2. _(reconstruction)_ to snatch, to grab
