## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwōtō

### Etymology

Verbal noun of *hwētaną (“to attack, stab”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷɔː.tɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ threat
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ reprimand
