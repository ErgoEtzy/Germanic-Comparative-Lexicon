## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hesô

### Etymology

Cognate with Proto-Celtic *kasnī (“hare”), Old Prussian sasins (“hare”), Sanskrit शश (śaśa, “hare”), suggesting an original Proto-Indo-European paradigm of *ḱh₁és-ō ~ *ḱh₁s-n-és (“hare”), from *ḱh₁es- + *-ō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-ō, *ḱh₁es-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑ.sɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ hare
