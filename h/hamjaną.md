# *hamjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hamjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hamjaną

### Etymology

According to Kroonen, probably related to *hamô (“rod, shaft”). Orel prefers to take the word as a verbal formation from *hammō (“shinbone; ham”), though this is semantically more tenuous than Kroonen's theory.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No PIE form is listed or machine-resolvable in this snapshot.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑm.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ To press; pinch
2. _(reconstruction)_ To obstruct; restrict; restrain; hinder; hamper
3. _(reconstruction)_ To hem in; enclose
