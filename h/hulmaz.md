# *hulmaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hulmaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hulmaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *kl̥Hmos, likely from an original mn-stem; from Proto-Indo-European *kelH-. Cognate with Latin columen (“summit, peak”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kelH-, *kl̥Hmos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxul.mɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ small island, hill, mound
