# *hauhalīkaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hauhal%C4%ABkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hauhalīkaz

### Etymology

From *hauhaz (“high”) + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *kewk-, *kówk-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑu̯.xɑ.ˌliː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ relatively high
