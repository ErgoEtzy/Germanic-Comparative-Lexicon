# *hnipōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hnip%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hnipōną

### Etymology

Kroonen reconstructs this as an iterative verb from Proto-Indo-European *knibʰ-néh₂-ti. Related to Lithuanian knibti (“to sink, collapse”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *knibʰ-néh₂-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxni.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be downcast
2. _(reconstruction)_ to bow
