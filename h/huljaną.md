# *huljaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/huljan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *huljaną

### Etymology

Zero-grade present, pre-Germanic *ḱl̥ye/o-, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- (“to hide”), the root of *helaną (“to hide, conceal”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxul.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cover, veil
