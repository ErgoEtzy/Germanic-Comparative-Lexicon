[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hailis%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hailisōną

### Etymology

From *hailaz (“hail, healthy”) + *-isōną (“denominal suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *kéh₂ilos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑi̯.li.sɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to greet; salute
2. _(reconstruction)_ to inquire; beseech
