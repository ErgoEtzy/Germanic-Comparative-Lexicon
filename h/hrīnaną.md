# *hrīnaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hr%C4%ABnan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrīnaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *krey- (“to strip, touch”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *krey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxriː.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to touch, brush (against), make contact (with), graze
