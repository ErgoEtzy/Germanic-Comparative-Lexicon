[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hauniz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hauniz

### Etymology

Derived from the Proto-Indo-European verbal root *keh₂u-, related to Lithuanian kuvetis (“to be ashamed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑu̯.niz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ despised, poor, miserable
