## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hundaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *ḱun-tós, *ḱwn̥tós, an enlargement of Proto-Indo-European *ḱwṓ (“dog”). For the enlargement compare Latvian sùnt-ene (“big dog”) (and perhaps Old Armenian սկունդ (skund, “dog, puppy”), but this etymology is highly problematic, as the initial contradicts the certainly inherited word շուն (šun, “dog”), so an unrelated loanword, probably from Iranian, is more likely).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱwṓ

### Pronunciation

### Noun
