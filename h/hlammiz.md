## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlammiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *klem- (“to cry; shout; scream; make a noise”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *klem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxlɑm.miz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ noise; crash; bang
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a cover; lid
