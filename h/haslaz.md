# *haslaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/haslaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *haslaz

### Etymology

From areal Proto-Indo-European *kóslos, which may be of substrate or Wanderwort origin.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kóslos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑs.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ hazel
