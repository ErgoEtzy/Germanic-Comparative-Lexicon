## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hitjǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kéHid-ih₂ ~ *kHid-yéh₂-s, ultimately from *keHy- (“to heat; to become hot”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *keHy-, *kéHid-ih₂ ~ *kHid-yéh₂-s

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ heat
