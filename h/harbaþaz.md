### Etymology

Though attested only in Old Norse compound names, there are indications that the word must be old, from *karpátos. It must have been borrowed from Latin Carpatēs or a close relative prior to the occurrence of Grimm's law, and thus predates the split of the Germanic languages. Ultimately from the same source as the Latin, whether pre-Germanic or another language.
