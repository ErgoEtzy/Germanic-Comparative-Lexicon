# *hiurijalīkaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hiurijal%C4%ABkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hiurijalīkaz

### Etymology

From *hiurijaz (“trusted; familiar”) + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *ḱey-, *ḱeywo-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxiu̯.ri.jɑ.liː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ smiling, friendly
