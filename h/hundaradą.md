# *hundaradą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hundarad%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hundaradą

### Etymology

From *hundą (“hundred”) + *radą (“count”), a neuter variant of *radō (“row, line, series”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *ḱm̥tóm

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxun.dɑ.ˌrɑ.ðɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction, with-genitive)_ a great hundred (120)
2. _(neuter, reconstruction, with-genitive)_ a hundred (100)
