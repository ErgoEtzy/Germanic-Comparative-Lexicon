## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hōraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kéh₂ros (“loved”), from *keh₂-. Cognate with Latin cārus, Latvian kārs.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *keh₂-, *kéh₂ros

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ adulterer
