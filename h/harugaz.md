# *harugaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/harugaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *harugaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱerh₂k- (“fenced or enclosed area”), from *ḱerh₂-, from which also *hurną (“horn”). Cognate with Latin carcer (“fence; prison”), Irish carn (“heap of stones”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱerh₂k-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑ.ru.ɣɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ Sanctuary; halidom; grove.
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ Pile of stones; (heathen) altar.
