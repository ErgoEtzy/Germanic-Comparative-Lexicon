# *hrugjaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hrugjaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrugjaz

### Etymology

Uncertain. Appears to stem from a Proto-Indo-European *(s)krewk- (“heap, hill; back, spine?”), with potential cognates listed at Latin crux (“cross (frame, symbol)”), though this is highly speculative. Whether the hypothetical root is a derivative of *(s)ker- (“to turn, bend”) is far from clear.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)krewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxruɣ.jɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ back
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ spine
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ ridge
