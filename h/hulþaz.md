## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hulþaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱl̥-tó-s, from *ḱel- (“to lean, incline, bend”) + *-tós. Cognate with Latin auscultō (“incline one's ear, listen, listen in, heed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-tós, *ḱel-, *ḱl̥-tó-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxul.θɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ inclined, favourable (towards)
2. _(reconstruction)_ gracious, devoted, loyal
3. _(reconstruction)_ graceful
