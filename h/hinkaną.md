# *hinkaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hinkan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hinkaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *keng-. Cognate with Old Irish cingid (“to tread, step”) (< Proto-Celtic *kengeti).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)keng-, *keng-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxin.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to limp, to hobble
