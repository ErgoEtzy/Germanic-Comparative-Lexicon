# *haltaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/haltaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *haltaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *koldos, from Proto-Indo-European *kelh₂-d-. Related to Old Irish coll (“destruction”), as well as possibly Sanskrit कड (kaḍa, “dumb, mute”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kelh₂-d-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑl.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ lame, halt
