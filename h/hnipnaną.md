## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hnipnaną

### Etymology

Closely related to *hnipōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxnip.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(intransitive, reconstruction)_ to become sad
2. _(intransitive, reconstruction)_ to be downcast, droop
