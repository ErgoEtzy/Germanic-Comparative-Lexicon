# *harjōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/harj%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *harjōną

### Etymology

From *harjaz (“army”) + *-ōną.
Compare typologically Latin populor. Also compare Russian как Мама́й прошёл (kak Mamáj prošól).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ker-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑr.jɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to overrun with an army; lay waste; ravage; devastate
