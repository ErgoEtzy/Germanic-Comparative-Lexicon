# *hramjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hramjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hramjaną

### Etymology

From *hramō (“frame, rack”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)ker-, *(s)krem-, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *ḱremh₂-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to frame, enclose with a framework or fence
2. _(reconstruction)_ to hem in, restrain
