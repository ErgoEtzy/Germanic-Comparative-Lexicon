[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/h%C5%8Dnij%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hōnijǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *keh₂n- (“to sing”). Related to *hanô (“rooster”), *hanjō (“hen”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *keh₂n-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɔː.ni.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ hen
