# *hagô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hag%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hagô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kágʰō, perhaps earlier *kágʰn̥ ~ *kagʰéns whence also Proto-West Germanic *hagn.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kagʰ-, *kágʰō

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑ.ɣɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ enclosure, yard
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ pasture
