## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *haljawītiją

### Etymology

From *haljō (“hell, underworld”) + *wītiją (“punishment”)

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *weyd-, *ḱel-, *ḱel-yo-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ eternal punishment, hell-torment
