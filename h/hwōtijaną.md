# *hwōtijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hw%C5%8Dtijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hwōtijaną

### Etymology

According to Kroonen this verb is a merger of:
* A causative verb equivalent to *hwētaną (“to attack, assault”) + *-janą.
* A denominative verb equivalent to *hwōtō (“threat”) + *-janą.
Both contributing terms derive ultimately from the same root.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)kewd-, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *kʷeh₁d-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxʷɔː.ti.ja.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to threaten
2. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to reprimand
