## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hallą

### Etymology

Uncertain. Perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *kHlnom (“encrustation, hardening”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kHlnom

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ that which is dried up
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ dearth, scarcity, lack
