# *hnīpaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hn%C4%ABpan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hnīpaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kneybʰ-. The root final -p was adopted from the iterative *hnippōną.
Likely related to *knīpaną, but it's unclear how the variation of the initial consonant arose.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kneybʰ-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to pinch, to squeeze
