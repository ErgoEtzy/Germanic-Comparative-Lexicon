## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *halþōną

### Etymology

From *halþaz (“sloping, inclined”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *kelH-, *ḱel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑl.θɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make incline, tilt, incline
