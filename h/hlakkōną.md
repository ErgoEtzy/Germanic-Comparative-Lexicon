## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hlakkōną

### Etymology

Secondary o-grade iterative of *hlahjaną (“to laugh”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxlɑk.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to laugh
