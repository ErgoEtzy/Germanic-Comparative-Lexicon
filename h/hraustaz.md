[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hraustaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hraustaz

### Etymology

Probably an adjectival formation from *hreusaną (“to fall, rush”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ strong
2. _(reconstruction)_ active
