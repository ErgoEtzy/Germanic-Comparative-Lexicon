[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hagj%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hagjō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kagʰyóm (“enclosure”), whence also Latin cohum, Welsh cae.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kagʰyóm

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑɣ.jɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ hedge
