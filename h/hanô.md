## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hanô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *keh₂n- (“to sing”), possibly through earlier *hanā as evidenced by Proto-Finnic *kana. Cognate with Latin canō, cantō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *keh₂n-
