# *haldaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/haldan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *haldaną

### Etymology

Uncertain. Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- (“to hide, conceal”). Other theories connect the word to Proto-Indo-European *kelH- (“to rise”), *kel- (“to drive”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kelH-, *ḱel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑl.dɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to hold, to keep
