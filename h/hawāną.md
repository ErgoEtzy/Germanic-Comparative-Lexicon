# *hawāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/haw%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hawāną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *kew-. Cognate Lithuanian kuvḗtis (“to be ashamed”).

The verb could also be reconstructed as *hēwijaną, but in view of Latvian kavêt (“to prevent”), *hawāną is more likely. Also cognate with Latin caveo (“to take care, beware”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kew-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑ.wɑː.nɑ̃/`
