# *hurnijǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hurnij%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hurnijǭ

### Etymology

From *hurną (“horn”) + *-jǭ (diminutive suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱerh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxur.ni.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a little horn; hook
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ an angle; corner; nook
