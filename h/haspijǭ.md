# *haspijǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/haspij%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *haspijǭ

### Etymology

Of unknown origin. Has been derived from a hypothetical earlier *hapsijǭ, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kamb- (“crooked; bent”), for which see *kh₂em- (“to bend, curve”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kamb-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑs.pi.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ clasp; hasp
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ reel
