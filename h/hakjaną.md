## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hakjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *keg-, *keng- (“to be sharp; peg; hook; handle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *keg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑk.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ To peck; chip
2. _(reconstruction)_ To stick; pierce; prick
