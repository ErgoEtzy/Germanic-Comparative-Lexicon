## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *herdō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)kerdʰ- (“file, row, herd”). Cognate with Russian череда́ (čeredá, “train, sequence”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)kerdʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxer.ðɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ herd
