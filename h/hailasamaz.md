# *hailasamaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hailasamaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hailasamaz

### Etymology

From *hailaz + *-samaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *kéh₂ilos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑi̯.lɑ.ˌsɑ.mɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ wholesome, sound, healthy
