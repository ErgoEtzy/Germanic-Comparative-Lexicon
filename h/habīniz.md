# *habīniz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hab%C4%ABniz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *habīniz

### Etymology

From *habjaną (“to have, hold”) + *-īniz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *keh₂p-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑ.βiː.niz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ the state of holding, having
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ something held or had
