## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hellaną

### Etymology

A verbal formation from Proto-Indo-European *kelh₁- (“to call, make noise”). The semantic shift from "make noise" > "loud" > "clear, bright" in the High German descendants parallels that of Latin clārus (“loud, clear”), from the same root.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kelh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxel.lɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to resound, make a sound
