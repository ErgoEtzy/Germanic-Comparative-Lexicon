# *hragilą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hragil%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hragilą

### Etymology

Uncertain, though probably from Proto-Indo-European *krek- (“to beat, weave; web”). See Proto-Slavic *kresati (“to strike fire”) for cognates.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *krek-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ garment; dress; mantle; robe
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ clothing; attire
