## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hrifilingaz

### Etymology

Probably from *hreflōną (“to sew, mend”) + *-ilingaz. Alternatively, from *hrefaz (“womb, belly, body”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-dʰlom, *-e-ló-s, *-lós, *-n̥kʷo-, *-tlom, *-trom

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxri.ɸi.liŋ.ɡɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ shoe
