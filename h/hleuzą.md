# *hleuzą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hleuz%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hleuzą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ḱlews- (“side of the head, temple, ear”), that is, the area around the ears (side of the head).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ḱlew-, *ḱlews-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxleu̯.zɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ ear
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ temple (of the forehead)
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ side of the head or face; profile; cheek
