# *hammō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hamm%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hammō

### Etymology

Uncertain. Perhaps from earlier *hanmō, from Proto-Indo-European *kónh₂méh₂-, from Proto-Indo-European *kónh₂m (“leg”), and thus cognate with Old Irish cnáim (“bone”) and Ancient Greek κνήμη (knḗmē, “tibia”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *kónh₂m, *kónh₂méh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈxɑm.mɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ shinbone
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ the hollow of the knee
3. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ ham
