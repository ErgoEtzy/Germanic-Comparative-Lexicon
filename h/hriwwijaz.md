# *hriwwijaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/hriwwijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *hriwwijaz

### Etymology

From *hrewwaną (“to grieve, to feel sorrow; to cause regret, to rue”) + *-jaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-yós, *krew(H)-, *krewh₂-

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ sad, sorrowful
2. _(reconstruction)_ regretful, penitent
