## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dagawerką

### Etymology

From *dagaz + *werką.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *dʰegʷʰ-, *dʰǵʰyés, *wer(k/ḱ)-nó-, *werǵ-, *werǵʰ-nó-, *wérǵom

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɑ.ɣɑ.ˌwer.kɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ a day's work
