# *deuzaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/deuzaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *deuzaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰews-ó-s, vṛddhi derivative of weak stem of *dʰwḗs ~ *dʰus-és or schwebeablaut of earlier *dʰwes-ó-s, both from *dʰwes- (“to breathe; to smoke”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰews-ó-s, *dʰwes-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdeu̯.zɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ wild, fierce, bold
