## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *daniskaz

### Etymology

From *daniz (“Dane”) + *-iskaz (“-ish”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-iskos, *dʰenh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɑ.nis.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ Danish (of the Danes)
