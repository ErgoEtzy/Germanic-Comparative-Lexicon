# *dubnaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dubnan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dubnaną

### Etymology

From *dubaz (“dull”) + *-naną. Related to *daubaz (“stunned”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-néh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈduβ.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to become weak
