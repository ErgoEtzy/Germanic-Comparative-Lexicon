# *diurijalīkaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/diurijal%C4%ABkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *diurijalīkaz

### Etymology

From *diurijaz (“dear”) + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *dʰegʷʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdiu̯.ri.jɑ.liː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ glorious
2. _(reconstruction)_ valuable
