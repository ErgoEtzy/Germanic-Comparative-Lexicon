## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *delō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₁(y)- (“to suckle, nurse”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰeh₁(y)-
