## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dunkaraz

### Etymology

Similar to *dunkalaz (“dark”), a zero-grade adjective related to *dinkwa-, *dankwaz (“dark”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈduŋ.kɑ.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ dark
