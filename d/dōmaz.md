# *dōmaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/d%C5%8Dmaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dōmaz

### Etymology

From earlier *dṓmos, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰóh₁mos (“thing put”), from *dʰeh₁- (“to put, do”). Related to *dōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰeh₁-, *dʰóh₁mos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɔː.mɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ judgement
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ fame, repute
