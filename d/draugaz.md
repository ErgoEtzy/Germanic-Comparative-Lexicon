## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *draugaz

### Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Indo-European *dʰrówgʰos.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰrewgʰ-, *dʰrówgʰos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdrɑu̯.ɣɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, masculine, reconstruction)_ based on cognates: phantom (mirage), ghost, revenant and thereof; potentially demon, troll, ghoul etc
