# *dungōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dung%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dungōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰengʰ- (“to cover, hide, conceal”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰengʰ-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cover
2. _(reconstruction)_ to pour over
3. _(reconstruction)_ to pile up
