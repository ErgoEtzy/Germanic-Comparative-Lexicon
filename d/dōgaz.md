## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dōgaz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *dagāną (“to dawn; to become day”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No PIE form is listed or machine-resolvable in this snapshot.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɔː.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ daily, diurnal, pertaining to a day
2. _(East, Germanic, in-compounds, reconstruction)_ indicating a duration of days
