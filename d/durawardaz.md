## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *durawardaz

### Etymology

From *durz (“door”) + *wardaz (“guard”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *dʰwer-, *wer-, *wert-, *wor-tós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdu.rɑ.ˌu̯ɑr.ðɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ door-keeper, porter
