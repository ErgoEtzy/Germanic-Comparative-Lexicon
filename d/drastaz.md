## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *drastaz

### Etymology

From earlier *drahstaz, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰragʰs-tó-s, from *dʰrā́ks (“dregs, sediment”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰragʰs-tó-s

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ dregs, lees
