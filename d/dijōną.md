## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dijōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰh₁éyeti (“to suckle, nurse”). Cognate with Latin fellō (“suck”), Old Church Slavonic доити (doiti), Sanskrit धयति (dháyati, “he sucks, drinks”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰeh₁(y)-, *dʰh₁éyeti

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to suck, suckle
