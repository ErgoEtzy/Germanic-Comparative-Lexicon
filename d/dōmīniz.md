[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/d%C5%8Dm%C4%ABniz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dōmīniz

### Etymology

From *dōmijaną (“to judge”) + *-īniz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰeh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɔː.miː.niz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a judging, judgement
