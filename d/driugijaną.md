## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *driugijaną

### Etymology

From *dreugaz (“enduring, lasting”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(e)-ós, *(ó)-os, *-os, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *-ós, *dʰrewgʰ-

- IPA: `/ˈdriu̯.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to make enduring
2. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to eke out, supplement
