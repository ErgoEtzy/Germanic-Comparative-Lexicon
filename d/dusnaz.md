# *dusnaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dusnaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dusnaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰus-no-s (cognate with Proto-Celtic *dusnos (“dark, brown”)), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰwes-, seemingly related to Proto-Indo-European *dʰewh₂-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰewh₂-, *dʰus-no-s, *dʰwes-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdus.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ brownish; brown
2. _(reconstruction)_ yellow
