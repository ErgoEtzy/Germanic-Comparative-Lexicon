# *draugiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/draugiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *draugiz

### Etymology

Seemingly from a Proto-Indo-European *dʰrewgʰ- (“to strengthen; become hard or solid”), from *dʰer- (“to hold, hold fast, support”). Related to Proto-Germanic *druknaz (“dry”); see there for more.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰer-, *dʰrewgʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdrɑu̯.ɣiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ hard, solid
2. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ dry
