# *dūnaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/d%C5%ABnaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dūnaz

### Etymology

From *dū- (“to whisk, whip up, agitate, storm”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewh₂- (“smoke, dust, haze”).

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰuh₂-nós, from the root *dʰewh₂- (“to smoke, fume, raise dust”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰewh₂-, *dʰuh₂-nós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈduː.nɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ an accumulation, pile, heap
