# *dauniz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dauniz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dauniz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰowh₂-nis, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewh₂- (“smoke, fume, mist, haze”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰewh₂-, *dʰowh₂-nis

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ haze, smoke
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ fume; smell, odor
