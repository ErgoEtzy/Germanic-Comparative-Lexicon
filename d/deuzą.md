## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *deuzą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰews-ó-m (“having breath; animal”), vṛddhi derivative from weak stem of *dʰwḗs ~ *dʰus-és or schwebeablaut of earlier *dʰwes-ó-m, both from *dʰwes- (“to breathe; to smoke”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰews-ó-m, *dʰwes-
