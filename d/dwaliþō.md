## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dwaliþō

### Etymology

From *dwalaz (“foolish, misled”) + *-iþō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-teh₂, *dʰwel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdwɑ.li.θɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ foolishness
