# *derbaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/derban%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *derbaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰerHbʰ- (“to work, perish, die”). Related to Lithuanian dirbti (“to work”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰerHbʰ-, *sterbʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈder.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to work
2. _(reconstruction)_ to perish
