## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dulją

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰel(h₂)- (“curvature, hollow, bend”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰel(h₂)-, *dʰel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdul.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, neuter, reconstruction)_ tube, pipe
2. _(Germanic, West, neuter, reconstruction)_ nozzle, spout
