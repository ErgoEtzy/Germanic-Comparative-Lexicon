## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dragjō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰragʰ-yeh₂, from *dʰrā́ks.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰragʰ-yeh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdrɑɣ.jɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, feminine, reconstruction)_ dregs, sediment
