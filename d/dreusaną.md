# *dreusaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dreusan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dreusaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰrewHs- (“to fall down, break off”). Cognate with Russian дры́хнуть (drýxnutʹ, “to sleep”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰrewHs-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdreu̯.sɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to fall
