## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dustą

### Etymology

Substantivation of unattested *dustaz (“dusty”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰus-tó-s, from *dʰwes- (“to breathe; to smoke”) + *-tós.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-tós, *dʰus-tó-s, *dʰwes-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ dust
