# *dwelaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dwelan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dwelaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰwelH- (“to err, obscure”). See the related *dulaz (“confused; foolish”) for cognates.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰwelH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdwe.lɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to go astray
