## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *drōbuz

### Etymology

A u-stem nominal related to *drabaz (“dregs”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdrɔː.βuz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ turbid, disturbed, troubled
