# *darbijaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/darbijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *darbijaz

### Etymology

From *derbaną (“to perish”) or from *darbijaną (“to destroy”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɑr.βi.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ hostile
