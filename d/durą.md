## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *durą

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *dʰur-o-m, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰwer-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰwer-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ big door, gate
