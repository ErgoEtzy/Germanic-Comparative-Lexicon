## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dailǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰayleh₂, from *dʰayl- (“part, watershed”). See *dailiz for more.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰayleh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɑi̯.lɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ part, portion, deal, division
