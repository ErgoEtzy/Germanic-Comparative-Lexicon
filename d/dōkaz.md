# *dōkaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/d%C5%8Dkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dōkaz

### Etymology

Of uncertain origin. Traditionally derived from a Proto-Indo-European *dʰweg- (“cloth”), and compared with Sanskrit ध्वज (dhvajá, “flag, banner”). However, Kroonen rejects this theory, noting the irregular sound shift from *dʰw- > *do required for cognacy, and leaves the origin open.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰweg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɔː.kɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ cloth, rag
