# *drībaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dr%C4%ABban%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *drībaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰreybʰ- (“to drive, push”). Cognate with Irish drip (“bustle”) and possibly Lithuanian dri̇̀bti (“to fall, trickle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰreybʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdriː.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to drive, push, compel to go
