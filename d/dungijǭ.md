# *dungijǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dungij%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dungijǭ

### Etymology

Uncertain. Perhaps from *dungō (“earth, dung”) + *-jǭ. Kroonen, however, derives this via an intermediary verb *dungijaną (“to fertilise”). See Proto-West Germanic *dungijan.

Possibly from *dungz (“vault, cave, cellar”) + *-jǭ.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰengʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈduŋ.ɡi.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ earthen mound
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ dunghill
