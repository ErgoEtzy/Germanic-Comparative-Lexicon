# *dēaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/d%C4%93an%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dēaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₁(y)- (“to suckle, nurse”). Cognate with Latin fellō (“suck”), Old Church Slavonic доити (doiti, “to suckle”), Sanskrit धयति (dháyati, “he sucks, drinks”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰeh₁(y)-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɛː.ɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to suck, suckle
