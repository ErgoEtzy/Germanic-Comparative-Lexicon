# *drinkaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/drinkan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *drinkaną

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *dʰrenǵ- (“to draw into one's mouth, sip, gulp”), nasalised variant of *dʰreǵ- (“to draw, glide”). Cognate with Lithuanian drė́gti (“to become moist”), Sanskrit ध्रजति (dhrájati, “to glide, swoop”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰrenǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdriŋ.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to drink
