## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dreugaz

### Etymology

Derived from *dreuganą (“to serve, be loyal”) + *-az (deverbal adjective suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰrewgʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdreu̯.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ enduring, lasting
