# *duppōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dupp%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *duppōną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *dʰubʰ-néh₂- from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewbʰ- (“deep”), thus "go deep" > "dive". By synchronic analysis, an iterative verb related to the strong verb *dūbaną (“to dive”). Compare also West Germanic *duppijan.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰewb-, *dʰewbʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdup.pɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to dip
