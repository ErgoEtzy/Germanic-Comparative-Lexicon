## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *diganą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰeyǵʰ- (“to knead”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰeyǵʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdi.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to knead
2. _(reconstruction)_ to create out of clay or dough
