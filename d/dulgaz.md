## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dulgaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰlegʰ- (“debt”). Cognate with Old Irish dligid (“lays claim to, deserves”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰlegʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdul.ɣɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ debt
