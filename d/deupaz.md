## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *deupaz

### Etymology

Etymology tree
Proto-Indo-European *dʰewbʰ-der.
Proto-Indo-European *dʰewbʰnós
Proto-Germanic *deupaz
From pre-Germanic *deuppaz < *dʰewbʰnós, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewbʰ-. The devoicing of what came from the root-final *-bʰ- is due to Kluge's law.
Cognate to Old Church Slavonic дъбрь (dŭbrĭ, “valley”), Lithuanian dubùs (“deep, hollow”), Albanian det (“sea”), Welsh dwfn (“deep”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰewbʰ-, *dʰewbʰnós
