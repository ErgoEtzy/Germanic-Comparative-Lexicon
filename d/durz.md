[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/durz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *durz

### Etymology

Likely back-formed from *dʰur-ih₁, an old neuter dual form, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰwer-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰwer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/durz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ door, hatch, flap
