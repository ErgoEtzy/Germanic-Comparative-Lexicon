## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *duttaz

### Etymology

Unknown. Perhaps of onomatopoeic origin, also compare Dutch doetje (“silly person, softie”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdut.tɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ wisp; tuft of grass or grain
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bundle; group; clod clump
