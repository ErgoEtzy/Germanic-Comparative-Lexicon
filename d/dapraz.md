# *dapraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dapraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dapraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰob-, *dʰeb- (“thick, fat, heavy”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰob-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɑp.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ stout; solid
2. _(reconstruction)_ heavy
3. _(reconstruction)_ bold
