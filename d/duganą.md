## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *duganą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰedʰówgʰe, from *dʰewgʰ- (“to produce”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰedʰówgʰe, *dʰewgʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdu.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be useful, to avail
