[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dulg%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dulgą

### Etymology

Maybe related to *dulgaz (“debt”) or perhaps to Ancient Greek θέλγω (thélgō, “to enchant, cheat”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ fight
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ injury, wound
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ scar
