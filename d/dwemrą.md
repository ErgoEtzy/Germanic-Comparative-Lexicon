# *dwemrą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dwemr%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dwemrą

### Etymology

From *dwemaną (“to smoke; fume”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewh₂- (“to smoke, raise dust”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰewh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdwem.rɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, neuter, reconstruction)_ smoke, vapour
2. _(Germanic, West, neuter, reconstruction)_ illusion, phantom
