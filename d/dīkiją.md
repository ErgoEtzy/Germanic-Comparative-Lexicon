## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dīkiją

### Etymology

From *dīkaz + *-ją.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *dʰeygʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdiː.ki.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, neuter, reconstruction)_ dike, trench, ditch
