# *dwaiskijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dwaiskijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dwaiskijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰwey- (“to slip away; dwindle; die”), from *dʰew- (“to dwindle, die”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰwey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdwɑi̯s.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ (transitive) to suffocate, extinguish
