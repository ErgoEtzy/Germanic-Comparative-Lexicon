## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dauþudagaz

### Etymology

From *dauþuz (“death”) + *dagaz (“day”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tus, *dʰegʷʰ-, *dʰew-, *dʰówtus, *dʰǵʰyés

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɑu̯.θu.ˌdɑ.ɣɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ deathday
