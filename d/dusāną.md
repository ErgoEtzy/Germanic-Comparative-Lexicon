# *dusāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dus%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dusāną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰwes- (“to breathe”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰews-, *dʰwes-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be stunned, swoon
2. _(reconstruction)_ to be dizzy
3. _(reconstruction)_ to slumber
