# *dawjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dawjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dawjaną

### Etymology

Often compared with Proto-Indo-European *dʰew- (“to flow; to die”), and *dʰweh₂-, but Kroonen prefers to connect it with Hittite [script needed] (tuḫḫušzi, “to end”), from *dʰuH-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰew-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɑu̯.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to die
