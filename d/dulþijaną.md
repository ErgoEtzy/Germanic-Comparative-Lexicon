[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dul%C3%BEijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dulþijaną

### Etymology

From *dulþiz (“celebration, festival”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *dʰwelH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdul.θi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to celebrate, to have a festival
