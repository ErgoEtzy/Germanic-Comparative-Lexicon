[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/deupal%C4%ABkaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *deupalīkaz

### Etymology

From *deupaz (“deep”) + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *dʰewbʰ-, *dʰewbʰnós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdeu̯.pɑ.liː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ deeply
