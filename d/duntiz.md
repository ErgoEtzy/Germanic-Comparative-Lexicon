# *duntiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/duntiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *duntiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰen- (“to strike, hit”), synchronically analyzed as a nominal formation from *duntijaną (“to fall, drop”). Cognate with Albanian gdhend (“to plane”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰen-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdun.tiz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ shock, impact, blow
