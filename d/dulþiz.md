## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dulþiz

### Etymology

Uncertain. Possibly from *dwelaną + *-þiz, an early ti-stem based on the root's original meaning of "to lose one's senses".

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *dʰwelH-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdul.θiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ celebration, festival
