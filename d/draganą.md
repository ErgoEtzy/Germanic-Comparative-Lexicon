# *draganą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dragan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *draganą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰrógʰ-e-ti, traditionally derived from *dʰregʰ- (“to pull, draw, drag”) (though Kroonen is skeptical and leaves the origin open).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰrógʰ-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdrɑ.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to draw, to pull, to carry
