## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dungz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰéngʰ-s ~ *dʰn̥gʰ-és, from *dʰengʰ- (“to cover, hide, conceal”) + *-s.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-s, *dʰengʰ-, *dʰéngʰ-s ~ *dʰn̥gʰ-és

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ cellar
