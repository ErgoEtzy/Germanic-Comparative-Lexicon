# *digraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/digraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *digraz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic Proto-Indo-European *dʰiǵʰrós (“(well-)formed”), equivalent to *diganą + *-raz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰeyǵʰ-, *dʰiǵʰrós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdiɣ.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ big, great, thick
