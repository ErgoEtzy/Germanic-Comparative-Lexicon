## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dauþuz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰówtus, equivalent to *dawjaną + *-þuz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰówtus

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɑu̯.θuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ death
