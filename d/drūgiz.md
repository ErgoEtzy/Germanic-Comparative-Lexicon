# *drūgiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dr%C5%ABgiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *drūgiz

### Etymology

Possible vṛddhi gerundive of otherwise unattested *drugijaną, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰr̥ǵʰ-yé-ti, from *dʰerǵʰ- (“to strengthen”), from *dʰer- (“to hold, hold fast, support”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰr̥ǵʰ-yé-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdruː.ɣiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ hard, solid
2. _(reconstruction)_ dry
