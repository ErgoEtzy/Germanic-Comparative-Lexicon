# *duppaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/duppaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *duppaz

### Etymology

From earlier *dubná-, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewb- (“deep, hollow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰewb-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ hollow, depression, well
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ bowl
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ shell
