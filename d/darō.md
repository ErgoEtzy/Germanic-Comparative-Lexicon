# *darō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dar%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *darō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰórh₃-eh₂, from *dʰerh₃- (“to leap, spring”). Cognate with Sanskrit धारा (dhā́rā), Avestan 𐬛𐬁𐬭𐬁 (dārā).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰórh₃-eh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɑ.rɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ injury; damage; harm
