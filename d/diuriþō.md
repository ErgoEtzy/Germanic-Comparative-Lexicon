# *diuriþō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/diuri%C3%BE%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *diuriþō

### Etymology

From *diurijaz + *-iþō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *dʰegʷʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdiu̯.ri.θɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ dearness, costliness, preciousness
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ glory, honour
