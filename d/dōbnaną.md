# *dōbnaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/d%C5%8Dbnan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dōbnaną

### Etymology

From an unattested *dōbaz (“dull, numb”) + *-naną, perhaps ultimately from the root of *dapraz (“heavy, solid, stout”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-néh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɔːβ.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to become dull, numb
