# *denaraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/denaraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *denaraz

### Etymology

Thematicized form of Proto-Indo-European *dʰénr̥ (“flat of the hand, palm”). Cognate with Ancient Greek θέναρ (thénar, “palm of the hand; sole of the foot”). See *dʰénwr̥ for this and related reconstructions.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰénr̥

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ the palm of the hand
