## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *duniz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰún-is, from *dʰwen- (“to roar; boom; resound”). Cognate with Sanskrit ध्वन् (dhvan, “sound”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry
