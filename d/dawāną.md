# *dawāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/daw%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dawāną

### Etymology

Kroonen considers the word to be from Proto-Indo-European *dʰh̥₂w-eh₁-, from a root *dʰeh₂w- (“to look at, marvel”), and compares Ancient Greek θαῦμᾰ (thaûmă, “a wonder, marvel”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰh̥₂w-eh₁-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to marvel
