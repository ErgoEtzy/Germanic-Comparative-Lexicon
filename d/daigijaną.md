## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *daigijaną

### Etymology

From *daigaz + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *dʰeyǵʰ-, *dʰóyǵʰ-o-s, *dʰóyǵʰos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɑi̯.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to make soft
