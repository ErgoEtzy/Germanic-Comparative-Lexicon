## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *draumaz

### Etymology

From earlier *draugmaz, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰrówgʰmos, a derivative of Proto-Indo-European *dʰrewgʰ- (“to deceive, injure, damage”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰrewgʰ-, *dʰrówgʰmos
