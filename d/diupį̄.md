## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *diupį̄

### Etymology

From *deupaz (“deep”) + *-į̄.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-i-h₂, *-ih₂, *dʰewbʰ-, *dʰewbʰnós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdiu̯.pĩː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ depth
