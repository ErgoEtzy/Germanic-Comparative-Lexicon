[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/daigaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *daigaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰóyǵʰ-o-s, from *dʰeyǵʰ- (“to build, mold, form, knead”). Cognate with Proto-Slavic *dě̄žà (“dough trough”), Latin fingō (“mold”, verb).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰeyǵʰ-, *dʰóyǵʰ-o-s, *dʰóyǵʰos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɑi̯.ɣɑz/`
