# *drēnuz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dr%C4%93nuz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *drēnuz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰreh₁n- (“bee, hornet, drone”). Cognate with Lithuanian tranas (“drone”), Serbo-Croatian trut (“drone”), Czech trubec (“drone”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰreh₁n-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdrɛː.nuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ male bee or wasp; drone
