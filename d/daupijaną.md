[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/daupijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *daupijaną

### Etymology

Causative from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewb-, from which also *deupaz (“deep”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰewb-, *dʰewbʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɑu̯.pi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to dip (in a liquid), to immerse
