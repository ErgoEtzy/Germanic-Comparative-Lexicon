## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dīkaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰeygʷ- (“to ditch, plant, set up”).

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰeyǵʰ-n-, from *dʰeyǵʰ- (“to form, shape”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰeygʷ-, *dʰeyǵʰ-n-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdiː.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to try, check
2. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ to measure, calibrate
