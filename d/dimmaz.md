# *dimmaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dimmaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dimmaz

### Etymology

According to Kroonen, from either Proto-Indo-European *dʰem(H)-nó-s or *dʰembʰ-nó-s. In the former case, perhaps related to Middle Irish deime (“darkness”). Formally, it would be an adjective from *dʰemH- (“to whisk, smoke, blow; dust, haze, cloud; obscure”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰem(H)-nó-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdim.mɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ dim
2. _(reconstruction)_ gloomy
