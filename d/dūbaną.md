# *dūbaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/d%C5%ABban%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dūbaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰewbʰ- (“deep”); by synchronic analysis, an apparent back-formation from the iterative *duppōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰewbʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈduː.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to dive, to sink
