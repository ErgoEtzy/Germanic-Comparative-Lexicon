## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *darōþuz

### Etymology

From *darō (“injury; damage, harm”) + *-ōþuz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *dʰórh₃-eh₂

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a light spear; javelin; dart
