## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dunkalaz

### Etymology

Zero-grade adjective related to *dinkwaz, *dankwaz (“dark”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈduŋ.kɑ.lɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ dark
