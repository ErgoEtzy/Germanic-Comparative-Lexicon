[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dankwijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dankwijaną

### Etymology

From *dankwaz (“dark”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *dʰóngʷ-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɑŋ.kʷi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to darken
