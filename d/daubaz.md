## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *daubaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰewbʰ- (“to whisk, be obscured”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰewbʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɑu̯.βɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ stunned
2. _(reconstruction)_ deaf
