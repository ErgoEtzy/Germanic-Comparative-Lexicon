## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *draibō

### Etymology

Derived from *drībaną (“to push, move, drive”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No PIE form is listed or machine-resolvable in this snapshot.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdrɑi̯.βɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a drive, push, move
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ that which is driven; a herd
3. _(Germanic, West, feminine, reconstruction)_ a way, lane, track
