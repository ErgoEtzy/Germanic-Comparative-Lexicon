# *drītaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dr%C4%ABtan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *drītaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *dʰreyd- (“to have diarrhea”). Perhaps cognate to Russian дриста́ть (dristátʹ, “to shart”), as well as Persian درد (dord, “dregs”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰreyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdriː.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to defecate
2. _(reconstruction)_ to urinate
