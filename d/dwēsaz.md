[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/dw%C4%93saz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dwēsaz

### Etymology

Compare *dusāną (“to slumber”); also Latvian dusēt (“to sleep, rest”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdwɛː.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ foolish, stupid
