# *dawwą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/daww%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *dawwą

### Etymology

Uncertain; possibly from Proto-Indo-European *dʰowh₂-o-m, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewh₂- (“smoke, haze”). Cognate with Proto-Indo-Iranian *dʰuHmás (“mist; smoke”), Lithuanian dúlis (“mist”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *dʰewh₂-, *dʰowh₂-o-m

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈdɑw.wɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ moisture
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ dew
