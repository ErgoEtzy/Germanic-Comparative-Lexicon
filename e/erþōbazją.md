[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/er%C3%BE%C5%8Dbazj%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *erþōbazją

### Etymology

From *erþō (“earth”) + *bazją (“berry”). Compare Polish poziomka, Russian земляни́ка (zemljaníka) (related to земля́ (zemljá)).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈer.θɔː.bɑz.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ strawberry
