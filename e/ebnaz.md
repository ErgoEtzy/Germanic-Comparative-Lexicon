# *ebnaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ebnaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ebnaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(h₁)ém-no- (“equal, straight; flat, level, even”). Cognate with Sanskrit अम्नस् (amnás, “just now”, adv.), with differing accent. Whether the root is a derivative of *h₁em- (“to take, distribute”) is unclear.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(h₁)ém-no-, *h₁émnos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈeβ.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ even, level
