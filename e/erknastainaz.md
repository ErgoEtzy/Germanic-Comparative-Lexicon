# *erknastainaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/erknastainaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *erknastainaz

### Etymology

From *erknaz (“holy, genuine, precious”) + *stainaz (“stone”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₁erg-, *h₂erǵ-, *steyh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈer.knɑ.ˌstɑi̯.nɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ gem
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ precious stone
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ pearl
