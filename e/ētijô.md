## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ētijô

### Etymology

From *etaną (“to eat”) + *-ijô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₁édti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɛː.ti.jɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ eater, one who eats
