## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ebnalīkaz

### Etymology

From *ebnaz + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(h₁)ém-no-, *h₁émnos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈeβ.nɑ.ˌliː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ equal, fair
