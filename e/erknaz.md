# *erknaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/erknaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *erknaz

### Etymology

Unlikely to be from Proto-Indo-European *h₂erǵ- (“white, shining”), which would give Proto-Germanic *ark-, but instead seems to represent a Proto-Indo-European *h₁erg- or *h₁erǵ-. Possibly cognate with Tocharian A yärk- (“to honor, venerate”), Tocharian B yarke (“honor, reverence”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁erg-, *h₂erǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈer.knɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ pure, holy, genuine, precious
