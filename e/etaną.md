## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *etaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁édti, from *h₁ed- (“to eat”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁édti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈe.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to eat
