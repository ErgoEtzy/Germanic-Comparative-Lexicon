# *etulaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/etulaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *etulaz

### Etymology

From *etaną (“to eat”) + *-ulaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁ed-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈe.tu.lɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ consuming
2. _(reconstruction)_ gluttonous
