# *erþōkundaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/er%C3%BE%C5%8Dkundaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *erþōkundaz

### Etymology

From *erþō (“earth”) + *kundaz (“born”). It is uncertain whether this adjective already existed in Proto-Germanic; the apparent descendants may also be explained as parallel post-Proto-Germanic formations. The Gothic term is commonly cited as a calque of Latin terrigena.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₁er-, *h₁érteh₂, *ǵenh₁-, *ǵn̥h₁tós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈer.θɔː.ˌkun.ðɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ terrestrial, of earthly birth
