## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ēdraz

### Etymology

Unknown. Lithuanian otrùs (“quick”) and Latvian ātrs (“idem”) are probably loans from the Germanic, rather than inherited.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɛː.ðrɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ quick, speedy, prompt
