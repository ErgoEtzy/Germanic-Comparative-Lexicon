# *ermunduliz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ermunduliz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ermunduliz

### Etymology

From *ermunaz (“world, earth”) + *dulą (“valley”) + *-iz (“-er”). The second element is a variant of *dalą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-mn̥, *-yes-, *-yōs, *h₁er-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a member of the Hermunduri, a well-documented and powerful Germanic tribe
