## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Ermunahildiz

### Etymology

From *ermunaz (“strong, whole”) + *hildiz (“battle, fight”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-mn̥, *h₁er-, *kelh₂-

### Proper noun
