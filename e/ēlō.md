## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ēlō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁ólos or *ēl- (“awl, prong”). Cognate with Sanskrit आरा (ā́rā).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁ólos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɛː.lɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ awl
