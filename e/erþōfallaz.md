# *erþōfallaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/er%C3%BE%C5%8Dfallaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *erþōfallaz

### Etymology

From *erþō + *fallaz, with the latter derived from *fallaną (“to fall”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₁er-, *h₁érteh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈer.θɔː.ˌɸɑl.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a fall to the ground
