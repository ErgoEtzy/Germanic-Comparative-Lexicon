# *ēmǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C4%93m%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ēmǭ

### Etymology

Possibly from a variant *h₁eh₁-mó-s of Proto-Indo-European *h₁oh₁-mó-s (“raw, bitter”), thus “raw skin” > “erysipelas”, based on Kroonen's unorthodox reconstruction of this root. Despite superficial similarities, unrelated to *ammōną (“to irritate”), which derives from a different root.

Of unknown origin, with no certain cognates outside of Germanic.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁oh₁-mó-s, *h₂eh₃mós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɛː.mɔ̃ː/`
