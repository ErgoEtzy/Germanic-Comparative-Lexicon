# *etunaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/etunaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *etunaz

### Etymology

From *etaną (“to eat”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₁ed- (“to eat”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁ed-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈe.tu.nɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ an eater
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ an eater; overeater, glutton
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a giant
