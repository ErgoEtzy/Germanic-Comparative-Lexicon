## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *artijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂erd- (“sharp point, stinger”). Cognate with Old Irish aird (“point, ord, end point”), Ancient Greek ἄρδις (árdis, “arrowhead”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂erd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑr.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to tease, taunt, grill, annoy, irritate, provoke
