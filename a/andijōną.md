# *andijōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/andij%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *andijōną

### Etymology

From *andijaz (“end”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂ent-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑn.di.jɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to end, bring to an end, finish
