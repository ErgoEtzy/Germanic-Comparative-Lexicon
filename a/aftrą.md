# *aftrą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/aftr%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aftrą

### Etymology

From the accusative and/or allative form of Proto-Indo-European *h₂ep(o)teros, contrastive form of *h₂epó (“off, away”) (whence *ab, *af, *aba-). Compare Sanskrit अपतरम् (apataram, “further away”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂ep(o)teros, *h₂epóteros

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑɸ.trɑ̃/`

### Adverb

1. _(reconstruction)_ back, backwards
2. _(reconstruction)_ again, once more
