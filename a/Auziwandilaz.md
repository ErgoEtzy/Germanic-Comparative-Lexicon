## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Auziwandilaz

### Etymology

From *auzi (“dawn”) + *wandilaz (“wanderer”). The first element from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ews-, cognate with Latin Aurora, Ancient Greek Ἠώς (Ēṓs) (compare *austraz, *Austrǭ). For the semantics of the second element, compare Ancient Greek πλάνης (plánēs, “wanderer; planet”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂ews-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑu̯.zi.ˌwɑn.di.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ The morning star
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ Aurvandill, a figure in Germanic legend
