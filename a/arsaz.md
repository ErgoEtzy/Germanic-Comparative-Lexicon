## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *arsaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁órs-o-s (“rear-end”). Compare Ancient Greek ὄρρος (órrhos), Hittite 𒅈𒊏𒀸 (ar-ra-aš /⁠arraš⁠/), Old Irish err, and Middle Armenian ոռ (oṙ).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁órs-o-s, *h₁órsos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑr.sɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ arse/ass, anus, buttocks
