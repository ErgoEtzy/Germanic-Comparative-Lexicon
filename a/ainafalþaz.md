## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ainafalþaz

### Etymology

From *ainaz + *-falþaz. Compare Latin simplex (“folded once”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₁óynos, *pel-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯.nɑ.ˌɸɑl.θɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ onefold, simple
2. _(reconstruction)_ whole, single
