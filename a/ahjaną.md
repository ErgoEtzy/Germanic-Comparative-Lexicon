## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ahjaną

### Etymology

Uncertain. Has been compared to *h₃ekʷ- (“to see”) (by Osthoff) and to Ancient Greek ὄκνος (óknos, “hesitation”) (by Pokorny). Kroonen is unconvinced by the previously-mentioned proposals, and instead suggests it could be identical to *ahjaną (“to graze”), whence Old Norse æja, if the intermediate meaning was “to ruminate”; on the other hand however, he prefers to reconstruct *ahjaną in the "graze" sense as *aiwjaną, which would preclude a relation.
