# *Austrǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/Austr%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Austrǭ

### Etymology

Often cited as deriving from *h₂ews- (“dawn; to dawn”), whence Proto-Balto-Slavic *auštrā́ˀ (“dawn, morning”); however, a derivation from *austrą, *auzrą, a metathesized form of *wazrą (“spring (season)”), + *-ǭ, is more semantically plausible.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂ews-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑu̯s.trɔ̃ː/`

### Proper noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ Easter, festival celebrated at the spring equinox
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ Easter, goddess of spring
