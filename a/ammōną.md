## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ammōną

### Etymology

From earlier paradigm *ammōþi ~ *amunanþi, from pre-Proto-Germanic *omə-nā́-ti ~ *om-un-ánti, iterative from Proto-Indo-European *h₃emh₃- (“to insist, urge”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃emh₃-
