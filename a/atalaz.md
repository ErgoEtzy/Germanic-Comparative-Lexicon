# *atalaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/atalaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *atalaz

### Etymology

Possibly from Pre-Germanic/Proto-Indo-European *h₃édolos, from the root *h₃ed- (“to hate”); related to *atjaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃édolos

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ fierce
2. _(reconstruction)_ terrible, loathsome
3. _(reconstruction)_ hideous
