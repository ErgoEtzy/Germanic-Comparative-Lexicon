## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *alamann-

### Etymology

From *ala- + *mann-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(dʰ)ǵʰmō, *(dʰ)ǵʰmon-, *mon-, *ǵʰmṓ

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ mankind
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ one of the Alemanni
