## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *alizō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂élis- (“alder”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂élis-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.li.zɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ alder
