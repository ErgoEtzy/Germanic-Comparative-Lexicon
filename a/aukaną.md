# *aukaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/aukan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aukaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂éwg-e-ti, from *h₂ewg- (“to increase”). Cognate with Latin augeō (“I augment”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂ewg-, *h₂éwg-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑu̯.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to increase, to grow
