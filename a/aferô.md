## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aferô

### Etymology

Possibly a substantivization of *aferą (“after, following”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂epó.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂epó

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.ɸe.rɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ offspring, descendant, successor
