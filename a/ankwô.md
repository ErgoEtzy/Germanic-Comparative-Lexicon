[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ankw%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ankwô

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *óngʷô, from Proto-Indo-European *h₃éngʷō, collective of *h₃éngʷn̥ (“fat, oil, ointment”), from *h₃engʷ- (“anoint”). Cognate with Old Armenian օծ (ōc), Latin ungō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃engʷ-, *h₃éngʷō

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑŋ.kʷɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ ointment
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ butter
