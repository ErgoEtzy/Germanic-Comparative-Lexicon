## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aljaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂el- (“to grow, nourish”). Alternative etymology derives from *h₁elh₂- (“to spur, drive”). If so, then related to *lanō (“lane”).

From *alaną + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry
