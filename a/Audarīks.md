## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Audarīks

### Etymology

From *audaz (“wealth, riches”) + *rīks (“king, ruler”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₃rḗǵs

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑu̯.ðɑ.ˌriːks/`

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
