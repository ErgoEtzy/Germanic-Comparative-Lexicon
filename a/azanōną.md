[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/azan%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *azanōną

### Etymology

From *azaniz (“summer, harvest”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)h₁es-, *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *h₁os-on-i-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑzɑ.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to harvest, reap, labor
