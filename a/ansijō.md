# *ansijō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ansij%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ansijō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂ens-yeh₂. Cognate with Ancient Greek ἡνία pl (hēnía, “rein”), Latin ānsa (“handle”), Old Irish éisi pl (“reins”), Lithuanian ąsà (“jug handle”), Latvian ùoss (“jug handle”), Old Prussian ansis (“hook”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂ens-yeh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑn.si.jɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ handle (of a cup or bowl which forms a loop or half-loop)
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ eyelet, eye (of a needle)
