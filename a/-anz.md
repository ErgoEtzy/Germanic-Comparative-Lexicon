## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -anz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *-oms (o-stem accusative plural), from *-os (o-stem suffix) + *-ms (accusative plural suffix); compare Ancient Greek -ους (-ous) (dialectal -ως (-ōs), -οις (-ois), -ονς (-ons)), Hittite -𒍑 (-uš), Latin -ōs, Lithuanian -us, and Sanskrit -आन् (-ān).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-ms, *-oms, *-os

### Suffix

1. _(morpheme, reconstruction)_ Forms the accusative plural of masculine a-stem nouns.
   - _stainaz (“stone”) + *-anz → *stainanz (“stones”)_
