## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *apaldraz

### Etymology

From *aplaz (“apple, fruit”) + *-draz (tree suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂ébōl

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.pɑl.drɑz/`

### Noun
