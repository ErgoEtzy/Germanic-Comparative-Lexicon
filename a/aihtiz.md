[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/aihtiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aihtiz

### Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Indo-European *h₂éyḱ-tis. By surface analysis, *aiganą + *-þiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂eyḱ-, *h₂éyḱ-tis

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯x.tiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ possessions, property
