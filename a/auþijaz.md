# *auþijaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/au%C3%BEijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *auþijaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂ewtyos (“forlorn, deserted”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ew (“away from”). Particularly close cognate with Ancient Greek αὔσιος (aúsios, “idle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂ew, *h₂ewtyos

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ void, empty, barren, deserted, waste
