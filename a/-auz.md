## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -auz

### Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Indo-European *-ows (u-stem genitive singular ending), from *-us (u-stem suffix) + *-s (genitive singular suffix). Compare Old Church Slavonic -оу (-u), Latin -ūs, Lithuanian -aus, and Sanskrit -ओः (-oḥ).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-ows, *-s, *-us

### Suffix

1. _(morpheme, reconstruction)_ Forms the genitive singulars of u-stem nouns.
   - _sunuz (“son”) + *-auz → *sunauz (“son's”)_
