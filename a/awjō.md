## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *awjō

### Etymology

From earlier *agwjō, originally a substantive adjective related to *ahwō (“water; stream, river”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ékʷeh₂ (“water”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂ékʷeh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑu̯.jɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ floodplain; meadow
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ island
