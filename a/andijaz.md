## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *andijaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂entíos (“front, forehead, end”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂ent-, *h₂entíos

- IPA: `/ˈɑn.di.jɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ the endpoint of something, whether the front end or back end
