## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *agiþō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂oḱ-et-eh₂ (“harrow”), from *h₂eḱ- (“sharp”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂eḱ-, *h₂oḱ-et-eh₂, *h₂óḱets

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.ɣi.θɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ harrow
