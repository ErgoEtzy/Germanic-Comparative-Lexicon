# *anstijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/anstijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *anstijaną

### Etymology

From *anstiz (“favour, affection”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tis, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *h₃neh₂-, *h₃n̥-né-h₂-ti, *h₃n̥néh₂ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑns.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to ask, request
2. _(reconstruction)_ to like, to appreciate
3. _(reconstruction)_ to rejoice
