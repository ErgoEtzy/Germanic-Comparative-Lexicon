# *abalagjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/abalagjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *abalagjaną

### Etymology

From *aba- + *lagjaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *legʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɑ.βɑ.ˈlɑɣ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to lay down, set aside, put away
