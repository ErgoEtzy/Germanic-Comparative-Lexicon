## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *andawurdijaną

### Etymology

From *andawurdiją (“answer”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *h₂énts

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑn.dɑ.ˌwur.ði.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to answer
   - _/*andiwerdōną_
