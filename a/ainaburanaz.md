## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ainaburanaz

### Etymology

From *ainaz (“one”) + *buranaz (“carried, borne”). The latter is the past participle of *beraną (“to bear”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *bʰer-, *h₁óynos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯.nɑ.ˌbu.rɑ.nɑz/`
