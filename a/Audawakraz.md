## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Audawakraz

### Etymology

From *audaz (“wealth, riches”) + *wakraz (“awake, watchful, alert”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *weǵ-, *wóǵ-ros

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑu̯.ðɑ.ˌwɑk.rɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
