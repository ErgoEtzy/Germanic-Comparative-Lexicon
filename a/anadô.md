## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *anadô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂enh₁-dʰō, from *h₂enh₁- (“to breathe, blow”); compare *ananą. Related to Latin anima (“breath, spirit”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂enh₁-, *h₂enh₁-dʰō

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.nɑ.ðɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ breath
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ spirit
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ zeal
