## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *argijaną

### Etymology

From *argaz (“unworthy, cowardly”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *h₃orǵʰ-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑr.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to spoil, to ruin
2. _(reconstruction)_ to make into a coward
