# *aldiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/aldiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aldiz

### Etymology

From *alaną (“to grow”) + *-þiz.

From *alaną + *-þiz, of the same origin as the "age" sense of Etymology 1.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂el-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑl.diz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ age, generation
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ lifetime
