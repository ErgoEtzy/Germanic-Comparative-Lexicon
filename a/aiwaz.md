## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aiwaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂óyu ~ *h₂yéws, reformed as a thematic (a-stem) noun. See the West Germanic descendant *aiw for more on the “law” sense.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-os, *h₂ey-, *h₂óyu, *h₂óyu ~ *h₂yéws

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯.wɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ long time, age, eternity
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ law
