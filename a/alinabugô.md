# *alinabugô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/alinabug%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *alinabugô

### Etymology

From *alinō (“ell, ulna, forearm”) + *bugô (“bend, bow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Heh₃l-, *bʰewgʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑl.in.bu.ɣɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ elbow
