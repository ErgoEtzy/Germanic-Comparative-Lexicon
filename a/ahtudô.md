# *ahtudô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ahtud%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ahtudô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *oḱtowós, from *oḱtṓw. An ordinal to *ahtōu (“eight”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *oḱtowós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑx.tu.ðɔːː/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ eighth
