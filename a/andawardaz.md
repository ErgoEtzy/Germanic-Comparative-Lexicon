# *andawardaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/andawardaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *andawardaz

### Etymology

From *anda- + *wardaz, literally “that which is turned facing (in front)”. Compare the semantics of Latin praesēns.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂énts, *wer-, *wert-, *wor-tós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɑndɑˈwɑr.dɑz/`

### Adjective
