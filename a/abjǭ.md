## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *abjǭ

### Etymology

From earlier *abjō, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂epyéh₂, from *h₂epó (“from, off, away”), that is, a substance that flows or comes off of something.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂epyéh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑβ.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ ebb
