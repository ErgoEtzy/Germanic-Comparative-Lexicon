## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ahslō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂eḱs-(i)l-eh₂-, from *h₂eḱs- (“axis”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂eḱs-(i)l-eh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑx.slɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ shoulder
