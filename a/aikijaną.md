# *aikijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/aikijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aikijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂oygéyeti, causative of *h₂eyg- (“to stir, set in motion”). Equivalent to *aikaną + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂oygéyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ to irritate, annoy
