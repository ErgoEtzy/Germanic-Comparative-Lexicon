## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *alu

### Etymology

From earlier *álut, from Pre-Germanic *álud, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂elut- (“beer; ruddy”), a root of disputed interpretation and descendants. Cognate with Proto-Slavic *olъ, Old Armenian աւղի (awłi).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂elut-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.lu/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ beer, ale
