## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *austraz

### Etymology

From either Proto-Indo-European *h₂éwsteros (“in the direction of dawn, east”) with syncope of the internal vowel, or (according to Gąsiorowski) from *h₂ewsér, thematicized as *h₂ewsrós, with a change *-sr- > *-str- (compare *straumaz for the sound change). Possibly a merger of the two.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂éwsteros
