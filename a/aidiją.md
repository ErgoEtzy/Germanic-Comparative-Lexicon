## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aidiją

### Etymology

Ultimately derived from Proto-Indo-European *h₁ey- (“to go”). Kroonen suggests nominalization of a possible gerundive *aidijaz (“passable”), from a Pre-Germanic *h₁oytyos.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁ey-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯.ði.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, neuter, reconstruction)_ isthmus, strait
