## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *abraz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *h₃(e)prós, equivalent to the lost strong verb *afaną + *-raz. See *aflą (“power, strength”) for further origin.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃ep-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑβ.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ strong, severe, exceeding, immense, great, huge, terrible
