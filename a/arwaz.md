## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *arwaz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *Herwos, which could come from either Proto-Indo-European *h₂er- (“to order, arrange, fit”) or *h₃er- (“to set in motion”). Compare, in particular, Proto-Tocharian *ārwer (“ready”), Avestan 𐬀𐬎𐬭𐬎𐬎𐬀𐬧𐬙 (aᵘruuaṇt, “quick”).

An old s-stem continuing Proto-Indo-European *Hérus (“scar, wound”); compare Sanskrit अरुस् (arus, “wound”). Probably cognate to Proto-Germanic *reufaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Hérus, *h₂er-, *h₃er-
