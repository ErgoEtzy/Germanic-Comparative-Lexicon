## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *anǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂en- (“ancestor”). Cognate with Latin anus (“old woman”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂en-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.nɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ ancestor, foremother
