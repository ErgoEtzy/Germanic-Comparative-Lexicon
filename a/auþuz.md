## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *auþuz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂éw-tu-s, itself a derivation from *h₂ew- (“to enjoy, consume, perceive”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂éw-tu-s
