## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *albiz

### Etymology

Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂elbʰós (“white”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *albʰós, *h₂elbʰós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑl.βiz/`

### Noun
