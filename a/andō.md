## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *andō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂énHteh₂ (“doorjamb, doorpost”), and related to Latin antae (“door pilasters”), Sanskrit आता (ā́tā, “door frame”), Old Armenian դրանդի (drandi, “doorpost, threshold”). The relation, if any, of the root to *h₂ent- (“face, front”), which has been suggested as an alternate derivation of the Germanic, is uncertain.
