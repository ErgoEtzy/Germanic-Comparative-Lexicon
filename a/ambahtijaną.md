# *ambahtijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ambahtijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ambahtijaną

### Etymology

From *ambahtaz (“servant”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂eǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑm.bɑx.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to serve, minister
