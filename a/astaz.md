## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *astaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₃ósdos (“branch”). Cognate with Ancient Greek ὄζος (ózos, “branch, twig”) and Old Armenian ոստ (ost).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃ósdos

### Pronunciation

### Noun
