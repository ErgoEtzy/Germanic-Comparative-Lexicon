[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/arjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *arjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂éryeti, from *h₂erh₃-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂erh₃-, *h₂éryeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑr.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to plough/plow
