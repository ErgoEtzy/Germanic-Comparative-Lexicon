# *alamahtīgaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/alamaht%C4%ABgaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *alamahtīgaz

### Etymology

From *ala- + *mahtīgaz, perhaps a calque of Latin omnipotēns.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *megʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑl.ɑ.ˈmɑx.tiːɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ almighty
