## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *asunz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *os(e)n- (“summer, harvest-time, harvest”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)h₁es-, *os(e)n-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ hireling, day laborer
