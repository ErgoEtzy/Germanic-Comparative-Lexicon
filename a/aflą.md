## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aflą

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *h₃éplom, from Proto-Indo-European *h₃ep- (“to work”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃ep-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑɸ.lɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ power, strength, might
