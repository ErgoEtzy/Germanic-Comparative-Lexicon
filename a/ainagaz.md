## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ainagaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *oynokós, equivalent to *ainaz + *-gaz. Cognate with Latin ūnicus.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁oy-no-kós, *oynokós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑɪ̯.nɑ.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ only; sole; singular; unique
