## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *alinō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *Heh₃l-én-eh₂, from *Heh₃l- (“to bow, elbow”). Cognate with Ancient Greek ὠλένη (ōlénē), Latin ulna, Welsh elin.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *Heh₃l-én-eh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.li.nɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ elbow
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ ell, cubit
