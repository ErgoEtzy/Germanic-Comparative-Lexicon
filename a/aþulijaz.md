## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aþulijaz

### Etymology

From *aþalą (“nobility; kin, lineage”) + *-jaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-yós, *h₂el-, *h₂et-, *h₂et-olo-

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, West, reconstruction)_ noble
