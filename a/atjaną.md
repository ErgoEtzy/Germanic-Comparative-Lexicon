## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *atjaną

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *h₃odéyeti, causative of Proto-Indo-European *h₃ed- (“to hate”).

From *etaną + *-janą (causative suffix). Diachronically, perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *h₁odéyeti; compare Sanskrit आदयति (ādáyati, “to feed”). However, the LIV considers the Germanic forms to be innovative formations.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁odéyeti, *h₃ed-
