## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Aþalagaizaz

### Etymology

From *aþalaz (“noble”) + *gaizaz (“spear”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *ǵʰey-, *ǵʰoysós

### Pronunciation

### Proper noun
