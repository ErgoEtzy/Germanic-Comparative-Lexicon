# *aitrōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/aitr%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aitrōną

### Etymology

From *aitrą (“pus”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂eyd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯.trɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to fester
2. _(reconstruction)_ to poison
