## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *anguz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂énǵʰus (“narrow, tight”), from *h₂enǵʰ- (“to constrict”). Cognate with Latin angustus, Sanskrit अंहु (áṃhu), Proto-Slavic *ǫzъkъ (“narrow, tight”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂enǵʰ-, *h₂énǵʰus
