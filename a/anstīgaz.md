# *anstīgaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/anst%C4%ABgaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *anstīgaz

### Etymology

From *anstiz + *-gaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃neh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑn.stiː.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ gracious, favorable
