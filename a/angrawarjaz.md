**Reconstruction:** *angrawarjaz

### Etymology

From *angraz (“meadow”) + *warjaz (“dweller”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂eng-, *h₂éngʷʰis

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a meadow-dweller, a member of the Angrivarii
