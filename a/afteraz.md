**Reconstruction:** *afteraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂epó + *-teros (contrastive suffix). See *ab.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂epó

- IPA: `/ˈɑɸ.te.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ back, behind
