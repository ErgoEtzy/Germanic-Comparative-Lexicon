# *abahandijaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/abahandijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *abahandijaz

### Etymology

From *aba- (“away, off”) + *handuz (“hand”) + *-jaz (“-ed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-yós, *h₂epó, *ḱómt

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.βɑ.ˌxɑn.di.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ out of one's hand, lost to one
