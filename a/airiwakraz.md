# *airiwakraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/airiwakraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *airiwakraz

### Etymology

From *airi (“early”) + *wakraz (“awake, watchful”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂éyeri, *weǵ-, *wóǵ-ros

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯ri.ˌwɑk.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ early-awake
