## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *awjô

### Etymology

From *awjō (“island”) + *-ô (“-er”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ō, *h₂ékʷeh₂

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a member of a Nerthus-worshipping Germanic tribe mentioned by Tacitus, living in either Öland or on the Jutland Peninsula
