# *aþna-

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/a%C3%BEna-)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aþna-

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂étnos (“that which goes; sun; year”), *h₂et-. Cognate with Sanskrit अत्न (atna, “the sun”), Latin annus (“year”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂étnos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑθ.nɑz/`
- IPA: `/ˈɑθ.nɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(East, Germanic, masculine, neuter, reconstruction)_ a year
