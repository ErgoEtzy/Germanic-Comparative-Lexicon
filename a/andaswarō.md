## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *andaswarō

### Etymology

From *anda- + *swarō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *swer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑn.dɑ.ˌswɑ.rɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ answer, reply
