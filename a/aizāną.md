## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aizāną

### Etymology

From *aizō (“reverence, honour”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂eys-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to honour
