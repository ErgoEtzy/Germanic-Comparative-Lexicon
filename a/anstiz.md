# *anstiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/anstiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *anstiz

### Etymology

From *unnaną (“to grant, bestow”) + *-þiz, with intrusive *-s- after nasals.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃neh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑns.tiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ favour, affection
