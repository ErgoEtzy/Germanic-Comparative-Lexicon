## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ailidaz

### Etymology

Substantive past participle of *ailijaną (“to kindle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂eydʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯liðɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ fire, pyre
