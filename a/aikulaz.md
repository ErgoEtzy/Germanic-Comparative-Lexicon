# *aikulaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/aikulaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aikulaz

### Etymology

Derived from the verb *aikaną (“to make one's own”)

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ frightened, terrified
2. _(reconstruction)_ excited; trembling
