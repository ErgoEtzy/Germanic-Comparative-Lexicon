## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Audawarduz

### Etymology

From *audaz (“wealth, riches”) + *warduz (“guard”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑu̯.ðɑ.ˌwɑr.ðuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
