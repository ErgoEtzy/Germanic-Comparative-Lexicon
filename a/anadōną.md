# *anadōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/anad%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *anadōną

### Etymology

From *anadô (“breath, spirit, zeal”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂enh₁-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to breathe; exhale
2. _(reconstruction)_ to puff
3. _(reconstruction)_ to sputter; snort
