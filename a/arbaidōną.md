## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *arbaidōną

### Etymology

From *arbaidiją, *arbaidiz (“labour, work, hardship”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *-tis, *h₃erbʰ-, *h₃órbʰos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈˈɑr.βɑi̯.ðɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to work, to labour
2. _(reconstruction)_ to toil
