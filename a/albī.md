# *albī

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/alb%C4%AB)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *albī

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *albʰih₂, derived from *albʰós (“white”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *albʰih₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑl.βiː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ river
2. _(feminine, reconstruction, as a proper noun)_ the river Elbe
