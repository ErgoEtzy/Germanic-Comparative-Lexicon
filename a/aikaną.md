## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aikaną

### Etymology

Derived from *aiganą (“to own”).

From Proto-Indo-European *oyk- (“to be out of tune, be grumpy or cranky”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *oyk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯.kɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb
