# *aljamarkiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/aljamarkiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aljamarkiz

### Etymology

From *aljaz (“other”) + *markō (“borderland”) + *-iz (“X-ed”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-yes-, *-yōs, *h₂élyos, *mórǵs

### Adjective

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction)_ foreign (literally, “other-borderland-ed”)
