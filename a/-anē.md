## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -anē

### Etymology

Possibly from a Proto-Indo-European *-neh₁, which would also be the source of the suffix in Sanskrit वि-ना (vi-nā, “without”). The final vowel itself is uncertain; it is usually given as *-ē based on the possibly related forms. See also *fernaz.
The first vowel *-a- might not have originally been part of the suffix at all, possibly being detached from the forms the suffix was attached to; but it is usually found as such in Germanic languages.
