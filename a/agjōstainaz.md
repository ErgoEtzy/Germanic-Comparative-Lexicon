## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *agjōstainaz

### Etymology

From *agjō (“edge”) + *stainaz (“stone”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂eḱ-, *h₂éḱ-ih₂ ~ h₂ḱ-yéh₂-s, *steyh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑɣ.jɔːˌstɑi̯.nɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ sharp-stone, cornerstone
