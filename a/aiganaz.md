# *aiganaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/aiganaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aiganaz

### Etymology

Adjectival formation from *aiganą (“to own, possess”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂eyḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯.ɣɑ.nɑz/`

### Verb

1. _(form-of, participle, past, reconstruction)_ past participle of *aiganą

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ own
