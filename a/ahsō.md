## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ahsō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂eḱs-eh₂, from *h₂eḱs- (“axle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂eḱs-, *h₂eḱs-eh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑx.sɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, feminine, reconstruction)_ axle
