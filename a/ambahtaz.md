# *ambahtaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ambahtaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ambahtaz

### Etymology

Borrowed from Gaulish ambaxtos (“vassal”), from Proto-Celtic *ambaxtos (“servant”). Unlike *rīks (“ruler, king”), which was also borrowed from Celtic, this word entered Germanic after the characteristic Germanic sound shifts took place.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂eǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑm.bɑx.tɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ servant
