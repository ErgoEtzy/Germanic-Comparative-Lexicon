# *austrōnijaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/austr%C5%8Dnijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *austrōnijaz

### Etymology

From *austraz + *-ōnijaz, the former from Proto-Indo-European *austero- (“eastern”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *austero-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑu̯s.trɔː.ni.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ eastern
