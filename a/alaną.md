# *alaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/alan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *alaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂életi, from *h₂el- (“to raise, feed, nourish”). Cognate with Latin alō (“nourish”, verb).

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁elh₂- (“to drive, move, go”). Compare Ancient Greek ἐλαύνω (elaúnō, “move”), Latin alacer (“lively, active, brisk”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁elh₂-, *h₂életi

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.lɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to grow
2. _(reconstruction)_ to grow old, to mature
3. _(reconstruction)_ to breed
