## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *anþeraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂énteros (“other”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂énteros

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑn.θe.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ second
2. _(reconstruction)_ other (of two)
