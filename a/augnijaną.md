# *augnijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/augnijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *augnijaną

### Etymology

From *augô (“eye”) + *-janą, this verb retains the nasal sound that is present in the inflection of the noun. Piecewise doublet of *augijaną which was formed earlier and lacks the nasal.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *-ō, *h₃ekʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑu̯ɣ.ni.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to show, to display
2. _(reconstruction)_ to watch, to peer
