[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ainaw%C4%ABgij%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ainawīgiją

### Etymology

From *ainaz (“one”) + *wīgiją (“battle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₁óynos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯.nɑ.ˌwiː.ɣi.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ single combat
