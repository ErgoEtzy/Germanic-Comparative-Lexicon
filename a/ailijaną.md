## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ailijaną

### Etymology

From *ailą (“fire”) + *-janą (“denominal suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂eydʰ-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to kindle
