# *augijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/augijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *augijaną

### Etymology

From *augô (“eye”) + *-janą, or perhaps inherited from Proto-Indo-European *h₃okʷéyeti (“to cause to see”), from *h₃ekʷ- (“to see; eye”) + *-éyeti (causative), with the same metathesis *agw- > *aug- seen in the noun; in the latter case it is unclear which word influenced the other. Piecewise doublet of *augnijaną, an apparently more recent formation.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-éyeti, *h₃ekʷ-, *h₃okʷéyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑu̯.ɣi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to show, display, cause to see
