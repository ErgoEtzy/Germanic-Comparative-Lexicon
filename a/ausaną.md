## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ausaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂ews- (“to scoop”). Probably related to Latin hauriō (“to draw, scoop up”) if the initial h- is indeed hypercorrect.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂ews-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑu̯.sɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to scoop, to draw (a liquid)
