# *ainakulaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ainakulaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ainakulaz

### Etymology

From *ainaz + *-kulaz, the second element being of uncertain origin. Compare Proto-Germanic *ainahaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ occurring alone or as one; sole; single
