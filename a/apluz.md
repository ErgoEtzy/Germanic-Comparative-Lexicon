## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *apluz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂ébōl (“apple, apple tree”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂ébōl

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑp.luz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, alt-of, alternative, masculine, reconstruction)_ alternative form of *aplaz
