## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *awǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂éwh₂eh₂, from *h₂éwh₂os (“grandfather”) + *-eh₂ (feminine suffix), or equivalent to *awô + *-ǭ.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂éwh₂eh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.wɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(East, Germanic, feminine, reconstruction)_ grandmother
