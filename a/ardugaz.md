## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ardugaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₃érdʰ-u-s, from *h₃erdʰ- + *-us. Cognate with Latin arduus.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-us, *h₃erdʰ-, *h₃érdʰ-u-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑr.du.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ steep
