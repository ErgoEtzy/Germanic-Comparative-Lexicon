## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aukô

### Etymology

Derived from *aukaną (“to increase”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂ewg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑu̯.kɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ increase, addition
