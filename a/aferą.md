## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aferą

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *h₂ép-erom, accusative/allative to an adjective *h₂ép-eros, itself a comparative formation from Proto-Indo-European *h₂epo (“off, away”); compare Sanskrit अपर (apara, “posterior, later; following”) and अपरम् (aparam).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂epo

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.ɸe.rɑ̃/`

### Adverb

1. _(reconstruction)_ after, following, later
