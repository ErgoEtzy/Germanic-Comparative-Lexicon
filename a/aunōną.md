# *aunōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/aun%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aunōną

### Etymology

From *aunaz (“lamb”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *h₂egʷnós, *h₂egʷ⁽ʰ⁾nós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑu̯.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to lamb, yean
