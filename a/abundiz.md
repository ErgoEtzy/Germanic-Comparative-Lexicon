# *abundiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/abundiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *abundiz

### Etymology

Possibly from *aba- (“away from”) + *undiz (“favor, affection”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tis, *h₂epó, *h₃neh₂-, *h₃n̥-né-h₂-ti, *h₃n̥néh₂ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.βun.diz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ envy
