# *arjaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/arjaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *arjaz

### Etymology

Plausibly cognate with Sanskrit आर्य (ārya, “honourable, respectable, orthodox, Aryan”), Proto-Celtic *aryos (“nobleman, free man”). If so, Proto-Indo-European *h₂eryos could be reconstructed.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂eryos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑr.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ distinguished, esteemed
