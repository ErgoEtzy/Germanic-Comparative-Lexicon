# *argaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/argaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *argaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₃orǵʰ-o-s, from *h₃erǵʰ- (“to copulate; ardent”). Cognate with Lithuanian eržùs, aržùs (“ardent, voluptuous, stubborn”), Russian ёрзать (jórzatʹ, “to fidget”), Hittite [script needed] (arkari, “to mount, cover, copulate”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃orǵʰ-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑr.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(derogatory, offensive, reconstruction)_ unmanly, effeminate, indecent
2. _(derogatory, offensive, reconstruction)_ cowardly, dishonorable, unworthy
