# *arbiją

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/arbij%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *arbiją

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₃orbʰ-yo-m, from Proto-Indo-European *h₃erbʰ- (“to change ownership”) (from which also *h₃órbʰos (“orphan”)).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃erbʰ-, *h₃orbʰ-yo-m

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑr.βi.jɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ heritage, inheritance
