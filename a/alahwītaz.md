# *alahwītaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/alahw%C4%ABtaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *alahwītaz

### Etymology

From *ala- + *hwītaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *ḱweyt-, *ḱweytōr

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌɑ.lɑ.ˈxʷiː.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ very white
