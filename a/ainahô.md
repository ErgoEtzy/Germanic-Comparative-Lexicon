# *ainahô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ainah%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ainahô

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *HoynHóḱo-, an adjectival derivation from Proto-Indo-European *h₁óynos (“one”). Compare Latin ūnicus (“only, sole”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁óynos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯.nɑ.xɔːː/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ only, single, sole
