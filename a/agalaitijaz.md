## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *agalaitijaz

### Etymology

Possibly haplologically syncopated from earlier *aglalaitjaz, from *aglaz (“shameful”) + *laitjaz (whence Rhinelandic German leizig (“annoying; excited”))..

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂egʰ-, *h₂egʰ-lo-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɑ.ɣɑ.lɑi̯.ti.jaz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ licentious
