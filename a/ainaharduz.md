# *ainaharduz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ainaharduz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *ainaharduz

### Etymology

From *ainaz (“one”) + *harduz (“hard”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)ker-, *h₁óynos, *kort-ús, *kret-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯.nɑ.ˌxɑr.ðuz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ courageous, constant
