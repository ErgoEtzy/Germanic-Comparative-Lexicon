# *aihtēr

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/aiht%C4%93r)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aihtēr

### Etymology

From *aiganą + reflex of Proto-Indo-European *-tḗr. Compare Sanskrit ईशितृ (īśitṛ, “owner, proprietor”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-tḗr, *h₂eyḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯x.tɛːr/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ owner, possessor
