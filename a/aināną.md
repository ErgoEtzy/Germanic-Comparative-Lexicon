# *aināną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ain%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aināną

### Etymology

From both *ainaz (“one”) + *-āną (stative-fientive suffix) and *ainaz (“one”) + *-āną (factitive suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti, *h₁óynos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯.nɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(intransitive, reconstruction)_ to be alone
2. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to isolate
