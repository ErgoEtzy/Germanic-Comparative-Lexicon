## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *akiz

### Etymology

Zero-grade i-stem derived from *akaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.kiz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ ache, pain
