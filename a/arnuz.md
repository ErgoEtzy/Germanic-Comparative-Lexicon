## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *arnuz

### Etymology

Reanalyzed as a u-stem from *arnunz, accusative plural of *arô (“eagle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ eagle
