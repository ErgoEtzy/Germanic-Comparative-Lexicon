[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/agalait%C4%AF%CC%84)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *agalaitį̄

### Etymology

From *agalaitijaz (“annoying, importunate”) + *-į̄.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-i-h₂, *-ih₂, *h₂egʰ-, *h₂egʰ-lo-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.ɣɑ.lɑi̯.tĩː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ impudence, importunity
