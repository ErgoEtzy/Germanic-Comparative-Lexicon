## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *auk

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂ew (“away, again”) + *ge (intensifier); compare Ancient Greek αὖ (aû, “again”), γε (ge, “at least, at any rate”), also found as Ancient Greek αὖγε (aûge). But compare also Proto-Indo-European *h₂ewg-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *gʰe, *h₂ew, *h₂ewg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɑu̯k/`

2. _(reconstruction)_ furthermore, in addition

### Conjunction
