# *aiganą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/aigan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aiganą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂eh₂óyḱe, from *h₂eyḱ- (“ability, possession”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂eh₂óyḱe, *h₂eyḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑi̯.ɣɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to possess, to own, to have
