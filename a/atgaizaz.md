## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *atgaizaz

### Etymology

From *at + *gaizaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂éd, *ǵʰey-, *ǵʰoysós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑt.ɣɑi̯.zɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ spear, pike, javelin
2. _(Germanic, North, masculine, reconstruction)_ halberd
