## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *alkǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₁el- (“a kind of bird”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₁el-

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, feminine, reconstruction)_ a kind of sea bird, auk
