# *aldrą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/aldr%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *aldrą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂el-dʰr-o-m or *h₂el-tr-ó-m, an instrumental formation from *h₂el- (“to grow, nourish”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂el-dʰr-o-m

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑl.drɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ life
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ lifetime, age
