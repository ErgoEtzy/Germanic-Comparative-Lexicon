## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *awô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂éwh₂ō, an on-stem variant of *h₂éwh₂os. Compare in particular Proto-Celtic *awon-tīr (“uncle”) and Latin avunculus (“~ maternal uncle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂éwh₂ō

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑ.wɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, masculine, reconstruction)_ grandfather
