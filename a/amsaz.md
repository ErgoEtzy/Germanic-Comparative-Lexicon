## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *amsaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₃émsos (“shoulder”). Compare Latin umerus, Sanskrit अंस (áṃsa).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃émsos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈɑm.sɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(East, Germanic, masculine, reconstruction)_ shoulder
2. _(Germanic, North, masculine, reconstruction)_ mountain ridge
