[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/abis%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *abisōną

### Etymology

From *ab (“away”) + *-isōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *h₂epó

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈa.βi.sɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cut, shear
