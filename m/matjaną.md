## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *matjaną

### Etymology

Traditionally derived from Proto-Indo-European *m̥h₂d-yéti ~ *m̥h₂d-yónti, from *meh₂d- (“to be wet”). Equivalent to *matiz (“food”) + *-janą; see *matiz for an alternative theory.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *meh₂d-, *m̥h₂d-yéti ~ *m̥h₂d-yónti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑt.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to eat, to have one's meal
