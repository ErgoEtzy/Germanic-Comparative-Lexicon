## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mainiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *moynis (“common, mutual”), from Proto-Indo-European *mey- (“to change, exchange, interchange, swap, share in common”). Compare Lithuanian mai̇̃nas (“exchange”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *mey-, *moynis

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑi̯.niz/`

### Adjective
