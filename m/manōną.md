# *manōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/man%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *manōną

### Etymology

Derived from Proto-Indo-European *men- (“to think”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *men-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑ.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to bring to mind; remind
2. _(reconstruction)_ to admonish; warn
3. _(reconstruction)_ to urge
