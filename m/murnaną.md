## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *murnaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)mer- (“to think; remember”).
For the meaning development compare commemorate, Russian помина́ть (pominátʹ), поми́нки (pomínki), помина́льный (pominálʹnyj).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)mer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmur.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to feel sorrow or grief; to mourn
