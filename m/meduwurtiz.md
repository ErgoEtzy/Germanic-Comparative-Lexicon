# *meduwurtiz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/meduwurtiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *meduwurtiz

### Etymology

From *meduz + *wurtiz, so-called for its use in mead.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *médʰu

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈme.ðu.ˌwur.tiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ meadwort, meadowsweet
