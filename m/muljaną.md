# *muljaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/muljan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *muljaną

### Etymology

Zero-grade ye-present, pre-Germanic *ml̥h₂ye-, from the root of *malaną (“to grind”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *melh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmul.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to crush (into pieces), grind
