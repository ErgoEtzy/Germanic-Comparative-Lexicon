## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mauraz

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *mowros, in which *mowr- is believed to be a metathesis of Proto-Indo-European *mor-w- (“ant”). Compare the synonym *miurijǭ.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *mor-w-, *morwi-

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, masculine, reconstruction)_ ant
