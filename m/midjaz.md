[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/midjaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *midjaz

### Etymology

From earlier *medjaz, from Proto-Indo-European *médʰyos (“middle”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *médʰyos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmið.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ middle, mid
