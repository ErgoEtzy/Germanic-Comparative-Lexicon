## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mēþiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *méh₁-ti-s ~ *m̥h₁-téy-s, from *meh₁- (“to measure”) + *-tis.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-tis, *meh₁-, *méh₁-ti-s ~ *m̥h₁-téy-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɛː.θiz/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, feminine, reconstruction)_ measure, degree
