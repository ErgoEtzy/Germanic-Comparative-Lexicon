# *muldô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/muld%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *muldô

### Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Indo-European *ml̥Hdʰṓ; identical to Sanskrit मू॒र्धन् (mūrdhán) and possibly derived from Proto-Indo-European *melh₃- (“to step forward”). Also compare Tocharian A malto (“firstly”) and perhaps Ancient Greek βλωθρός (blōthrós, “high (of trees)”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *melh₃-, *ml̥Hdʰṓ

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ The top of the head; the crown.
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ A summit or peak.
