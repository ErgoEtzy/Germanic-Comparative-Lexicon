## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mētiz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of *metaną (“to measure”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *med-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɛː.tiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ moderate, appropriate
2. _(reconstruction)_ appreciable, estimable
