## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *maþlijaną

### Etymology

From *maþlą (“speech, gathering, legal case”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *d

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑθ.li.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to speak
