## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *marǭ

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *mor- (“malicious female spirit”), from *mer- (“to die”). Cognate with Serbo-Croatian мо̏ра (“nightmare”), Old Irish Morrígan (“phantom queen”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *mor-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ evil (female) spirit; succubus; incubus
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ nightmare
