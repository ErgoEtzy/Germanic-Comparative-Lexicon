## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *managiþō

### Etymology

From *managaz + *-iþō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *monogʰos, *méǵh₂-, *méǵh₂s

- IPA: `/ˈmɑ.nɑ.ɣi.θɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ quantity; amount
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ multitude
