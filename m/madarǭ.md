## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *madarǭ

### Etymology

Perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *modʰro-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *modʰro-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑ.ðɑ.rɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ madder (Rubia tinctorum)
