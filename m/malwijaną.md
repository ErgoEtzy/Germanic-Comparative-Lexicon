## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *malwijaną

### Etymology

A causative verb related to *malaną. From Proto-Indo-European *melh₂- (“to beat; crush; grind”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *melh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑl.wi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to crush, grind, break
