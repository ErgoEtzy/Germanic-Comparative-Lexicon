# *mulkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/mulk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mulkōną

### Etymology

From *h₂ml̥ǵnéh₂-, a zero-grade iterative formation to Proto-Indo-European *h₂melǵ- (“to milk”). Closely related to the strong verb *melkaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂melǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmul.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to milk
