# *missijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/missijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *missijaną

### Etymology

From *missaz (“alternate, abberant, wrong”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *meyth₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmis.si.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to go wrong, fail
2. _(reconstruction, with-genitive)_ to miss
