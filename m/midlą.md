## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *midlą

### Etymology

From *midjō + *-ilą; see *midjaz (“middle”) for more.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

- IPA: `/ˈmið.lɑ̃/`

### Noun
