# *minnisōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/minnis%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *minnisōną

### Etymology

From *minniz (“less”) + *-isōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *mi-n-u-is

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmin.ni.sɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make less, make smaller
