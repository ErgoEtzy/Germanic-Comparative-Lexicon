## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mundōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *men(s)dʰh₁-, "to put in mind", a compound of Proto-Indo-European *men- (“to think”) + *dʰeh₁- (“to put”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *men(s)dʰh₁-, *men-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmun.dɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to pay attention, observe
