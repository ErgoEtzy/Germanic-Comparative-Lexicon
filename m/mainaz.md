## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mainaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *meh₂y-, which could be reduced from *(s)meh₂- (“to beckon”) + *-yeti. See also Latin maereo (“I am mournful”), Sanskrit मेनि (meni, “wrath, punishment”), Proto-Slavic *mamiti (“to deceive”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)meh₂-, *meh₂y-, *yeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑi̯.nɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ mean, damaging, hurtful
2. _(reconstruction)_ unjust, false
