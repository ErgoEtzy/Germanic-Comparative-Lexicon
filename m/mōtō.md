# *mōtō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/m%C5%8Dt%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mōtō

### Etymology

Unknown. Pokorny derives this word from Proto-Indo-European *med- (“to measure”), similar to *metaþs, *metaną, etc.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *med-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɔː.tɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ tax
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ toll
