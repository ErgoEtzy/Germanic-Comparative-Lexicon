# *maidijaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/maidijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *maidijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *moyth₂-éye-ti, from *meyth₂- (“to change, exchange”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *meyth₂-, *moyth₂-éye-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑi.ði.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to exchange, change
2. _(reconstruction)_ to damage, hurt
