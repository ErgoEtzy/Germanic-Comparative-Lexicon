# *mildijaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/mildijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mildijaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *melh₂- (“to beat, pound, grind, mill”). Cognate with Latin mollis (“soft, gentle”), as well as perhaps Sanskrit म्लात (mlātá, “soft”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *melh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmil.di.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ mild, soft, tender
