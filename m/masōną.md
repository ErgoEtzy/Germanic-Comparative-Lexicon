# *masōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/mas%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *masōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *mh₃-s(é)h₂-, from *meh₃-. Related to *mōjaną (“to tire”) and *mōþuz (“tired, weary”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *mh₃-s(é)h₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑ.sɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to labour, struggle with, be confused
