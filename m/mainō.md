## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mainō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *moy(-)n-éh₂, apparently from a Late PIE root *mey(-)n- (“intent; mind”); see *mainijaną for cognates.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *moy(-)n-éh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑi̯.nɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ mind; opinion
