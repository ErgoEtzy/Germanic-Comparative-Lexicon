# *mainą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/main%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mainą

### Etymology

From the same ultimate source as *mainijaną (“to think, mean”), evolved from an earlier sense of "false oath."

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑi̯.nɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ damage, hurt
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ unjustice, sin
