# *melwą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/melw%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *melwą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *melh₂-wo-m, from *melh₂- (“to beat; crush; grind”); by synchronic analysis, a wa-stem nominal formation from *malaną (“to grind”). Cognate with Latin molō (“mill; grist; grind”, verb).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *melh₂-, *melh₂-wo-m

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmel.wɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ ground corn, meal, flour
