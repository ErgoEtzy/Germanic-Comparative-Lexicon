[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/m%C4%93n%C5%8D%C3%BEs)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mēnōþs

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *meh₁n-ot-s, a derivative of Proto-Indo-European *mḗh₁n̥s (“month, moon”), related to *mēnô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *mḗh₁n̥s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɛː.nɔːθs/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ month
