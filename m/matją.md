# *matją

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/matj%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *matją

### Etymology

An ablaut variant of the root *mēs-, mōs, from Proto-Indo-European *meh₂d- (“to drip, ooze; grease, fat”). Compare *matiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *meh₂d-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑ.tjɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ food
