## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *maginōną

### Etymology

Derived from *maginą (“ability, power, might, main”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *megʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑ.ɣi.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to strengthen, to become strong
2. _(reconstruction)_ to have the power to, to be able to
