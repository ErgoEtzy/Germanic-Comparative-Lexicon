# *midjasumaraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/midjasumaraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *midjasumaraz

### Etymology

From *midjaz (“mid”) + *sumaraz (“summer”). Cognate with Proto-Celtic *medyosamonyos.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *médʰyos, *sm̥-h₂-ór-o-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˌmið.jɑ.ˈsu.mɑ.rɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ midsummer
