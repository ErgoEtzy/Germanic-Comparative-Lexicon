## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mundiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *mén-ti-s ~ *mn̥-téy-s (“thought”). Equivalent to *munaną (“to think”) + *-þiz (“nominal suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *men-, *mén-ti-s, *méntis

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmun.diz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ thought, remembrance
