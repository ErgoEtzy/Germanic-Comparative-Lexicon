## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *missalīkaz

### Etymology

From *missa- + *-līkaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *meyth₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmis.sa.ˌliː.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ diverse, various
