# *mōtaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/m%C5%8Dtan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mōtaną

### Etymology

A preterite-present derived from a secondary perfect of *mḗd-e-ti (“to measure, ensure compliance, take care of”): *me-mṓd-e (literally “to have measured, taken care of”). Alternatively, from the same root as *mōtą (“meeting”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *med-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɔː.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be allowed, to be permitted to, may
