## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mōijǭ

### Etymology

From *mō- (“mother”) + *-ijǭ (feminine diminutive ending).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɔː.i.jɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, feminine, reconstruction)_ aunt (mother's sister)
