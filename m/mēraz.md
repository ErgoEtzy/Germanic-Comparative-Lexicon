## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mēraz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *meh₁-ró-s (“measurable, great”), from *meh₁- (“to measure”) + *-rós. Cognate with Proto-Celtic *māros (“big, great”)

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-rós, *meh₁-, *meh₁-ró-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɛː.rɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ great; excellent
2. _(reconstruction)_ famous
