# *murþrą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/mur%C3%BEr%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *murþrą

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *mŕ̥trom, an instrumental formation to Proto-Indo-European *mer- (“to die”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *mer-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmur.θrɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ murder
