## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mēdwō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂meh₁- (“to mow”) + *-dwō; see also Proto-Germanic *mēþą (“a mowing”). Cognate with Latin metere (“to cut down; reap; mow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂meh₁-

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, feminine, reconstruction)_ meadow
