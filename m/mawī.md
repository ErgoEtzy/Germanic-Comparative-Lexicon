# *mawī

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/maw%C4%AB)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mawī

### Etymology

From earlier *magwī, from *maguz (“boy”), with feminine suffix; ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *megʰ- (“young”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *megʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑ.wiː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ girl
