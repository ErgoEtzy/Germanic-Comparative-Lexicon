[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/makk%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *makkô

### Etymology

Kluge's law alternant of *makô ~ **umkkaz after paradigmatic leveling of the stem to *makk-, from Proto-Indo-European *mógō ~ *m̥gnós, or perhaps *mōkô ~ **makkaz from *méh₂gō ~ *mh₂gnós; see the main entry for more on the root.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *mógō ~ *m̥gnós

### Noun

1. _(alt-of, alternative, masculine, reconstruction)_ alternative form of *makô
