## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mērijaz

### Etymology

Vṛddhi gerundive of otherwise unattested strong verb *meraną, the original meaning being "shining", with these terms apparently tracing back to a Proto-Indo-European *mer- (“to shine, sparkle, excel”). Compare Ancient Greek μαρμαίρω (marmaírō, “to flash, sparkle”), Latin merus (“pure”), as well as Old Church Slavonic мѣръ (měrŭ), Old Irish már, mór.
