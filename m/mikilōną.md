# *mikilōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/mikil%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mikilōną

### Etymology

From *mikilaz (“great, large”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-dʰlom, *-e-ló-s, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *-lós, *-tlom, *-trom, *méǵh₂s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmi.ki.lɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to make great, magnify
