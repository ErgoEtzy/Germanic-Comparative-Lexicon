# *mōtōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/m%C5%8Dt%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mōtōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *med- (“to measure”). Equivalent to *mōtǭ (“leisure, idleness, opportunity”) + *-ōną, (compare German Muße (“leisure”)).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *med-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be idle, at leisure
2. _(reconstruction)_ to be available, unrestricted
