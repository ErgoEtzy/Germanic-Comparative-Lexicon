# *mōdagaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/m%C5%8Ddagaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mōdagaz

### Etymology

From *mōdaz (“mind; courage; zeal”) + *-gaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *mō-tó-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɔː.ðɑ.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ brave; courageous
2. _(reconstruction)_ bold
