# *midjanahts

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/midjanahts)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *midjanahts

### Etymology

From *midjaz (“middle”) + *nahts (“night”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *médʰyos, *nókʷts

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmið.jɑ.ˌnɑxts/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ midnight
