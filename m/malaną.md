[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/malan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *malaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *melh₂- (“to beat, crush, grind”). Cognate with Latin molō (“mill, grist, grind”, verb).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *melh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑ.lɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to grind
