# *maisǭ

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/mais%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *maisǭ

### Etymology

Unknown; compared to Proto-Celtic *mesal-(s)kā (“blackbird”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂mes-l- (Latin merula). Or, possibly related to Norwegian Nynorsk meis (“weak”) and Flemish mijzen (“shatter”), possibly related to Proto-Germanic *maisa- (“small”), or possibly related to Dutch miezerig (“small, measly”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂mes-l-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑi̯.sɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ titmouse, chickadee
