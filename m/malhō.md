[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/malh%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *malhō

### Etymology

Traditionally derived from Proto-Indo-European *molko- (“leather pouch”) and connected with Ancient Greek μολγός (molgós, “leather bag”). However, Kroonen notes a formal mismatch between the Germanic and Hellenic terms, and suggests that both terms were borrowed from Thracian, or otherwise from some prehistoric Wanderwort.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *molko-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑl.xɔː/`

### Noun
