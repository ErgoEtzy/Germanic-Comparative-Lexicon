# *meduz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/meduz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *meduz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *médʰu. Cognate with Sanskrit मधु (madhu), Avestan 𐬨𐬀𐬜𐬎 (maδu), Ancient Greek μέθυ (méthu).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *médʰu

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈme.ðuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ intoxicating drink
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ mead
