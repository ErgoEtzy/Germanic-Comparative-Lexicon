## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *marsaz

### Etymology

Unknown, though possibly related to *marzijaną (“to hinder, impede”).

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑr.sɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a member of the Marsi, a Germanic tribe known for their participation in the battle of the Teutoburg Forest on the side of Arminius
