## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mastaz

### Etymology

Uncertain. According to Kroonen, the term is likely a Celto-Germanic isogloss. Kroonen rejects the connection with Latin mālus, arguing that it requires a phonologically problematic pre-form *mazd-slo-. The term may be further related to Proto-Celtic *mazdyo- (whence Middle Irish maide) and Proto-Slavic *mostъ (“bridge, bridgework”), though Kroonen considers the Slavic terms to be borrowings from Germanic.
