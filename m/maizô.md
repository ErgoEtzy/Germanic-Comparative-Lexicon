## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *maizô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *méh₂-is-on-, a comparative formation in *-is- from *meh₂- (“good; opportune”). Cognate with Proto-Celtic *māros (“big, great”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *méh₂-is-on-
