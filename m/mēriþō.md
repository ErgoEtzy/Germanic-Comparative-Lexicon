## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mēriþō

### Etymology

From *mēraz + *-iþō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-rós, *-teh₂, *meh₁-, *meh₁-ró-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɛː.ri.θɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ fame; popularity; celebrity; renown; reputation
