# *maiwaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/maiwaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *maiwaz

### Etymology

Probably of non-Indo-European substrate origin, as no certain cognates exist outside of Germanic, aside from French mauve (“seagull”), which may or may not be ultimately borrowed from the Germanic. Or, of imitative origin.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑi̯.wɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ gull, seagull
