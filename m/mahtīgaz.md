[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/maht%C4%ABgaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mahtīgaz

### Etymology

From *mahtiz + *-gaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *megʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑx.tiː.ɣɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ mighty
