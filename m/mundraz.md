# *mundraz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/mundraz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mundraz

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *mn̥-dʰh₁-ró-s, from Proto-Indo-European *men(s)dʰh₁- (“to see; strive; long for; be zealous for; be lively”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *men(s)dʰh₁-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmun.drɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ awake; alert
2. _(reconstruction)_ lively; brisk
3. _(reconstruction)_ eager; zealous; avid
