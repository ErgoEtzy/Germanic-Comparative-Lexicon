# *mōhô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/m%C5%8Dh%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mōhô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *méh₂k-ō ~ *m̥h₂k-né-s, from *meh₂k- + *-ō. Close cognate with Ancient Greek μήκων (mḗkōn, “poppy”). Also related to Proto-Slavic *makъ (“poppy”), Albanian mokth (“pheasant's eye”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-ō, *meh₂k-, *méh₂k-ō ~ *m̥h₂k-né-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɔː.xɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ poppy, poppyseed
