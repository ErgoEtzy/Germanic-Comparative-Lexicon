# *melhmô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/melhm%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *melhmô

### Etymology

From Pre-Germanic *mélḱmō, derived from a lost verb *melhaną, from Proto-Indo-European *melḱ- (“to cloud, overcast”). Compare Swedish moln, Swedish mulen.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *melḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmelx.mɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ cloud, overcast
