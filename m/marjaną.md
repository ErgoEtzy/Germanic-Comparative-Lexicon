## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *marjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *morh₂-éye-ti, iterative of Proto-Indo-European *merh₂- (“to grab, crush, squash”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *merh₂-, *morh₂-éye-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑr.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to pound; crush; grind
