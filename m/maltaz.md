# *maltaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/maltaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *maltaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *moldos, a derivation of *(s)meld- (“to soften, melt”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *moldos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑl.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ weak, soft, mild
2. _(reconstruction)_ bitter, sour, having an unpleasant taste
3. _(reconstruction)_ spoiled, gone bad
