## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mulkną

### Etymology

Derived from *melkaną (“to milk”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂melǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmul.knɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ dairy, products made from milk
