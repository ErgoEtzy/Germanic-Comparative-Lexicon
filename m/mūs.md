## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mūs

### Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Indo-European *múHs. Cognates include Proto-Slavic *myšь (“mouse”). More cognates: see there.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *múHs

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/muːs/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ mouse
