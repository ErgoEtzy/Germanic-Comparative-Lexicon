## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mundō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *mh₂-nt-éh₂ (“the beckoning one”), from *(s)meh₂- (“to beckon”). Cognate with Proto-Italic *manus (“hand”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *mh₂-nt-éh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmun.dɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ hand
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ protection, security
