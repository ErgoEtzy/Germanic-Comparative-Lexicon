[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/mur%C3%BE%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *murþą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *mr̥tós (“dead”), from *mer- (“to die”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *mer-, *mr̥tós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmur.θɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ death
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ killing, slaying, murder
3. _(especially, neuter, reconstruction)_ a killing in secret where the body was hidden (which was considered particularly shameful)
