# *munaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/munan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *munaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *memóne, from *men- (“to think”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *memóne, *men-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmu.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to think, to consider, to believe
2. _(reconstruction)_ to remember (with genitive of the thing/things being remembered)
