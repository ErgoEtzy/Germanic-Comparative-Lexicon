## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mēkijas agjō

### Etymology

From *mēkijaz + *agjō. The phrase may well have originated in a later Germanic language and spread to the others through diffusion during late Antiquity or the early Middle Ages, meaning the term needs not necessarily have existed in Proto-Germanic.
