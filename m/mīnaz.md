## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mīnaz

### Etymology

From pre-Proto-Germanic *mey-no-s, from Proto-Indo-European *éǵh₂ ~ *h₁mey, whence also *miz (“me”), + *-nos (adjectival suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *éǵh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmiː.nɑz/`

### Determiner

1. _(reconstruction)_ my, mine; 1st person singular possessive
