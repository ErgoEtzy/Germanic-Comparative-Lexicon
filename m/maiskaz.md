## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *maiskaz

### Etymology

From earlier *maihskaz, from Proto-Indo-European *moiḱ-sḱ-o/eh₂-, from *meyḱ- (“mix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *meyḱ-, *moiḱ-sḱ-o/eh₂-

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, masculine, reconstruction)_ mixture, mash
