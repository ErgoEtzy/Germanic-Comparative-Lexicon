## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *manją

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *mony-o-m, from *mony- (“crest, mane”), probably from *mon- (“neck”). Compare Sanskrit मणि (maṇi, “neck ornament, gem”), Old Irish muin (“neck”), Latin monīle (“necklace, collar; jewels”).
