# *mōjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/m%C5%8Djan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mōjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *meh₃-ye-ti, from *meh₃- (“to cause toil, be a burden”). Cognate with Tocharian A māskā- (“to be difficult”), Proto-Slavic *màjati (“to detain, tire, exhaust”), Ancient Greek μῶλος (môlos, “turmoil”), Latin mōlēs (“burden, mass”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *meh₃-ye-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɔː.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to tire
