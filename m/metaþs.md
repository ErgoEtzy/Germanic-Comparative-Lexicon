## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *metaþs

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *medéts, oblique *medot-, a t-stem derived from Proto-Indo-European *med- (“to measure”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *med-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈme.tɑθs/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ measure
