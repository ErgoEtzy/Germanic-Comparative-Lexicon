# *mōdērlausaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/m%C5%8Dd%C4%93rlausaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mōdērlausaz

### Etymology

From *mōdēr + *-lausaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *lewh₁-, *méh₂tēr

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɔː.ðɛːr.ˌlɑu̯.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ motherless; without a mother.
