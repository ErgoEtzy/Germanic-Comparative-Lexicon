[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/mulkiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mulkiz

### Etymology

Verbal adjective derived from the original stative of *melkaną (“to milk”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂melǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmul.kiz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ milkable, able to be milked
2. _(reconstruction)_ milch, giving milk
