# *matilausaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/matilausaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *matilausaz

### Etymology

From *matiz (“food”) + *-lausaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *lewh₁-, *meh₂d-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑ.ti.lɑu̯.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ foodless, lacking food
