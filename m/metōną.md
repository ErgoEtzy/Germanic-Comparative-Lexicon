[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/met%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *metōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *med- (“to think about, reason, decide”). Cognate with Latin meditor (“think, reflect, consider”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *med-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈme.tɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to measure, mete, assign
2. _(reconstruction)_ to weigh in mind, consider
