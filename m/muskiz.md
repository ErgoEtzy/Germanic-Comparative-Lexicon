## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *muskiz

### Etymology

From *muskaz (“moisture”), from Proto-Indo-European *mews-, *mūs- (“moist, damp; swamp, moss, mildew”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *mews-

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ mildew, mould
