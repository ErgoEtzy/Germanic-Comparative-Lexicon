## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mahtijaną

### Etymology

From *mahtiz (“power, might”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(me)mógʰe, *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *megʰ-, *mógʰtis

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑx.ti.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to empower, to strengthen
2. _(reconstruction)_ to use force, to act violently
