[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/m%C4%93gaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mēgaz

### Etymology

Suggested to be related to *maguz (“boy, relative”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɛː.ɣɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ son-in-law; or more generally, a relative through marriage
