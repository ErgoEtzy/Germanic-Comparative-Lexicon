## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mują

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *mū- (“fly, mosquito”). Cognate with Latin musca.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *mū-

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, North, neuter, reconstruction)_ fly (insect)
