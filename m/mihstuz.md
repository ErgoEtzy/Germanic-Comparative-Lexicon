## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mihstuz

### Etymology

Probably from *mihsą (“dung”) + *-þuz, the former related to *mīganą (“to urinate”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₃meyǵʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmix.stuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ manure
