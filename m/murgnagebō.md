[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/murgnageb%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *murgnagebō

### Etymology

From *murginaz (“morning”) + *gebō (“gift”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(o)-éh₂, *-eh₂, *-oh₁, *-éh₂, *gʰe, *gʰebʰ-, *gʰebʰ-e-ti, *gʰo-, *h₁ep-, *merkʷ-, *ḱóm

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmurɣ.nɑˌɣe.βɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ morning gift
