# *munāną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/mun%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *munāną

### Etymology

From *munaną (“to think, consider”) + *-āną or *muniz (“mind, desire”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti, *memóne, *men-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to intend, to aim for
2. _(reconstruction)_ to value, to hold in high regard
