## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *manō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *mon- (“neck, nape; ornament”), which is sometimes further derived from Proto-Indo-European *men- (“to project, peak, tower over, protrude”). Compare with Latin monile (“necklace”) and Old Irish muin (“neck”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry
