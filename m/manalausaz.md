# *manalausaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/manalausaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *manalausaz

### Etymology

From *mann- (“man”) + *-lausaz (“less”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(dʰ)ǵʰmō, *(dʰ)ǵʰmon-, *lewh₁-, *mon-, *ǵʰmṓ

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑ.nɑ.ˌlɑu̯.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ without a man, without a husband
