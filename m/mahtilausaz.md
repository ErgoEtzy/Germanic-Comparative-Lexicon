# *mahtilausaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/mahtilausaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mahtilausaz

### Etymology

From *mahtiz (“might”) + *-lausaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(me)mógʰe, *lewh₁-, *megʰ-, *mógʰtis

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑx.ti.ˌlɑu̯.sɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ powerless, mightless
