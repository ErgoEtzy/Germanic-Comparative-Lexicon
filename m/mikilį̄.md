## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mikilį̄

### Etymology

From *mikilaz (“large, great”) + *-į̄.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-dʰlom, *-e-ló-s, *-i-h₂, *-ih₂, *-lós, *-tlom, *-trom, *méǵh₂s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmi.ki.lĩː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ largeness, immensity, multitude
