## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *midi

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *meth₂, from *me (“with”). Cognate with Ancient Greek μετά (metá, “among, between, with”), Sanskrit स्मत् (smat, “together, at the same time”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *meth₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmi.ði/`

### Preposition

1. _(reconstruction)_ with, by, through
2. _(reconstruction)_ along, together
