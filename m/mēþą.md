## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mēþą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *h₂méh₁-to-, from *h₂meh₁- (“to mow”), synchronically analyzed as a to-stem nominal formation from *mēaną (“to mow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *h₂meh₁-, *h₂méh₁-to-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɛː.θɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ a mowing
