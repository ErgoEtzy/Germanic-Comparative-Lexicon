## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mainafullaz

### Etymology

From *mainą (“damage, hurt”) + *-fullaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑi̯.nɑˌɸul.lɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ evil, wicked, nefarious
