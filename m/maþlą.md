# *maþlą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/ma%C3%BEl%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *maþlą

### Etymology

Uncertain. Perhaps from a zero-grade stem of *mōtaną (“to be permitted to, to be allowed”) (compare *mōtą (“meeting”) for the semantics) + *-tlom (“instrument suffix”). In the case of *maþlą, the root-final Proto-Indo-European *d of their common root may have been lost before the instrumental *-þlą ( < *-tlom) suffix, as in Proto-West Germanic *seþl from *séd-tlom; however, the existence of such a phonetic process is disputed, as a sequence of two dentals normally yields Germanic *-ss-, and there are alternative explanations for *seþl.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *d

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑθ.lɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ gathering
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ speech
3. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ legal case
4. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ agreement
