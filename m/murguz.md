# *murguz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/murguz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *murguz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *mréǵʰus ~ *mr̥ǵʰéws (“short”). Cognate with Latin brevis (“short, small, narrow, shallow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *mréǵʰus, *mréǵʰus ~ *mr̥ǵʰéws

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmur.ɣuz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction, of time or duration)_ short; brief
2. _(reconstruction)_ slow; leisurely
