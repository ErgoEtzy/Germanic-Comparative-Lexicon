# *midlōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/midl%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *midlōną

### Etymology

From *midlą (“middle”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmið.lɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to join in the middle, to meet in between
2. _(reconstruction)_ to split in the middle, split in halves
3. _(reconstruction)_ to mediate
