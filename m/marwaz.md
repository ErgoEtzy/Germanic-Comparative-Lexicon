# *marwaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/marwaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *marwaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *merw- (“to rub, pack”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *merw-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑr.wɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ brittle; delicate; soft; mellow
