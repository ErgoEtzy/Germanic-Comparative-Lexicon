## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mainaswarô

### Etymology

From either *mainaz + *swarô or *mainaswarjaną + *-ô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(s)meh₂-, *-ō, *meh₂y-, *swer-, *yeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑi̯.nɑ.ˌswɑ.rɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ perjurer
