## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *manalīkô

### Etymology

From *manalīkaz (“manlike”) + *-ô. Equivalent to *manô (“man”) + *-līkaz (“-like, -ly”) + *-ô.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(dʰ)ǵʰmō, *(dʰ)ǵʰmon-, *-ō, *mon-, *ǵʰmṓ

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑnɑˌliː.kɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ image, humanlike figure
