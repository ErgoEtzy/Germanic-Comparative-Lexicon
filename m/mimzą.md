# *mimzą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/mimz%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mimzą

### Etymology

Thematicized variant of Proto-Indo-European *mḗms (“meat, flesh”). Cognate with Latin membrum (“member, limb”), Sanskrit मांस (māṃsa, “meat, flesh”), Old Armenian միս (mis, “meat”). More at member.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *mems-, *mḗms

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmim.zɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ flesh
