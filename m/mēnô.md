# *mēnô

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/m%C4%93n%C3%B4)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *mēnô

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *méh₁nōs, from *mḗh₁n̥s (“moon, month”). Originally s-stem, later remodeled as n-stem based on nominative singular. Cognate with Lithuanian mė́nuo.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *méh₁nōs, *mḗh₁n̥s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɛː.nɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ moon
