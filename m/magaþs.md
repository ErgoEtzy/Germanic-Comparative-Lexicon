# *magaþs

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/maga%C3%BEs)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *magaþs

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *magʰ-ot-(i-), a feminine derivative of *maguz (“boy”) (with the suffix also seen in *brūdiz (“bride”)), but this pair of words is likely of foreign or substrate origin, as is the likely cognate Proto-Celtic *magot-axtā (“young girl, servant girl”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmɑ.ɣɑθs/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ maiden, girl
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ virgin
