# *maisō

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/mais%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *maisō

### Etymology

Uncertain. Possibly from *maisaz (“sheepskin sheath”), from Proto-Indo-European *moysós (“sheep, sheepskin, fleece, hose”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *moysós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈmai̯.sɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ fleece satchel or backpack
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ container, vessel, basket, crate
