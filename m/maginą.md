## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *maginą

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *megʰ- (“to be able”). Synchronically analyzed as a nominal formation from *maganą.

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ ability, power, might, main
