# *minþilą

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/min%C3%BEil%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *minþilą

### Etymology

Of uncertain origin. Possibly from *menþ- (“teeth, mouth, palate”), from Proto-Indo-European *men-t-, from *men- (“to stick out, project”); or, alternatively from Proto-Indo-European *menth₂- (“to stir, grind, chew”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *men-t-, *menth₂-

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ denture
2. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ bit (of a bridle)
