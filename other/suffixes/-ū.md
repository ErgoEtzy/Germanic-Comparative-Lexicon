## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -ū

### Etymology

Inherited from Proto-Indo-European *-uh₁ (u-stem instrumental singular), from *-us (u-stem suffix) + *-h₁ (instrumental singular suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-h₁, *-uh₁, *-us

### Suffix

1. _(morpheme, reconstruction)_ Forms the instrumental singular of masculine/feminine u-stem nouns.
   - _sunuz (“son”) + *-ū → *sunū (“with the son”)_

### Germanic descendants

- **Proto-West Germanic:** *-ū
  - **Old High German:** -u — _inherited; uncertain_
