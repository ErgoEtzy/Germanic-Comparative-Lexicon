## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -āną

### Etymology

Somewhat uncertain. Reconstructed by Ringe as originating from earlier *-ajaną, from Proto-Indo-European *-oyéti (factitive verb suffix). The reconstruction of this Indo-European suffix is uncertain, but it would also be the origin of the Ancient Greek -όω (-óō) contracted verbs.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-oyéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ Creates stative - fientive verbs from other verbs, from adjectives and from nouns.
2. _(reconstruction)_ Creates denominative verbs from nouns.
3. _(reconstruction)_ Creates factitive verbs from adjectives.
