## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -į̄

### Etymology

Compare Proto-Indo-European *-i-h₂, whence Proto-Germanic *-ī.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-i-h₂, *-ih₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ĩː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ -th. Forms abstract nouns from adjectives which represent 'the state, quality or measure of' the adjective.
