## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** -ōnǫ̂

### Etymology

From *-ō (ō-stem suffix) + *-anǫ̂ (an-stem genitive plural suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(o)-éh₂, *-eh₂, *-oh₁, *-éh₂

### Suffix

1. _(morpheme, reconstruction)_ Forms the genitive plurals of ōn-stem nouns.
   - _sagǭ (“story”) + *-ōnǫ̂ → *sagōnǫ̂ (“stories', of stories”)_
