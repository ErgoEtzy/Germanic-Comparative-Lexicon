## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *Þeudōberhtaz

### Etymology

From *þeudō (“people, nation”) + *berhtaz (“bright, shining”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tós, *bʰer(H)ǵ-tó-s, *bʰerHǵ-, *tewtéh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθeu̯.ðɔː.ˌberx.tɑz/`

### Proper noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ a male given name
