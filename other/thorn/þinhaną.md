# *þinhaną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEinhan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þinhaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *tenk- (“to be suitable”). Possibly related to Lithuanian ti̇̀kti (“to fit, suit”), pati̇̀kti (“to appeal, like”), Latvian tikt (“to arrive at”), Proto-Slavic *tęknǫti (“to point, to provide”), and perhaps Proto-Celtic *tonketos (“destiny”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tenk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθĩː.xɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to thrive
