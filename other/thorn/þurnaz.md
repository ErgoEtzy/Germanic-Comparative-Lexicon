[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEurnaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þurnaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *tr̥nós (“sharp, stiff; thorn”). Related to Sanskrit तृण (tṛ́ṇa, “grass”), Proto-Slavic *tьrnъ (“thorn”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tr̥nós

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθur.nɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ thorn, briar
