# *þrawō

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEraw%C5%8D)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þrawō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *trowH-eh₂-ti, from *trewh₁- (“to endure, suffer”). Cognate with Lithuanian trivóti (“to endure”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *trowH-eh₂-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθrɑ.wɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ a longing, suffering
