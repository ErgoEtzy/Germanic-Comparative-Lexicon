## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þar

### Etymology

From *þa- (“that”) + *-r (“locative suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *só

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/θɑr/`

### Adverb

1. _(demonstrative, reconstruction)_ there, in that place
