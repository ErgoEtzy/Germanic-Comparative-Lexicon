# *þaggōną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEagg%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þaggōną

### Etymology

Factitive/causative counterpart to *þagjaną, from Proto-Indo-European *t(H)k-, *tak-. The voicing of -gg- may be analogical, if this reflects an older -neh₂-stem (which is expected to result in *þakkōną).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *t(H)k-, *te(H)k-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθɑɡ.ɡɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(Germanic, North, reconstruction, transitive, with-accusative)_ to silence
