# *þurstuz

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEurstuz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þurstuz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *térs-tu-s ~ *tr̥s-téw-s, from *ters- (“dry”) + *-tus. Cognate to Proto-Celtic *tartus.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-tus, *ters-, *térs-tu-s ~ *tr̥s-téw-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθurs.tuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ dryness, thirst
