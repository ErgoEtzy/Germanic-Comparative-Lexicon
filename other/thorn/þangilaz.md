## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þangilaz

### Etymology

According to Orel, from *þinhaną (“to thrive”) + *-ilaz; compare *þingą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-dʰlom, *-e-ló-s, *-lós, *-tlom, *-trom, *tenk-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, poetic, reconstruction)_ prince, leader
