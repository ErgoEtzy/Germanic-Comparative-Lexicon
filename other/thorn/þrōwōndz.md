[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEr%C5%8Dw%C5%8Dndz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þrōwōndz

### Etymology

From *þrōwōną (“to thrive, prosper”) + *-ndz (“-er”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-onts

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ an inhabitant of the area roughly corresponding to modern-day Trøndelag
