## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þigjaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *tek-yé-ti, from the root Proto-Indo-European *tek- (“to take by the hand, receive, obtain”). Cognate with Old Irish techtaim (“I possess”), Lithuanian tèkti (“to be granted”), Ancient Greek κτάομαι (ktáomai, “to acquire”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tek-, *tek-yé-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθiɣ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to accept, receive
2. _(reconstruction)_ to implore, beg
