# *þarbaz

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEarbaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þarbaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *terp- (“to satisfy; to need”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *terp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθɑr.βɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ useful, beneficial
2. _(reconstruction)_ necessary
3. _(reconstruction)_ needy, having need
