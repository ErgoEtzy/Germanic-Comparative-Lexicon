# *þwangiz

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEwangiz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þwangiz

### Etymology

From earlier *þwanguz, from *þwinhaną (“to press”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθwɑŋ.ɡiz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ restraint, pressure
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ belt, strap, thong
