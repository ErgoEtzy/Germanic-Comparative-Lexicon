# *þwinhaną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEwinhan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þwinhaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *twenk- (“to squeeze, press, pack; pressure”). Cognate with Tocharian A twāṅkā- (“to wear”), Lithuanian tveñkti (“to dam up”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *twenk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθwĩː.xɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to constrain
2. _(reconstruction)_ to force
