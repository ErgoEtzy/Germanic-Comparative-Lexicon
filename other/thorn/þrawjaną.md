# *þrawjaną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BErawjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þrawjaną

### Etymology

From *þrawō (“suffering, longing”) + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti, *trowH-eh₂-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθrau̯.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to desire, long for
2. _(reconstruction)_ to suffer, endure
3. _(reconstruction)_ to threaten
