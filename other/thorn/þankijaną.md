# *þankijaną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEankijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þankijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *tong-éye-ti, causative of *teng- (“to think, feel, perceive, know”). Cognate with Latin tongeō (“know”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *teng-, *tong-éye-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθɑŋ.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to perceive, to think
