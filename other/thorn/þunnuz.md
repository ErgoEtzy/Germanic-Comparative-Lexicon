## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þunnuz

### Etymology

From earlier *þunwuz, from Proto-Indo-European *tn̥h₂wús, variant of *ténh₂us (“thin”). Cognate with Sanskrit तनु (tanú), Latin tenuis.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tenh₂-, *tn̥h₂wús

### Pronunciation
