# *þrinhwaną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BErinhwan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þrinhwaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *trenkʷ- (“to beat, hew, press”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *trenkʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθrĩː.xʷɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to press, to push
2. _(reconstruction)_ to force
