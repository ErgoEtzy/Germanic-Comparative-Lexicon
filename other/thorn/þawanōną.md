[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEawan%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þawanōną

### Etymology

Closely related to *þawjaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθɑu̯.ɑ.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to thaw, melt
2. _(reconstruction)_ to moisten
