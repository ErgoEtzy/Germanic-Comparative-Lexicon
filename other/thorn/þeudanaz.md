## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þeudanaz

### Etymology

From *þeudō (“people, tribe”) + *-anaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *(e)-ós, *(ó)-os, *-os, *-ós, *-ō, *tewtéh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθeu̯.ðɑ.nɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ king, leader of the people
