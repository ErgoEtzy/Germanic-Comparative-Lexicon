# *þrēaną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEr%C4%93an%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þrēaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *tréh₁-e-ti, from *treh₁-, *terh₁- (“to rub, turn, pierce”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tréh₁-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθrɛː.ɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to twist, to turn
