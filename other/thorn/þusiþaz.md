## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þusiþaz

### Etymology

The past participle of a lost verb, *þusjaną (“to be calm”), from Proto-Indo-European *tews- (“to be happy”). Cognate with Sanskrit तुष्यति (túṣyati, “to be satisfied, to become calm”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tews-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθu.si.θɑz/`

### Adjective
