## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þulāną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *telh₂- (“to bear, to undergo, to endure”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *telh₂-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to bear, to endure
