# *þingijaną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEingijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þingijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ti-n-k-yé, cognate with Lithuanian tikėti.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ti-n-k-yé

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθiŋ.ɡi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to believe, to trust
2. _(reconstruction)_ to hope
