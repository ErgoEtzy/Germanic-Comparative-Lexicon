## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þiudiskaz

### Etymology

From *þeudō + *-iskaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *tewtéh₂

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθiu̯.ðis.kɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ of the people, popular
2. _(reconstruction)_ of the tribe
