[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEr%C4%93duz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þrēduz

### Etymology

From *þrēaną + *-þuz. Also corresponds to a hypothetical Proto-Indo-European *treh₁tus.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *treh₁tus

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθrɛː.ðuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ thread, twisted fibre
