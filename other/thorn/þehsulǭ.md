[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEehsul%C7%AD)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þehsulǭ

### Etymology

From *þehs- (“to hew; hackle”), from Proto-Indo-European *teḱs- (“weaponry”). Cognate with Latin tēlum (“throwing axe, weapon, spear”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *teḱs-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθex.sɑlɔ̃ː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ axe
