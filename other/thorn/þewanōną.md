# *þewanōną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEewan%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þewanōną

### Etymology

From *þewanaz (“servant”) + *-ōną. Compare *þewaz (“servant, slave”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tekʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθɛ.wɑ.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to serve
