[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEurz%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þurzāną

### Etymology

From *þursuz (“dry”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-oyéti, *ters-, *térs-u-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθur.zɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be dry
