# *þewanōstasamaz

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEewan%C5%8Dstasamaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þewanōstasamaz

### Etymology

From *þewanōstaz (“service”) + *-samaz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *tekʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθe.wɑ.nɔː.stɑˌsɑ.mɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ able to serve, diligent, obliging
