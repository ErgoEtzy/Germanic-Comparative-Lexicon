## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þurznaną

### Etymology

From *þursuz (“dry”) + *-naną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ters-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθurz.nɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(intransitive, reconstruction)_ to dry out
2. _(reconstruction)_ to wither
