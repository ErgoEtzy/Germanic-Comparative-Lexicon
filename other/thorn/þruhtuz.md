## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þruhtuz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *treg- (“firm, fast”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)terg- (“to be stiff; rigid”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)terg-, *treg-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθrux.tuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ strength; fortitude
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ endurance; suffering
