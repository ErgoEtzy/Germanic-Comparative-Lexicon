[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEarb%C4%81n%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þarbāną

### Etymology

From *þarbō (“need”) + *-āną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *terp-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθɑr.βɑː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to need, to want, to lack
