# *þukkōną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEukk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þukkōną

### Etymology

From earlier paradigm *þukkōþi ~ *þugunanþi, from pre-Proto-Germanic *tuk-nā́-ti ~ *tuk-un-ánti, iterative from Proto-Indo-European *(s)tewk- (“to shove, push”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)tewk-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθuk.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to shove, push out of the way
