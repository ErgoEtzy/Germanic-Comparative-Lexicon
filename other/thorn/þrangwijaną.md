# *þrangwijaną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BErangwijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þrangwijaną

### Etymology

From *þrinhwaną + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *trenkʷ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθrɑŋʷ.ɡʷi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to press
2. _(reconstruction)_ to push, to force
