# *þiudijaz

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEiudijaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þiudijaz

### Etymology

Uncertain. Kroonen suggests a possible derivation from a pre-form *tewh₂-tiό-, itself possibly from the root *tewh₂-. Alternatively, Orel suggests that the term ought to be derived from *þeudō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθiu̯.ði.jɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ good, kind
2. _(reconstruction)_ friendly
