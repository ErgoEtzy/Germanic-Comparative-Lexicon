# *þebōną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEeb%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þebōną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *tep- (“warm; hot”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tep-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθe.βɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ To heat; make hot
2. _(reconstruction)_ To be heated; be hot
