# *þagjaną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEagjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þagjaną

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *tHk- (“to be silent”). Cognate with Proto-Italic *takēō. Kroonen reconstructs a stative formation common to the Latin and Germanic.

Iterative-intensive formation derived from *þigjaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tHk-, *te(H)k-, *tek-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθɑɣ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to be silent, to not speak
