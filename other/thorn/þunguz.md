## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þunguz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *téngʰ-u-s ~ *tn̥gʰ-éw-s (“heavy; to pull back”), cognate with Lithuanian tingùs (“heavy”), Old Church Slavonic тѧжькъ (tęžĭkŭ, “idem”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tengʰ-, *téngʰ-u-s ~ *tn̥gʰ-éw-s

### Pronunciation

### Adjective
