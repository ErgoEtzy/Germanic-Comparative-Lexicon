# *þēhtuz

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BE%C4%93htuz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þēhtuz

### Etymology

From a lengthened-grade of Proto-Indo-European *tetḱ- (“to hew, fashion”) + *-þuz (“result noun suffix”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tetḱ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθɛːx.tuz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ wick, strand of rope
