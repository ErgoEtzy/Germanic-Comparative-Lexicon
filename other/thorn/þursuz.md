# *þursuz

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEursuz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þursuz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *térs-u-s, from *ters- (“dry”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ters-, *térs-u-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθur.suz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ dry, lacking water
