## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þaką

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *tog-o-s, from *(s)teg- (“to cover”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)teg-, *tog-o-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθɑ.kɑ̃/`

### Noun

1. _(neuter, reconstruction)_ roof, cover
