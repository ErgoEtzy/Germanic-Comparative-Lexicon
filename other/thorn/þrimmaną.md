## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þrimmaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *trem- (“to tremble (in fear)”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *trem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθrim.mɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to swell
