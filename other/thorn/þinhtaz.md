# *þinhtaz

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEinhtaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þinhtaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ténktos, from *tenk- (“to contract”). Cognate with Sanskrit तञ्च् (tañc, “to contract, coagulate”); see there for more cognates.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tenk-, *ténktos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθĩːx.tɑz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ tight
