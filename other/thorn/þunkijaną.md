## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þunkijaną

### Etymology

Zero-grade ye-present, pre-Germanic *tn̥g-ye/o-, from Proto-Indo-European *teng- (“to think, perceive”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *teng-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθuŋ.ki.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to seem, (impersonal, with dative subject) to think
