[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEilj%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þiljōną

### Etymology

From *þiljǭ (“board, plank”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθil.jɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cover with planks
