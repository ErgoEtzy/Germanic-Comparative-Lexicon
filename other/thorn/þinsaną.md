# *þinsaną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEinsan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þinsaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *tens- (“to stretch”). Related to Sanskrit तंस् (taṃs, “to shake about, move”), Lithuanian tęsti (“to stretch (out); to continue, extend”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tens-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθin.sɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stretch
