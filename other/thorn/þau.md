## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þau

### Etymology

From pre-Germanic *to-we, "or that/than", from Proto-Indo-European *to- (“that”) + *-we (“or”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-we, *to-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/θɑu̯/`

### Adverb

1. _(East, Germanic, reconstruction)_ in that case
2. _(East, Germanic, reconstruction)_ or
3. _(East, Germanic, reconstruction, after comparative)_ than
