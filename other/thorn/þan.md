## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þan

### Etymology

Unclear. Probably from demonstrative *sa + an unclear suffix, possibly an earlier *-no or *-ne.
As an alternative, from earlier *þam, from Proto-Indo-European *tóm (the accusative form of *tó), whence also Latin tum.
Dunkel derives *þana “then” from Proto-Indo-European *tó-na (“so, that way”), from the pronominal stem *tó- + *-na (instrumental adverbial suffix).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tó-na, *tóm, *tóna
