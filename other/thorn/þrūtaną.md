[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEr%C5%ABtan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þrūtaną

### Etymology

Possibly from Proto-Indo-European *trewd- (“to push, to thrust”). Cognate with Latin trūdō.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *trewd-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθruː.tɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to swell
