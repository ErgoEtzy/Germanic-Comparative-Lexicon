# *þrabōną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BErab%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þrabōną

### Etymology

From *þrebaną, from Proto-Indo-European *trep- (“to scamper, trip, trample, tremble, step”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *trep-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθrɑ.βɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to trot, trample
2. _(reconstruction)_ to press, drive, urge, compel
3. _(reconstruction)_ to quarrel, flite
