# *þarzijaną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEarzijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þarzijaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *tors-éye-ti (“to make dry”), causative thematic present of *ters- (“dry”). Equivalent to *þersaną + *-janą.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tors-éye-ti, *torséyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθɑr.zi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to cause to dry
