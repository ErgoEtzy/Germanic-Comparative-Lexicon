## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þegō

### Etymology

Derived from *þigjaną (“to accept, receive”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tek-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθe.ɣɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(Germanic, West, feminine, reconstruction)_ a taking, receiving, acceptance
