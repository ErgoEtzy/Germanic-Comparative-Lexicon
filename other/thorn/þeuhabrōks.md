## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þeuhabrōks

### Etymology

From *þeuhą + *brōks.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ loincloth
2. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ trousers
