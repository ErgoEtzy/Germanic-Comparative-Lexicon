# *þurzijaną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEurzijan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þurzijaną

### Etymology

By surface analysis, *þursuz (“dry”) + *-janą. Based on the comparison with Sanskrit तृष्यति (tṛṣyati), a Proto-Indo-European verb *tr̥syéti can be reconstructed.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tr̥syéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθur.zi.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to parch, make dry
2. _(reconstruction, transitive)_ to make thirsty
3. _(impersonal, reconstruction, with-accusative)_ to be thirsty
