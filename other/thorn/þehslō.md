## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þehslō

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *tetḱ-ló-s, or *tétḱ-dʰlo-m, with cluster simplification, both from *tetḱ- (“to cut, hew; to create”). Cognate with Old Irish tál (“adze”), Proto-Slavic *tesla (“adze”). See also Proto-West Germanic *þehsan (“to fashion, break flax”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tetḱ-ló-s

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθɛx.slɔː/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ adze
