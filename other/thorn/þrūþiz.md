## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þrūþiz

### Etymology

From *þrewwaną (“to grow, to thrive”) + *-þiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-tis, *trew(H)-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθruː.θiz/`

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ strength
