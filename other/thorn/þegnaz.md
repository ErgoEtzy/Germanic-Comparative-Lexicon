# *þegnaz

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEegnaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þegnaz

### Etymology

Often compared with Ancient Greek τέκνον (téknon, “child”). Kroonen instead sees a derivation from *þigjaną (“to request, beg”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθeɣ.nɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ retainer, servant
