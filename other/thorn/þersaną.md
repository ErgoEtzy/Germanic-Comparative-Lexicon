## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þersaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ters- (“dry”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ters-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθer.sɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to dry out, to make dry
