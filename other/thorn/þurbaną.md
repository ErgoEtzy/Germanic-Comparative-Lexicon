# *þurbaną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEurban%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þurbaną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *tetórpe, from *terp- (“to satiate”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *terp-, *tetórpe

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθur.βɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to need, to require
2. _(reconstruction)_ to have to
