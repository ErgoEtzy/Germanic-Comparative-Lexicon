## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þurstiz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *térstis (with the oblique stem generalized), from *ters- (“dry”). Equivalent to *þersaną + *-þiz.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *ters-, *térstis

### Noun

1. _(feminine, reconstruction)_ dryness, thirst (lack of/need for water)
