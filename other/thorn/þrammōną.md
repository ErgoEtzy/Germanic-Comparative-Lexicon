# *þrammōną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEramm%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þrammōną

### Etymology

An o-grade iterative formation to Proto-Indo-European *trem- (“to tremble (in fear)”), closely related to the strong verb *þrimmaną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *trem-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθram.mɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to trample, to press
2. _(reconstruction)_ to make a noise
