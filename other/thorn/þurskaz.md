## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þurskaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *ters- (“to dry; wither; thirst”) + *-ḱos.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *-ḱos, *ters-
