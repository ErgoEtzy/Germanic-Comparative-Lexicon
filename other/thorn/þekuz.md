## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þekuz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *tégus (“thick”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tégus

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθe.kuz/`

### Adjective

1. _(reconstruction)_ thick
