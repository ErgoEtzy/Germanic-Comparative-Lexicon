## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þramuz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *terh₂- (“to penetrate, cross over, overcome, overtake”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *terh₂-

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ rim, edge, brink
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ side
3. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ end
