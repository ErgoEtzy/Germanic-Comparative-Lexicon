# *þewaz

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEewaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þewaz

### Etymology

Uncertain; the quasi-Indo-European reconstruction is *tew-o-s. Possibly either from earlier *þegwaz, from Proto-Indo-European *tekʷ- (“to run”), or from *tew(h₂)- (“to swell, grow”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tekʷ-, *tewh₂-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθe.wɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ slave, servant
