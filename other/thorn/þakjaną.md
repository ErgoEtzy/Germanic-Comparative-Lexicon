## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þakjaną

### Etymology

From *þaką (“roof”) + *-janą.

Possibly from a Proto-Indo-European *teh₂ǵ- (“burn”), and tentatively cognate with Ancient Greek τήγανον (tḗganon), τάγηνον (tágēnon, “frying pan”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)teg-, *teh₂ǵ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθɑk.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb
