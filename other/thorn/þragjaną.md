# *þragjaną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEragjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þragjaną

### Etymology

Apparently from a Proto-Indo-European *tregʰ-, which may be a variant of *dʰregʰ- (“to run”). Or otherwise perhaps related to Proto-Slavic *tъ̑rkъ (“to run”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *tregʰ-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθrɑɣ.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to run
