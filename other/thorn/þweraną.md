# *þweraną

[Alphabetical index](../../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/%C3%BEweran%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *þweraną

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *(s)twérH-e-ti, from *(s)twerH- (“to stir; to propel”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *(s)twérH-e-ti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈθwe.rɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to swirl, turn, stir
