# *pikkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/pikk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *pikkōną

### Etymology

Unknown; from a stem *pikk-, of apparently imitative origin, + *-ōną. Compare Proto-Germanic *bikjaną, but also Latin beccus and Latin bucca.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈpik.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to pick, peck, prick
