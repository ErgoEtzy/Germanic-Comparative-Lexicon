# *prikkōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/prikk%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *prikkōną

### Etymology

Probably from Pre-Germanic *bri(g/ǵ)néh₂-, though very likely not of Proto-Indo-European origin. Possibly onomatopoeic.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No Proto-Indo-European ancestor is listed.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈprik.kɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to prick
