# *prikjaną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/prikjan%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *prikjaną

### Etymology

From *prikiz (“prick”) + *-janą. Likely a variant of *pikkōną (“to strike, peck”) with an emphatic -r-.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-ye-ti, *-yeti, *-yé-ti, *-yéti, *-éh₁-ti, *-éh₁ti, *-éye-ti, *-éyeti

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈprik.jɑ.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stick; prick
