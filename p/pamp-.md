# *pamp-

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/pamp-)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *pamp-

### Etymology

Uncertain. Perhaps from Proto-Indo-European *bamb- (“round object”). Compare Lithuanian bamba (“navel, belly button”), Lithuanian bumbulas (“bulge, knob, bulb”), Proto-Slavic *pǫpъ (“bud, navel”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bamb-

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to swell
