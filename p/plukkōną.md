## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *plukkōną

### Etymology

Possibly represents a Pre-Germanic *blu(gʰ/ǵʰ)néh₂-, with Kroonen leaving the origin open. Alternatively, borrowed from Vulgar Latin *pilūc(i)cāre, but the historical evidence is shaky.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No PIE form is listed or machine-resolvable in this snapshot.

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to pluck, pull out or off
