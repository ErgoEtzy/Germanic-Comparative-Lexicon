# *preunōną

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/preun%C5%8Dn%C4%85)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *preunōną

### Etymology

From *preunaz (“pin, needle”) + *-ōną.

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**PIE form(s) resolved through Proto-Germanic components:** *-(e)h₂yéti, *-eh₂yéti, *-h₂ti, *-h₂yéti, *brew-, *brewk-, *brewn-

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈpreu̯.nɔː.nɑ̃/`

### Verb

1. _(reconstruction)_ to stab
2. _(reconstruction)_ to stitch
