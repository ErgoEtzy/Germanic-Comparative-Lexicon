# *pullaz

[Alphabetical index](../_Index.md) · [View on Wiktionary](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Germanic/pullaz)

## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *pullaz

### Etymology

From Proto-Indo-European *bl̥nos (“bog, marsh”). Cognate with Lithuanian balà (“bog; marsh; swamp; pool”), Latvian bala (“a muddly, treeless depression”), Russian боло́то (bolóto, “swamp; bog; marsh”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

**Directly mentioned PIE form(s):** *bl̥nos

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈpul.lɑz/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ pool, puddle
2. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ ditch, creek
