## Proto-Germanic

**Reconstruction:** *pāwô

### Etymology

Borrowed from Latin pāvō (“peacock”).

### Proto-Indo-European ancestry

No PIE form is listed or machine-resolvable in this snapshot.

### Pronunciation

- IPA: `/ˈpɑː.wɔːː/`

### Noun

1. _(masculine, reconstruction)_ peacock, peafowl
